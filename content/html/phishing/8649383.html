Smart Protocols

Get Started

Smart Protocols is an open source protocol for connecting decentralised applications to mobile wallets with QR code scanning or deep linking. You can interact securely with any Dapp from their mobile phone, making Smart Protocols wallets a safer choice compared to desktop or browser extension wallets.
