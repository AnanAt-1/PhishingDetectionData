FACULTY
FACULTY
Dr. Kenneth Lee
ABOUT
Dr Kenneth Lee received his Bachelor of Dental Surgery from the University of Sydney in 1988 and has been practising as principal dentist of Todays Dental in Sydney, Australia, for over 30 years. He is a founding faculty member and principal lecturer for IAADent and regularly lectures and teaches nationally and internationally.
QUALIFICATIONS
Bachelor of Dental Surgery (Sydney University)
Masters of Science in Oral Implantology (Goethe University)
Masters of Dental Science in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics (Jaume I University)
Fellow of the International College of Dentists
Fellow of the Pierre Fauchard Academy
Past President of the International Association for Orthodontics (USA) 2015/2016
