Step 1: Unbox Your Trezor

In your Trezor package, you’ll find:

  • A Trezor hardware wallet (Trezor Model One or Model T).
  • A USB cable for connecting the wallet to your device.
  • Recovery seed cards for writing down your recovery phrase.
  • Quick-start instructions.

Step 2: Visit Trezor.io/Start

  1. Open your browser and go to Trezor.io/Start.
  2. Verify the authenticity of the website to avoid phishing scams.

Step 3: Install Trezor Suite

  1. Download Trezor Suite:
    • Trezor Suite is the official app for setting up and managing your wallet.
    • Download it for desktop (Windows, macOS, Linux) or access the browser version.
  2. Install the App:
    • Follow the installation instructions for your operating system.

Step 4: Connect Your Device

  1. Use the USB cable to connect your Trezor device to your computer or smartphone.
  2. Launch Trezor Suite and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your device.

Step 5: Initialize Your Trezor Wallet

  1. Create a PIN:
    • Set up a secure PIN to protect your wallet from unauthorized access.
  2. Backup Your Recovery Phrase:
    • Write down the 12- or 24-word recovery phrase displayed on your Trezor.
    • This phrase is crucial for recovering your wallet if the device is lost or damaged.
    • Store the phrase securely offline and never share it with anyone.

Step 6: Add Cryptocurrency Accounts

  1. Use Trezor Suite to add accounts for the cryptocurrencies you want to manage.
  2. Install apps for specific coins if required.

Step 7: Start Managing Your Crypto

  1. Send and Receive Crypto: Use Trezor Suite to conduct secure transactions.
  2. Track Your Portfolio: Monitor your balances and transaction history.
  3. Explore Advanced Features: Buy, exchange, or stake cryptocurrencies directly in Trezor Suite.
