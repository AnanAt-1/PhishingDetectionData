Welcome to GWEN, the Global Warming Education Network!

GWEN is a U.S.-based nonprofit organization dedicated to spreading awareness and encouraging action relating to global warming and its adverse impacts.

Check out the various parts of our website (above) to find out how you can help slow global warming! One easy and immediate way you can help is by signing our "Please Educate and Lead" petition to President Barack Obama here.

To get more information about GWEN and our activities, please e-mail us at info@gwenet.org or call us during U.S. Eastern Time business hours at 781-863-1400.  If you would like to help the climate movement, take a look at our Take Action page to find some ideas on how you can help slow global warming.

You may also want to learn more about the common misconceptions that make global warming so controversial and confusing. To find out even more about what you can do to help slow global warming, or to get more information on the science behind global warming please visit our Links page.

Please vist our Ongoing Activities page for some other examples of what GWEN is doing.
