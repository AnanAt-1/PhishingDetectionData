1. Selecting Your Device

Upon arriving at the page, you will first need to select the Ledger device you own. This ensures that you receive the correct setup instructions tailored to your specific model.

2. Installing Ledger Live

The setup process involves downloading and installing the Ledger Live application, which is the official software used to manage your cryptocurrency assets. Ledger Live is available for both desktop and mobile platforms, and it allows you to manage your crypto, check balances, and perform transactions.

3. Setting Up Your Ledger Device

After installing Ledger Live, you'll be guided through the steps to physically set up your Ledger device. This includes connecting the device to your computer or smartphone, choosing a PIN code, and writing down your recovery phrase. The recovery phrase is crucial, as it is the only way to recover your funds if your device is lost or damaged.

4. Adding Accounts

Once your device is set up, you can add accounts for various cryptocurrencies within Ledger Live. The platform supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies, and you can manage multiple accounts simultaneously.

5. Secure Your Device

The setup guide emphasizes the importance of security throughout the process. You'll receive tips on how to keep your recovery phrase safe, avoid phishing scams, and ensure that your Ledger device remains secure.
