Why us?

Style Meets Substance

Your website’s design is your brand’s identity, but a mismatched Google Form can disrupt the experience. With Formfacade, you can seamlessly match the look and feel of your forms to your website, ensuring your brand stays consistent while delivering a polished and professional user experience.

Turn Forms into Lead Magnets

Go beyond a basic contact form—build forms that increase conversion. With Formfacade, you can analyze potential customer needs by asking thoughtful questions and scoring their responses. Unlike standard Google Forms, you can assign points to answers, allowing you to analyze and generate insights thats useful for your customers.

Gain a 360° Customer View

Feedback is essential for retention. With Formfacade, you can gather continuous feedback about your products or services directly from customers. By combining lead scoring with ongoing feedback, you can build a comprehensive view of your customers’ journey, enabling you to refine your offerings and improve satisfaction over time.
