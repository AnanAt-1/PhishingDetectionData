Updates

Well it's been just over 7 years now since I came out to California from Florida to work in the wonderful world of Television. I LOVE LA! The weather, the mountains, the crazy creative energy and the amazing melting pot of people all out here to follow their dreams of being a part of the Entertainment industry! I've been steadily continuing my profession of Producing on various TV Productions here in Hollywood. I've been fortunate to have Produced, Directed, Written and Coordinated for some of the biggest studios and networks in town like Disney, Paramount, Warner Bros, G4TV, ABC, FOX, G4TV, E!, MTV, NBC, VH1, LTN (wow, that’s a lot of 3 lettered networks). I've taken a bit of hiatus from production now and am back pursuing one of my other career loves, that of working full-time in Motion Picture Marketing for the Walt Disney Studios. Juggling that along with a family you can imagine my time is maximized, but always full of fun and new things so keep checking back for updates frequently! Thanks... -Steve C
