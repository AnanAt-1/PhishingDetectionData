Welcome to Power Electro Mechanical Engineering

 Leading Manufacturer of Electrical Engineering Products

At Power Electro Mechanical Engineering, we specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality electrical engineering products that power infrastructure and industrial projects across the region. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we have earned a reputation as a trusted partner for engineering solutions.

Our Expertise

We provide a comprehensive range of products designed to meet the highest standards of performance, durability, and safety in the electrical engineering sector. Our manufacturing processes are supported by cutting-edge technology and a team of highly skilled professionals.

Our Product Range

Cable Trays

Our robust and corrosion-resistant cable trays are designed to support insulated electric cables used for power distribution and communication. Available in a variety of materials and finishes, they are suitable for commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects.

Street Lighting Poles

We manufacture street lighting poles that combine strength, aesthetics, and long-lasting performance. These poles are engineered to withstand harsh weather conditions while providing reliable support for lighting systems in urban and rural areas.

Earthing Materials

Safety is paramount in any electrical system. Our range of earthing materials, including electrodes, strips, and grounding accessories, ensures effective fault current dissipation, protecting both people and equipment from electrical hazards.

Solar Structures

In support of the global shift to renewable energy, we design and produce solar mounting structures that are durable, easy to install, and optimized for maximum solar panel efficiency. Suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial solar energy projects.
