  2. Diversification: iTrustCapital allows you to diversify your retirement portfolio beyond stocks and bonds. This diversification can help mitigate risk and potentially enhance returns, especially in volatile market conditions.

  4. Ease of Use: iTrustCapital’s user-friendly interface makes it easy to manage your investments, monitor market trends, and execute trades.
  5. Cost-Effective: iTrustCapital offers a cost-effective solution with transparent pricing. The platform charges a low monthly fee and a flat-rate transaction fee, ensuring that you know exactly what you’re paying for.

Creating an iTrustCapital Account

Before you can log in to iTrustCapital, you need to create an account. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting started:

  1. Visit the iTrustCapital Website: Go to the official iTrustCapital website (https://www.itrustcapital.com).
  2. Sign Up: Click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the homepage.
  3. Enter Personal Information: You will be prompted to provide your personal information, including your name, email address, phone number, and date of birth. Make sure to enter accurate information as it will be used to verify your identity.
  4. Create a Password: Choose a strong password for your account. Ensure that it is unique and not easily guessable. A combination of upper and lower case letters, numbers, and special characters is recommended.
  5. Verify Your Email: After completing the sign-up form, you will receive a verification email. Click on the link provided in the email to verify your email address.
  6. Complete KYC (Know Your Customer): iTrustCapital is required to comply with regulatory standards, which includes verifying your identity. You will need to upload a government-issued ID and possibly additional documentation to complete this step.
  7. Fund Your Account: Once your account is verified, you can fund it by rolling over an existing IRA, transferring funds from a different retirement account, or making a new contribution.
