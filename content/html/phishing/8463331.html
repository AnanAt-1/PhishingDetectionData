Frequent ask questions

What is a link shortener?

It is a tool to shorten long URLs. This way, you get a shorter URL that will be automatically redirected to the original URL securely.

How to shorten a link?

Copy the URL you want to shorten, paste it in the 'URL?' field, click on 'Shorten URL'. Done! Your URL has been shortened, and you can even generate a QR code for it.

What are the benefits of shortening links?

A short link is easier to remember, and you can share it much faster with your audience and generate quicker traffic to your links.

Is the link shortener free?

Yes, you can use it as many times as you want without paying anything.

Is tinu.be safe?

Yes, no personal data is collected or shared to shorten URLs. We also do not control the content of the URLs used; that is the responsibility of their creators.
