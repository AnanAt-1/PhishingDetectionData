Setting Up Your Trezor Hardware Wallet

Setting up your Trezor hardware wallet begins by visiting Trezor.io/start and selecting your device model. Once installed, you'll connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable and follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your wallet and generate a secure seed phrase.
