The Faculty Guide to Getting Started With AI

Education leaders are still looking for guidance on how to implement AI in ways that align with academic values and support long-term student success. This guide is your starting point.

Created in collaboration between The University of Texas at Austin and Grammarly, this guide offers 20 practical AI activities and 9 lesson plans that are easy to use and adaptable to various subjects. Whether you're just starting to explore AI or looking to expand its use in the classroom, this guide will help transform AI from a transactional tool into a powerful catalyst for new thinking and creativity.

What You'll Get:
  • 20 AI-based activities for faculty and students
  • 9 lesson plans to foster transformational AI use
  • Ready-to-use template to replicate at your institution
  • Practical guidance to improve both student learning and faculty workflows
Download the guide today and continue your AI journey with confidence!
