    Welcome to our villa

    Villa ANDREAS is a proposal for relaxation and rest in the village of Makriotika in the northern part of Kefalonia. The village has traditional taverns, a super market, and a bakery that you can visit on foot. It is in a privileged position as it is 10 minutes from the famous beach of Myrtos, 5 minutes from the picturesque Agia Efimia, 25 minutes from the beautiful village of Assos, 35 minutes from the cosmopolitan Fiskardo, 20 minutes from the beach of Antisamos, 20 minutes from the famous caves of Mellisani and Drogarati, as well as 30 minutes from the capital of Argostoli. It offers peace and can accommodate up to 6 people. It has a swimming pool, barbecue and a large garden. Three bedrooms with three bathrooms and is ideal for peace, relaxation, rest and a starting point for your excursions.
