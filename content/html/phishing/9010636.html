Official Site® | Ledger.com/Start® - Getting started

For setting up a Ledger device, you can follow these steps:

  1. Visit Ledger's Website: Go to ledger.com/start.
  2. Choose Your Device: Select your Ledger device model (e.g., Ledger Nano S, Ledger Nano X).
  3. Download Ledger Live: If you haven't already, download and install Ledger Live, Ledger's companion app for managing your device.
  4. Set Up Ledger Live: Open Ledger Live and follow the on-screen instructions to create a new account or restore an existing one using your recovery phrase.
  5. Connect Your Ledger Device: Use the provided USB cable to connect your Ledger device to your computer.
  6. Initialize Your Device: Follow the prompts on your Ledger device's screen to initialize it. This involves setting up a PIN code and writing down your recovery phrase (24-word seed phrase) securely.
  7. Install Apps: Within Ledger Live, install the apps for the cryptocurrencies you want to manage on your Ledger device.
  8. Manage Your Assets: Once apps are installed, you can use Ledger Live to manage, send, and receive your cryptocurrencies securely.
  9. Security Tips: Always verify any transactions directly on your Ledger device's screen and never share your recovery phrase with anyone.

For specific details or troubleshooting, refer to the official Ledger website or Ledger Live app guides.
