Home

Heartline Wellness Center is a quiet healing space to receive high quality massage, physical therapy, manual therapy and acupuncture treatments.

We offer individually tailored treatments for stress relief, chronic pain management, symptoms of illness/surgery, insomnia, headaches, lymphedema, repetitive use injuries,  anxiety & depression, PTSD, mobility impairment, and injury/trauma rehabilitation.

We are open Monday to Saturday, by appointment.

Sanitation is a top priority to keep clients and practitioners safe. Our office is following infection control recommendations made by the the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and the office, retail, and salon/spa procedures outlined by the New Mexico Department of Public Health’s Covid Safe Practices.

Please contact each individual practitioner for additional information and to book appointments. You can purchase instant gift certificates with Amy and Dani on this page.
