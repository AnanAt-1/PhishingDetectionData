    Woodland Property Group

    Serving the Real Estate Community in Austin and Central Texas

    Woodland Property Group is a full-service and diversified real estate firm headquartered in Austin, Texas.

    We have over 70 years of experience developing, managing, leasing, and selling retail shopping centers, light industrial warehouses, office space, and undeveloped land. From taking the project from the ground up to purchasing assets for investment, we have the capability to handle any phase of the asset life cycle.

    Woodland Property Group can work with your organization in a variety of ways such as construction, financing, tenant representation, leasing, property management, asset management, and investment sales. We have proven experience in all of these areas and can assure you that we will develop, lease, or manage your asset as if it were one of our own.
