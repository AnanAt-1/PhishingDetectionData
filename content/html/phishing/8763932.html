Ledger® Live: Download | Getting Started with Ledger®

To download Ledger Live, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Official Ledger Website: Go to the official Ledger website at www.ledger.com.
  2. Navigate to Ledger Live: Locate the section or menu on the website related to Ledger Live. Typically, this can be found under "Products" or "Wallets."
  3. Choose Your Platform: Select the appropriate version of Ledger Live for your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux) or mobile device (iOS, Android).
  4. Download the Application: Click on the download link provided for your chosen platform.
  5. Install Ledger Live: Once the download is complete, open the installer file and follow the on-screen instructions to install Ledger Live on your device.
  6. Setup and Pairing: After installation, follow the setup instructions to pair your Ledger hardware wallet with Ledger Live for secure access to your cryptocurrencies.
  7. Start Managing Your Assets: Once setup is complete, you can start managing your cryptocurrency assets securely through Ledger Live.
