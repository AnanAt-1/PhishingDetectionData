Trezor.io/Start® - Trezor Suite App (Official) | Trezor®

Trezor.io/Start is the official starting point for setting up your Trezor® hardware wallet and accessing the Trezor Suite App, a powerful tool for managing your cryptocurrencies. Here’s how to get started with the Trezor Suite App:

  1. Connect Your Trezor® Device: Begin by connecting your Trezor® hardware wallet to your computer using the included USB cable. This establishes a secure link between your device and your computer.
  2. Visit Trezor.io/Start®: Open your web browser and go to Trezor.io/Start®. This official site provides clear, step-by-step guidance to set up your Trezor® device and access the Trezor Suite App.
  3. Install Trezor Bridge: If prompted, download and install Trezor Bridge, the software that enables your Trezor® device to communicate securely with your computer.
  4. Initialize Your Device: Follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your Trezor® device. You will be guided through creating a new wallet and generating a recovery seed. Write down the 12, 18, or 24-word recovery seed and store it securely offline.
  5. Download Trezor Suite App: Once your device is initialized, download the Trezor Suite App from Trezor.io/Start®. This app is available for desktop and web, providing a comprehensive interface for managing your digital assets.
  6. Set Up a PIN: Secure your wallet by setting up a strong PIN. This adds an extra layer of protection, preventing unauthorized access to your funds.
  7. Manage Your Crypto Assets: Use the Trezor Suite App to manage your cryptocurrencies. The app offers features like sending and receiving funds, monitoring your portfolio, and accessing advanced security settings.

By following these steps, Trezor.io/Start ensures a smooth setup process, enabling you to use the Trezor Suite App with confidence to manage your cryptocurrency investments securely.
