To get started with Ledger Live, the official desktop application for managing Ledger hardware wallets like the Ledger Nano S and Ledger Nano X, follow these steps:

Downloading and Installing Ledger Live

  1. Visit Ledger.com:
    • Open your web browser and go to the official Ledger website: Ledger.com.
  2. Navigate to Ledger Live:
    • Find the section or link for Ledger Live on the website. Usually, it's located under the "Products" or "Wallets" section.
  3. Download Ledger Live:
    • Click on the download link provided for Ledger Live. Ensure you download the version compatible with your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux).
  4. Install Ledger Live:
    • Once the download is complete, open the installer file and follow the on-screen instructions to install Ledger Live on your computer.
