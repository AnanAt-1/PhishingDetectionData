How to Get Started with Ledger

Getting started with Ledger is easy and only takes a few simple steps. Follow these instructions to begin securing your crypto today:

  1. Purchase Your Ledger Hardware Wallet: Visit Ledger.com/Start® to choose your Ledger device. You can choose between:
    • Ledger Nano S: A compact and affordable option for beginner users.
    • Ledger Nano X: A more advanced model with Bluetooth support, ideal for mobile use and enhanced convenience.
  2. Download Ledger Live: After you receive your Ledger device, download Ledger Live, the app that lets you manage your crypto. Ledger Live is available for both desktop and mobile, giving you full control of your assets on any device.
  3. Set Up Your Ledger Device: Follow the easy-to-follow instructions in Ledger Live to set up your hardware wallet. You’ll create a PIN code for added security and receive a 24-word recovery phrase. This phrase is critical – store it safely as it’s your backup in case you need to restore your wallet.
  4. Install Cryptocurrency Apps: Ledger devices support more than 1,800 cryptocurrencies. To start managing your crypto, install the relevant apps on your device via Ledger Live. You can install apps for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and many other digital assets.
  5. Send and Receive Crypto: Once your Ledger wallet is set up, you can send and receive cryptocurrencies securely. Ledger’s built-in security features ensure that your transactions are signed directly on the hardware device, protecting your private keys at all times.
