Ledger Live Desktop is a desktop application developed by Ledger to provide users with a convenient and secure platform for managing their cryptocurrency portfolios. It serves as the desktop counterpart to Ledger's hardware wallets, offering a seamless integration between hardware and software for enhanced security and usability.

Ledger Live Desktop provides up-to-date information on asset balances, transaction history, and market trends, empowering you to make informed decisions about your portfolio.
