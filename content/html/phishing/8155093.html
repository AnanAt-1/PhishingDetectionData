Latest Works

Born on the Gold Coast of Australia, Josh Bystrom spent the majority of his youth in the water, first through local board riders clubs, then lifeguarding for the Gold Coast City Council.

This gave Josh the knowledge and ability to feel comfortable in waves of consequence, which has become his medium of choice separating his imagery from others. Josh’s work has been featured internationally in print and digital media and his portfolio now encompasses a variety of different imagery capturing his love of  oceanic environments.
