WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2006

SmackDown Countdown 2005: Sylvain Grenier

Now that's what you call an entrance!

IGN's most popular recurring feature is back and bigger than ever! Returning for the fifth year in a row, the SmackDown Countdown is the world's coolest sneak peek at the lineup of THQ's latest installment in its line of incredibly successful wrestling videogames.

As has been expected every year, WWE SmackDown! vs. RAW 2006 is the largest and flashiest version of the game thus far. Not only have the online elements been improved beyond the barebones connectivity of last year, but the overall number of gaming modes has been advanced as well. Bar Room Brawls, Buried Alive Matches, and more realistic Cage confrontations are just a few of the refinements that have been made while additional upgrades to the AI, graphics, and grappling mechanics have been implemented as well.

Of course, one of the most dramatic changes this year has been to the wrestlers themselves. Not only have all their textures and polygon counts been increased over last season, but their entrances have been motion captured as well. Each grappler also has been granted two additional attribute types over last year (Hardcore and Stamina) and roughly 50% of all moves in the game have been reanimated. If that's not an improvement, we don't know what is.

While all these new features can't be experienced by our readers until SmackDown ships later this November, we're still dedicated to bring WWE fans the best pre-release coverage they could hope for. Over the next 11 weeks, we'll be highlighting all 50 current superstars and divas as well as more than a dozen different legends from WWE history. We'll be doing a new wrestler every week day from now until then and as a special treat for SmackDown and RAW fans, we'll make sure to customize our Monday and Friday grapplers to match the shows from which they hail. Sound cool? Cool!

But enough with the buildup already, let's get this party started! Sit back, relax, and enjoy today's Countdown superstar, Sylvain Grenier!

A former model in Europe, Sylvain enjoys the finer things in life from expensive clothes (which he designs himself) to gorgeous women. Strutting to the ring, posing for the camera whenever he gets the chance, Sylvain is all about himself and no one else. After being involved in a blockbuster Draft Lottery trade in June that saw Sylvain traded to SmackDown, the former model decided to invest in the only sure commodity on the market -- Sylvain.

Sylvain originally debuted as a member of La Resistance with former tag team partner Rene Dupree on RAW. But when Dupree left for SmackDown!, Sylvain continued La Resistance with the talented Rob Conway and went on to be a two-time World Tag Team Champion.

Sylvain hungry for more competition and success blasted on the scene in August 2005 seeing the Smackdown as the perfect global force to raise the profile of Sylvain. With fame and fortune as his goal, Sylvain looks to parlay his model good looks, and outstanding athletic ability into a blossoming career with WWE.
