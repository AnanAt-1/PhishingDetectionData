Want a chance to discuss the QLL leagues? Come on over to www.wingszone.com/nllmb - it is primarily an NLL board, but you can find some posts on Quebec Lacrosse in the Express forum and in Other Lacrosse. Hope to see you there!

Upcoming Events
The Cult is in attendance at most of the QLL games in the Montreal area (Lasalle, Montreal & Longueuil). A few of us even made it to Shawinigan and hope to get to Windsor and Sherbrooke before the season's done. If there is a game in your area, go check it out - it's cheap, great fun and a good way to support the growth of lacrosse in Quebec!
