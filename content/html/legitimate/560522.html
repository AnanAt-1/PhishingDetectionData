Financial Literacy

Aimed at empowering individuals and families with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate the world of finance and create lasting generational wealth. Led by Pastor Hines, who has spent over 35 years working to rewrite the financial narrative for the community, our Financial Literacy Program is a cornerstone of our mission. The program aims to provide equitable opportunities for children, teens, and adults to develop a deep understanding of money management, budgeting, and wealth-building strategies.

The Mission

In 1999, Pastor James L. Hines had a vision from God to build a center where youth could learn life skills, improve grades, and play sports. He worked diligently with a small group of faithful members to raise 2.5 million dollars to build the center. The center has continued to grow and serve in new facets, like its student improvement programs, Financial Literacy classes, many community events, and scholarships.
