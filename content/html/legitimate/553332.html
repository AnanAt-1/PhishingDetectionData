Affiliated medical office and clinic locations

With this website, it is easy to find Kaiser Permanente locations for your doctor’s office or clinic that is closest to you.

To access your own city directly, press and hold the control -F (CTRL-F) on your keyboard. 

The search box will look like this:

You can then enter in your City by the flashing cursor and hit “next”.

This will bring you right to that location on the page without searching for it manually!
