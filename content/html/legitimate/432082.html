Transformers: Rescue Bots is a Transformers franchise marketed to the ages 3-5 demographic. The fiction and play pattern of the franchise focus on the activities of the Autobots and humans in the Rescue Bots, who promote teamwork and cooperation in rescuing people. It is part of Playskool's Playskool Heroes multi-property brand.

The Rescue Bots franchise consists of:

  • A toyline
  • A cartoon series
  • A video-based Choose Your Own Adventure
  • Pack-in storybooks
  • An educational game
  • Two educational apps
