    About Us
    Cambridge Youth Soccer(Cambridge, Massachusetts) is an independent charitable organization which exists to provide a healthy team sports experience for Cambridge children and youth. Currently CYS provides programs for ages 4-18 with an in-town league and travel teams in the New England Club Soccer League (NECSL).Cambridge Youth Soccer is affiliated with the Massachusetts Youth Soccer Association (MYSA).
