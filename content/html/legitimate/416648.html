Comma

Jessica Ruble

  • Childhood
  • Death
  • Life
  • Loss
  • Love
  • Nature
  • Reflective
Promoted
We got a lot of problems
I'll be honest
I can't promise
I won't fuck up again
Cuz I'm still chasing karma
Still letting demons in
Bearing witness to lifes drama
Tryin to find my zen
With new strains of marijuana.

*cough*
Comma

Alcohol
Been nothing but a bother
Been riding that monster
Tryna forget about my father
Even though I always answer when he calls
Always Something about biological warfare and skin cancer and mankind's downfall
But I still talk to him
Brings me some kind of peace to listen
To know I'm the bigger person
Even if I don't give a single shit about reminiscing
I still pretend I've forgiven
Cuz that's just like me to be so damn forgiving
Even when they're dead to me I still keep them living
Like a collection of ghosts I can't get rid of
An assortment of almosts I can't escape
Bad decisions I've chosen to live with
My girl can't seem to cope with
She's my world and I'm so broken
Just a dumb bitch who's misspoken
Just someone to fix
To break open
I'm hopeless
And she knows this
And she still loves me like I'm smokeless
See, that's the thing she hates the mostest
I'm always taking drags when she wants to grope this
It's how I'm coping
It's a nasty habit I wish I'd broken
Before I ever met her
Before I end up croaking

*cough*
Comma

I wish she were my baby momma
Guess she kinda is
To these two kids
And I suck at being one to hers
But I'm trying to get better
Cuz it's what these 4 kids deserve
I just hate this fucking weather
And wish the outside didn't hurt
Wish the noise didn't echo
Wish they'd just listen
Wish I were more patient
Wish we could get out of this ghetto
But one day, right?
Well all still be together?
Raising teenagers and reaching for forever?

*cough*
Shit.
That's no excuse, Jess
You're better than this, Jess
You can be a good girlfriend
You're better than the rest, Jess
you don't always have to destroy yourself when you're stressed, Jess.
You can enjoy the little things if you'd just stop focusing on the messes.
But I guess that's what you do, huh?
Just focus on the messes without cleaning this shit up.
Maybe you're not so good afterall.
You can't even make a simple phone call.
You can't even pick yourself up when you fall.
You can't even trick your brain enough to crawl.
You're just a lazy fat piece of shit
Barely a mother at all
Is that hard to admit?
I wish they'd nail you to the wall
Take turns throwing stones until your eyeballs start to bawl.

Ugh. There we go again with that self pity
Always feeling shitty bout myself.
 *cough*
Comma.
Let's start over shall we?

I'll be honest.
We've got a lot of problems.
But I can promise
I can handle all the drama
I will take your traumas and turn them to sunshine
And rainbows
I'll make them smaller
Cuz that's how loves supposed to go
I'm supposed to give all or nothing
And I'm sorry
For only giving something's
I'm sorry
For all the times I gave you nothing
And I'm sorry
For all the times I've been unbecoming.
And I'm sorry
For stopping you from running
When all you needed was a moment
I felt like you'd never be coming home again
I'm sorry im not better.
I'm sorry im not what's best for you
I'm sorry when im forgetful
I'm sorry I don't offer you your dreams on a silver platter
You're my girl
And you deserve the world
And every fucking thing about you matters
More to me than all the atoms and subatomic particles
More than every farcical thing I'll ever do
More than all the planets and their moons
More than every chapter ever written
More than every disaster
More than every darkness hidden
More than anything ever or hereafter
More than God, more than religion.

*cough*
Comma.

We got lists of problems.
But loving you's not on em.
I give you everything I have
But I don't have a lot to offer.
I wish I were a better girlfriend, a better fiance, a better person, a better momma.
But loving you is the best thing I've ever done, and I feel like I'm even doing this wrong.
With you I hit the jack pot
Im a winner, and I'm your problem.
I'm your exclamation, and baby, you're my comma.

About this poem

For my love, I'll never stop trying.
