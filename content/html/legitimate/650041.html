About

Caroline Rhea is a 61 year old Canadian Actress. Born Caroline Gilchrist Rhea on 13th April, 1964 in Westmount, Quebec, Canada, she is famous for her roles as Hilda Spellman in the ABC/WB sitcom Sabrina, the Teenage Witch, Fridays’ Melanie Chartoff in the 1999 biographical comedy-drama film Man on the Moon, Lynette Hansen in the 2000 Disney Channel original made-for-television horror-comedy film Mom's Got a Date with a Vampire, Mrs. Konquist (voice) in the ABC Kids animated crime/mystery/suspense series Fillmore!, Ilsa Schicklgrubermeiger-Von Helsing der Keppelugerhofer in the Disney Channel original teen sitcom The Suite Life of Zack & Cody, Linda Flynn-Fletcher and Grandma Betty Jo (voices) in the Disney Channel original animated musical comedy series Phineas and Ferb, Noleta Nethercott in the Logo comedy series Sordid Lives: The Series and in the 2017 sequel film A Very Sordid Wedding, Bonnie in the CBS sitcom 2 Broke Girls, and Judy Reynolds in the Disney Channel original comedy series Sydney to the Max, in addition to her numerous comedy specials including three one-hour standup specials for Comedy Central, HBO and Showtime in a career that spans 1986–present. Her zodiac sign is Aries.

Caroline Rhea has been in 9 on-screen matchups, including Douglas Sills in Sabrina the Teenage Witch (1996), Gary Sandy in Sabrina the Teenage Witch (1996), Geoff Pierson in Sabrina the Teenage Witch (1996), Martin Mull in Sabrina the Teenage Witch (1996) and Matt Battaglia in Sabrina the Teenage Witch (1996).
