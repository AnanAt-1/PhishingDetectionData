Beauty for every occassion

At Main Florist, our mission is simple yet deeply meaningful: to cherish your most precious moments as if they were our own.

For over 115 years, our family-owned boutique has been a cornerstone of Montreal and its suburbs, spreading joy and beauty through four generations of dedication. From exquisite floral arrangements and unique home décor to weddings, corporate events, and special occasions, we are committed to excellence in everything we do.

We take great pride in offering same-day flower delivery and nationwide service through our trusted network of partner florists. More importantly, we value the relationships we build with our clients. Every arrangement we create is crafted with care and passion, ensuring it speaks the emotions words often cannot. At Main Florist, we’re honored to help make your moments unforgettable.
