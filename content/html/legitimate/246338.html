The School of Management at SJSU is accredited by both AACSB and WASC. These accreditations, held by less than 5% of business schools worldwide, affirm the college's commitment to excellence in business education. The standards of AACSB and WASC ensure a high-quality education that meets rigorous academic and intellectual standards.
