Location of Resolution Cave in Alola.
Pokémon world locations

Resolution Cave (Japanese: エンドケイブ End Cave) is a location on Poni Island in Alola, located at the northwest end of Poni Meadow.

In the manga

Pokémon Adventures

Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon arc

Resolution Cave appeared in Destroy!! Results of the Training!, where Hau and Hala were shown training there together. After defeating Hala's Bewear in a practice battle, Hau's Brionne evolved into Primarina, after which the two Trainers exited the cave. While traversing through it, they discussed about Guzma.

Trivia

  • The bottom floor of Resolution Cave has a practically identical layout to Zygarde's chamber in Kalos's Terminus Cave. In Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, Zygarde is found at the center, just like in Terminus Cave, while in Pokémon Sun and Moon, a Zygarde Cell is found at the center instead.
