Date Bran Muffins

Serves: 12
Total Calories: 101

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups wheat bran cereal
1 1/2 cups fat-free milk
1/3 cup margarine, melted
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
4 1/4 teaspoons Equal measure or 14 packets EQUAL sweetener or 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons EQUAL Spoonful
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 cup pitted dates, chopped

Directions:

1. Combine cereal and milk in medium bowl let stand 5 minutes. Stir in margarine, egg, and vanilla. Add combined flour, EQUAL Measure, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon, stirring just until mixture is blended. Stir in dates.

2. Spoon batter into greased muffin tins bake in preheated 375°F oven until muffins are browned and toothpicks inserted in centers come out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool in pans on wire rack 5 minutes remove from pans and cool on wire racks.
