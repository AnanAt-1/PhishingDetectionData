Cineflix Studios

Cineflix Studios creates channel-defining scripted content for the US, Canada, and international markets. Partnering with top flight showrunners and third-party producers, Cineflix Studios brings creative, co-financing, and distribution expertise to premium projects.

Studios Team

Peter Emerson

President

Peter Emerson

President

As President, Cineflix Media, Peter is focused on the company’s growth initiatives. In addition to building on the existing brands and revenue streams, Peter is driving the dramatic expansion of the scripted television slate at Cineflix Studios and Cineflix Rights. He is currently executive producing three series for Cineflix including the new supernatural drama Reginald the Vampire (SYFY, Amazon), the International Emmy® Best Drama award-winning Tehran for Apple TV+, and global hit series Coroner (CBC, The CW Network, NBCUI, and Sky Witness).

Previously, Peter was President of eOne Television International where he oversaw all aspects of the company’s international television activities, including the global roll-out of The Walking Dead, and many other high-profile shows through an output deal with AMC Studios. Prior to eOne, Peter built Oasis International and was a partner in Blueprint Productions, which were both acquired by eOne in 2008 to form eOne Television.

Brett Burlock

SVP, Scripted

Brett Burlock

SVP, Scripted

Brett spearheads the company’s scripted division, Cineflix Studios, with Peter Emerson. His executive producer credits at Cineflix include the new supernatural drama Reginald the Vampire (SYFY, Amazon); global hit series Coroner (CBC, The CW Network, NBCUI, and Sky Witness) that airs in 150 territories worldwide; multi-award-winning cult LGBTQ+ phenom Wynonna Earp (SYFY, CTV Sci-Fi); and the award-nominated series Pure (WGNA, Hulu, Super Channel, and CBC).

Brett came to Cineflix from Lionsgate’s Canadian JV Sea to Sky where he developed TV series for US and international outlets—including NBC, Fox, ABC, Hulu, RTL, TF1, and C4—and oversaw first-look deals with Kevin Costner and former ABC Entertainment Head Stephen McPherson. Prior to that, Brett led a development initiative with Take 5 (The Borgias, Vikings) and was VP of Development for CTV where he guided all creative matters for scripted drama and was instrumental in driving a period of critical and ratings successes for CTV’s original production slate with Flashpoint, The Borgias, and The Bridge.

Brett graduated cum laude from Dartmouth College where he lettered in hockey. He also holds a law degree from Dalhousie University and was a human rights lawyer in his previous life.

Sonia Hosko

Vice President, Current

Sonia Hosko

Vice President, Current

Alongside Peter Emerson and Brett Burlock, Sonia oversees production on Cineflix Studios content and works closely with the company’s co-production partners. With a focus on the Canadian market, Sonia leverages her strong relationships with writers, showrunners, and producers to source and develop new scripted series.

Previously, Sonia was co-executive producer on the hit series Saving Hope and police procedural The Detail for CTV and Ion, CBC’s Burden of Truth, and the award-winning ABC/Global drama Rookie Blue. She also served as a writer, producer, and director on HBO Canada’s short film anthology Little Films About Big Moments. Sonia’s other film credits include major features such as Sarah Polley’s Away from Her, Brian De Palma’s Redacted, and Atom Egoyan’s Adoration. Committed to diversity in onscreen representation, Sonia is also a co-creator of the Lexa Pledge which promotes positive LGBTQ+ storylines.
