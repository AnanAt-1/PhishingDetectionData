Box Office Hours
Expand
arrow_drop_up arrow_drop_down

Open on show/event dates only.

If there are events in both the Great Cedar Showroom and the Premier Theater, both Box Offices will be open. Otherwise only the Box Office adjacent to the performing theater will be open.

For events taking place in venues other than those listed above, please refer to the “Learn More” section for those events.

For shows starting at 7PM or later, the Box Office will be open from 2PM to one hour after showtime.

For shows starting before 7PM, opening times will vary; please refer to the “Learn More” section for those events.

If you have questions regarding our hours, please email them to FWTicket@foxwoods.com and we will respond to all inquiries as soon as possible.

Event lineup and/or the order of appearance are subject to change. No refunds will be issued should such a change occur.
