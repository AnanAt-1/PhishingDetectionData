About kinglong inverter

Types of Kinglong Inverter

Kinglong inverter is a key component of Kinglong electric and hybrid buses, converting direct current (DC) power from batteries into alternating current (AC) power to drive electric motors. The inverters enable smooth acceleration, deceleration, and overall responsive performance, enhancing the driving experience while ensuring energy efficiency and lower emissions. Different types of Kinglong inverters suit various bus models and customer needs.

  • 1. Voltage Level

    Kinglong inverters are categorized into low-voltage (LV) and high-voltage (HV) inverters. LV inverters are used in buses with LV battery systems, usually 600V or less, to power electric motors. They convert the LV DC from the batteries into AC. HV inverters are used in hybrid and fully electric buses with HV battery systems. The HV inverters manage the HV DC, ensuring efficient and stable power delivery to the electric motors, enabling longer ranges and higher performance in hybrid and electric buses.

  • 2. Inverter Type

    Kinglong inverters use different technologies to meet various performance and efficiency requirements. The most common types include:

    - Kinglong IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) Inverter: This is the most widely used inverter in electric buses. IGBT inverters offer high efficiency, fast switching speeds, and excellent thermal stability, making them suitable for various driving conditions and bus loads.

    - Kinglong SiC (Silicon Carbide) Inverter: SiC inverters are at the forefront of inverter technology. They provide ultra-high efficiency and performance, especially in high-frequency applications. SiC inverters can significantly reduce energy losses, enhance the overall energy efficiency of the bus, and enable more extended ranges and higher speeds in electric buses.

    - Kinglong Multilevel Inverter: This type of inverter uses multiple voltage levels to generate a smoother and more precise AC output. Multilevel inverters enhance the reliability and efficiency of the electric drive system, making them suitable for heavy-duty buses with strict performance and durability requirements.

Specifications and Maintenance of Kinglong Inverter

  • Output voltage

    The output voltage of the Kinglong inverters is 110 VAC or 220 VAC. The output voltage is compatible with the standard voltage used in most residential and commercial electrical systems.

  • Monitor the operating temperature

    The Kinglong inverter is designed to operate within a specific temperature range.     Periodically conduct system tests to verify the functionality of the inverter and connected components. Test the AC output voltage and waveform using a multimeter or oscilloscope. Simulate loads to ensure the inverter performs well under different load conditions.

Q and A

Q1: What is a Kinglong inverter?

A1: A Kinglong inverter is a device used in Kinglong electric and hybrid buses to convert DC (direct current) electricity, which is stored in batteries, into AC (alternating current) electricity. Q2: Why are inverters important for electric buses?

A2: Inverters are crucial for electric buses because they enable the conversion of stored DC electricity from batteries into AC electricity. This allows the electric bus's propulsion system to draw power from the batteries, ensuring smooth and efficient operation of the vehicle.

Q3: What are the key features of the Kinglong inverter?

A3: The key features of Kinglong inverters include high efficiency (over 97%), compact design, reliability, advanced control technology, and integration with regenerative braking systems.

Q4: What is the warranty period for the Kinglong inverter?

Q5: Can the Kinglong inverter be used for other bus brands?
