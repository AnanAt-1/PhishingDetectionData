ARTIST OF THE MONTH

Eleanor Dennis sings Senta in the new production of Der fliegende Holländer for Opera Holland Park, opening on 27 May 2025.

HOJOTOHO !

Lee Bisset sings Gerhilde, Katie Lowe sings Ortlinde and Catherine Carby sings Siegrune in the new production of Die Walküre, opening at London’s Royal Ballet and Opera on 1 May 2025. The production will be streamed to Cinema on 14 May 2025.

CATCH UP WITH MESSIAH

Rebecca Afonwy-Jones is Mezzo-Soprano Soloist for Gareth Malone’s Messiah, broadcast over the Easter Holiday. Catch up with all three episodes on BBC iPlayer.

HANDEL FOR ALL

Hilary Cronin is Soprano Soloist for Handel’s Brockes Passion, now streaming on The English Concert’s Handel for All.
