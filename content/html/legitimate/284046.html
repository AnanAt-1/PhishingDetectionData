   - Kens Pinball Machine Pages -

Thank you for visiting the pinball pages.  I hope you find everything here you are looking for.  I have been an avid pinball player and fan ever since  I would play pinball at the Gold Mine Arcade before making my way over to the drive in theater.

There is no game like pinball.  The steel ball and the glass and plastic come together to form an American masterwork and sport like no other.  Designer Harry Williams always said, "The Ball Is Wild." He was very much correct.  Unlike a video game with its' repeating patterns and storyline every individual game of pinball is unique.

Here you will find my custom cards, videos, world wide pinball locator, and dedicated machine pages seen below.
