WOODBURY SCHOOL OF BUSINESS: A PLACE FOR YOU

Welcome to the Woodbury School of Business (WSB) at Utah Valley University (UVU), where your educational investment is a bold step towards shaping your future as a dynamic leader. Imagine yourself conquering the business world armed with knowledge gained in our cutting-edge Keller Building. It's not just a place; it's a vibrant hub where academic advising, resources, and hands-on projects collide for the ultimate student experience.

At the WSB, we're not just talking the talk; we're in the business of student success. We transform grads into influential professionals, offering fantastic employer connections, real-world learning experiences, and access to resources like the Entrepreneurship Institute, Money Success Center, SMARTLab, Bloomberg lab, and more. Our mission is crystal clear: through exceptional business education, we mold students into successful professionals who don't just ride the wave but actively shape their communities.

Ready to level up? Apply now.
