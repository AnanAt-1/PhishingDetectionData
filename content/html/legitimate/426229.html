List of SGA Giants Bobbleheads

Hi Folks,

I've recently done some research into bobbleheads to find out how many
were ever made and discovered that I'm missing some of the hardest ones
to find and want to add them to my collection.

By my count, there were 50 bobbleheads given away by Giants since 1999.
But there may be more out there that I do not know about.
You can help me by letting me know if any are missing.

Thanks and Go Giants!
