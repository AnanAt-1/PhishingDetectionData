Collection: Higher Standards

Higher Standards aims to provide true dry herb and concentrate connoisseurs with everything they need to enhance their sessions. The cleaning supplies, accessories, and glass from Higher Standards represent the perfect mix of form and function. All of Higher Standards' pipes, beakers, and rigs are made from borosilicate glass in the U.SA. 

Every glass piece from Higher Standards from their Heavy Duty Beaker to the Taster is constructed from lab-quality Borosilicate glass that is designed to prevent shattering when dropped and withstand every day drops and tumbles. 

The clean look of your HS glass can be kept in shape using the the Higher Standards Clean Kit, and you can check out how it can help make your glass look as good as new here.
