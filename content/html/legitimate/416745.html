Episcopalian. Member, Council on Foreign Relations. One of five retired diplomats who co-signed a famous 1954 letter protesting U.S. Sen. Joe McCarthy's attacks on the Foreign Service. Died in Manhattan, New York County, N.Y., September 27, 1982 (age 94 years, 348 days). Interment at Princeton Cemetery, Princeton, N.J.
  Relatives: Son of George Allison Armour and Harriette (Foote) Armour; married, February 2, 1919, to Princess Myra Koudacheff.
