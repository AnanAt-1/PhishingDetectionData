 Farmington UT

Law Enforcement Memorial

Thu, May 15 (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)
Davis County Sheriff's Office
 Farmington UT

Please join us as we honor the brave men who made the ultimate sacrifice in service to our community. We will pay tribute to Trooper George Rees of the Utah Highway Patrol (End of Watch July 2, 1960), Deputy Don Jensen of the Davis County Sheriff’s Office (End of Watch May 14, 1971), Lieutenant Thomas Rettberg of the Utah Highway Patrol (End of Watch February 11, 2000), and Officer Charles B. Skinner of the North Salt Lake Police Department (End of Watch November 8, 2008). Their courage and dedication will never be forgotten.
