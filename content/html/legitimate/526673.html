Hair & Makeup

Self-taught and passionate, Nicolas Blanchet puts his heart and soul into his creative process. As a makeup artist, he developed a keen understanding of the contours of beauty from an early age and learned to use visual composition in all its complexity. Considered a true color chemist and light expert, he has become one of the most appreciated artists of his cohort.

Nicolas is known for his ability to sculpt and create looks with unmistakable malleability, ranging from sleek aesthetics to flamboyant glamour. Over the course of his career, he has collaborated on high-profile campaigns for Lise Watier, Marcelle, and L’Oréal, as well as renowned brands such as Simons, Birks, and Mackage.

With an extensive client list that includes Sarah-Jeanne Labrosse, Evelyne Brochu, and Caroline Vreeland, he maintains a timeless approach that seeks to capture his subjects in all their strength and potential. As a makeup artist, photographer, and art director, Nicolas lives by the principle that beauty is first and foremost a state of mind.
