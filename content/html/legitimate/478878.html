    Program Structure and Hours

    Students must complete twenty courses of three credits each, totalling 60 credits to graduate. This comprehensive program is divided into four semesters, over a one year period. All subjects must be successfully completed in order to receive a Diploma.

    Program Schedules

    Global Marketing: Fashion and Social Media program students attend five classes, three day per week basis. Classes are scheduled for a total of twenty hours per week. Class schedules are available M-T-W or W-Th-F.

    The college reserves the right to change course offerings, course content, kit contents, facilities, faculty, tuition fees, and course schedules without notice.
