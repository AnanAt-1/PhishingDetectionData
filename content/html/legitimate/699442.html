Longitude of Ascending Node of the Earth

Longitude of the ascending node is the term associated with the space science, whereas its used to identify the orbit of an object revolving in the space. It is an angle from the reference direction to the ascending node direction. The Longitude of Ascending Node of the Earth is 6.08665006 radians.
