About

Abhishek Bachchan is a 49 year old Indian Actor. Born on 5th February, 1976 in Mumbai, India and educated at Bombay Scottish School and Modern School, Vasant Vihar, New Delhi, and Aiglon College in Switzerland, he is famous for Yuva in a career that spans 2000-present. His zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Abhishek Bachchan has been in 7 on-screen matchups, including Bipasha Basu in Zameen (2003), Kareena Kapoor in Refugee (2000), Rani Mukerji in Bas Itna Sa Khwaab Hai... (2001), Aishwarya Rai Bachchan in Kuch Naa Kaho (2003) and Deepika Padukone in Khelein Hum Jee Jaan Sey (2010).
