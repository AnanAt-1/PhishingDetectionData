 The songs goes like this:



   Intro/Verse (Acoustic)

   Verse (acoustic/distorsed/clean)

   Chorus (distorsed)

   Intro/verse (acoustic/clean)

   Verse (acoustic/distorsed/clean)

   Bridge (distorsed)

   Intro/verse (acoustic)

   Verse (acoustic/clean at the end)
