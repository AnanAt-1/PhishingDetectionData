Swing l'été is now one of the most revered swing event in Quebec, attracting more than 400 enthusiasts!!!

Many dance schools from Montreal, including Cat's Corner, Studio 88 Swing, Swing ConneXion and JiveStudio participate, offering introduction course, intermediate course and a swing show. There are also many guest DJs and a LOT of dancers.

Swing l'été is the perfect opportunity to let your family and friends discover how fun swing dancing is (and finally answer their question: What is swing dancing?)

See you under the bright sun of Verdun!

FREE Entry!
