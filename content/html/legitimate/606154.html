Transformers fans, it’s time to roll out with the best quotes from this blockbuster! Directed by Michael Bay and released in 2007, Transformers took the world by storm with its incredible CGI and high-octane action sequences. The film stars Shia LaBeouf as Sam Witwicky and Megan Fox as Mikaela Banes, with thrilling voice performances from Peter Cullen as Optimus Prime and Hugo Weaving as Megatron. Following Sam's discovery that his car is actually an Autobot named Bumblebee, he gets caught in the middle of an epic battle between the heroic Autobots and the villainous Decepticons. The film received mixed reviews but was a commercial success, launching a franchise that captivated audiences worldwide.

Several standout, and even funny, quotes from Transformers have become iconic, each offering something unique. A lot of people feel especially passionate about the many classic Optimus Prime quotes in the film. One particularly memorable line is Optimus Prime’s “You may lose your faith in us, but never in yourselves. From here, the fight will be your own.” Then we have Jetfire’s humorous line “My ancestors have been here for generations. My father was a wheel! The first wheel! And do you know what he transformed into? Nothing! But he did it with honor! Dignity, d*mmit!”

Speaking of humorous sayings, we also have Ironside’s memorable line “Ironhide: “Bad Mojo! Ugh, my foot's gonna rust.” On the darker side, there’s Megatron's chilling "You still fight for the weak! That is why you lose!" which perfectly captures the Decepticons’ ruthless ideology. Now it’s your turn to decide which quote reigns supreme! Dive into this list and cast your vote for the most epic line from the Transformers movie.
