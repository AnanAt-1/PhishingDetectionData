Welcome to
Philadelphia Volleyball

We strive to provide an inclusive and welcoming community for volleyball players of all skill levels, while promoting the growth and development of the sport in Philadelphia. Volleyball is a great way to stay active, meet new people, and have fun too. Whether you’re a beginner or a college star, by yourself or with a team, we have ways to play.

Learn how to get started

Pickup

No long term commitment — play once, or come back every week.

Tournaments

Day long (or overnight) competitions. Grab your team and start competing!

Leagues

Weekly leagues running every quarter. Grab your friends or sign up as a free agent.

How to get started

We use the app Opensports to organize and publicize our pickup events, tournaments, and leagues. Events are open to the public, but you must get a ticket on the app to reserve your spot. No need to have a team or a ball, just sign up for an event and bring yourself!

Learn more

What we do

Philly Volleyball is a non-profit that hosts volleyball events in the Philadelphia region. We provide opportunities for players to come together, socialize, and enjoy the game in a fun and competitive atmosphere.
