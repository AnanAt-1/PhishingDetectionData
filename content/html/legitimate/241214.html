Facts about Sarah O'Hare

Sarah O’Hare is 53 years old
Born: May 31, 1971
Birthplace: London, England
Best known as: Former Wonderbra model
     

Sarah O’Hare Biography

 

Sarah O’Hare succeeded Eva Herzigova as the “Wonderbra woman,” posing in international ads for the mod brassiere. (The same high-profile job was later taken by Magdalena Wrobel.) Born in England, O’Hare grew up in Australia; in 1999 she married Lachlan Murdoch, son of Australian media mogul Rupert Murdoch. She has appeared in ads for Revlon and Ralph Lauren and posed for the famous Sports Illustrated swimsuit issue. She began a feature film career with a part as a plastic surgery addict in the 2001 Freddie Prinze Jr. movie Head Over Heels.
