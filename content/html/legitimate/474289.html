The 1974 Kansas City Royals played 162 games during the regular season, won 77 games, lost 85 games, and finished in fifth position. They played their home games at Royals Stadium (Park Factors: 106/106) where 1,173,292 fans witnessed their 1974 Royals finish the season with a .475 winning percentage.
