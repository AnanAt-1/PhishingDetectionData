NHL AWARDS AND HONORS
1974-75: Played on first Washington Capitals team.
Miscellaneous: Set Washington record (since broken) for fastest goal from the start of a game when he scored 20 seconds into Nov. 26, 1975, game vs. Los Angeles. ... Set Washington record (since broken) for most shots on goal in one season with 168 in 1975-76.
NON-NHL CAREER
Post-Draft Teams: Dayton (IHL); Hershey (AHL); Springfield (AHL); Oklahoma City (CHL); Fussen (West Germany)
NON-NHL AWARDS AND HONORS
WHA Draft Pick: 1974 (by New England, No. 174 overall in Round 12)
Miscellaneous: Played entire 1974-75 season as amateur, including five-game tryout with Washington (NHL). Did not sign first pro contract until June 1975. ... Missed part of 1977-78 season with knee injury.
Personal: Full name is Anthony Raymond White.
HOW HE GOT AWAY
FREE AGENCY: Washington released White in 1978. He signed with Minnesota as an unrestricted free agent on September 17, 1979.
