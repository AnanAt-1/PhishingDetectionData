Welcome to Beaver Lake Cottages

AN EXTRAORDINARY ESCAPE WITH 25 YEARS OF EXCELLENCE

Join us for a perfect romantic getaway at one of our Beaver Lake cabin rentals. Located in Eureka Springs, Arkansas, we offer the best view of the beautiful Ozark Mountains from our premier glass-front cabins.

SPECTACULAR VIEWS OF BEAVER LAKE

Relax with a romantic Eureka Springs, Arkansas cabin getaway. Beaver Lake Cottages offers premier glass-front Beaver Lake cabins overlooking the beautiful Ozark wilderness. Youre sure to find your perfect escape with our selection of suites and cabins in the Ozark Mountains of Arkansas.

Our guests return each year to experience the intimate surroundings of our Beaver Lake cabins. Our Eureka Springs lodging includes one and two-bedroom cabins that have separate living spaces and kitchens like a private home. All our cabins and suites offer beautiful views of Beaver Lake, spacious decks, Jacuzzis for two, and complimentary wine or sparkling juice and snacks on your first night. Snuggle up to a cozy fireplace, relax in a romantic Jacuzzi and doze off while gazing at the stars and tree tops through the glass roof gables.

Go beyond your Eureka Springs cabin to experience all the best things to do in Eureka Springs and download our free vacation guide to start planning your trip. Eureka Springs is full of untouched nature and beautiful views. Watch birds feed on your deck and deer roaming just footsteps from your door. The surrounding Ozark Mountains are an outdoor lovers paradise where you can explore nature and hiking trails, go horseback riding, relax in natural springs and view the mountains from a zip line.Take a trip to downtown Eureka Springs to marvel at the historic buildings, shop the boutiques, and dine at award-winning restaurants. If you want to unwind and experience the charm of mountain living, this is the place to do it.

Our cabins in the Ozarks of Arkansas are perfect for memorable anniversaries or honeymoons. You wont find a more intimate and relaxing getaway anywhere else. Check out at our Eureka Springs vacation packages to save when you book your Beaver Lake, Arkansas cabin through us. We also feature Eureka Springs elopement packages to help you plan your wedding ceremony. Our location overlooking Beaver Lake provides a beautiful backdrop for your intimate elopement.

An extraordinary escape with 25 years of excellence, Beaver Lake Cottages will make you feel at home within the untouched wilderness of the Ozark Mountains. Relax, reflect, and renew amid our nature trails with gorgeous rock bluffs and creek beds leading to the shore of Beaver Lake. A stay in our Eureka Springs, Arkansas cabins is sure to create memories that last a lifetime.
