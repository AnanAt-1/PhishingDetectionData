Seeking a Friend for the End of the World

Variety report that Billy is currently in negotiation to appear in the upcoming romantic comedy, SEEKING A FRIEND AT THE END OF THE WORLD.   The movie currently stars Steve Carell and Kiera Knightley.   Variety also report production is due to start “later this month” [May 2011].

William Petersen, who’s been extremely selective in choosing feature roles since leaving “CSI,” is in negotiations along with “Scream 4” star Adam Brody and Rob Corddry to join Steve Carell and Keira Knightley in Mandate Pictures’ “Seeking a Friend at the End of the World.”

Movie Synopsis c/o IMDB

As an asteroid nears Earth, a man finds himself alone after his wife leaves in a panic. He decides to take a road trip to reunite with his high school sweetheart. Accompanying him is a neighbor who inadvertently puts a wrench in his plan.

IMDB also states the movie will be released in 2012.
