Note: The eastern section of Yorktown, New York, still bears the name of Amawalk, New York, probably an abbreviation and corruption of the Mohegan term Appamaghpogh, which had been applied to the whole Indian territory within the Manor, west of Cortland Town, and it was under this name on 24 Aug 1683 that the grant was made to Stephanns VAN CORTLAND of New York City, from the Indian sachems Powemine, Oskewans, Turham, Querawighint, Siecham, Isighers & Prackises. Yorktown, New York, was formerly called Hanover, from a part of it Stephenstown was set off 7 Mar 1788; and in 1808, it became Somers, New York.

Amawalk, Westchester County, New York, Friend's Monthly Meeting Records : beginning before the separation caused by the preaching of Elias HICKS, and have since been kept in the hands of the Amawalk Orthodox Friend's. This copy made from the tow books in Jun 1908 in possession of Mr. Jacob McKEEL, of Yorktown Heights, Westchester County, New York, the secretary being a Mr. KEAR.
