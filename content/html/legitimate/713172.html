SKU: 318 Category: Transmission

BG Premium Full Synthetic CVT Fluid replaces every CVT fluid currently used in belt- and chain-driven CVTs. It provides excellent protection of internal components and offers superior thermal stability. This state-of-the-art lubricant is designed to provide exceptional value and superior durability to CVT power systems.
