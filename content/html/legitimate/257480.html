Our on-line universal availability calendar is state of the art with immediate real time updates. It's like having a 24 hour 7 day a week reservation assistant.

Vacation Rental Universal Availability Calendar that Shows up to 24 months on one screen, Multi-language, Message area. Property Management style shows 12 months on one screen, Second color for pending, Message area, Searchable, Blocked Months (Seasonal). Great application for the lodging property owner.
Bed & Breakfast Our on-line reservation calendar is easy to use and inexpensive for small B&Bs. Internet visitors can instantly check online availability. It is easy for the Innkeeper to setup and maintain. It has an owner message area, reserved dates, pending reservations, blocked months for seasonal operations and can be customized to match your existing site. Show all rooms on one display screen. The calendar features the following: Two colors to show reserved and pending dates, Message area, Room names link to your web page, Universal application.
B&B Association Calendar has the ability to allow individual login and edit of single properties.
Pet Sitting Installed on your existing web site shows visitors to your site your availability. Can be configured to show All Availability, Some Availability, Fully Booked. Larger Companies can select from the Multi-name calendar to show Individual Availability.
House Sitting Install this calendar on your web site an tell your visitors when you are available. Up to 24 month display, message area, multi-language support. Customized to match your existing web page.
Freelance For all different services, Freelance Videographers, Photographers, Personal Chefs, Disk Jockeys,Radio Personalities,Bands....Many many more !!
All three styles of calendars can be used here. The single, Single (Multi-color) that allows the ability to Search for availability and the multi-name for larger businesses.
Houseboat Rentals Use the Property Management style calendar if you have a couple of houseboat rentals. If you have an entire fleet of houseboats, use the (B&B) Multiple style calendar and show all rentals available on one screen. Makes selecting a houseboat rental easy for your clients.
