What to expect at The Budweiser Stage

The Budweiser Stage is set just outside downtown Toronto. Its waterfront setting makes it a unique venue to watch live music — it’s close enough to downtown to take advantage of all the amenities on offer, while also feeling like you’re watching a show out in open nature.

Shows run from May to October. Most take place in glorious summer weather. However, events run come rain or shine — we always recommend that visitors prepare for all weather conditions. It’s always a good idea to bring a blanket, poncho, or light jacket, even if sun is forecast.

Once you’ve arrived, you’ll leave your parking lot or public transportation and join the security lines. These make thorough checks to ensure all guests’ safety. Then, you can enter and find your spot.

The venue has a number of facilities that make event day even more enjoyable for fans. The acoustics and sound technology are state-of-the-art. Likewise, there are large video screens to make sure nobody misses out on the show. 

With its massive choice of seating, high-tech productions, and world-class performers, events at the Budweiser Stage are always incredibly popular. Booking your tickets early and arriving with time to spare on the day will guarantee you have the best show possible.
