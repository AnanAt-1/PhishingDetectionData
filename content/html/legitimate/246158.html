OUR APPROACH

Why Do Leading Broker Dealers, Banks, and Retirement Recordkeepers Choose Delta Data for Mutual Fund Sub-Accounting?

Empowerment Through Flexibility

Adaptable and rules-based design to seamlessly accommodate evolving business needs. Whether it’s adjusting to regulatory changes or accommodating new product offerings, the modular approach ensures that our solution evolves with our clients business.

Comprehensive Investment Fund Coverage

Handle any type of pooled asset from a mutual fund to collective fund, hedge fund to stable value product within a single integrated solution. This comprehensive coverage allows clients to expand their product portfolio with confidence.

Integration Simplicity

Seamless integration with clients existing ecosystem whether through REST APIs, XML messages, or text files, our solution effortlessly integrates within your operational infrastructure, delivering enhanced operational efficiency through automation and unparalleled data accuracy.

Enhanced Customer Experience

Elevate the client experience with self-service through an intuitive web portal, strengthening client relationships through convenient, user-friendly access to account and fund information. Provide advanced customer statements with more efficient delivery methods to better satisfy your customers.

Streamlined Trading Processes

Facilitates omnibus trading seamlessly across numerous trading platforms such as NSCC, Canadian FundServ, Schwab, and DST TIP.

Extensive Reporting
