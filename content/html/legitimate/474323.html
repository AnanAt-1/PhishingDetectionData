1984 Kansas City Royals Roster | Research by Baseball Almanac

The 1984 Kansas City Royals played 162 games during the regular season, won 84 games, lost 78 games, and finished in first position. They played their home games at Royals Stadium (Park Factors: 101/101) where 1,810,018 fans witnessed their 1984 Royals finish the season with a .519 winning percentage.
