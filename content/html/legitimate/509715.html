CHRISTOPHER YOUNG basketball profile


Christopher Young is USA-Lithuanian basketball player born on June 30 1979 in Southfield, MI. He is a 6'9'' power forward / center who most recently played at Okapi Aalstar in Belgian Pro Basketball League. Young graduated University of Michigan in 2002. He is represented by Court Side agency. Young has played in Belgium, France, Germany, Italy, Poland and USA.

Weight: 116kg / 255lbs

Team: Okapi Aalst (Belgium) (2010-14)

Nationality: USA-Lithuania

Agency: Court Side

College: Michigan '2002

High School: Catholic Cent. MI Nv '1999
