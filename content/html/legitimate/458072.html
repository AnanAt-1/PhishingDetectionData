Protect Life
Life Is Safeguarded
Guaranteeing the right to life from conception to natural death.
We advocate for laws and legal precedents that protect the value and dignity of each individual human being’s life.
Religious Freedom
Religious Freedom Prevails
Restoring religious freedom as a fundamental right.
We promote the freedom of every person to live out their religious convictions in the public square, just as in the home or a place of worship.
Free Speech
All Can Speak Freely
Securing free speech for all.
We protect free speech in every sector of society including in public schools and universities because today’s students are tomorrow’s leaders.
Marriage & Family
Marriage And Family Are Protected
Ensuring the law respects God’s created order for marriage, the family, and human sexuality.
We advocate for laws and precedents that promote human flourishing by recognizing the important differences between men and women and honoring God’s design for marriage between one man and one woman.
Parental Rights
Parental Rights Are Guaranteed
Guaranteeing the fundamental rights of parents to direct the upbringing and education of their children.
We advocate for laws and precedents that protect parental rights with the same strict legal standard that protects other fundamental rights.
