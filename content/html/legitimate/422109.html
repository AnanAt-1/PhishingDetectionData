    Bio

    Ross Robinson was born in Rhode island in 1958 and lived mostly in Massachusetts until 2014 when he moved to the state of Washington. Ross has been playing and performing authentic Piedmont and Delta blues since 1989. He sings, plays slide guitar, fingerstyle guitar and writes songs. While in the Boston area Ross performed at Club Passim, The Original House of Blues, The Boston Blues Festival and dozens of other venues. Currently Ross is performing throughout western Washington and Seattle area.  The Olympia Farmers Market, Gas Station Blues in Issaquah and the International Blues Challenge in Centrallia are some of the recent Washington area venues that Ross has played.
