The LIVE Centre is your one-stop shop for navigating hundreds of volunteer opportunities. Enrich your time at university, gain experience and support a cause you admire. 

Drop in and see us at the LIVE Centre

Stop by Hall Building (H-746) to find a volunteer opportunity and to get help with your volunteer application. No appointment necessary!
