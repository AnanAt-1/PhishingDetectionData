The 1993 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 68 games, lost 94 games, and finished in seventh position. They played their home games at Oakland Coliseum (Park Factors: 95/96) where 2,035,025 fans witnessed their 1993 Athletics finish the season with a .420 winning percentage.
