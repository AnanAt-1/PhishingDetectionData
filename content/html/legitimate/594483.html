President's Initiatives

At Oakland University, we offer a world-class education that sets our students on a path to lifelong success. The OU Pledge is a commitment to provide our undergraduates with opportunities for an internship, research or other form of experience-based learning that enhances their academic knowledge. See it in action in a video highlighting the internship journey of Josue Garcia, CAS ’24.

Vision Statement

Oakland University will unlock the potential of individuals and leave a lasting impact on the world through the transformative power of education and research.

Mission Statement

Oakland University cultivates the full potential of a diverse and inclusive community. As a public doctoral institution, we impact Michigan and the world through education, research, scholarship and creative activity.
