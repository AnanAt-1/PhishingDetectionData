Welcome to Brown's Collision Center

Brown’s Collision is your One-Stop Auto Collision and Repair Shop, specializing in major and minor collision repair, hail damage, and auto refinishing. We are recognized by all insurance companies as a leader in the auto field, backing every repair with a lifetime warranty. When you’re faced with the unfortunate event of an automobile accident, we’ll work hard to put you back on the road as a satisfied customer. At Brown’s Collision Center, our ultimate goal is to help you forget all about your accident or damaged vehicle. When we repair your vehicle, you will never be able to tell it was ever damaged.
