Andrea Nugent (born November 1, 1968) is an RMT[1] and Canadian former competition swimmer who specialized in butterfly and freestyle events.[2] Nugent won the bronze medal with the women's 4x100-metre medley relay team at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea, together with Canadian teammates Allison Higson, Jane Kerr and Lori Melien.
