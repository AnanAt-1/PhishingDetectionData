AMERICAN STEEL MOTORCYCLE CLUB

WORLD WIDE

First and foremost, we ARE NOT a veterans club. Although most of us are veterans, there is no requirement to be a veteran. We support those who support us, plain and simple.  American Steel Motorcycle Club formed in 1983 on the island of Okinawa, Japan. The membership originally consisted of US Government civilians and active duty military riding US made motorcycles. As the years passed, these ASMC Brothers moved to other assignments in different continents and different countries. This was the seed from which American Steel MC has grown, expanded and spread throughout the world.
