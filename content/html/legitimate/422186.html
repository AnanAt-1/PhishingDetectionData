Aston at the Waikiki Banyan Mobile Site
Aston at the Waikiki Banyan is a family favorite resort offering extra space, extra comfort, and extra privacy in large One-bedroom condominium suites located just over a block from the beach in the heart of Waikiki. Our welcoming, beautiful open air lobby with waterfall fishpond is graced with photos of "Old Hawaii" recalling our island heritage, and warm aloha spirit.

All suites feature a fully-equipped kitchen and a large lanai. This is a family-friendly resort where every arriving child receives a personal welcome with a sand pail filled with surprises. Every member of the family will enjoy our spacious recreation deck offering a large heated swimming pool, 2 hot tubs, sauna, tennis court, basketball court, barbecues and picnic area.
