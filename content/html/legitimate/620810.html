    ANKLE AND FOOT PAIN TREATMENT IN SAN ANTONIO

    Experience, Compassion, and Quality Care

    At South Texas Podiatry, board-certified foot and ankle specialist Dr. Ed Davis believes that a doctor and patient become a team for treating an individual's feet. When you become our patient, Dr. Davis and his staff spend most of their time listening to understand your concerns and responding with the best treatment options for you.

    We’re also passionate about patient education, and we want our patients to be informed about the ankle and foot pain treatment options they have when they receive our care. We believe that informed patients make better decisions about their health and well being.

    Our services include:

    • Regular office hours 9:30 am to 6 pm.
    • Emphasis on conservative treatment for foot pain and biomechanics.
    • Radiology and diagnostic ultrasound equipment on site.
    • Diabetic shoes and durable medical equipment dispensing.
    • Hospital and ambulatory surgery center procedures.
    • Handicap accessible office.
    • Acceptance of most insurance plans.

    Because convenience for San Antonio area residents is important to their selection of a podiatrist, we’ve ensured that our location is as convenient and accessible to as many people as possible. Please feel free to contact us with any questions you have or to request an appointment.

    If you’re ready to get off the sidelines and get started with a compassionate, knowledgeable, and experienced physician who has your best interests at heart, schedule your first appointment at South Texas Podiatry today.

    Extensive Foot Care in San Antonio, TX

    Cutting-Edge Conservative Treatments

    At South Texas Podiatry, our emphasis is on non-invasive and minimally invasive procedures, and we’re passionate about providing the least invasive treatment available for your foot or ankle condition. We offer state-of-the-art, non-invasive treatment of plantar fasciitis, diagnostic ultrasound, diagnostic x-ray and fluoroscopy in the office, advanced treatments for heel pain, ESWT (extracorporeal shockwave therapy), radiofrequency ablation of painful nerves, podiatric biomechanics, and more. We even provide a full range of custom molded orthotics used for work, exercise and sports such as running, tennis, soccer, and skiing.

    Request Appointment

    Expert Pediatric Foot Doctor

    Specializing in Pediatric Foot & Ankle Care

    Having healthy feet is important for anyone, but it’s especially vital for children, as their feet and ankles are continually developing. Injuries, gait abnormalities, biomechanical errors, and other podiatric issues that commonly affect children can have a lifelong effect on their ability to walk properly, and can result in serious pain in the future. Dr. Ed Davis specializes in pediatric podiatry, and he will work closely with you and your child to ensure their foot health now and in the future.
