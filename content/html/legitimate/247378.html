Pop-Up Happy Hour at the Harbour Centre Gallery

Date: Thursday, May 22
Time: 3:00 - 6:00pm
Location: Harbour Centre Gallery, 515 W. Hastings St.

Join the SFU community for a fun-filled afternoon in the Harbour Centre Gallery! Enjoy delicious food, lively music, and great vibes in a warm and welcoming space. Each ticket includes a drink of your choice, three gourmet sliders, salad and other sides, and dessert. Open to all faculty, staff, and students — don't miss out!
