Claim to Fame SGU Stargate Universe

Mike Dopud (Serbian: Majk Dopuđ, Мајк Допуђ; born June 10, 1968) is a Canadian actor, stuntman, and former professional football player known for his supporting roles on television. His best-known roles include Vic Hadfield in the miniseries Canada Russia '72, Jason Micic on Power, Kimbo Comstock on Arctic Air, Roy McAfee on Cedar Cove, Viktor Mikalek on Arrow, and Michael Vinson on The 100. He also took on several minor roles on Stargate SG-1 and Stargate Atlantis, as well as performing many stunts on both shows, before taking on a bigger role as Varro on Stargate Universe. He was nominated for a Taurus World Stunt Award in 2005 for his work in Walking Tall.
