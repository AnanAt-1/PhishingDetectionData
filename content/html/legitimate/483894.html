A Vibrant Sober Community "By the Sea" in Carlsbad

Getting initial sobriety isn't easy, so we applaud you for that.  But can the next six to nine months actually be even harder? Some people in recovery say that there is a "pink cloud" that makes the first thirty days of sobriety uplifting and exciting.  But after that awakening, settling in to 'long term sobriety' can be tricky for many people.  There is a discouraging statistic that 90% of those who complete an inpatient rehab program will relapse within the first year*.

Where many men and women run into trouble are the weeks and months that follow initial sobriety, especially if they return home to the setting in which they were using drugs or alcohol.  As you progress in your life and begin the process of  re-entering the workforce or resuming your educational career, it's crucial to stay plugged into a 'recovery community.'  Not only will you be surrounded by others who are serious about maintaining continuous sobriety at By The Sea, our on-site staff  will be there to provide professional guidance and support so you can achieve long term sobriety.

While living at our San Diego sober living, you will be immersed in a community of successfully recovering men who are pursuing successful careers, relationships, and physical health.  Our San Diego sober living home is within walking distances to many opportunities to work as well as the gym, recovery meetings, public transit, basketball courts, and restaurants. We are a short drive (or public transit ride) to the beach. Carlsbad was rated 'one of the best places to live in America', receiving an A+ score from niche.com

 Our comprehensive sober living program includes:

  • Bi-weekly drug testing, and breathalyzing
  • Access to outpatient therapy/ psychiatry/ family therapy
  • Zero tolerance (creates a positive culture of recovery)
  • Support and mentoring
  • Transitional living planning
  • Reviewing and resolving personal credit issues
  • Family communication
  • Daily schedule reviews and time management support
  • Organizational skills development

MAT (Medication-Assisted Treatment) in our Sober Living

We ensure that you can safely continue your MAT or medication-assisted treatment program while living in sober living. Suboxone, Naloxone, and Buprenorphine are medications that save lives. Medical supervision of medication-assisted treatment while living in a safe and alcohol and drug-free sober house can get you the greatest benefits of science and results. Let our sober homes be the support for a sustained recovery. The staff at By the Sea Recovery sober living is qualified and trained to secure and oversee client self-administration of medication like Suboxone and Subutex (brand names for buprenorphine) that is managed and prescribed by a licensed physician. These standards have been adopted from our DHCS licensed detox facility to ensure procedures are safe and supportive to client needs. These policies represent our exceptional dedication to providing the best recovery environment possible. Read more about MAT on the SAMHSA website.
