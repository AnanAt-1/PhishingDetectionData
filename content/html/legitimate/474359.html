The 1995 Cincinnati Reds played 144 games during the regular season, won 85 games, lost 59 games, and finished in first position. They played their home games at Riverfront Stadium (Park Factors: 99/98) where 1,837,649 fans witnessed their 1995 Reds finish the season with a .590 winning percentage.
