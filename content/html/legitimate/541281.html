Etymology of Daoust

What does the name Daoust mean?

The Daoust family name is thought to be derived from a place name in L'Ile de France. The original bearer of the name Daoust, probably lived, held land, or was born in the beautiful Ile de France region.

Early Notables of the Daoust family

Notable amongst the family was the Picardy branch, who were elevated to the nobility in 1454 as the Seigneurs of Jumelles. Later they were raised to the title of the Marquis de Jumelles, and thence to the Marquis d'Aoust in 1739.
