Roster for the 2004 Oakland Athletics | Baseball Almanac

It is a comprehensive team roster and player names are sorted by the fielding position where the most number of games were played during the regular season. "The only disappointment was we (the 2004 Oakland Athletics) didn't make the playoffs, but there was a small margin of error, and Kenny (Macha / Manager) did a nice job with it. The 2004 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 91 games, lost 71 games, and finished in second position. They played their home games at McAfee Coliseum (Park Factors: 97/96) where 2,201,516 fans witnessed their 2004 Athletics finish the season with a .562 winning percentage.
