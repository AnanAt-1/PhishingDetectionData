The Charley Chase Filmography

Listed below are (nearly) all the films that Charley Chase starred in and directed. No such list is entirely definitive, especially considering the scores of shorts that Chase produced early in his career.
The creation of this filmography would not have been possible without the tireless efforts of Rob Farr, David Glass, Chris Seguin, and Steve Massa.
