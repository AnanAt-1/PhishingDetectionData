Contact Us

Do you have any questions, comments, or concerns about Maple Valley School District? If so, we would love to hear from you.

Please note that personal information will not be shared without your permission. Expect to hear a response within two business days.

Help for What is a good email address? If you do not have an email, please enter noemail@mvs.k12.mi.us
Help for What is a good phone number for us to contact you? Providing a phone number is optional. If you decide to include one, please enter your phone number with +1 and area code. No spaces or symbols are needed.
