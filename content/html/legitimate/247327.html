Westwind Communications

Westwind Communications is a public relations and marketing firm that gets results. We create and implement marketing, advertising, and communications strategies for a wide variety of small to medium sized businesses including Doctors, Lawyers, Inventors, Authors and Entrepreneurs. From conception to implementation we serve as an integral member of our client’s marketing team.

Westwind Communications excels at helping clients get all the publicity they deserve and more. Our extensive media contacts have produced volumes of clippings and hours of television and radio coverage on both a local and national level. Westwind Communications’ clients have been featured by Good Morning America, CNN, ABC Nightly News, Nightline, TIME, PBS, Los Angeles Times, USA Today, Investor’s Business Daily, Wall Street Journal, Washington Post, Family Circle, Woman’s World, and Entrepreneur, to name a few. Contact Scott Lorenz at scottlorenz@westwindcos.com or call 734-667-2090.

Book marketing and publicity is a specialty of Westwind Communications. The key to successful book marketing and book promotion is to identify the book’s potential readers, then contact the media outlets those readers are most likely to use. We write and send pitches and press releases as well as make follow up phone calls to broadcast and print media outlets persuading reporters and reviewers to interview the author and write about our client’s book. Click here for book marketing.
