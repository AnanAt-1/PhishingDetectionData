Briefly about us

We are programmers and system administrators, we help others to get these professions, because we believe that everyone should be given the opportunity to develop and build their future.

Our school modestly intends to become a center of IT education and a place of communication for both professionals and beginners.

Our goal is to produce the most professional staff, regardless of what initial knowledge they came to study. We have gathered the best team of practicing teachers who have managed to build their own successful careers in IT companies, and now, having developed effective training programs, are ready to share their knowledge.
