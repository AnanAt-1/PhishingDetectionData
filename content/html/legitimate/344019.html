Welcome to Cherry Hill Historic House and Farm!

The mission of the Friends of Cherry Hill Foundation is to preserve and protect the integrity of the mid-19th century house, barn and outbuildings along with support educational programs that increase appreciation for life in mid-19th century rural Virginia when the Blaisdells, a middle class farming family, lived at Cherry Hill between 1856 and 1868.
