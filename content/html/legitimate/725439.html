        Leading process technologies for industry and production

        ProXES successfully combines three leading process technology brands under one roof: FrymaKoruma, Stephan and Terlet. Together, ProXES combines innovative and long-standing expertise as a manufacturer of machines, process lines and solutions for the food, pharmaceutical, cosmetics and advanced materials industries. As an agile partner - with 5 technology centres around the globe - ProXES supports its customers in turning new application ideas into desirable and sustainable products. 


        About

        HIGHEST QUALITY MEETS HIGHEST EFFICIENCY

        Robust and compact design, latest homogenizing technology and extremely easy operation: that's the ProxxD. With an improved homogenizer and an increased production capacity of up to 6,000 kg, the ProxxD significantly enhances your production benefits. Learn more >

        PERFECTLY HUMMUS & TAHINI PROCESSING

        Achieve the finest texture with our food processing machines – efficient, scalable, and designed for industrial hummus and tahini production. With advanced grinding and emulsifying technology, ProXES ensures consistent quality, high output, and optimized production efficiency. Learn more >

        PROXES IS WORLD MARKET LEADER 2023

        Innovation and quality pay off: We are among the world market leaders 2023 in the WirtschaftsWoche ranking. The reasons for this success are a strong team, an extremely positive response to our co-creation approach in the food sector, and an innovative and target group-oriented product portfolio. Learn more >

        MAYONNAISE PROCESSING MADE EASY

        Ensure stable emulsions and perfect consistency with our advanced homogenizing technology. ProXES food processing machines guarantee efficient mayonnaise production, reducing processing time while maintaining premium product quality and high process reliability. Learn more >

        PERFECTLY COOLED FOR PERFECT TASTE

        From chunky pasta sauces to juicy marinades and creamy hummus, the Terlet Terlocool is the perfect solution for fast and efficient cooling for product volumes of 600-1,200 liters. At the same time, both taste and texture remain unchanged. Learn more >

        COMBITHERM: SMART HEATING & COOLING

        Optimize industrial food production with Combitherm, the versatile food processing system for precise heating, cooling, and emulsifying. Designed for flexibility and efficiency, it ensures consistent results, improved energy efficiency, and seamless integration into your production line. Learn more >

        Fast order, even faster delivery

        From high-quality emulsification and fine grinding to innovative all-in-one solutions: We have a range of pre-produced machines in stock, just waiting to be used. Available are MaxxD, ProxxD, a selection of different milling machines and universal machines. Learn more >

        Seminar: Emulsions from development to production

        This compact seminar from May 08.09 provides essential fundamentals on emulsion production and stabilization, including practical sessions on pilot-scale equipment. Ideal for professionals in lab, production, R&D, analytics, engineering & quality in the pharmaceutical, cosmetics & food industries. Learn more >
