Mickey Mouse Clubhouse is an animated television show that aired on Disney Junior from 2006 to 2016. The show features Mickey Mouse alongside his friends Minnie Mouse, Donald Duck, Daisy Duck, Goofy and Pluto as they solve problems and embark on adventures in Mickey's Clubhouse.

Each episode begins with a problem that needs to be solved, and Mickey and his friends employ different tools to help them overcome obstacles. They use a magical "Mousketool" from Mickey's Tool Shed, which can transform into different objects to help them on their mission. They also call on Toodles, a helpful robot, who always has the right Mousketool for the problem at hand.

The show is geared towards preschool-aged children and incorporates basic educational concepts, such as colors, shapes, numbers, and problem-solving skills. The characters also teach social skills and friendship, as the gang works together to achieve their goals.

Tony Anselmo voices Donald Duck, Tress MacNeille voices Daisy Duck, Bill Farmer voices Goofy and Pluto, Russi Taylor voices Minnie Mouse, Corey Burton voices Ludwig Von Drake, April Winchell voices Clarabelle Cow, Will Ryan voices Pete, Jim Cummings voices Pete's sidekick, Peg-leg Pete, Bret Iwan voices Mickey Mouse, Dee Bradley Baker voices Space Goof, and Wayne Allwine voices Mickey Mouse in archive audio.

Mickey Mouse Clubhouse was praised by parents and critics for its educational value and positive messages about friendship and teamwork. It was also a hit with young children who enjoyed watching their favorite Disney characters go on exciting adventures.

The show aired for ten seasons, with 125 episodes in total. In addition, there were several special episodes, including a Christmas special and a Halloween special.

Overall, Mickey Mouse Clubhouse is a fun and entertaining show that teaches young children valuable skills while keeping them engaged and entertained. Whether you're a child or an adult who loves Disney, this show is definitely worth watching.
