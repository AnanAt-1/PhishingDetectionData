We Are Purpose Driven

The purpose of the Northeastern Michigan Board of REALTORS® (NEMBOR) is to serve its members through promoting high ethical and professional standards of practice; to provide educational opportunities to the members; to provide quality services and opportunities for them to become better businessmen and women; and to promote community awareness of private property issues.

We represent area REALTORS® and business people helping you with your real estate needs in many communities in Northern Michigan encompassing Arenac, Iosco, and Ogemaw Counties as well as surrounding areas.

Our real estate professionals service Northern Michigan and Michigan's Sunrise Side along Lake Huron, the AuSable River, Huron National Forest and many inland lakes in Northeastern Michigan. Our REALTOR® professionals have access to real estate MLS information that is current and verified for data integrity.
