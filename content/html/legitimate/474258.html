The 1956 Kansas City Athletics played 154 games during the regular season, won 52 games, lost 102 games, and finished in eighth position. They played their home games at Municipal Stadium (Park Factors: 100/104) where 1,015,154 fans witnessed their 1956 Athletics finish the season with a .338 winning percentage.
