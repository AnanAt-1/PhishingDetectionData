About Hilda Stahl

Hilda Stahl is a published author of children's books and young adult books. Some of the published credits of Hilda Stahl include Mystery at Bellwood Estate, Uninvited Guests (Elizabeth Gail Revised Series #9), Unexpected Letter (Elizabeth Gail), and Trouble from the Past (Elizabeth Gail).
