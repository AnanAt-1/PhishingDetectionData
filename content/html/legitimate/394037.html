ABOUT LILY JACKSON EXQUISITE HAIRDRESSERS SYDNEY

We are celebrating our 23rd year this year!
An outstanding accolade for our CEO and Director Julie Peacocke and proof of our commitment to high quality hairdressing and outstanding customer service.

Whether you need a great haircut, hair colour, hair extensions, hair straightening, false eyelashes, special occasion or wedding hair and makeup or make up for a commercial photo shoot, our team of professionals will create a look that you’ll love.

We research and trial all the hair & makeup products that we use to make sure they are the best option for you, your hair and your skin.

We are located in Potts Point, Sydney.
Conveniently located for clients from Surry Hills, Paddington, Woollahra, Edgecliffe, Rose Bay, Rushcutters Bay, Elizabeth Bay and the surrounding Sydney suburbs.

But people travel from all over Sydney, New South Wales and even Australia wide to visit the best hair salons. Conveniently located just minutes from the Sydney CBD, our hair & makeup salon environment is so relaxing, you won’t want to leave!
