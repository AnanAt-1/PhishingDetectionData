Bob Dernier Baseball Stats | Baseball Almanac

Bob Dernier, the Major League Baseball player, was born on Saturday, January 5, 1957, in Kansas City, Missouri. Dernier was 23 years old when he broke into the major leagues on September 7, 1980, with the Philadelphia Phillies.
