Durham University Treasure Trap

is the world’s oldest continuously-running Live Roleplay group. We meet twice a week to play roles in a fantasy mediaeval world – either in the congenial atmosphere of a mediaeval inn for our Tavern Nights each Monday, or in the woods around Durham for Adventures each weekend. We also have run an annual banquet and a weekend-long end-of-year game, and send groups to larger live roleplay events around the country.
