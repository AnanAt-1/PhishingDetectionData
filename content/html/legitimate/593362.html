Perfumers ~ A to E

Below you will find perfumers, or "noses", with last names beginning with the letters A through E. Some perfumers have their own pages — if the name is a link, click on it.

Jump to Perfumers ~ F to K, Perfumers ~ L to S, Perfumers T to Z or to the Glossary if you'd like to see a definition of a nose.

This page was last updated on 14 August 2021.

Disclaimer: Please note that accurate information about who created a fragrance is very hard to come by. I make no claims about the accuracy of anything listed here! Many thanks to everyone who has kindly sent in additions or corrections.
