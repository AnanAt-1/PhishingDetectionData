DUFRESNE Family

First Generation

1. Pierre1 Dufresne was born 1627.(1892) His body was interred 1 December 1687 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1893)

He married Anne Palin 1657.(1894) Anne was born 1634.(1895)

Pierre Dufresne and Anne Palin had the following children:

+ 2 i. Pierre2 Dufresne.

3 ii. Jeanne Dufresne. Her body was interred 6 February 1711 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1896) She married twice. She married Rene Minaud in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 27 October 1682.(1897) Rene was born 1658.(1898) Rene was the son of Jean Minaud and Jeanne Caille. His body was interred 19 January 1687 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1899) She married Gabriel Rouleau in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 25 November 1687.(1900) Gabriel was the son of Gabriel Rouleau dit Sanssoucy and Mathurine Leroux. His body was interred 20 December 1730 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1901) He was baptized in Chateau-Richer, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 15 July 1665. Religion: religion unknown.(1902) (See Gabriel Rouleau for the continuation of this line.)

She was baptized in Ste. Famille, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 24 November 1666. Religion: religion unknown.(1903)

4 iii. Catherine Dufresne. She married twice. She married Guillaume Rouleau in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 3 February 1688.(1904) Guillaume was the son of Gabriel Rouleau dit Sanssoucy and Mathurine Leroux. His body was interred 7 March 1703 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1905) He was baptized in Chateau-Richer, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 3 May 1662. Religion: religion unknown.(1906) She married Claude Plante in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 6 January 1706.(1907) Claude was the son of Jean Plante and Francoise Boucher. His body was interred 19 November 1729 in Ste. Famille, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1908) He was baptized in Quebec, Quebec, PQ, Canada, 26 January 1653. Religion: Roman Catholic.(1909)

She was baptized in Ste. Famille, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 9 February 1668. Religion: religion unknown.(1910)

5 iv. Louise Dufresne. Her body was interred 12 February 1687 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1911) She was baptized in Ste. Famille, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 22 May 1672. Religion: religion unknown.(1912)

6 v. Marie Dufresne. Her body was interred 3 August 1687 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1913) She was baptized in Ste. Famille, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 13 October 1674. Religion: religion unknown.(1914)

7 vi. Guillaume Dufresne. His body was interred 28 December 1745 in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada.(1915) He married Genevieve Ruel in St. Laurent, Ile d'Orleans, Montmorency, PQ, Canada, 13 November 1702.(1916)
