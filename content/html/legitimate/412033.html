Earl of Dartmouth weds Fiona Handbury

After a failed relationship with Claire Kavanagh, who bore him a son out of wedlock (born March 2005), the nearly-60-year-old aristocrat has wed long-time girlfriend Mrs Fiona Handbury (nee Campbell), Australian-born mother of three. The civil ceremony took place at Haldon Belvedere, a folly near Exeter, followed by a reception at which William Cash and his wife Vanessa Neumann (formerly linked to Mick Jagger and William Stirling, nephew of the Marchioness of Salisbury) were present. The newly-weds are off on honeymoon in Biarritz.
