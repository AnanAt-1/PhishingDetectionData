      Studying Moringa

      Using moringa for healthy living.

      I did not discover anything new. I just learned about a source of health that hasn't hit the mainstream in the U.S.

      Our garden is 5 years old with some lush moringa trees. I use my moringa trees for studying and testing the effects on my health, but I now have so much that I would like to share it with others. Above all else, I want to learn so that I can know the best items to eat to stay healthy.
