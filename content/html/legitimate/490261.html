    We Are The Leading Provider of Christian Debt Consolidation Services

    Are you going through a difficult financial period? Are you scared to answer your phone? Do you feel you are drowning in debt and getting nowhere?

    Most people experience this situation sometime during their lifetime. This situation occurs from reduced or loss of income, medical bills, unsecured loans, pay-day loans and high interest bearing credit card debt etc.

    However, rather than dwelling on the problem you need to focus on finding the right solution to become debt free. Here at Christian Debt Services we can assist by assessing your debt situation and devising a plan best suited for your current financial needs.

    Who Is Christian Debt Consolidation?

    Christian Debt Services is a team of specialists who can help you consolidate your debts into small monthly manageable payments. Our Professionally Trained and Certified Debt Counsellors will assess your current financial situation providing you with personalized options that are based on your level of debt, your current employment situation and your financial goals.

    How Does Christian Debt Consolidation Work?

    Debt consolidation is the process of combining all of your unsecured accounts into one lower monthly payment reducing or eliminating high interest rates plus late and over the limit fees giving you a chance to become debt free in less than 4 – 5 years.

    Examples of unsecured accounts are: Credit Cards, Bank Loans, Pay Day Loans, Medical Bills, Old Utility Bills and Collection Accounts. By making minimum payments or a little above minimum to your creditors it will take you years to repay your balances in full. That cycle is reversed by enrolling in one of our programs. You will definitely have peace of mind knowing that you now have a goal of becoming debt free within a much shorter time period while saving money. The hassles of paying individual creditors will be eliminated and you will have one convenient monthly payment instead. Those pesky collections calls will be a thing of the past and you will no longer endure anxiety attacks and harassment but instead enjoy peace of mind.
