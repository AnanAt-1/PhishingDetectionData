Road Traffic Control Game - Traffic Manager

Rating: 5.3/10 - 4 votes

Don’t let them crash! Traffic Manager will let you flex those wits and strategy skills, even though it seems so easy. In this game, you will have to make sure that the cars on the road don’t collide with each other. That’s so simple, right? Press play and see for yourself. Think fast! Tip: This game is great on both computer and mobile.

How to Play: You have only several seconds on the timer to observe the road before the traffic barrier automatically lets the cars pass. If you think it’s better to let a car out before the seconds run out, click on that car quickly! You must meet the minimum number of cars who have passed successfully to complete a round.

This HTML5 game works on PC/Mac and mobile device browsers.
