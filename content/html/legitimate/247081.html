      I don't have a web site yet. Can I apply to be an Affiliate anyway? 
      Unfortunately, no - when we receive an application, we must review your site to make sure it's acceptable.

      What are my responsibilities as a KKDS Affiliate? 
      Your responsibilities are to set up links on your site to KKDS. Make sure your links are correct to guarantee you get credit for sales from your links. The number of links you have is entirely up to you.

      How does the KKDS Affiliate Program work? 
      Easy. You, as an affiliate, place tracked links to KKDS on your website. These links can be to specific products or section. When your visitors come to KKDS through one of these links and make a purchase, you get credit for the sale during that client. Every month you'll receive a check for a percentage of the sales your links were responsible for generated by those links.

      What does it cost to join? 
      Nothing! It's absolutely free to become a KKDS Affiliate and start receiving checks from us.

      How is KKDS's Affiliate Program different from the others? 
      KKDS pays a generous commission! We sell products that are unique and in demand, which mean easy money for you! We'll also try our best to help each site!

      What are KKDS's responsibilities? 
      We will take care of everything related to orders, including shipping products, billing customers, paying your commissions, and managing customer service. We will also provide banners and graphics to help you sell videos on your site. We'll try our best to help each site.

      Can I use the KKDS name and logo on my site? 
      Of course, but please do not alter our logo in any way. If you need a certain graphic on your site, contact us and we'll see what we can do.

      Should I include price information when I promote KKDS products? 
      No, for a very good reason. KKDS runs promotions often and offers very good discounts to customers. Since we don't want to confuse anyone with conflicting prices affiliates may not state KKDS prices on their sites.

      Is my site eligible to become a KKDS Affiliate? 
      Most likely, yes. Anyone may submit an application, no matter how large or small and we review each and every potential Affiliate web site before accepting them into the KKDS Program. We do reserve the right to refuse a site if it contains objectionable material, including explicit language or content, or support for violent groups.
