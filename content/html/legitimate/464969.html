We connect you to the community like nothing else.

AmericanTowns is a virtual "town square," plugging you in to the information and resources related to your city, town and neighborhood. Whether it's town issues and community activism or school calendar events and sports schedules, AmericanTowns delivers the info faster and helps you use it better.

Each community can also use our easy to use tools to post local events, news and announcements, press releases, take donations, create a web site and more.
