History
Jan Lettens 27/08/2008
HMS VIMIERA; Destroyer built for the British Navy in 1917 by Swan, Hunter & Wigham Richardson; 900 tons; 312x29.5x10.9 ft; 27,000 s.h.p.; 34 knots; turbine engines and Yarrow boilers; She was equipped with 4 x 4´ guns, one 2pdr. and 5 machine guns

On January 9th, 1942, the British Navy destroyer Vimiera (Lt. Cdr. Mackenzie), was sunk by a mine, off the Nore. In total, 1 officer and 90 crew were killed.
