More about us


Our focus on both New and Used/Rebuilt sanitary equipment allows us to keep experienced staff members with extensive backgrounds in dairy processing, food production, beverages, brewing, pharmaceuticals and more. Our team consists of engineers, designers, and maintenance personnel who have real-world experience from the industries we serve.

We have 5 acres of new & used food processing equipment at our Denver-area headquarters. Whether you’re looking for new equipment, maintaining an older machine, or hunting for parts for obsolete model, we hope to be a resource you can count on.

Dairy Engineering Company has been supplying sanitary processing equipment and services to a wide variety of customers since 1981.

Sanitary equipment essentially originated in the dairy industry - as our name would suggest. However, the sanitary market has since expanded into the beverage, food processing, pharmaceutical, meat & oils, bakery, brewing, microbrewing, distilling, confectionery, wine, CBD & cannabinoid extraction from hemp, and other industries that require the non-contaminating advantages of sanitary stainless steel.
