Miyako - Meaning of Miyako

  • The meaning of Miyako is "beautiful night child".
  • The most common Kanji used in the name Miyako is 美夜子.
  • The name meaning can vary if alternative Kanji (Japanese characters) is chosen by the parents.
  • Miyako is a Japanese girl name.
