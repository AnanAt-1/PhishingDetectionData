Get ready to be wowed by these amazing options of pink hair. This color is a timeless trend and, after all, what person doesn’t like pink? There are so many pretty pink shades! Take a look at these inspiring and unique pink hairstyles, and maybe you’ll find your ideal hair color to rock.

Exploring the myriad shades and color placements of pink hairstyles in this article was an absolute delight. From vibrant magentas to soft pastels, each hue infuses a unique happiness into the soul. Plus, the neutralizing power of cool pinks ensures a beautiful, brass-free canvas as it fades.

As someone who's proudly rocked pink hair for over a decade, I can attest to its transformative power and timeless appeal. Pink isn't just a color—it's a power hue, and I wouldn't have it any other way. Here's to embracing the joy of pink hair and all the vibrant possibilities it brings!”

Vanessa Koeb
Colorist

In Vogue Pink Hair Ideas

Well, some of these looks are funky, while others are rather exquisite and can be pulled by anyone who desires a creative but subtle-looking hair color on the base of their natural hair shade. The latter include subtle rosewood, strawberry blonde or pastel pink hair color ideas. However, dark pink hair and different color combinations of pink with gray, purple or black are amazing too. Choose your next look from the variants below!

1. Pink Balayage on Natural Brown Hair

@kaansayar1

This lady focused a warm shade of pink on the ends to create an awesome look. Soft pink highlights produce a dynamic effect for this gorgeous hairstyle, and the face-framing streaks enormously flatter her beautiful skin tone.

2. Rose Gold Hair to Turn Heads

This rich mane boasts a multitude of rose gold shades paired with loose waves to create a mind-blowing combination, while dark roots provide a cool but not harsh contrast. A perfect solution to elevate the beauty of long hair.

@georgeeblancoo

3. A Long Shag Cut with Pink Face Frame

Without any doubt, the cream blonde base and pastel pink face-framing ribbons create a stunning look. The hues work well together on a long shaggy haircut with curtain bangs.

@stylistricardosantiago

4. A Captivating Combination

Be the star on the scene with this out-of-this-world hairdo. The pastel pink base smoothly melts into the deep pink that transitions into the contrasting black on the tips. What makes this hair even more amazing is that the unusual color transitions are emphasized by waves.

@hair_slayerz

5. Rosé Crop

We fell in love with this rosé crop that looks daring and feminine at the same time. An excellent choice for self-confident people who knows what they want in life.

@stepthebarber

6. Pink Champagne for a Princess

Beautiful strands of long shiny hair fall down the back like a waterfall, and hues of pink champagne enhance the fairy-tale-like feel of this ethereal hairstyle. Either perfectly straight or wavy, this color hits the mark in both cases.

@mariahthestylistt

7. Feminine Cool Pink Lowlights

If you want to add a romantic vibe to your sandy blonde, look no further. Blush pink lowlights combined with messy waves are everything you need to upgrade your style.

@craftyourhair

8. Marvelous Strawberry Hues

Just have a look at this stunning mixture of strawberry hues on long hair. We particularly love the elaborate transition from darker roots to light pink ends the stylist has beautifully achieved. Side parting helps to create an ultra-feminine look by enhancing the hair’s volume.

@thescissorsammurai

9. Icy Pink Lob

If you want to change your hair color without compromising a classy look, opt for an icy pink tone, as it is subtle and very close to platinum blonde. Paired with messy waves, a light pink color is what you need to totally transform your everyday lob.

@rossmichaelssalon

10. Hot Pink Highlights

Vibrant pink strands on a dark brown base look absolutely amazing! Pay attention to how beautifully hot pink highlights frame the face, flattering the skin tone. Steal this idea if you want to get a beachy look with a touch of pink for the next summer season.

@jenghair

11. Luscious Berry Pink

Do you love berry ice cream? If yes, here’s a perfect example inspired by your favorite dessert – a delicious color blend you’ll definitely want to try!

@msnataliejean

12. Blush Pink Bob

This blush pink messy bob is just a variation of the iconic bombshell blonde, which, in this case, the subtle strawberry shade makes it stand out. Great, isn’t it? Steal this idea to refresh your blonde look.

@seolahair

13. Pink Ringlets

Go no further if you’re a curly hair owner, who’s looking for a sophisticated solution that can provide a queen-of-style vibe. Curls and pink can create a winning combo that is certain to make your hair look captivating. Pastel hues of strawberry pink go exceptionally well with the tight curls, don’t they?

@bel_pipsqueekinsaigon

14. Purple & Pink Balayage

This is a great example of a dark hair color working well with magenta pink and purple balayage. The deep and vibrant pink shade smoothly transitions into jet black with just a few touches of purple. It keeps the overall look bright and shiny elevating the darker colored roots.

@rwhair_

15. Flamingo Pink

Shine bright with this perfect blend of flamingo pink and vanilla blonde. An excellent choice for people with blue eyes and fair skin, what more can we say? And yes, this bed-head hairstyle brings in a very cool vibe.

@bel_pipsqueekinsaigon

16. Dragon Fruit Pink

Check out this adorable pink hair color. We love multiple hues of pink that create dimension, and, together with loose waves, make the hair look more dynamic.

@georgeeblancoo

17. Bubblegum Pink Highlights

Could you ever imagine that deep red and bubblegum pink go so well together? A great choice for long, straight hair, indeed. Due to the sharp contrast, the pink pieces draw a bright contour to the beautiful face.

@lizzvargashair

18. Rock Star Pink

This charming lady has gone for a breathtaking pink and blonde balayage, making a perfect choice. Although this pink hue looks sweet like candy, the messy ‘do can reveal a strong personality. So, if you feel this rock star vibe, do steal this look.

@bel_pipsqueekinsaigon

19. Subtle Powder Pink Bottom

While most two-tone hair ideas are all about a darker bottom section and a lighter top section, you can always go against the grain and do the exact opposite. For instance, try mixing a brunette top with a subtle rose bottom section like this girl.

@hairloungenyc

20. Magical Magenta

Deep, vibrant magenta shade is exactly what you need if your mission is to turn heads! Shiny waves and dark roots create dimension and movement. You’ll feel like a queen with this rich shade of pink, without any doubt!

@makeupbyfrances

21. Straight Brown Hair with Pink Highlights

Pink highlights are one of the easiest ways to turn a hairstyle trendy. Out of all the pink hair color solutions, some soft pink streaks on your natural hair color are a top-rated idea. This style doesn’t look Barbie-like, as it might happen with other pink hair colors.

@hair_phlip

22. Dare to be Different

Check out this beautiful peach-orange, and cotton candy pink hair. The colors are light and of a pastel nature, so they actually work well together. Play up the multi-dimensional strands with braids like it’s shown here.

@tomsmithhd

23. Red Hair with Pink Highlights

There are countless options for pink hairstyles, with pinks varying from pastels to almost reds. This color work boasts a perfect fade. Soft pink ends and very thin copper highlights are added to lighten up the dark red, burgundy base.

@susan.aw

24. Dark Brown Hair with Fuchsia Highlights

It is something that will work on almost any hair length, but it looks particularly beautiful on this angled bob. The fuchsia pops fantastically, contrasting the dark base.

@foxandjane

25. Reddish Brown Bob with Pink Streaks

Pink highlights work well as accent pieces combined with a reddish-brown base. This striking color work also embraces a beautiful caramel balayage. It may sound like a lot of colors for one head of hair, but it is still cohesive.

@larisadoll

26. Bob with Salmon Pink Highlights

When we think about pink hair, we are likely to imagine cool shades like cotton candy and bubble gum. However, you should also remember the warmer options like coral and salmon. These warm hues are flattering against golden blondes and lighten up warm browns. Her medium blonde bob with warm pink streaks is something you can use for inspiration.

@katierosehair

27. Fairy’s Dream

These curly locks are effortless and carefree. The pastel shade is the finishing touch to make this hairstyle absolutely gorgeous!

@beautybystacey_

28. Blonde Hair with Reddish Pink Highlights

Check out those colors, looking like a beautiful sunset thanks to the mix of golden blonde, muted brown, white blonde, and reddish pink. The long, soft waves are the best texture for this romantic highlighted look.

@alix_maya

29. Sweet Sherbert

These silky straight strands are ice-cream colored – all they need is a cherry on top. Indulge in this heavenly peachy pink hue from roots to tips and incorporate a scattering of subtle lowlights into the mix for a dimensional effect. Such a hair color has an incredible allure!

@ilyrascissorhands

30. Auburn Base with Peach Highlights

Not all peach highlights are bold and bright. This gorgeous hairstyle shows how to work peachy tones into your hair for a more natural effect. Soft coral streaks are added to reddish hair, and the result is something anyone can pull off!

@katierosehair

31. Light Blonde and Bright Pink Hair

Neon pink highlights like these would pop against any hair color. However, the pink appears even brighter when it’s set off with platinum blonde hair. Mix chunky and thin highlights for a personalized look.

@hairbyangelaalberici

32. Ripple Effect of Pastel Pink Ombre Highlights

This long hairstyle features pale pink beachy waves that look great on brown hair. As you see, pink pastel hair shade doesn’t have to be a solid color. The roots can be your natural brown hair color, and the ends can be teased with some delicate peach undertones. Pink ombre highlights are painted on softly, so there isn’t a harsh line but more of a soft blend where the two colors meet.

@garrbarr

33. Strawberry Dream

Pastel pink hair dyes can be an idea for light blonde strands if you want to achieve a gorgeous, creamy hue. These locks look like candy, so pretty and unique. If you want to know how to get sweet pink hair, you must see a professional colorist who knows how to lighten your hair as much as possible before applying any permanent or temporary pastel pink color.

@christinakreitel

34. Hot Pink Short Hair with White Highlights

Bright white and neon pink are two very different colors that work very nicely together. The pure white hue cools down the warm brightness of this neon pink. And when the shades blend together, they create a pretty lollipop-look mix.

@choptopskc

35. Light Brown Base with Pink

The pink in this hairstyle is so subtle that you almost don’t notice it. The colorist has chosen muted pink highlights that blend with the medium brown base flawlessly. If you want to try a colorful look that doesn’t stand out, this is the go-to.

@hairbynoora

36. Brown Hair with Pink Tinted Ends

Subtle pinks are perfect for romantic looks. You can blend them with medium brown or blonde hair. And even if you are a brunette, you can embrace subdued pinks by blending them through chestnut browns.

@frances_hairartist

37. Beautiful Purple Fade

Purple and pastel pink hair color swirl and melt together fantastically in this sweet hairstyle. Even if the hair is cut medium length, you still have enough length for an awesome ombre effect, that showcases from dark roots to light ends.

@glamourghoulhair

38. Brown Hair with Warm Pink Highlights

Black hair with pink highlights is a cool idea, but if you don’t want that much of a contrast, brown and pink hair will be just as stunning. You can mix ombre and balayage in one look for a unique touch on the pink hair trend.

@pinupjordan

39. Fun Color Dip

Take a look at this captivating style. The blonde and toned strands look like they took a dip in a pool of pink milkshake! The pastel pink hair has a pretty softness to it. This look is really on trend and works perfectly for younger people looking for something fun and different.

@modernsalon

40. Candy Colored Locks

Try out this yummy color that looks good enough to be eaten! The cotton candy pink strands glow in the sunlight, appearing super fun. This pastel pink shade is done amazingly: there are strands that look almost white in the front bang area, and there are light brown underparts.

@stephhstyles

Now that we’ve proven the point that pink hair color is awesome, we know that you don’t have to stick to just to blonde, black, red, or brown anymore. Shades of pink are ultra-feminine, and rosy hues do really complement most skin tones. So what are you waiting for? Give pink a try!
