Agnes Browne is a heartwarming comedy-drama movie released in 1999, directed by Anjelica Huston, who also stars as the titular character. Agnes is a strong-willed and courageous woman who works hard to make ends meet for her family. Despite her struggles, she never loses her sense of humor and spirit, which endears her to everyone she meets. Agnes is fiercely protective of her children, and when she learns that she may lose her house because of her husband's debts, she becomes determined to find a way to keep her family together.
