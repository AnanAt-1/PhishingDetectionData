John Mayberry Jr. sent to Lehigh Valley IronPigs as Phils activate Shane Victorino

  • Updated: Jun. 03, 2011, 5:25 p.m.
  • |Published: Jun. 03, 2011, 4:25 p.m.
By
  • Express-Times sports staff
View full sizeExpress-Times File PhotoJohn Mayberry Jr.
John Mayberry Jr.

is the odd man out.

The Philadelphia Phillies have known for some time that

a roster move was needed when Shane Victorino returned from the disabled list.

Victorino was activated for tonight's game in Pittsburgh.

To make room for Victorino, the Phils optioned Mayberry, the righthanded-hitting outfielder, to the Lehigh Valley IronPigs.

Mayberry is no stranger to the Pigs, although this is his first stint with the Triple A affiliate this season. He spent portions of the last two seasons in Allentown.

After a strong start with the Phils, particularly as a pinch hitter,

Mayberry has cooled off in the last month.

He batted .231 with three homers in 45 games with the Phillies.
