Welcome to Mont Fleur

Conference Venue

Exclusively yours. A hidden retreat in the heart of the Stellenbosch winelands.

Looking forward to
welcoming you to Mont Fleur

We are a small, intimate venue exclusive to a single group at a time, a “home away from home” where everyone feels special.

Mont Fleur, a family run conference venue, has been spoiling groups since 1989. We are situated in the Blaauwklippen Valley on the outskirts of Stellenbosch, nestled in the Stellenbosch & Helderberg Mountain ranges amidst fynbos and vineyards. Mont Fleur is blessed with amazing natural beauty and offers you a welcome escape from the hustle, bustle and stress of city life. Despite being a secluded mountain retreat, Mont Fleur is only 30 minutes from Cape Town International Airport and 45 minutes from Cape Town.

Conference

Mont Fleur specializes in conferencing. In particular, conferences with accommodation as opposed to day conferences. We offer a fully inclusive 24 hour conference package and we can host a maximum of 36 delegates.

The heart of Mont Fleur is the majestic conference room which has an abundance of natural light. The tall windows frame the views of the valley and French doors open out onto the grass deck. All conference equipment is provided including a data projector and wi-fi throughout the venue.

Food

Mont Fleur is known for great food which is prepared and presented with passion and style by our chefs. All our pastries and breads are homemade daily. We have our own vegetable and herb garden and use an abundance of fresh seasonal fruit and vegetables.

Fresh fruit platters and baskets as well as fruit juice are served at tea breaks. Filter coffee and a selection of teas are served and available at all times outside the conference room for impromptu breaks at no additional cost.

Bedrooms

Our bedrooms are warm, comfortable and homely. They all have double beds and en-suite bathrooms. They lead onto the grass deck with stunning views of the mountains. All rooms have work areas, tea and coffee facilities, heaters, hot blankets and percale linen. Our rooms are serviced daily. We are able to set up rooms with twin beds if required. Mont Fleur can accommodate 22 singles and 30 sharing with twin beds.

Teambuilding

Our neighbours are few and far between which make Mont Fleur the ideal venue for your team building. We have a selection of fun activities which we are able to arrange ourselves or thorough an outside company.

Some of the firm favorites include a hike with Michael, sundowners in the fynbos, winetasting on a neighbouring farm, stir-fry or potjie competitions.
