Stylish and Stemless
Riedel Sommeliers Stemless Tasting Glass

Riedel has long been known for creating glasses that bring out the best in wine, and the Sommelier collection is no exception. Each Sommelier glass is handmade with over 24% lead crystal (which explains the $39 pricetag), and the extra-large bowl allows wine to breathe as it should. This Riedel glass features curved thumb and finger indentations, allowing for easy wine swirling while simultaneously preventing the wine from being warmed by your hand. The stemless glass is both attractive and functional, and while Riedel isn’t exactly reinventing the wheel, it’s a quality product sure to please anyone who takes their wine tasting seriously.

For more information on this glass and Riedel's other stemware, visit www.riedel.com

Price: $39 per glass
