SASS

a Speak & Spell Emulator on the Windows platform

<< Go to Speak & Spell emulators list

Actually, this is more of a simulator than an emulator, but it's close enough. SASS, which stands for "Speak and Spell Simulator", is a Visual Basic-coded simulator that simulates Texas Instrument's "Speak & Spell' toy. This electronic toy spoke to you in a computerized voice, and helped young children learn how to speak and spell. At least, that was the idea. I had one, and it was loads of fun. This simulator lets you relive the memories. Remember to copy the ttf font included to your fonts directory.
