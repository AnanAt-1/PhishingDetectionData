Track Listing for the Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen Score

Posted on June 11, 2009 at 8:26 am by Tim Formas under Transformers Movie Toys & Products

[preview]The product page for the score to Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen on Warner Music Group – Central Europe has revealed the track listing for the CD. To view the track listing for the score by Steve Jablonsky, clicking on the title above or thumbnail to the left.[/preview]

The product page for the score to Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen on Warner Music Group – Central Europe has revealed the track listing for the CD. The listing is as follows:

1. Prime
2. Einstein’s Wrong
3. NEST (Contains instrumental excerpt from “New Divide” by Linkin Park)
4. The Shard
5. The Fallen
6. Infinite White
7. Heed Our Warning
8. The Fallen’s Arrival
9. Tomb Of The Primes
10. Forest Battle
11. Precious Cargo
12. Matrix Of Leadership
13. I Claim Your Sun
14. I Rise, You Fall
