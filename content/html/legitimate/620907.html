    First Look at a New Adventure - The Sandstone Caves

    We bring you an exclusive sneak peak at what is coming next to Pocket Legends:

    The Sandstone Caves

    Once a haven to miners and prospectors, the Sandstone Caves are littered with precious stones – and the broken bones of the foolish who have tried to take them!

    The Sandstone Caves are designed for characters level 20 to 25. It fills in content for this level range, providing more unique fun and adventure for those brave enough to battle the dangers the sandy rocks provide.

    • Delve into the new Towne and numerous adventure areas.
    • Complete a series of quests as you explore.
    • Face new and unique monsters and Bosses.
    • Outfit your would-be miner with all new weapons, armor and items.

    The Sandstone Caves will be available the first week of May in the Pocket Legends Store for 10 Platinum.

    Check in with us over the coming days and follow us on Facebook and Twitter - we will have more details about the Sandstone Caves to share!
