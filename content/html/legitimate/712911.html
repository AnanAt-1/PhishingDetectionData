UMN ME produces the leading engineers of tomorrow and research that solves the world's most pressing problems.

Engineering the Future: Innovating for a Better Tomorrow

The University of Minnesota Department of Mechanical Engineering takes an interdisciplinary approach to solving complex challenges in energy, sustainability, and human health. With a dedicated team of scientists, researchers, and engineers, the department advances theoretical and applied research in design, thermal sciences, robotics, and manufacturing. Through innovation and collaboration, UMN engineers are shaping a future where technology drives real-world solutions to society’s biggest challenges.
