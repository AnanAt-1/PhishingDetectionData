Bob Baun

Robert Neil "Bob, Boomer" Baun
Defence 1956-57 to 1966-67 and 1970-71 to 1972-73
739 Games 29 Goals 140 Assists 169 Points 1155 PM
Career: 964 Games 37 Goals 187 Assists 224 Points 1491 PM



Memorial Cup Winner 1955 and 1956
Stanley Cup 1962-1963-1964 and 1967


Baun is remembered for his performance in game six of the 1964 Stanley Cup finals against the Detroit Red Wings. On April 23, 1964, having broken his ankle earlier in the game, he returned in Overtime and scored the game winning goal. The Leafs won the next game 4–0 to win the best of seven series, and secure their third consecutive league championship.






Baun was one of the hardest hitters during his time in the NHL
