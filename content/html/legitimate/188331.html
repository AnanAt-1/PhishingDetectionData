Who We Are

Tile Factory Outlet is one of Australia’s largest and most affordable tile outlet stores, providing residential and commercial customers with a comprehensive range of high-quality tiles for walls, floors décor and more.

What sets us apart from other tile providers? We import our stock directly from big brand tile manufacturers, which results in significant savings that we then pass on to you. We guarantee you won’t find prices like this anywhere else.

Additionally, our warehouse is one of the largest in Australia, with more than 750,000 m2 of tiles ready for fast shipping or pickup. That means that not only do you have more tile options to choose from, you’ll also receive your order faster than almost anywhere else.

With over 100 years of combined experience in the tiling industry, our expert team is well equipped to help you with any and all of your tile needs. From kitchens and bathrooms to outdoor living areas and swimming pools, you’re guaranteed to find something fitting from our collection of premier tiles. Whether you need ceramic floor tiles for your hallways or decorative mosaics to add a splash of colour to your patio, our team will help you find the best tiles in Sydney at the best prices – that’s our promise to you.

Our team is committed to helping you find the best tiles in Sydney at prices you can afford, no matter where you’re located. While our warehouse and showroom are based in Smithfield, our products ship Australia-wide. We’re happy to serve customers in Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth and beyond.

Browse our range of online tiles or visit us in person to speak to one of our passionate team members about your project. We can’t wait to help you turn your dreams into a reality.

Our Range

At TFO, we’re proud to offer an ever-changing range of high-quality tiles. From porcelain to travertine, large panels to colourful mosaics, we’ve got it all – and more. Our team is constantly searching for new products to add to our line. So you’ll always find the latest tile styles and trends among our collection, each one sourced from professional tiling businesses around the world.

Shop online or in person today and explore our collection of:

• Porcelain. Our range of porcelain is perfect for both indoor and outdoor projects, thanks to its sturdy, durable and beautiful nature. We carry polished, semi-polished and matte tiles, large format panels, and a variety of colours and textures for you to choose from.

• Natural Stone. From travertine and marble to limestone and more, our selection of natural stone tiles and pavers come in a variety of shapes and styles that are all uniquely beautiful.

• Feature Tiles. Add visual interest to your space with our eye-catching feature tiles, available in options such as stack stone, 3D ceramic tiles, porcelain and more.

• Mosaics. Our mosaic line offers an elegant and sophisticated finish that’s easy to maintain. You’ll find mosaics of various materials and finishes, including the ever-popular Carrara marble.

In addition to tiles, we also stock a selection of other renovation, remodel and tile DIY necessities such as:

• Adhesives, sealers and cleaners

• Vanities, toiletries and bathroomware

• Taps, rails and other hardware

No matter your project, budget or taste, you’ll find exactly what you need at Tile Factory Outlet.
