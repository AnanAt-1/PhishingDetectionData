Annabelle Chvostek Is in Session

Bob Harris Sunday

Canadian singer-songwriter Annabelle Chvostek plays live and discusses her latest album, Live from Folk Alley. Steve Cropper and friends from The Animals are also in session.

One of Bob's heroes is in session tonight. Steve Cropper plays three signature songs from his tour with friends from The Animals and discusses his amazing career.

Bob's other live music guest Annabelle Chvostek was born and raised in a musical family in Toronto; she made her professional debut with the Canadian Opera Company at the age of 7, and appeared in La Bohème at age 8. She has been performing ever since.

Annabelle plays live for Bob after 1am and will discuss her latest album 'Live from Folk Alley' as well as her work with Bruce Cockburn, Ani DiFranco, The Wailin' Jennys and Mary Gauthier.
