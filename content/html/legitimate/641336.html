About Stoneriver

At Stoneriver Apartments, quality, convenience, and comfort are the focal points of each detail, inside and out. Conveniently located in the Westwood area of Houston, this community enjoys easy access to I-59, Sam Houston Tollway, and Loop 610. That places the city's greatest entertainment, dining, shopping, museums, business, sports, and entertainment venues all within easy reach. The nearby Brays Bayou Park and famous PlazAmericas Shopping Centre are great destinations to relax and indulge after busy work days.
With eight spacious floor plans ranging from 675 square feet to 1,250 square feet, every family can find a perfect home in a one, two, or three bedroom apartment. This gated community requires a passcode at the front gate for every entry, giving residents added security and peace of mind. Notable interior amenities include wall-to-wall carpeting, frost-free refrigerators, spacious pantries and walk-in closets, dishwashers, and private patio balconies. Take a swim in the gorgeous resort-style pool, wander the paths through the professionally landscaped grounds, or sit beneath the cool shade of the surrounding trees. The maintenance and management teams are quick to respond to any requests, large or small. Stoneriver Apartments are an affordable and convenient community to call home.
