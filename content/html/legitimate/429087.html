Star Trek Soundtrack

The track listing of the soundtrack of the upcoming Star Trek movie directed by J.J. Abrams has been unveiled:

Star Trek Soundtrack - Tracklist


1. Star Trek
2. Nailin' the Kelvin
3. Labor of Love
4. Hella Bar Talk
5. Enterprising Young Men
6. Nero Sighted
7. Nice to Meld You
8. Run and Shoot Offense
9. Does It Still McFly?
10. Nero Death Experience
11. Nero Fiddles, Narada Burns
12. Back from Black
13. That New Car Smell
14. To Boldly Go
15. End Credits

The music of the movie Star trek was composed by Michael Giacchino:

"I grew up listening to all of that great Trek music, and that's part of what inspired me to do what I'm doing [...] You just go in scared. You just hope you do your best. It's one of those things where the film will tell me what to do."
Music Composer Michael Giacchino

Michael Giacchino (who composed music for Alias, Lost, Mission: Impossible III, and Speed Racer) kept the original theme by Alexander Courage so fans won't be lost.
