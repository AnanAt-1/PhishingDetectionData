Our Impact

The Frank Lloyd Wright Building Conservancy is the only organization devoted to the preservation of all of Wrights remaining buildings.

26 Legal Protections Through preservation easements, we safeguard 26 of Wright's designs from demolition, ensuring the legacy endures.

8 Sites on the World Heritage List The Conservancy led the charge to inscribe eight Wright-designed structures on the UNESCO World Heritage list.

36 Years Helping Future Stewards Find Their Homes Our Wright on the Market listings connect preservation-minded buyers to spaces designed to provide transformative intellectual, emotional, and physical benefits.

What We Do
Wright on the World Heritage List

The 20th-Century Architecture of Frank Lloyd Wright

Including eight major works and spanning 50 years of Wrights career, this series represents the first modern architecture in the U.S. to be added to this prestigious list. The Conservancy coordinated the inscription process.
