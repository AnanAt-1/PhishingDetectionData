The 1957 Kansas City Athletics played 153 games during the regular season, won 59 games, lost 94 games, and finished in seventh position. They played their home games at Municipal Stadium (Park Factors: 102/104) where 901,067 fans witnessed their 1957 Athletics finish the season with a .386 winning percentage.
