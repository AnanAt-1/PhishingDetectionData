  • Sky Event Almanacs

    
The Sky Event Almanacs consist of a series of web pages,  
each of which gives a concise list of astronomical events (with date and time) for a single year. 
Astonomical events listed include: Moon phases, Equinoxes and Solstices, eclipses, planetary oppositions and conjunctions, 
conjuctions of the Moon with planets and bright stars, meteor showers, and more.

    

    
The Sky Event Almanacs cover every year of the 21st Century, and for twenty-four time zones around the world. 
The index table above directs you to the desired decade and region of the world. 
From there, you may choose a year and a time zone to display an Sky Event Almanac.
