About us

Black & Veatch is an employee-owned, global leader in building critical human infrastructure in Energy, Water, Digital Connectivity and Government Services. Since 1915, we have helped our clients improve business operations and the lives of people in over 100 countries through consulting, engineering, construction, operations and program management. Our purpose is building a world of difference and our vision is THE leader in sustainable infrastructure.     In remote West Texas, our team tackled the #AgingInfrastructure and surging power demands head-on. But not without challenges. As the #EPC contractor for the Cassava Substation project, we addressed environmental concerns, from using weed wash stations to prevent the spread of noxious weeds, to following best management practices for low-water stream crossings.
