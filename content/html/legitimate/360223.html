Dolenji Suhadol (pronounced [dɔˈleːnji suxaˈdoːu̯] or [dɔˈleːnji suˈxaːdɔu̯]) is a settlement in the hills east of Novo Mesto in southeastern Slovenia. The entire City Municipality of Novo Mesto lies in the traditional region of Lower Carniola and is now included in the Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region.[2]
