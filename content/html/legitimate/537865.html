So long from Histopolis...

Histopolis was established as a free, collaborative source for genealogy and history information about people and places in the United States and Puerto Rico.
After 16 years we have decided to retire the site effective February 11, 2025. To those who have referenced and contributed to Histopolis over the years, thank you!
