Welcome to Pete Alewine Pool & Spa

Best Pool Builder in Evans & Aiken

Welcome to Pete Alewine Pool & Spa, your premier swimming pool builder serving the Central Savannah River Area, Aiken, Columbia, Lexington and the surrounding areas since 1998. We provide design, construction and renovation services for swimming pools, hot tubs and outdoor living spaces, and we look forward to creating and caring for the backyard you’ve always dreamed about.

Your Swimming Pool Design & Build Experts

We specialize in new pool design and construction, including custom gunite pools, vinyl liner pools and fiberglass pools. No matter your style or budget, we have a pool for everyone in the CSRA area and beyond. If your existing swimming pool is in need of some attention, we also offer pool renovations. Let the professionals at Pete Alewine create a beautiful outdoor design that includes an award-winning swimming pool and hot tub design, and gorgeous concrete decking. Whatever it is you’re looking to incorporate into your outdoor living space, we can make it happen.

Click here to look through our Sales Brochure!

Professional Pool Care & Supplies

But, we don’t stop there. Once your pool is complete, let us handle the rest. Your time is valuable, and we understand that. That’s why we offer a variety of pool and hot tub services and regular maintenance schedules. From equipment upgrades/repairs and vinyl liner replacement to regular maintenance and safety cover installation, our fully staffed service department has the knowledge and expertise to keep your pool operating efficiently and effectively every day.

We invite you to stop by our retail store at 4470 Washington Road in Evans, Ga., to find everything you need to keep your swimming pool pristine. We have pool and hot tub chemicals, parts, energy-efficient equipment and upgrades, along with the most knowledgeable staff in the area, which are all available to help make your swimming pool ownership as easy and stress free as possible.

Don’t live nearby or running short on time? Order your pool chemicals through our online store and we’ll deliver it directly to your home and perform a water chemistry test for you!

Contact Us

Be sure to stop by in person, or give us a call to speak with a Pete Alewine Pool & Spa professional to set up a free consultation to learn more about all that we offer. We look forward to having you become a part of the Pete Alewine family today!
