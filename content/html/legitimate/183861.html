International Work-Study Degree

Learning & Career Advisory Session

16th Nov 2024, Saturday (11AM – 12:30PM)

Problem with traditional degree pathway

Traditional degree pathways typically take around 5 to 7 years to complete. When factoring in National Service (NS) liability for males, this could extend to a 9-year journey before graduates can step into the workforce. 

Is this the only option, or can N-Level and O-Level students complete an internationally recognised bachelor’s degree and begin their careers much earlier? 

A new route to stay ahead of your peers

Join us for an insightful session for N-Level and O-Level students to discover a new international work-study degree pathway that can be completed in just 2.5 to 3.5 years, enabling students to gain a superior career head start and potentially earn up to $240K in total income before their peers even enter the workforce.

We will also address the following key areas of concern:

Is IT or digital business-related specialisation too saturated and competitive in today’s digital economy?
Are superior O-Level results really necessary to enter these specialisations?
Do we need to invest 5 to 7 years after O-Levels to secure a recognised bachelor’s degree?
Are traditional degree pathways sufficient to remain competitive in a skills-focused workplace, where employers value what you can do rather than where you graduated from?

Learning & Career Advisory Session Agenda

We will cover the following topics and more to ensure our attendees fully understand the options beyond secondary school and can make the most practical, well-suited decision moving forward.

Academic Aptitudes & Career Interest

Assess various possible learning and work journeys for students of different academic aptitudes and career interests.

Current Education Pathways

Analyse the different degree pathways, factoring in elements such as examination results, time needed, and potential costs incurred.

International Work-Study Degree Pathway

Showcase our international applied degree pathway, which can be completed in 2.5 to 3.5 years, where students acquire work-ready skills and enjoy job placement assistance, giving them a superior head start over their peers.

In-demand High Growth Digital Careers

Deep dive into various IT and digital career options, highlighting market demand, pay, and career progression.

About the University

You will be awarded either the Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Computer Science or the Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Business Management and Marketing from the University of Roehampton through our international applied degree pathway. Based in London, this university has a proud history in higher education, stretching back 180 years. While still rooted in its pioneering values and teaching heritage from its 19th-century collegiate origins, the university has developed a diverse curriculum that reflects London’s multicultural and creative spirit. It offers a high-quality UK education experience to more than 10,000 students from 141 countries.

If you are a parent of an N-Level or O-Level student, or a student yourself, join us for our Learning & Career Advisory Session to find out more.
