Kirkwood Children’s House is a Montessori school dedicated to the natural education of children ages 18 months to six-years-old since 1985. By nurturing and respecting children’s authentic curiosity to explore the world, we help them reach their full potential and develop a life-long love of learning.

“Natural education in a community of joy and peace.”
