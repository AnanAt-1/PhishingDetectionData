Pyrotek Innovation & Patents

Pyrotek is proud that many of our new solutions are awarded patents. Below you can find a list of our patents. Pyrotek is committed to the protection of its Intellectual Property and vigorously enforces its patents. If you have any questions, please contact our Legal Department.
