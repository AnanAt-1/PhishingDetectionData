FootieManager - The Browser Based Football Manager Game

Welcome to FootieManager, the online football/soccer manager game where you take control of a football club and compete against other managers from all over the world in your bid for the domestic championship and international cup glory.

As a football manager, you have full control over your club including managing the club finances, wheeling and dealing in the transfer markets, managing club staff and a myriad of other options that will challenge even the best budding football managers.

FootieManager is one of the first and original browser based football/soccer manager simulation games. There is a very friendly community of players who will gladly help new players to find their feet. What are you waiting for?
