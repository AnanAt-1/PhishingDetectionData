GURPS In Nomine Conversions

Official Conversions

  • GURPS In Nomine – The official GURPS version of In Nomine. This book contains rules for converting between the systems (both precisely and more thematically), setting details, information on all the major Superiors, Choirs, and Bands, and additional information on the Grigori.

Unofficial Conversions

  • A conversion, by S. John Ross and Sean "Dr. Kromm" Punch, that predates the official version.
