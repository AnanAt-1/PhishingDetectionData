Our main publications comprehensively cover five main areas of the global fibre, textile and apparel industries:
  • Textile Outlook International is our flagship title and provides independent analysis and insight into the global fibre, textile and apparel industries;
  • Performance Apparel Markets is our fastest growing publication. It gives readers all they need to know when planning business strategies in the niche area of high performance fibres, yarns, fabrics and apparel;
  • Technical Textile Markets is a well-established and highly regarded source of independent analysis into key technological developments and business trends related to technical textiles, including fibres, yarns and nonwovens for high value markets like automotive, aerospace, construction, medical and protective apparel and many more;
  • Global Apparel Update is our monthly executive e-briefing, supplied in electronic format and containing up-to-the-minute independently verified product developments and business news in the international apparel sector;
  • Home Textiles Update is our monthly executive e-briefing, delivered to you by email and containing up-to-the-minute independently verified bulletins about the latest technological and strategic developments affecting global home textiles supply chains.

About Textiles Intelligence

Textiles Intelligence is a leading provider of research-based business information about the global fibre, textile and apparel industries. We produce printed and digital publications, via an annual subscription or customised packages of individual reports.
The company was formed in 1992 as a spin-off from the Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU). Through its range of publications, Textiles Intelligence provides a vital source of information to subscribers in more than 60 countries across five continents.
Textiles Intelligence has an extensive team of expert contributors based in Asia, North and South America, Europe and Africa with experience in the fields of fibres, textiles and apparel. The team is headed by Robin Anson, a recognised leading worldwide authority on strategic issues related to trade in textiles and clothing.
We supply customers with up-to-date and reliable data relating to all aspects of the global fibre, textile and apparel industries, including but not limited to product design, market trends, manufacturing and trade data, international trade policy analysis and insight, product developments and innovations, and business strategy.
