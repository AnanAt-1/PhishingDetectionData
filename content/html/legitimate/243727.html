The latest model of Tragi series is Tragi702. The airfoil profile of Tragi series has been changed by each model from MH32 to RG15A. TUN Modellbau classified Tragi701 as F3J, and Tragi702 as F3B. However the plan of Tragi701 specifies F3B-F3J and Tragi702, F3B-F3F. Both model has two version, that is, Glass and Carbon. Glass version will be used for F3J. Carbon version will be used for F3B. Tragi702 has a progressive wing from RG15A(1.76/8.9) root to RG15A(1.5/7.0) made by Tragi Ltd 1999.
