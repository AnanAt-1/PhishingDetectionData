Union Baptist Church Cemetery
Laurens County, Georgia

2124 GA Hwy 29 S
East Dublin GA 31027

Lat: 32°29'38"N, Lon: 82°46'06"W

Contributed by Leon Moore, Mar 25, 2002 [lmoore@nlamerica.com]. Total records = 386.

To get to the cementery, take GA 29 South from East Dublin, GA and travel 5 3/4 miles to Union Baptist Church. The church will be on the left. It is located in the NE corner of the intersection of Pughes Creek and GA Hwy 29.

The cemetery is active and is being expanded to approximately twice its current size. It is owned by Union Baptist Church.

We have transcribed the graves as accurately as we could. There are blank spaces in the rows which may quite possibly contain unmarked graves. No attempt was made to count the blank spaces as graves, therefore the grave numbers are counted sequentially from the southern-most end of the Cemetery, next to Pughes Creek, counting only the graves with markers or slabs. There are 411 graves.

The row numbers begin at the western-most end of the Cemetery. The first row is the row nearest to the Union Baptist Church. When you enter the Cementery next to Pughes Creek the first grave is Row 1, Grave 1.

In the cases where graves were in between rows, we used a .5 row indicator to indicate that the row is a partial row in between full rows, ie 5.5. The rows were determined by using the first grave in the row and doing a line of sight survey to determine the straightest row possible.

Transcription was done 6/22/2001- 6/27/2001 by Evelyn and Leon Moore. Verification was done 12/18/2001 by Dwayne Clements and Colby Clements.

The oldest grave that can be determined 10/19/1904, but there may be some which are unmarked that are older. Until now, the Church had no record of who is buried in the Cemetery.

- Leon Moore
