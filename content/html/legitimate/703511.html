Most of These 49 Christmas Gifts for Mom Arrive Before the Big Day

Go ahead and consider yourself the favorite child.

By Hannah JonesUpdated: Dec 17, 2024

Country Living editors select each product featured. If you buy from a link, we may earn a commission. Why Trust Us?

Sure, she's one of the most important women in your life, but that doesn't make her easy to gift for. If it seems like every time you ask your mom what she wants for Christmas she replies, "oh, nothing!" then you're not alone. Moms might just be some of the hardest folks to gift for, and that's saying something. Do you get something practical that you know will make her life easier? Or maybe a sentimental gift she'll cherish for years to come? Maybe just settle for a gift card that you know 100% she can't return?

YOUR HOLIDAY SHOPPING HEADQUARTERS

In my years as a shopping editor, I've learned a thing or two about giving great gifts for the ladies in my life, but nothing prepared me to know exactly what every mom wants better than becoming a mom myself. Of course, the ultimate gift is time spent with you (and more sleep, please, we are struggling), but there are tons of presents that I and so many of my fellow mothers would be utterly jazzed to receive, like a cooling pillow that's basically magic, a charm necklace that has a personalized touch, or just an excuse to hang out with our girlfriends. In case you're on a budget, tons of these inexpensive gifts come in at under $50, with plenty of presents available on Amazon. And since we're all shopping on a deadline at this point, we've marked which gifts will still arrive by Christmas (pssst: most of them will!).
