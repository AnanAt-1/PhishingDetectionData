Bus sightseeing tours around Trieste

Trieste Bus offers all kinds of high-quality bus services in the region of Trieste. We can organize a coach trip starting from Trieste to any close or far-away destination, but you also should not hesitate to ask us to plan your shorter bus transfers to any tourist attraction in Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy. Trieste Bus can also plan your sightseeing bus tours to neighbouring Slovenia and Istria: if you are considering some tourist exploration during your stay in Trieste, our Trieste Bus team will provide you with comfortable buses, coaches or minibuses as well as professional bus drivers that will drive you to any famous place you want to see. We can suggest various sightseeing bus tours including some of the most beautiful and interesting places in the surroundings, and arrange this sightseeing programme according to your own wishes and preferences.

Bus excursions in Friuli Venezia Giulia

You can ask us to organize your bus excursions in the very beautiful Northern Italian region of Friuli Venezia Giulia, divided into 4 provinces and 219 communes. Just relax and enjoy pure nature in Friuli Venezia Giulia whose territory includes 61% mountains and hills and whose plains are crossed by rivers (the Tagliamento and the Soča). You cannot get bored in Friuli Venezia Giulia: there is a lot to see and to visit in all four provinces of Trieste, Udine, Pordenone and Gorizia. For further information concerning the main tourist attractions in Friuli Venezia Giulia, please visit our special pages on sightseeing around Trieste, main sights in the Julian March and tourism in Friuli.

Sightseeing bus tours in Slovenia

If you decide on a coach trip to Slovenia, you will surely enjoy this green and wooded country crossed by the Julian Alps and part of the Karst region. We can organize for you a sightseeing bus or minibus tour in the unique capital city Ljubljana or in any other Slovenian city, as well as a coach excursion in the Slovenian countryside. There are a lot of different ways to enjoy fresh country air in Slovenia, including big numbers of spa centers and of natural caves where you might even be able to see an olm - a rare amphibian species known as the symbol of Slovenia. For further information as to what places are worth including in your sightseeing coach tour in Slovenia, please visit our sightseeing in Slovenia page.

Sightseeing bus trips to Istria

Trieste Bus can provide you with buses, coaches or minibuses and a professional bus driver that will carry you to the beautiful and typical Istrian peninsula in the Adriatic Sea. Istria is worth seeing for its breathtaking azure Mediterranean landscapes - especially in Umag, Novigrad and Pula - as well as its green inland natural landscapes and rich and varied culture and traditions. During your coach sightseeing tour in Istria, you could for example admire traces of the past almost immediately surrounded by untouched nature - like the Unesco World Heritage Euphrasian basilica in Poreč. For further information and advice concerning sightseeing in Istria, please read our page on sightseeing in Istria.
