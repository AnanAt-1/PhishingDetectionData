Three Ways This Year To Get Those Summer Blues…

..in the ‘Hood

Pick up summer-fresh blueberries near your home with Joe’s summer CSA. Two local pickups at eight different Chicago suburban locations with our zero-spray, sweet Michigan berries, hand-picked and right from the field. Blueberries never tasted better!

Order Summer CSA Here

…at the Farmers’ Markets

Get out of the house and have some fun at seven summer farmers markets throughout the Chicago area. In addition to our fresh, BLUE-licious summer berries, Joe and the gang will tempt you with any amazing array of other best-in-class goodies ranging from pure cold-pressed juice to savory salsas and sauces, tasty dried berries, soaps, cocktail shrubs and more.

…at the Farm

Plan your escape now for a fun day in the country. You can jump in the car this summer and head up to our Bangor farm, just a couple of hours from the Big City and only minutes from the popular beach town of South Haven, Michigan. Pick buckets of berries right off our bushes, then head to the beaches, shops and hangouts. On the way back home, take a break at one of the dozens of wineries. What a great day!
