    CHRISTOPHER DELEON AND RUDY FERNANDEZ


    Christopher de Leon said that after Rudy Fernandez' death, the movie stars should be conscious of their immortality and health.

    He mentioned about his family's friendship with the Fernandezes since he is one of the godfathers of Mark Fernandez, Rudy's and Alma's son and Rudy was the godfather of his son Miguel.

    They were together in family affairs, such as outings, cruises and parties. Sandy Andolong his wife is a friend of Lorna Tolentino.

    The actor reminded the co-stars that anytime, life may be cut shortened by sickness, accident or even murder like that of Nida Blanca.
