The Shurman Report is a live daily video/podcast of news with opinion from a broadcaster who knows how it’s done! Peter Shurman has been in the business of talk radio and broadcast management for well over 50 years. Trends come and go as do the media people use. Today, the internet brings you video and audio with no restrictions. Peter never minces words and tells you exactly what’s on his mind. In fact, using the internet over broadcast simply widens one’s ability to self-express! The world contributes the raw material…Peter Shurman gives it his spin. Monday to Friday 5pm for The Shurman Report live videocast on Facebook, X, YouTube and LinkedIn. Daily video posts to YouTube. If video isn’t easy for you (say, for example, you’re driving!), the audio portion can be found on Apple and Spotify or any other podcast site you choose.
