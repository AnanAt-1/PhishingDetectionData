          Tackling Climate Change

          • Tackling climate change

            Tackling climate change

            We aim for climate neutrality by 2040 through reducing emissions and investing in sustainable technologies. Our solutions include renewable materials, carbon capture, and efficient water treatment, supporting global efforts to limit warming to 1.5°C and helping you to reduce your carbon footprint.
            Tackling climate change
          • Get a deep dive into our strategy

            We become Climate Neutral by 2040

            We have developed a clear strategy and have already launched major project to recieve our aim. We will reduce emissions drastically, purchasing only very low-emission or climate-neutral energy, lower our emissions form 3.2 million tons to less than 220,000 tons by 2040.
            Get a deep dive into our strategy
          • Get insights of our sustainability ratings

            Yes, we can!

            Did you know that we are in the top position in several sustainability ratings? We are particularly proud of these awards. Sustainability is close to our hearts. And that is not always easy in an energy-intensive industry - nevertheless, we have already reduced our emissions by over 70% compared to 2005. Let´s move on!
            Get insights of our sustainability ratings
          • Buy Scopeblue products

            Scopeblue - our sustainable product range

            Scopeblue products are circular - recycled or biobased - raw materials or a particularly low carbon footprint compared to conventional products. The products contain at least 50% sustainable raw materials and 10% reduced PCF. The carbon footprint of these products is at least 50% lower than that of conventional products.
