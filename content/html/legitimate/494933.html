A Legacy Committed To Quality Legal Counsel With Proven Results

At Connors LLP, we are a litigation team that embodies the best qualities of the Buffalo-Niagara region itself: tenacious, adaptable, ready for hard work and committed to its causes. Practicing those homegrown values has made our firm one of nationwide influence. Fulfilling the vision of the remaining founding partner, Terrence Connors, who left his position as managing partner of a larger firm in 1986 to co-found his own, our firm draws on the country’s top legal talent, practices in venues across the United States and services both local and national clients.

Adaptable To Whatever Comes Our Client’s Way

We are not your ordinary litigation firm. Why? Because our work is far from ordinary. Recognizing that high-stakes litigation is never routine, Connors LLP was built to adapt to whatever challenge confronts our client – no matter the adversary, complexity of the case or legal issue involved. In every matter, our primary objective is developing legal strategies that win.
This approach has proven successful beyond our greatest ambitions. It has earned our firm a reputation for providing talented trial and appellate lawyers prepared to battle for clients in both civil and criminal matters, regardless of the state, federal or administrative forum. Our flexibility allows us to handle matters, including the litigation of complicated business disputes, the prosecution of claims for victims of catastrophic injuries, the representation of executives and corporations targeted by criminal investigations and many others. Perhaps the most telling indicator of the value we bring to our engagements is the fact that fellow attorneys and judges often come to us for help when they themselves need representation.

Talk To Us About Your Case

Our clients understand that whatever their problems are, attorneys at Connors LLP will respond with approaches tailored to their individual needs. We welcome you, too, to trust your toughest legal challenges to the team at Connors LLP. Call our firm at 716-852-5533 or reach us online.

We stand up for your rights and create customized strategies to help win your case.
