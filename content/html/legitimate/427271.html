WELCOME

Together, it is our primary delight and joy to glorify God by making much of our Lord Jesus Christ. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, through public and private worship, through teaching, compassion, fellowship ministries as well as through varied global and local outreach ministries we aim to build up the faith of believers who will, above all things, delight in beholding the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ.

When you walk through the doors of Sovereign Christ Bible Fellowship, we want you to experience the truth and the love of God in the person of Jesus Christ. We believe that as you gather with your friends at Sovereign Christ Bible Fellowship you will be encouraged to know Jesus better and to live out your life for His glory and your enjoyment of Him.

After worship, plan on spending a few extra minutes getting acquainted with some of the people of Sovereign Christ Bible Fellowship.

We hope that you find your visit to Sovereign Christ Bible Fellowship to be refreshing, faith-building and grace-giving.
