Judges Debate Talmudic Law

Quebec Superior Court Justices debated Talmudic and Quebec law at an event with Rabbi Shlomo Yaffe at Chabad of Westmount. Full Story

November 15, 2011 – 18 Heshvan 5772

Quebec Superior Court Justices debated Talmudic and Quebec law at an event with Rabbi Shlomo Yaffe at Chabad of Westmount. Full Story

Chabad of Westmount recently hosted its fourth bi- annual QJ Law Symposium. Presenters included Justice William Fraiberg, Justice Capriolo, Justice Herbert Marx, Senator Yoine Goldstein, and crown prosecutor Rafi Sachter, together with Rabbi Yossi Shanowitz and Rabbi Shlomo Yaffe.

Comparative law between the Talmudic law and Quebec law was discussed and debated.

“The fact that the Torah deals with progressive issues such as bankruptcy law and defending the guilty is fascinating,” said Michael Bergman, a leading human rights lawyer.

Participants were greatly impressed by Rabbi Shlomo Yaffe’s vast knowledge and depth; both in Secular and Talmudic law.
