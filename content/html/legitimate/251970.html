About

History:

  • 1 September 2006: I now divide my time between INRIA Rocquencourt and the new Microsoft Research-INRIA Joint Centre.
  • 1 September 2002: Research scientist (Chargé de recherche), Moscova Project, INRIA Rocquencourt.
