Welcome to Marymount Primary School. A Catholic Learning Community

Marymount is a co-educational Catholic Primary School providing educational excellence for students from Prep to Year Six. Upon completion of Year Six, many of our students enter Marymount College which provides for their education from Years Seven to Twelve. The College is situated on the same Campus as the Primary School. Read more from the Principal
