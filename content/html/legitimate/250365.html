Overview

IBAyes is a probabilistic reasoning tool that allows its user to model uncertain situations and to perform inference using Bayesian networks and its variants such as Influence Nets.

What makes IBAyes different from other Bayesin network modeling tool?

- Complete support to model Influence Nets.

- Support to capture conditional probabilities using Noisy-Or and CAST Logic.

- Availability of sensitivity of actions and sensitivity of links analysis
