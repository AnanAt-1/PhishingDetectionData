Stream: Curren$y, “#jetsgo”

By Amber Bravo
May 12, 2011



Curren$y just released the first track off his forthcoming album, Weekend at Burnies, which aside from winning Best Album Cover and Title of the summer (wouldn't even mind an improbable sequel), also sounds pretty promising. Just like on the recently released mixtape Covert Coup, Curren$y teamed up with the Alchemist for almost every track on the album—except this Rahki-produced number.

Stream: Curren$y, "#jetsgo"
