HR Spotlight

Plan Year 2025 Benefits Open Enrollment

Open enrollment for the Public Employees Benefits Program (PEBP) and NSHE voluntary benefits is from May 1 - May 31. During open enrollment, benefits-eligible employees can make changes to their benefits offered through PEBP and NSHE. Benefit changes will be effective on July 1, 2024.

Rebel Future

UNLV is conducting a comprehensive compensation and classification study for administrative faculty positions. The study examines job descriptions, pathways for career progression, and how jobs are compensated compared to one another and similar positions in the external market.
