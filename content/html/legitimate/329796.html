The International Scientific Conference Agrarian Perspectives brings a number of organizational changes in 2025, as it builds on the successful link with conferences ERIE (Efficiency and Responsibility in Education) and Think Together, similar to last year.

The 34rd year of the AP will open a plenary session with two main topics based on the requirements of scientific research and educational activities as well as the needs of practice. The theme is also in line with the history and focus of our conferences and will offer participants a professional theme.

The one-day programme will also include a workshop thematically linked to precision agriculture.

We would like to invite you to a somewhat unconventionally organized conference that invites you to a professional discussion without the need to submit your own contributions. The conference is free of charge. Refreshment is provided in the form of a coffee break. The event is held in Czech language.

If you would like to combine your participation in the conference with the publication of a scientific paper (in English), you can use the faculty journal AGRIS ON-LINE PAPERS IN ECONOMICS AND INFORMATICS  online.agris.cz

If you are interested in the conference, please confirm your participation through email: agrarian_perspectives@pef.czu.cz by 12 August 2025.
