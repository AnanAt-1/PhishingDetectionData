About

Summary

Scentsy is a store of products for home, self care, fragrances and other nice products. Company was founded in 1979 by Orville Thompson. He wanted to become successful person and made everything possible to reach his goal. Today Scentsy is a direct selling company that is famous around the world. Company offers such products as warmers, plug-ins, scent circles, scent pak, fragrance foam, travel tin, room spray, Scentsy buddy, Scentsy bar, Scentsy brick. Also Scentsy has a wide assortment of fragrances including corner cafe, favorites, Scentsy man, spa, romance, spring and summer 2011, tropical. Scentsy is headquartered in Meridian, ID. Customer can contact consumer support via email or phone.
