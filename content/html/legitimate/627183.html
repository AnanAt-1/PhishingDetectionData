(3) CATHERINE PAINTER, the third child and eldest daughter of Christian and Mary E. Painter, was born July 18, 1783, near Timberville, Rockingham Co., Va. She was married, near the year 1807, to Philip Miller. She died Jan. 1, 1841, aged 57 years, 5 months and 27 days. She lies buried in the Painter Cemetery, near Middletown, Ind. An account of her early life can doubtless be found in the Sanders and Miller Family Record, soon to be published, they being her direct lineal descendants. To them were born two sons.
