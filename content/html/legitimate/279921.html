Mortgage Calculator

A program to calculate your mortgage repayments OR calculate the mortgage you can get for a given monthly repayment. The program is exceptionally easy to use, just enter the amount you want to borrow, the interest rate of the borrower and the number of years you wish to take to repay the loan. Then press the 'Calculate' button and you instantly get the amount of your monthly repayments. Features include Profiles, with profiles the user can get a list of lenders and their repayments for a specific mortgage thus making it very easy to find the cheapest mortgage. Also included is an estimated endowment policy cost. Finally you can get a print out detailing the full repayment schedule.
