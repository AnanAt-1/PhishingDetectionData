Kansas City Airport Guide

If youll be flying out of Kansas City Airport soon, weve collected together some important information along with advice and tips to help you have a great travel experience.
At the airport
Getting to MCI Airport

MCI Airport Check-In

MCI check in depends on when your flight is due to depart and the airline youre flying with. Your MCI Airport check in time can be confirmed by your airline in advance, so you know when you should be at the airport.

Kansas City Airport Security Screening

MCI security checkpoints are your next stop, and you should leave yourself plenty of time for this process. MCI security wait times vary based on how busy the airport is, and you dont want to feel rushed or stressed before your flight.

Boarding & Connecting Flights at MCI

Keep an eye on your nearest departure board to find out where your flight is boarding and when you need to be there.

You can get the latest MCI flight status information from these boards and online trackers to make sure you dont miss your flight or connection.

Kansas City Airport WiFi

All passengers can use Kansas City Airport WiFi while waiting for their flight, so youll always be connected to your friends and family.

Arriving at the airport

How early to arrive at MCI is dependent on the flight youre planning to take. International passengers should get to the airport three hours before departure, while domestic passengers should aim to be there two hours early. During peak periods, such as holiday season, you may want to plan in more time to account for longer queues and waits.

Getting to Kansas City Airport

When planning how to get to Kansas City Airport, think about timings, costs, group size, and other factors that will play a part.

You can get driving directions to KCI Airport by entering the destination into your satnav, but there are also public transport options like local and regional buses you can consider if youd rather not drive.
