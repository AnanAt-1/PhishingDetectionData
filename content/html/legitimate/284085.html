We regret to announce that Duracon will not be held in 2003, due to a number of circumstances beyond our control.

Duracon will return in the spring of 2004. Watch this space for updated information when it becomes available, or join the Duracon Information mailing list.

The Living Campaigns - General information - The RPGA sponsors a number of "living campaigns", in which players create their own characters to play in events at conventions down the street or across the country. As you play in more events, your character will accumulate a rich personal roleplaying history, as well as experience and treasure.

  • Living World of Greyhawk: The entire world of Greyhawk is the setting for the biggest shared-world campaign ever. Players play characters from different Greyhawk nations based on where they live. Check out the definitive 3rd edition D&D campaign, and be the hero in the world of Greyhawk. Visit the Living Greyhawk Campaign Home Page. Our region, North and South Carolina and Georgia or "Southeast 1", has been assigned the Greyhawk nation of Gran March and is administered by a "Triad" of regional gamers who will all be in attendance at Duracon. A wealth of information about the Gran March region can be found at The GranMarch Web Site. You can join the Gran March regional mailing list at The GranMarch Community.

  • Living City is a high fantasy D&D campaign set in and around the Forgotten Realms city of Ravens Bluff. You can find campaign background here, or the most recent character generation rules at the Living City News Center. The Living City campaign is administered by Organized Play, Inc. You'll want to register yourself with Organized Play before the convention, in order to save yourself some time in getting started later. Register at Organized Play's Living City Registration. Registration forms will also be available onsite at the convention.

  • Living Force is a living Star Wars campaign, set in the period following The Phantom Menace, in a star system called Cularin. You can find information about the Living Force premiere at the Star Wars Roleplaying Game Homepage.

  • Living Arcanis is a d20 system member-run campaign. Its World of Shattered Empires combines high fantasy adventure with political intrigue; brains and guile are as necessary as strength and power. Factions, secret societies and new slants on races take you to a whole new level. Learn all about it at the Living Arcanis Web Site The character generation guidelines and campaign world background are available for free download on their web site, at Living Arcanis Downloads. These free downloads contain enough information to get you started playing without the need to purchase any additional materials.

  • Living Death is a Gothic horror-style D&D campaign set in an alternate Earth in the 1890's. Living Death recently converted to D&D 3rd Edition, so you'll want to convert any existing 2nd Edition character you might have. Character generation guidelines and campaign information can be found at the Living Death Nexus.

  • Virtual Seattle is a living Shadowrun campaign, set in and around an alternate future Seattle. Character generation guidelines and campaign information can be found at the Virtual Seattle Index.
