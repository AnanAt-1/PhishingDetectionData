Amenities

Catered to you

Orchard Trails is an immersive student housing experience that offers an array of activities to enhance both your academic and personal life. Our fully furnished apartments in Orono provide an all-inclusive lifestyle specifically designed for students. At home, enjoy the convenience of newly updated stainless-steel appliances, in-unit washer and dryer, and included Wi-Fi. In your free time, take advantage of our 24-HR clubhouse, which offers a state-of-the-art fitness center, study rooms with free printing and coffee bar. Outside you can enjoy Orono’s nature trails, our basketball court, and the patio built in with gas grills and a fire-pit. See why Orchard Trails is the original student living.

Get to know

your neighborhood

Being just steps away from downtown Orono, you will have a variety of restaurants, breweries, and shops to discover. If you like the outdoors, you will be pleasantly surprised by the numerous hiking trails that overlook the Stillwater River. Just outside of Orono, the Bangor area provides additional shopping and entertainment that will not disappoint.
