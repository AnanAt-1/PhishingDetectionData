Forbidden Ecounters: UmbriaMusicFest 2011

About the Event

A romantic evening in a beautiful venue in the Umbrian countriside. Mauro Maur (trumpet) and Françoise de Clossey (piano) will present music by Morricone, Piccioni and Rota.

The UmbriaMusicFest is an international festival with a programme including classical music, jazz, opera, cinema, literature and the figurative arts. Thanks to interesting programmes, the festival boasts the participation of many great international artists and has become one of the most important and rich prestigious events on the international calendar.

Enjoy an excellent musical event at the UmbriaMusicFest this season.
