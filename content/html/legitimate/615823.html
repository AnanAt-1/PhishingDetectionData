Seven Stones Wines

Exquisite, well-aged wines from our cellar

Bold wines. Breathtaking views. A legacy rooted in the Similkameen Valley.

Nestled on a sun-drenched bench overlooking the dramatic beauty of British Columbia’s Similkameen Valley, Seven Stones Winery is a hidden gem among BC wineries—celebrated for its bold red wines, panoramic vineyard vistas, and the mysterious man-made caves that lie beneath.

Founded by visionary winemaker George Hanson, Seven Stones was built on a deep respect for the land and a passion for crafting wines of remarkable structure and character. Today, we carry forward his legacy—meticulously selecting premium fruit and applying minimal intervention in the cellar to allow each vintage to fully express itself.

Beneath the surface, our underground wine caves offer more than mystery—they provide a perfect environment for barrel ageing in fine French and American oak, adding complexity and elegance to every bottle.

With a commitment to sustainable viticulture and small-lot winemaking, Seven Stones Winery invites you to experience wines that are unmistakably shaped by their place of origin—and crafted with intention, patience, and care.

Come explore the magic of Seven Stones. Tour our vineyard, step into the caves, and savour the Similkameen—one glass at a time
