Our Services

Skills Development Programme 

Courses in many subjects;  IT ESOL , Dressmaking, Sewing, French language, digital photography, help with CV and job applications.  

Details
Health & Wellbeing Programmes 

Yoga classes, health awareness and information events, older people's awareness focus group, and various health sessions in partnership with NHS.

Details
Leisure & Recreation 

A wide and varied range of different social groups and clubs.  A range of leisure & recreational activities, including day trips to places of interest.  

Details
Advocacy Services 

The Forum provides various services to improve the quality of life of the BAME people in North Hertfordshire, including advice and guidance in many areas.  

Details
Children & Young People  

We provide various extra-curricula activities to improve the confidence and self-esteem and educational achievement of vulnerable children and young people.

Details
Senior Citizens Services 

We have various health, leisure and recreational activities for the elderly people to include Luncheon Clubs, Social Clubs, Day Trips helping to reduce their loneliness. We also provide various workshop on health, community safety, Fire safety and preventing crimes etc.

Details

The Queen honours our Chairman, Mr Sanjeev Kumar MBE

Her Majesty honouring our Chairman, Mr Sanjeev Kumar at the Buckingham Palace and recognising his leadership contributed to our outstanding support to the local community initiatives. Later Mr Kumar has been awarded an MBE in the Birthday honour list of HM Queen Elizabeth II for 2020.

Prince William meets our chairman, Mr Sanjeev Kumar MBE

The Duke of Cambridge, Prince William, presented the Forum's Chair, Mr Sanjeev Kumar, with his MBE at a special Investiture Ceremony at Windsor Castle on 7th December 2021.

Mr Sanjeev Kumar was awarded an MBE for services to the BAME community.
