Record Creator: Health, Department of
Description:
These records include Department of Health death certificate index entries for individuals who died in Washington from 1907-1960 and 1965-2017. The indexes for 1961-1964 are available on microfilm at the State Archives in Olympia. Death certificates for 1907-1960 were transcribed by volunteers and include more information than those for 1965-2017, which were provided by the Department of Health.
Related Records: This index was created by volunteers (1907-1960) and by a data extract from the Department of Health’s system (1965-2017). Images up to twenty-five years ago may be found in our collection of Department of Health, Death Certificates here.
Access Restriction Notes: This index is open for research.
