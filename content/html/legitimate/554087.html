Republic Tigers - Buildings & Mountains

Apr. 11, 2008 Best New Music

The Republic Tigers were created by Kenn Jankowski after the breakup of The Golden Republic. All five members of this Kansas City outfit take turns playing different instruments and each bring demos to the table for all to flesh out.

For today’s top tune, we feature the beautifully catchy “Buildings & Mountains”.
