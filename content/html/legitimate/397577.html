Boasting magnificent views over the vibrant metropolis, the four-star Marriott Château Champlain Montréal offers luxurious accommodation for business or leisure travelers in a superb central location. A number of major points of interest are nearby, such as Old Montreal, the Bell Centre, the Montreal Planetarium, and the city’s extensive underground shopping area.

Accommodation
The beautifully furnished rooms at the Marriott Château Champlain Montréal are equipped with first-rate technological amenities, including free wireless internet, cable HDTV showing premium channels, air conditioning, a safe, and a telephone with voicemail. In addition, a comfortable night’s sleep is assured thanks to blackout drapes together with plush signature bedding. Residents can relax with a hot drink or refreshments from the minibar as they peruse their courtesy newspaper, whereas those who need to keep up with their work can make use of the well-lit desk. The elegant en-suite bathroom is stocked with complimentary toiletries plus a hairdryer.

Hotel Facilities
Patrons at the Marriott Château Champlain Montréal can look forward to on-site fitness facilities, a heated indoor swimming pool, and a spa tub. For corporate visitors, there is a 24-hour business center along with generous versatile meeting space with state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment. What’s more, multi-lingual staff members at the front desk are on hand around the clock to assist guests with luggage storage, dry cleaning, currency exchange, arranging tours, or utilizing the safety deposit box. In terms of dining, the sophisticated Restaurant Samuel de Champlain serves delectable continental cuisine throughout the day while the Bar Le Senateur is a cozy place to enjoy casual meals alongside an array of tasty beverages.

Transportation
Parking is available at the Marriott Château Champlain Montréal; furthermore, the city’s main attractions are also easily accessible on foot or via public transport.
