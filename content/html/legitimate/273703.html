Empower your business office.

Save time and money with a seamless flow of information between tuition, fundraising, and accounting systems, eliminating redundancies and reducing manual processes.

  • Easily reconcile tuition payments to your general ledger through tuition and accounting integration

  • Track key financial performance metrics across all school offices for a clear view of financial performance
