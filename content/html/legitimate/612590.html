{ July At The Bergens!}

wow! hello world! 
between still getting settled in our new place and the everyday chaos we call life blogging has just NOT been  the priority! I've missed you fellow bloggers! Can we still be friends?? ;)

Ryans new obsession is with birds!!! Ryan and I are taking a marriage class and in this class they teach each individual in the relationship needs to have some kind of hobby thats just theirs. To Ryan that meant get a crap load of birds. We have chickens, quail, and ducks. You could say I'm less then thrilled.
so these are our birds, aka Ryans' self care. He's doing awesome in school! Kicking butt, taking names. Only 5 months till graduation!!! I'm so proud of him. This ON TOP of working 70 hour work weeks and taking this marriage class we're taking. Busy busy guy!!!

If you look closely, he built this chicken coop in the corner of our huge backyard. This picture was taken in the middle of the Haboob,,,,,,teehee.....that words makes me laugh :)

I've been doing my passion: decorating! I finally cleaned and organized our insanity of a den!
not too shabby right???? This is MY office!!! I've always wanted one. NO KIDS ZONE!!
I'm also doing my other passion: HAIR! This is a cut I did on myself. I was quite proud....YOU try cutting your own hair! ;)
oh did I mention it was MIDNIGHT??! Yeah...cause it was!
 Our little Alenna is swimming like a fish! We bought a pool and we cannot keep her out! We decided that she needs preschool, we were going to forgo it, thinking it wasnt a necassity but we decided she needs it. So she starts Aug 14th and is counting down the days!
Love this girl. Shes growing so fast and everyday is doing something new. She literally takes care of her brother and sister. So cute!
My little Brady man. Sigh....what do I say about him??? LOL.....Hes crazy. We love this kid to death but we worry about him too. We're getting his behaviors evauluted. The Dr thinks he may have high functioning Aspergers. We're not so sure. He def. has an anxiety disorder, like his momma, and some OCD. We sure love him alot though. He, like Alenna, starts preschool in Aug. Sniff! Two of my babies are going to school!!! What am I going to do with myself???! Brady sure loves his sisters!!!


Miss Shaylin McKell. Sweetest little angel. I say it all the time but she seriously is an angel in baby form! She sooooo sweet and we sure have enjoyed seeing her learn and grow. She anxious to keep up with her brother and sister!!!
She enjoys swimming too!!

So life is good.
