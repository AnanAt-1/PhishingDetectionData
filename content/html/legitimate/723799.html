How to Order

  1. Choose the level of copy editing you need (detailed descriptions below).
  2. Determine word count for your document in Word (see Review menu or bottom task bar). Round to the nearest 1000 words (3500 >> 4000).
  3. Select the payment method you prefer (PayPal, or Credit Card via custom secure order form). You will enter the number of 1000 words (4000 >> 4).
  4. Send your Word document as an email attachment. Please include any special instructions or requests. I will edit promptly, using Word’s Track Changes feature for your review, along with additional comments and suggestions.
