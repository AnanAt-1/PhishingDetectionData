Domain Name Generator

Domain Name Soup is a free domain name generator tool to help you find available domain names, watch taken domains, monitor and sort your own domains and ensure you don't let your domains expire. You can export your favourite available domains (for friends or customers) and receive alerts when they need renewing. Designed for both starters and portfolio owners and all features are free.


Search for domain name ideas


If you already have a name in mind such as a personal or business name

  • Try different combinations of hyphens to generate a Hyphenated Domain
  • Try different words in front your name eg Premium Domain Names, Cool Domain Names, Business Domain Names
  • Try changing individual letters of your domain using our Domain Name Mangle tool.
  • Try searching for common Typos to either catch typing mistakes
  • Try entering Multiple Choices for any of the words that make up your domain idea.
  • Use our Thesaurus to find alternative words for your domain name.
  • Try jumbling up your words to find different Word combinations
  • Or try out Advanced domain Name Search

If you are just looking for a good domain name

  • Search through Available Domain Names
  • Search for 3 letter domain names, 4 letter domain names, 5 letter domain names or 6 letter domain names
  • Search for 3 letter random pronounceable domain names, or 4 letters, 5 letters or 6 letters
  • Search through available dictionary words to find something that describes your business
  • Look for del.icio.us style domain names where the Top Level Domain extension (such as US or COM) forms part of the word.
