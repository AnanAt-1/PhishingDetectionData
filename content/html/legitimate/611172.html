About Rosemount Technology Centre

RTC is a modern facility with up-to-date technological equipment and comprehensive student services.

The skills, training and technical knowledge acquired in these programs are in great demand in business and industry throughout North America.

Learn more


Rosemount Technology Centre

RTC is one of the largest English technical career training centres in Quebec. It is a modern facility with up-to-date technological equipment and comprehensive student services.


Free Tuition

RTC offers full-time career programs authorized and funded by the Quebec Ministry of Education.


The Centre

The centre is structured as a business educational environment, similar to industry, with computer labs, technology rooms and workshops.
