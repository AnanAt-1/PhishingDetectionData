EXPLORE THE BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP

Ready for a healthier you? Your YMCA is easy to join with a wide range of activities, equipment, facilities, and programs throughout greater Kansas City. Find your Y and explore the benefits of:

 

  • Y All Access, Always! Use your membership at any YMCA of Greater KC location: Explore Locations
  • Traveling? Enjoy nationwide YMCA access: Learn More
  • FREE Classes! Group exercise classes are included with your membership: View Schedules
  • Get Started! FREE Member Orientation—visit or call your local Y: Learn More
  • Make a Splash! Swim at any Y indoor pool near you: Find Pools
  • Kids Zone Fun! Free childcare for kids 12 weeks-8 years: Find Kid Zones
  • Personal Goals? Reach them with Y Personal Training: Check Options
  • Discounts Galore! Save on youth sports, swim, and more—ask at your Y or register online. Explore Programs
  • YMCA360 Online! Free workouts, anywhere, anytime: Get Started
