Swimmer Of The Month - Learn to Swim

Swimmer of the Month is held on the first Wednesday of each month at Hebburn Centralpool starting at 7.45pm.

Children from the Wednesday classes are invited to come along to swim in their correct class on the night and then to join in with the Swimmer of the Month presentations.

A small trophy/medal is awarded to a swimmer from each Wednesday class who their teacher thinks has shown good attendance, behaviour and enthusiasm that month.

Similar presentations are alsoheld monthly for children from the Saturday and Sunday classes.

The trophy/medal will be theirs to keep.

Unfortunately the records of Swimmer of the Month are not complete. If you can help fill in the blanks, please supply the information via the Contact Page.
