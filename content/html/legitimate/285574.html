 Forgotten Suns  
Forgotten Suns is a 68 page, illustrated adventure for the Exalted® gaming system containing:
  • 25 unique, connected encounters
  • Detailed maps of a unique manse
  • Six new artifacts
  • Four new hearthstones
  • Two new creatures
  • 15 detailed NPCs

I also maintain a log of my own campaign that started with this adventure.

  
 
 When Autochthon Dreams  
When Autochthon Dreams is a 100+ page, illustrated sourcebook of artifacts for the Exalted® gaming system containing:
  • the work of 47 authors and six artists
  • over 100 new artifacts all across the power spectrum
  • new rules for materials, crafting and alchemy
