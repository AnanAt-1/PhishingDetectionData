MessageCleaner is a utility designed to facilitate the cleaning of messy email and newsgroup messages. If you've ever received email that has been forwarded from person to person numerous times, or if you've ever read a newsgroup message that has been chopped up by numerous responses, you're probably familiar with the unsightly (and nearly unreadable) mess that accumulates as words at the end of lines of text get pushed too far to the right and are word wrapped improperly.

This program is designed to help you clean up the mess with just a few mouse clicks. To see how it works, download MessageCleaner and try it for 30 days free of charge. MessageCleaner's online help files contain detailed tutorials. 

If you decide to keep using MessageCleaner after 30 days, the registration fee is $7.50. If you do not register after 30 days, MessageCleaner will still function; however you will be forced to endure a 30 second delay at program start-up.
