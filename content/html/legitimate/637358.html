Everyone needs a place to lay their weary head. For travelers visiting Mont Tremblant, Cap Tremblant Mountain Resort is an excellent choice for rest and rejuvenation. Well-known for its romantic environment and proximity to great restaurants and attractions, Cap Tremblant Mountain Resort makes it easy to enjoy the best of Mont Tremblant. Free wifi is offered to guests, and rooms at Mont Tremblant offer a flat screen TV and a kitchenette. During your stay, take advantage of some of the amenities offered, including baggage storage and express check-in and check-out. Guests of Cap Tremblant Mountain Resort are also welcome to enjoy a pool and an on-site restaurant. For travelers arriving by car, free parking is available. Close to Domaine Saint-Bernard (2.7 mi), a popular Mont Tremblant landmark, Cap Tremblant Mountain Resort is a great destination for tourists. While you’re here, be sure to check out some of the steakhouses, including La Forge and Bullseye - Saloon & Grillades, all of which are a short distance from Cap Tremblant Mountain Hotel. If you’re looking for things to do, you can check out Circuit Mont Tremblant (0.7 mi), which is a popular attraction amongst tourists, and it is within walking distance.
