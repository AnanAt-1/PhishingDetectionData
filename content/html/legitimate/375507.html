How This Works:
Spoiler:


Horror film suggestions are given, each title receives its own thread and discussion ensues.

In an effort to keep titles easily accessible, they must be streaming on Netflix and currently available on DVD.

All discussions will take place in the DVD Reviews and Recommendations forum.
Links to the discussion threads are in the above table schedule and posted in this thread immediately after they're put up.

Threads for the chosen films are posted on a weekly basis.
Random.org determines the order titles on the schedule appear.

Participation does not require you to pick a film, but if you do PLEASE at least make an effort to say something about it.

Feedback is always welcome.
