Jean Baptiste Nicaise / Marie Catherine Miou Family Genealogy

JEAN BAPTISTE NICAISE was born in Paris, France. He was married to MARIE CATHERINE MIOU on May 11, 1745.

MARIE CATHERINE MIOU was born in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama.

JEAN BAPTISTE NICAISE and MARIE CATHERINE MIOU had the following children:

i. Jean Baptiste Andre NICAISE was born on September 10, 1747 in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama.

ii. Jean Baptiste Noel NICAISE was born on December 27, 1748 in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama. He died in 1749.

iii. MARIE JEANNE NICAISE was born on December 22, 1749 in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama.192 She was christened on December 25, 1749. She died in De Isle, Hancock County, Mississippi.

iv. Jean Baptiste "Martial" NICAISE was born on April 4, 1753 in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama. He married Louise Baptiste Christian-Ladner (Jean Baptiste/Marie Louise (De Lorme) Fisseau).

v. JOSEPH NICAISE.

vi. Charles NICAISE was born on August 23, 1759 in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama.

vii. Marie Louise NICAISE was born on August 15, 1765 in Mobile, Mobile County, Alabama. She died about 1840.
