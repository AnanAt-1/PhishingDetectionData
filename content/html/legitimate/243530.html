Alesa Baker Designs

Known for her signature look of soft elegant colors, beautiful papers, and feminine design style, Alesa's art has graced the covers and pages of many art industry magazines. She also served as an artist on call for them as well. Her gentle watercolor palette and graceful, delicate images have become a favorite among rubber stampers. Alesa is a self-taught artist, wife, mother, mother-in-law, grandma to our sweet baby Ruby and puppy Auggie, and a collector of Everything Elegant.
