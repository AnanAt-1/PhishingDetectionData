About us

The Leidan Mitchell passion for beauty and compassion for people began in 2001 at our first location in Arcadia. Our founding owners Jacki Tatman and Erin Essert had a vision of creating a salon environment where stylists could create and build their own business with the support of a full service salon. The dream had come true: Independent salon professionals flourishing into two Valley locations! Our largest salon and spa is located in Chandler and was introduced to the East Valley in 2008.

With the growth and future of the salons in mind, Leidan Mitchell expanded the ownership team 2 years ago. Nicole Evans, Jessica Gallegos and Kelly Reid embraced the vision and passion of Jacki and Erin. We are committed to creating and building opportunities for future artists /salon professionals.

We’re overjoyed to announce our feature in Volume 3 of The Scout Guide Phoenix & Scottsdale! This curated city guide showcases exceptional businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations that contribute to our community in remarkable ways.

Click here to read the story behind our business and our commitment to the community: Member Spotlight: Leidan Mitchell – The Scout Guide

Please stop in one of our locations (or schedule an appointment) for a complimentary copy of this gorgeous annual print guide. We’re thrilled to be part of a collective dedicated to celebrating local businesses and the incredible individuals behind them.
