    Living in California

    One of the largest and most populous states in the country, California is full of notable cities, popular attractions, natural beauty and so much more.

    A state full of diverse landscapes and natural beauty

    California is a large state and covers a wide range of natural features, including mountains, beaches, lakes, forests, state parks and rivers. These natural sites allow residents to experience a variety of outdoor activities including skiing, surfing, hiking, biking, fishing, swimming and more. Known for its mild climate and beautiful seasons, California offers its apartment dwellers the opportunity to enjoy the outdoors all year long. Several popular parks and forests are spread across the state including the Mojave National Preserve, Death Valley National Park, Yosemite National Park and Lassen National Forest.

    You may want to live in Sacramento, the Capital, or rent an apartment near the beautiful Pacific Ocean beaches in San Diego. Explore the vineyards of northern California, get caught up in the excitement of DisneyLand, or experience the glitz of Hollywood. From the north to the south, you can find great attractions, restaurants, shops and natural features in the state of California.

    Home to Hollywood and higher ed

    Education is also a major attraction for California residents. The University of California offers several campuses in San Diego, Berkeley, San Francisco, Los Angeles and more. The University of Southern California, California State University and many other institutions provide excellent opportunities for higher education as well.

    Professional Sports in California

    If you like sports and the outdoors, California won't disappoint. Whether you make it out to a game or watch it on TV in the comfort of your home, you'll have a team to watch every season. The Angels, Dodgers and the A's provide MLB action in the spring and summer, and the Lakers offer basketball fans a team to cheer on. Hockey is also on hand with the Anaheim Ducks, and the Oakland Raiders offer entertainment on Sundays and Mondays. And with collegiate sports in addition to the professional teams, you'll be hard pressed to find time to keep up with all of California's sports.
