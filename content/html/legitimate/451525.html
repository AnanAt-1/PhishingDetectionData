About Us

CHARLIE ANDERSON
Creative Director

Charlie is a Bafta and Emmy award-winning creative whose credits include Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway, Big Brother, and a host of hit TV series. Over 25-years in the industry he has collaborated with a who’s who of talent - from  Morgan Freeman and Ricky Gervais to David Beckham and Lady Gaga.

BEN VIGNOLA
Head of Production

In a career spanning more than two decades, Ben has earned a reputation as a preeminent expert in live production: responsible for delivering some of the biggest shows in the global calendar. For the past 4 years, Ben has worked with Riot Games to produce their two flagship events: League of Legends MSI and Worlds.

PAT DOYLE
Executive Producer

Pat is an NTA and RTS winning executive producer and showrunner. With 25 years experience in TV and film. Pat has produced hundreds of hours of broadcast entertainment, including a 5-season stint as the Series Producer of BBC’s 'Top Gear' - at that time, the most-watched non-fiction TV show on the planet.  

LISA DE LA VEGA
Creative Producer

Lisa is a vasty experienced creative producer and celebrated designer of magical illusions. From the stages of Vegas and Coachella, to Netflix and MTV, she has collaborated with stars like David Blaine, A$AP Rocky, Jay Shetty and Justin Willman on some of the most ambitious stage and TV shows ever produced.
