Ivry Gitlis
To Philippe Girardin

"Just to tell you how moving it is for me to see, feel, touch and listen to your violins.

It is years since I met you for the first time and it is increasingly comforting to know that in this more and more mechanical, levelled world, where all that is extraordinary is swept away and repressed, there are still people devoted to the detailed and loving creation of these flowers of the spirit which are violins, and what violins!

Thank you! It is a way of redeeming a little what remains of humanity.

IVRY GITLIS   Paris 23rd of january 2012
