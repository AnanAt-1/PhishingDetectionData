NOVELTY FICTION publishes original works of quality fiction -- books, e-books, audiobooks and magazines.

Our readers appreciate our high literary and editorial standards, but also love our distinct artistic touch. From riveting romances to tales of contemporary Gothic fiction, we test the boundaries of human emotion, ranging from brightness to utter darkness.

NOVELTY FICTION GAZETTE  is our quarterly literary magazine, freely available online.
