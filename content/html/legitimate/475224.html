ABOUT US

Basin Electric Power Cooperative is a not-for-profit generation and transmission cooperative owned by 139 member cooperative systems across nine states serving 3 million consumers. We pursue a smart and affordable energy strategy, and take advantage of the benefits of renewables while maintaining baseload that ensures the reliability our members expect.
