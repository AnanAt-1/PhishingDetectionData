IMDB Top 10 Mummy Movies of All Time

Just in time for Hallowe'en!

They're spooky. They're creepy. They might even make you scream.
The top 10 Mummy movies, as rated on IMDB (The Internet Movie Database)
We've even included Netflix availability at the bottom of the title.
WARNING: not all are suitable for children, and maybe even some adults!

Photo: The Mummy (1932)

1. The Mummy (1932)

IMDB rating 7.3/10
Budget: $196,000 (estimated)
Drama/Horror
Not rated/NR
73 minutes
In 1921 a field expedition in Egypt discovers the mummy of ancient Egyptian prince Im-Ho-Tep, who was condemned and buried alive for sacrilege...
Available on Netflix: YES



Photo: Bubba Ho-tep 

2. Bubba Ho-Tep

IMDB rating: 7.2/10
Budget: $1,000,000 (estimated)

Gross: $2,003,420 (USA) (30 June 2004)
Restricted
Comedy
92 minutes
Elvis and JFK, both alive and in nursing homes, fight for the souls of their fellow residents as they battle an ancient Egyptian Mummy.
Available on Netflix: YES

IMDB rating: 6.9/10

Available on Netflix: NO

IMDB ranking: 6.2/10
Budget: $738,000 (estimated)
Not rated/NR
Action/Adventure/Comedy
79 minutes
Bud and Lou find themselves pursued by an Egyptian cult for a special medallion linked to a walking mummy.
Available on Netflix: YES
