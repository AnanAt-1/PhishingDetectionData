Colonial Full Block™ True Union Ball Valves

Valves are premium quality and ideally suited for the most demanding industrial, commercial, agricultural, and turf irrigation environments. Patented PVDF polymeric locking strip allows safe operation even when installed in the reverse direction. It also allows for ease of disassembly, should the valve require service. Valves are ideal where full port, full flow, and reparability are necessary.

  • PVC or CPVC
  • FKM O-rings
  • Double "D" stem design
  • Two stem stops for positive shut-off
  • Floating ball design for a positive seal
  • Design permits easy removal of the valve body for service
  • Bi-directional: can be installed in either direction
  • Vacuum rating: 26" Hg with less than 1" Hg loss
  • Pressure rating (non-shock water @ 73°F): 235 psi (1/2" to 2") & 150 psi (3" & 4")
  • NSF 61 certified
