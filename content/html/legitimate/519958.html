Welcome

Fakenham Superbowl offers 6 lanes of family friendly modern tenpin for your family delectation; if you've never done it before (where have you been?) then it's easy to pick up, the staff are happy to guide you and most kids love bowling: especially as you can use rolling ramps and pop up gutter guards to give them a chance!
Fakenham Superbowl is located in the market town of Fakenham in North Norfolk. We are a family run business providing bowling to the locals and visitors alike for over 25 years. We now include small amusements area, restaurant, a 3 level soft play area called MegaFun and recently we have added the first of our Escape Rooms.

Ten-Pin Bowling

You don't have to be an expert to enjoy ten pin bowling in fact, if you have never bowled before Fakenham Superbowl is a great place to learn.
It's a great family activity as the game can change at any time and unless you're a corker on the lanes yourself they generally stand as much chance as you: it's the great leveller of family activities!

  • Beginners are welcomed by our friendly staff, and there are special 'bumper lanes' available so that even small children can participate.

  • You don't need any special equipment just come along and have a go. The animated scorers are simple to use, just type in your names and off you go.

  • We have people bowling from all ages from small children to the older youngsters well into their eighties.

    Due to the nature of the equipment small children do require careful supervision.

  • We recommend you book a lane to be sure we can fit you in. Although not always essential we can get very busy at certain times. Bookings can be only be made in person or by phone 01328 856650.
