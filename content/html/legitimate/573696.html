Experience Messiah, a nationally ranked private Christian university in Pennsylvania

Messiah University is a top-ranked private university of the liberal and applied arts and sciences in the northeastern U.S. With a reputation for successfully integrating rigorous academics and Christian faith into all of our academic programs, we offer 150+ undergraduate and pre-professional degree options and 20+ graduate degree and certificate programs.

Jon C. Stuckey named interim president of Messiah University

Jon C. Stuckey has been appointed as the interim president of Messiah University, effective July 1, 2025, following the retirement announcement of President Kim S. Phipps. Stuckey, who has served as the vice president for advancement at the university and has a 25-year tenure at Messiah, is expected to lead the institution through its current transition while upholding its mission and values. His leadership is credited with significant fundraising success, contributing to the university's growth and development.
