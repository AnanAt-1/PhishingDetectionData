Greetings from
Coney Island USA!

The Nonprofit Arts Organization dedicated to the economic and cultural revitalization of Coney Island.

Located in the landmarked building at 1208 Surf Avenue, in the heart of South Brooklyn's amusement district.

Home to the Coney Island Circus Sideshow, the Coney Island Museum, the Mermaid Parade,
the Freak Bar, the Gift Shop and much more!
