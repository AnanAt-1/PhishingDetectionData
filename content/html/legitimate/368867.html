Introduction

[edit | edit source]

The Action/Reaction Theory [ART] is a theory which is constantly build up and therefore never finished. Like any other theories. The difference is that it is build up through happenings which can be readily observed and, if no such relevant action can be found, is left open ended and is not speculated on. Which basicaly makes sure that there is an answer before it is accepted (build into the theory). That such a relevant action can be found today is garanteed by the observation that everything is build up (adapted) from something which has been there before and that nothing is a new creation. With other words the meaning of action-reaction is that this happening feeds itself and it is only left to explain how it can evolve from something very simple to something very complicated.
The Natural Principles section tries to explain how observations made today can help to understand the past and even the future.

The idea of the ART is worked out in the "Growing through movement" section. It is not an extention to other theories (but might have many similarities). In fact how atoms became into being has only been hinted on through what might have happened before. This also gives the ART the possibilities that it reveals something which contradicts other knowledge. Therefore, to make sense of what the Newton Cradle offers and what is described in "Step 1" to "Step 6", is it the best not to try to compare it to what is 'known' - basically start new. The ART uses the natural action/reaction happening, which the Newton Cradle performs, to explain a natural build up of bigger and bigger matter systems which are created through evolution. And therefore is a possible explanation how the stage of evolution, as of today, evolved.

The "Extension of definition of the terms used" section, serves to adapt natural phenomenon which are also present and therefore described in other theories, to the ART. Since they usually have the same meaning is it the best to also use the same words to refer to them but it needs to be kept in mind that sometimes they might point out more and sometime even contradict. What the differences will be can only be determined when the ART is fully worked out.

For this the A/R theory needs to be dragged into the lab. If different matter sizes can be set up like a Newton Cradle and the energies which are send in and come out are made visible then certain tests and conclusions can be made. The ART can speed up to explain the changes which happen because it opens a possibility which could be looked into. If the changes can be connected to what the ART suggest, different changes with different placement of the matters, then it could be called an energy-signature. (A relevant action is observed through the spectrometer which reveals that different atoms leave their marks in the light spectrum according to their size and its relevant natural frequency. Which is also used to analyse the build up of stars.)
Until this is done everything is in limbo - it is still only a theory. Therefore any attempt to nail anything down is only an attempt to predict what it could be and serves only to satisfy the need to force answers with a fantasy (even if it has been proven with mathematics).
The ART is therefore a theory of reality. Which means that what happens naturally has to be real. Or, if something does not exist physically than it cannot be used. Even the fantasy used by nature is always build on real existence. Human have developed such a high level language that anything can be given any meaning with words. Unfortunately every meaning stored in the brain is real for the brain and if there was no specific notion that it relates to a fictional matter it will be recalled and used like any other real meaning. Therefore after a while, when used without this differentiation, all meanings are accepted as real. Therefore, for humans, what can be and what cannot be natural is lost because reality is mingled with fiction. This is what the ART tries to avoid by only storing real happenings.

Some of the examples are: Wave form movement:

A wave cannot be performed from a single matter (here it is only a graphical display). Unless there are a string of matters, physically connected, there will be no wave.
Passing on of energy:
Small space:
Big energies cannot be passed on by small matter unless it is able to move at a very high speed. But for this it needs a big empty space. But a big empty space prohibits solid (filled) matter.

Energy cannot be passed on unless there is a medium (matter) to be passed on to (filled space). Therefore if a space is empty (devoid of matter) then there cannot be any passing on of energy (radiation is not a passing of energy).
Reduction of size:
If matter is a filled mass which occupies a specific space, which cannot be occupied at the same time with another mass, then, realistically, it cannot be reduced to a space much smaller than this mass.

Fundamentals of physics

[edit | edit source]

When it was discovered that the atom is not a solid matter but build of other particles then the question of how the atom is constructed needed to be answered. There were two models which were in consideration. The 'plum pudding' and the 'nuclear' model. Short a space filled with matter and a mostly empty space. To get the answer, in 1911 Ratherford made a test. He shot alpha particles into a very thin gold plate and concluded his answer from the effect it had. Most alpha particles went right through and only some where deflected in different direction. He figured that since most went through the atom must be empty and the one which were deflected struck the nucleus. Or so he and other people thought. Apparently he had not seen a Newton Cradle in action. Otherwise he might have needed to consider that the alpha particles passed on a characteristic energy to the gold plate, which was passed through and catapulted like particles out on the other side. Which would have been another possibility and the test could not have been answered. Since the passed on energy can also be diverted in different direction the reflections could have been explained. ('Fundamentals Of Chemistry', ISBN 0-673-07876-0).

Which leads to the possibility that any energy, passed on or transformed can be overloaded with energy inherent in the matter through which it travels.

Which gives it the possibility to be used as a form of communication passing on information. A communication which only needs forms of matter to send or receive. A possibility which the ART tries to exploit.

Therefore what could be assumed is that the energy which is passed through might have picked up other energy information on its way through the matters. Energies, which might reveal the natural frequencies the matters in the line have, according to their size and build up. And if this natural frequencies are already known then it might also reveal the position of that matter in the line.
The ART uses this relevant action to explain how the world could have been built with passing energies from matter to matter.

Natural Principles

[edit | edit source]

In order to find out how something works is to observe it while it is working. When we look out of the window then we see a very diverse, and surly complicated moving environment created by nature. To figure out all the details within a lifespan of a human seem to be impossible. But what a human can do is to find some repetitions, which happen during the time he is observing, which give him some indications on what principles this world is based on. And the first surprise would be: there are not many!
The first obvious principle is that nothing would happen if there is no movement. When movements are analysed then it seems that there are no purposes to any. Things might happen because something else has happened. What is happening is defined by what has happened, on what it has happened and the circumstances in which it has happened. And the next thing to happen is because of what has just been described has happened. If some lifeless matter falls toward the earth then it does not know where it will fall to and what will happen after it made contact.
Another fact, which seem to need to be present so that something can happen, is that the parts which come together as a result of an action (happening) have to be in a state of opposition. Physically that means that if something warm comes in contact with a cold part then there will be an exchange of temperature. If something moving encounters a stationary object then a form of exchange of movement will take place to equal the state of movement according to the physical properties of the parts. If the minds of living creatures are in an opposite state when they encounter each other then there will be some form of war.
And even that there are many different things which are happening, they all seem to be highly repetitious. Meaning that alike actions always provoke the same reactions if it happens in the same environment (conditions). Changes (adaptations) are only happening if the environment has changed.

This observed principles can be connected to form some regularity:

  • Opposition creates movement.
  • Movement creates chance.
  • Chance creates repetition.
  • Repetition guaranties an ongoing evolution.

But there is one trend which seem to overlay all this happenings. Any change usually destroys something so that something can be build. Which is usually a bigger adapted version of something already working. With other words the trend is from small to big.
This are very important observations because they show very significant facts about nature. Nature has no goals, it could be termed 'physical happenings by chance'. It has no goal in its mind what it is supposed to create. It has no mathematics to calculate if something will work or not. It is also under no time pressure. And, most of all, it cannot say: "this did not work, lets start over". It seems to run on 'chemical reactions' which are guided by the physical properties of matter. Physical properties are all what is available to build every small or big physical part to be found in this universe. And that should be all what is needed to explain what is happening. But there must be more to guarantee an evolution like the one which we can observe. If you want to build something new from something old then you must have a memory of that something old. If you want to adapt it to changes which have occurred, then you must know how the new facts differ from the old ones. With other words, if something should be changed then there must be something which can be changed. And there must be some possibility to compare. Since there is no known overall control over the evolutionary happenings then there must be some basic intelligence build in the system, which enables what seem to be an endless chain reaction of the same principles.

Evolution - growing naturally

[edit | edit source]

Naturally, in this case, means that whatever happens is purely physical. There are no immediate or long term predefined purposes which guide what is happening, only the possibilities that a physical action can happen. And there are absolute no restrictions on what can happen.
The facts, that the evolution of matter goes from small to big, and that there is no knowing change of principles, makes the 'reconstruction' of the development which has happened in the universe, as known today, possible.
Therefore we start out with an endless space which is filled with an infinite amount of the smallest matter (mass) possible - the UrParticle [UP].
But this is enough. Matter, no matter how small, seem to have the possibility to bind with another matter to form something bigger. And to figure out how this is possible we need to combine what we have learned from the Newton Cradle and what else nature teaches us.
The universe is big enough so that we can use quite a big part to explain nature, without reaching its borders. Therefore for our purpose there is no exchange with any matter from outside of the universe. Which means we deal with a fixed amount of matter even if the universe is, for our feeling of size, endless.
But we also always have to have in mind that, since there are no predefined purposes, there are no 'mistakes'. Everything is right, no matter what is the outcome. There is also never a 'starting over' - once it started it just goes on. Therefore whatever are the principals today were the principles at the assumed start and will be in the future.

Step 1: growth by size - 3 dimensional

[edit | edit source]

If matter is moving then there is the possibility that it collides (make physical contact) with another one. And there is the possibility that after a collision they stay together and grow (build) into a new form of matter. This is possible if the movements of the matters are in harmony, which means that their frequencies, directions, strength and phase match. They can be added without that they move the UPs apart. Since there is no pulling force active between the UPs at this stage it needs pressure of the environment to keep the UPs together. But probably both UPs had their movements changed since an exchange of movement (adaptation) took place. If they actually stay together then they now move in unison in a new combined form of movement.
When there is enough empty space available in the environment then this movement can be a straight one with the appropriate direction and speed. But if the environment restricts or even prohibits free movement then the physical movement takes on an appropriate form or stops in which case the physical movement has to be passed on as a potential movement (potential energy). If none of the UPs can pass this potential movement on to the environment then it is passed on between the UPs which have collided and these may start to oscillate the potential movement between them. Since, in this case, this passing on of potential movement is directed toward each other it gives the impression that the UPs are drawn to each other. They physically stay together now on their own (no pressure needed). This rises the energy level of the now physically joint matter which can be termed that they are exited or charged or have a higher temperature. Therefore what is otherwise a one time happening, can become continuous while the two, now joint UPs, exchange a potential movement between each other. Specifically if energy is constantly supplied from the environment which restricts their movement and applies pressure on them.
Now, the new form of matter does not physically move and actually occupies the smallest space it can. Since the physical movement before the collision needed lots of space, room is now left into which the environment (pressure) can bring other UPs from outside. Which, under the right conditions, could join into the energy exchange and therefore grow the matter again. E.g. into a ring like formation where the potential movement is passed on in a circle. With the possibility that this ring formations can grow into any size.
If any matter in the ring is not physically touching the matter to which it is passing the energy then the passing on is interrupted (resistance). Therefore, on the next pass of energy (cycle), the inertia of the UP which has physical space available will transform the potential movement into a physical move, directed toward the next matter in line, until it touches the next matter and then pass on the remaining potential movement. Opening space in the ring will be closed consecutively by movements of matter, with the additional effect of a shift in the phase, until no space is left.
This passing on of energy in ring form provides the possibilities that any matter in this ring can be a member of more rings which pass energy through it. The amount of energies which can be passed through an UP in different directions at the same time is probably limited because at some point this energies will cancel each other out or add up to a form which is impossible to pass on. But the possibility is there. And if rings join then the matter can grow in different directions into matter which is build of interconnecting rings.
In multi-ring formations energy is passed on in different direction which might impose some physical regulations on the form which can be build. This would include of where rings can be joint, how many rings can be interconnected and in which direction energy can be passed before the form falls apart again. Which means that the many passed on energies within the form must sum up to an equilibrium of energies which is characteristic for this form - an energy signature [ES]. Which means that if an energy is passed on to this formation it could be imprinted with a specific sequence of energies which are active within when it is passed through. Or the reaction will be specific to this sequence if it is acted on regarding to changes, redirection or split up (functionality).
But this also provides the possibility that stable forms of matter can be build which react with always the same specific functions when the circumstances are the same (provides a specific feedback). Once an ES is reached through the building and arranging of rings then the growth of size of this form ends. The urparticles [UP] of matter have grown into an element matter [EM].

Charge

But the only way matter can reveal energy (charge) is through movement. Therefore different instruments are measuring different forms of movements. Like senses are tuned to different 'frequencies' (repetition) of movement.
Circumstances
action, environment, feedback, conditions or all of it
Comparison

This means that energy rings can also have different effects on any passed through energy depending on how they are located within the shape. This also applies if the ring does not go through an EM but also if it goes through the environment where it might form a force-field.
Energy

All demands which could be provided by a system which is simply following what is physically possible.
Energy rings
That an energy can be passed on between seemingly stationary masses can be proven with the gadget called Newton Cradle. But I dont know of any gadget which directs the passed on energy back to itself. It could be envisioned that if the Newton cradle would have been build in a ring-formation it could pass energy continuously along the ring. And, since potential energy (potential movement) is transformed into physical movement by inertia, as soon as physical space is available, their possible physical movement (which closes the gap) keeps them together.
Further 'rings' (energy paths) could be grown out of any of this so joint matter and form a three dimensional matter which is constantly virtually moving (oscillating) by passing on energy in different direction around different rings within the matter. Even if this 'ring formations' are shapes which are quite solid already on their own and will not likely rearrange their rings it seems to be always possible to make connections by forming an energy ring between them.
Energy Signature [ES]
If UPs have grown into forms which are build of energy rings then they must have energies flown through them which are in harmony otherwise an interruption of energy-flow will occur. Which might lead to that all energies within the EM are somewhat in harmony. Therefore they can add to an energy system which is the sum of all energies active in all rings called an energy signature. If this is not the case then the build up must be adapted to make this possible. This can be achieved by changing directions of the energy-flow, adapting frequencies by adding or subtracting UP within the rings or discarding one ring and build another one somewhere else.
When this ES is achieved then it makes the EM react in different ways to passed on energy depending if it is harmonic to the ES, as a sum of all energies in the EM, or not.
One is that, if the EM is passed on an inharmonious energy, that the EM reacts as a whole matter. It will try to separate itself from the source of the inharmonious energy as under pressure. The other is that, if the EM is passed on an harmonic energy, than this energy will be able to penetrate into the EM and will be passed through. On the way through the passing energies might be either be overloaded with inherited energies or the same energies might change the passed through energies by separating (selecting) the sequence of energies and steering them to different outputs where they can perform a specific action. This can be interpreted as feedback or reaction.
If the applied energy is passed through then it will be passed through as a sequence of energies if it is made up of such. But then it becomes important through which energy-rings it is passed. Which also form a sequence of energies. This sequence could also be different if the passed on energy enters the EM in different locations or is applied from different directions. Therefore the outcome depends on the entry point and angle.
Here is where the most useful natural selections occur since this type of selection also works in reverse. If only a specific frequency, or signature, makes this specific shape (or combination of shapes) to respond to or pass energy then this specific shape can be selected out of many in this environment through its reactions (feedback).
The Environment
The accumulation of matter (universe) form an environment which is the home of natural happenings. Any change in the physical constitution of the environment is an action. This action is naturally passed on to the physically neighbouring parts, which are normally also part of the environment, which react to equalize oppositional states which the action has created. Whatever happens (action) evokes a reaction (the equalisation of opposite states). Since this reaction occurs within the environment it constitutes a change in the environment and is therefore again an action a feedback. Whatever the reactions or the changes within the environments are depends purely on the physical constitution of the environment.
Evolution
Evolution is a process which is a functionality of all the possibilities described to grow system like formations out of available elementary forms by pure chance (naturally).
Feedback
Since the reaction defines the change of the environment which has happened also defines, since it is now an action, what the next reaction will be. This can also be seen as a feedback which defines the next action. This feedback can be supportive or restrictive. Therefore whatever can happen depends only on the possibilities this physical environment provides and the chances that this possibilities are met through the constant action/reaction chain reaction. The more sophis-ticated the parts become through natural growth, which build the environment, the more restrictive and diverse the feedback will be.
Force fields
Force fields are usually explained with the phenomenons a magnetic field provides. A magnetic field is obviously produced by an abundance of energy on one side of a matter and the lack of it on the opposite end of the matter. Following the explanation of the ART the environment of matter provides a media (return path) through which the abundant energy can be send back to the place where it is missing the equalisation of the energy levels is possible. That such energy passing rings have been formed is shown by the rearrangements metallic particles perform when placed into a magnetic field. If the rings are connected through a media which is already packed as tight as it can get, like air, then no physical movement will take place. But if the tight media is replaced by some bigger matter which can pass magnetic energy but has no restriction in movement then the potential energy, which is passed to it, is transformed into physical movement. Since this movement is directed toward the next passing matter in the ring it gives the impression that this matter is pulled in. The force field supplies the energy for the movement which can be explained with inertia but it cannot explain any pulling of the matter.
The earth is a big storage of a wide range of potential movements. And the universe, if filled with UP, is an environment which can pass a wide range of energies. Therefore it can be expected that there are many types of force fields created by the earth using the universe as return paths.
Frequency
Since the frequency (repetition) at which something can happen ranges from 0 to infinite, an infinite amount of selections can be made. One use of this is the selection which the natural oscillation of matter provides (inertia).
A single elementary matter (UP) has a specific mass which has a specific frequency in which it will oscillate if forced to move in a restricted space. When single element matter stay together after a collision, then they might become a new elementary mass in relationship to the environment. This new formed element mass has now its own natural frequency in which it oscillates. It also responds according to it to any outside energy which is being passed to it. Since the new mass is bigger then the mass of an UP its natural frequency in which it will oscillate is lower then the one of the UP.
Functions
When an action (energy change) occurs on a matter then it evokes a reaction (equalization of the levels) in the matter. If matter is just a single UP then this is usually just a simple single adjustment (movement). But as matter grows into formations with different placements of energy-rings then the action can start a reaction which is a sequence of movements depending what kind of rings it encounters as the energy is passed through the form. Collectively this is a function. If individual rings are also connected to other forms (environment) then its reaction, as part of the function, is applied to the connecting form. With other words the function of a form is to either pass the received action as a whole or dissect it into different harmonic energies (reactions). This individual reactions can then be used according to the build up and connections of the form which receives the reaction as a feedback. Functions of a form can differ, even if the action is the same, according to the place on the form or the direction in which the action is effective. This is specifically true with bigger forms.
Gravity
The pulling force of matter? What mass cannot produce on its own is a pulling force. If the UP, which we use to start life, should be as small as possible, e.g. that they cannot be divided any more, then it would be impossible to physically explain how it could produce such a force. Since this would mean that it is already build out of something which is able to produce some pulling force and is therefore not at its smallest possible state or pure. Therefore the phenomenon of gravity, the provoked movement of one mass toward another mass, has to be explained otherwise. What mass can create is an empty space (vacuum, through movement) into which environmental pressure can push matter like a water pump. Which has the same effect as if it was pulled in.
Growing - The Nature of
When matter is expected to grow by action (collision) then it cannot be assumed that matter can be divided indefinitely. Since if its size is 0 then its mass is also 0. Therefore there is no inertia an there cannot be an energy to pass on which is necessary to keep matter moving and therefore together.
When matter moves and collide then there can be a change. When everything is at its smallest possible shape then only growing (adding of shapes) can take place. If more and more matter grows into bigger shapes then the possibility that a collision separates grown matter grows too.
This depends now on more facts which have changed through the growing from the smallest size. One of this is dependent on the form it has achieved and the circumstances through which this form is attacked through the collision. Like strength, direction and duration. If an energy ring is hit at 90 to its energy passing direction then it might easier brake apart then when it is hit in the direction of it. It might easier connect if the colliding takes place on a ring which has the right frequency passing on then with another one in the form. And of course it varies with the strength of the collision.
All this possibilities grow if the matter is bigger and more complicated. With other words the sophistication of the reactions of the matter has grown with the size and sophistication of the form. It also varies with the characteristics of the environment in which the grown matter resides. If it is a busy environment in which a lot of growing happens then the forms and sizes are versatile and collisions have many more kinds of reactions.
What does not change are the principles which make the growing possible. These are the needs to store somehow the action and reactions which take place and also the environment which enabled the reaction to this action. And to limit the reaction to only one for a specific action and environment. If this is achieved then the growing takes on an intelligent level. If intelligence is described as the following of a specific reaction to a specific action in an specific environment then nature is reaching an always higher and higher intelligent level through the growing of forms.
When matter grows into a specific form after a specific action has taken place in an specific environment then this form is the prove that this action has taken place and is a memory of what has happened because it is only able to perform it again when the specific action takes place in the same specific circumstances.
On the other hand, if a certain action is performed on an unknown form in controlled circumstances then the reaction it makes can reveal its build up.
Harmony
The possibility that if two matters collide that they can change each others energy states. If they are not in harmony then their movement (energies) will move them apart (applies pressure) before an energy exchange can happen.
To make an exchange possible the properties of the movements of the matters need to be in a special relationship to each other. Like the frequency, phase and direction have to be either the same or a specific fraction of each other, like the harmonic tunes of music.
Inertia
The property of mass which transfers movement into energy. And is the only force available at the start.
Matter
We are not able yet to know how small the smallest part of matter is. We can only imagine that, by using our fantasy, we can get to it by cutting matter in half, and keep cutting it, until we cannot cut it any more or realistically it cannot be any smaller. This size is so small, even for us, that we have not been able to place a distance on it. But we can see that any matter is always build out of smaller matter. What is important is that no matter how big matter can grow, the system, which holds it together, has to be the same. But what we need to assume is that there is always enough matter to build anything as big as we can see. For this we cannot assume that the space, we are looking at, is empty because we do not have the ability to 'feel' or 'see' anything in this space. And, if we want to keep to the facts, then anything what is able to move through this space, but is not matter, needs to have a media (matter) which enables its movement - passing it on.
What we know about matter is that it has mass, which places it alone in the space it occupies and this mass has inertia. Which means that mass always strives to keep its state of movement. Which is also the reason for the phenomenon which we call energy. When matter moves then, if it collides with another matter, applies pressure on this matter and, by moving it away, does some work. Since the part which was moved is also matter, its inertia had to be overcome and some of the energy which was applied by the moving matter has now been passed to the now also moving matter. Moving matter claims more space then stationary one. E.g. it pushes other matter out of this space. And when energy is passed on from matter to matter by creating movement and vice versa then the space, which is claimed by the more and more moving matter, increases. When this happens within a restricted space, which is filled with masses, then it creates an overall pressure.
Memory
Since every mass/shape has its own set of reactions, which it can perform to a specific suitable action performed onto it, it can be said that it has now its own memory of what kind of reaction it can perform and when. It is also like a very low level of intelligence which reacts to certain actions with a pre-learned set of reactions. At the start stage only urmasses constitute the environment and each has the same memory. But as the masses evolve (grow, change) so does the memory, intelligence and the environment.
One form of memorizing is provided when ring forms or EMs are connected in sequence. This would make it possible to find them, compare or duplicate them or even change them through applied energy signatures. Which can be grown or adapted to its environment until the feedback agrees. Again, there is no goal involved in this and is happening by pure chance!
Movement
The physical explanation of energy.
As the first principle tells us, we need movement otherwise nothing will happen. We cannot know how this movement started but, since it is not relevant to explain what happens through movement, there is no need to guess or assume how movement started.
When matters are moving then the movement can take on different forms (frequency, direction, strength, phase) which would depend on the environment. This movement it translated through inertia to a specific energy (charge).
Opposition
If movements are in harmony (frequency, direction and phase match) then an exchange of energy can take place. Opposition referrers only to the value of the properties of the movement. If the direction or timing is the same (or within limits) but the value is different (+/-) then an adjustment will happen.
In a natural change the change always tries to establish the opposite of what has been. It could also be stated that if energies (movements) are able to change each other then they must be opposites. If the oppositions are equal in every property then the energies (movements) cancel each other to 0.
Passing of energy
The physical passing of energy through mass is the only realistic way energy can be used to provoke changes and growing when nothing else is present than smallest possible masses.
Physical properties
Size, mass, speed of mass, direction of the movement, frequency of the movement, duration and phase of movement
Pressure
Pressure is the effect which colliding masses have on each other if they cannot bind with each other after the collision. They force each other apart according to their physical properties and available empty space. This enlarges the space masses occupy under pressure.
Pressure, which is explained by movement (energy), has many effects in the universe. The most important is that it brings matter together through movement which would otherwise never have met. With other words it keeps the evolution going by keeping up the movement.
Radiation
When a matter, charged with energy (movement), travels through an empty space and collides with another matter than it might pass on this charge to the other matter. In this case the energy was not passed on from matter to matter to traverse the distance but had only one carrier.
Relevant Action
An action which performs something related to what raised a question and therefore reveals the answer.
Reproduction
If an energy is send though an energy ring into an EM and then collected on another one, then there is the possibility, that a sequence of energies was overloaded onto the passing energy by the energy rings through which it was passed on. Which could be used as a blueprint to reproduce the same build up of rings.
Vacuum
A vacuum, termed as a space without pressure (or void of moving matter), is produced by a moving matter which leaves an empty space behind every time it moves into a vacant space.
