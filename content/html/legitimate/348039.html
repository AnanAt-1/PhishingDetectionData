Notice on Recruitment Scams

We have been made aware of recent internet scams involving individuals falsely stating that they are recruiting for positions within The Walt Disney Company asking for personal information and/or money as part of our interview process. Our recruiters will only contact you from an official company email address including: @disney.com, @espn.com, @lucasfilm.com, @ilm.com, @pixar.com or @abc.com. They will not request funds or payment in any form or personal information such as photos of government-issued identification. If you receive a suspicious email, we advise you to mark the email as junk or spam so that your email provider will block them.
