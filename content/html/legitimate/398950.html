THE THUNDERBIRDS WENT UP TO UKIAH AND DEFEATED THE MENDOCINO COLLEGE EAGLES 78-57

THE THUNDERBIRDS DEFEAT THE NAPA VALLEY COLLEGE STORM 81-70

The THUNDERBIRDS played a solid game for 40 minutes and defeated the Napa Valley College Storm 81-70. The THUNDERBIRDS led at halftime 43-27. At one point the THUNDERBIRDS had a 25 point lead but in the last 4 minutes the THUNDERBIRDS were pressed and Napa closed the game to 11.

THE THUNDERBIRDS WERE LED BY:

(SOPH) MARCUS GREEN 17 POINTS 10 REBOUNDS AND 3 ASSISTS

(SOPH) ANTHONY KNIGHT 13 POINTS 7 STEALS 6 REBOUNDS AND 5 ASSISTS

(FROSH) KEILAND CALLUM 10 POINTS AND 8 REBOUNDS

(SOPH) JALIL EPPENGER 10 POINTS
