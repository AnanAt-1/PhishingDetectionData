Webroot Essentials FAQ

What is Webroot Essentials?

Webroot Essentials is the new name for Webroot SecureAnywhere products. We changed the name to simplify our different packages and make it easier for you to find what you need.
Webroot Essentials is designed to offer a comprehensive protection package for one, three, or five devices. It includes antivirus, anti-malware, browser protection, anti-phishing, and password manager solutions.

What Webroot products are available?

Webroot comes in three main packages:

1. Webroot Essentials offers antivirus, anti-malware, browser protection, anti-phishing, and password manager for one, three, or five devices.

What happened to Webroot AntiVirus Basic, Webroot Internet Security Plus, Webroot Complete?

These products are now respectively replaced by Webroot Essentials for 1, 3, or 5 devices. Webroot Essentials includes an antivirus, anti-malware, browser protection, anti-phishing, and password manager.

Can I still renew my Webroot AntiVirus Basic, Webroot Internet Security Plus, Webroot Complete subscription?

Yes, you always have the option to renew your existing plans or you can choose to switch to Webroot Essentials, Webroot Premium, or Webroot Total protection.
