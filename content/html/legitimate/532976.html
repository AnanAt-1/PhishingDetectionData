The 18-hole "Swope Memorial" course at the Swope Memorial Golf Course facility in Kansas City, Missouri features 6274 yards of golf from the longest tees for a par of 72. The course rating is 70.9 and it has a slope rating of 128. The estimated green fees for 18 holes is $57. Designed by A.W. Tillinghast, the Swope Memorial golf course opened in 1921.

Estimated Green Fees
$57
These rates are an estimate of what you might expect to pay at Swope Memorial with a cart. Actual rates may vary.
Fees are typically lowest Monday through Thursday and during twilight (late afternoon and evenings). Expect to pay the highest rates on weekends.

Homer H. Reviewed June 02, 2011

One of the most beautiful courses I've played, and at the current prices, it is one of the best values out there. The holes are challenging but not punitive, there is a lot of elevation change, and each hole is different in its own way. It was in pristine condition in June 2011 and was a joy to play. I will definitely be back.
