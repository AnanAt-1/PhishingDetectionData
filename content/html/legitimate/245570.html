Realize Sustainable Industrialization and Environment

Industrial Process & Environment

HORIBA's measurement and analytical solutions contribute to the optimization of all industrial processes. We support our valuable customers facing challenges such as improvement of productivity and safety, saving energy and reduction of pollution. This is well connected to protection of environment health such as air and soil quality, climate change, radiation exposure and human health eventually. We address emerging issues such as CCUS, microplastics, and PFAS (gas monitoring), which are gaining attention worldwide. As a primary solution partner, we work with the industrial sector to optimize processes, governments to set standards, and academia to drive scientific and technological innovation for sustainable industrialization and environmental protection.
