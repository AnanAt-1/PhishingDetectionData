Your Family’s Eye Care Partner

We’re proud to be your family’s destination for all things eye care.

Our BH Eye Care Optometry team believes in creating an experience tailored to your unique needs and is dedicated to providing comprehensive eye care for the whole family.

We offer a wide range of services, from routine eye exams and contact lens fittings to specialized services like myopia management and keratoconus care, to help keep your vision sharp and healthy.

We use technology and a thoughtful approach to care for your family’s overall eye health. Contact us to schedule an appointment today.

Your Vision, Our Passion

As a family-owned practice, we prioritize personalized eye care and exceptional service. Our focus extends beyond the exam to ensure your overall satisfaction.

We offer a wide selection of eyewear to suit the style and preferences of every member of your family. Plus, our in-house finish lab can provide quick and convenient repairs and adjustments!

Our team is always excited to help you find the right frames and lenses to enhance your vision and complement your personality. Visit us to explore our optical boutique and schedule your next eye exam today.
