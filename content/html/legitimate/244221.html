Camp Lohikan is currently recruiting college students, teachers, coaches, medical professionals and other support personnel for over 400 summer camp staff positions available at Camp Lohikan, Lake Greeley Camp and Camp Cayuga. All 3 summer camps are top-rated, coed residential summer camps located in the Pocono Mountains of Pennsylvania. Click Here for more info.

If you are an outgoing, friendly individual; a strong positive role model for children; and enjoy working with kids - we strongly encourage you to complete an employment application!

Positions include: General Cabin Counselors, Activity Instructors for over 65 daily activities, Assistant Instructors, Activity Department Heads, Division Directors, Evening Program Staff, Special Events Staff, Tripping Staff, Medical Staff, Kitchen Staff, Maintenance Staff, Driving Staff and other support staff positions.

International Applicants:

If you're an international applicant (a non United States citizen), please note that you must have a valid working visa, valid green card, or valid J-1 visa to be employed by us. If you do not have one of the above we can accept your application and interview you (via Zoom), but you'll need to obtain the appropriate visa prio to arriving in camp. . The J-1 visa can be obtained through any of the following organizations: Wild Packs, InterExchange, Camp America, Camp Counselors USA, IENA, CIEE, Camp Staff USA, Americamp, Work Play USA and Camp Leaders.

Canadian Applicants:

We've had a number of staff members from Canada over the years and have made arrangements with the IENA Agency (iena.org) to provide the necessary documentation and make the process easy. Canadians can apply for camp employment through the normal application process. Once hired the IENA Agency will be contacted to obtain the necessary visa.

Notice to All Applicants:

Camp staff members work closely with children during the summer.  As part of the staff recruitment process we must make certain that the individuals we employ are acceptable role models.  We thoroughly check the applicant's references, perform criminal background checks and also perform motor vehicle record checks.  We also check the internet presence of potential employees (e.g., Facebook, etc.) and conduct an interview with each applicant.  In addition to the above, applicants who accept a position with our camp also agree to submit to random drug testing if requested during the summer. Click here for more information about our staff screening process.

If you are hired to work at Camp Lohikan you will be responsible for someone's most precious possession... their child!  You will have an opportunity to impact in a significantly positive way the growth and development of your campers.  You may be considered by some to be uncool in school but you'll be idolized by your campers in camp.  You'll be treated like a big brother or sister and considered an expert in all things.  We need to be very careful who we entrust with this awesome responsibility.  Are you up for it?  Are you prepared to put the needs of your campers ahead of your own?  Can you be a positive role model, 24 hours a day, 6-7 days per week for 9 weeks?  If the answer is yes, let us know.
