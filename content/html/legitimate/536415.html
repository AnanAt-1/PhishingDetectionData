The federal budget proposal just passed by the US House of Representatives includes $880 Billion in spending cuts, most of which are likely to come from Medicaid.

If we don't act now, Republicans could cut healthcare funding and institute work requirements for Medicaid that would place impossible obstacles in the way of receiving coverage. In a quickly-tanking economy, that would mean millions of people barely getting by as it is could lose their access to healthcare entirely.

Join Our Rapid Response Team!

Right now in the U.S., our access to healthcare is under continuous attacks from all sides!

The current administration is aggressively pursuing an extreme, dangerous healthcare agenda: making deep cuts to Medicaid; privatizing Medicare and the VA; denying care to immigrants; and limiting our ability to seek reproductive care, gender-affirming care, and even vaccines. Thes are huge fights, and the stakes are high, but we can't afford to lose.
