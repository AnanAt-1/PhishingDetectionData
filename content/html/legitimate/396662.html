    MaestroRage — September 25th, 2023

Hello everyone.


Long time, no chat. The vast majority of people who are reading this probably have no clue who I am, and that's perfectly okay. But for the few that care, or for future creators that care. I want to make something very clear.


Please do not feel you have to ask me permission to use anything I uploaded on my account. While the license under which they are published are clear enough I feel, there are still some respectable people who want to reach out and get my permission first.


Let me be clear. You, who are still creating, have my permission in full, to use my material however you see fit, in whatever you see fit. Scat pr0n? A movie about a homicidal caterpillar? Gram gram's birthday party? Doesn't matter. So long as you're not out here trying to scam people or detract from the world, you have my blessing.


(edit: This includes commercial purposes, just send me a link so I can see the end product and credit me so maybe another creator can also take advantage of these works)


Please do not feel like you cannot if I don't get back to you.


Newgrounds is a place so dear and important to me. And yet I rarely log in. To write about how much this site has shaped my life would be a monumental saga that nobody would care to read anyway. All you have to know is that this site, DEFINED me. My account is a frozen chapter, a snapshot of time when music meant everything to me. But I became a statistic. I couldn't make it. So I shifted to something else like so many others before, and after, me.


I will always support this site, and the creative energy that lives here.


If my tracks from eons ago can give somebody just one tiny spark. Inspires even just one minute of creation. It would bring this tired composer immense joy. So please. Take it. It's yours.


In case I do not post again. I love you all.
