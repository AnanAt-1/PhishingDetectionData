TI Calculator Programs

  • Between Curves Tools - Calculus graphing program.
  • Curves Tools - Calculus graphing program.
  • Defaults - Restores most default settings of the calculator.
  • Dice - Simulates rolling dice.
  • Matrix Reduction - Reduces a matrix to row eschelon form.
  • Fizz Buzz - The classic game of Fizz Buzz.
  • FOIL - Algebra - First Outside Inside Last, Binomial multiplication.
  • Luck - A game of luck.
  • Maze - A game, solve mazes on your calculator screen.
  • Memory - A game that tests your memory. Can you remember 10 digits?
  • Nice Window - Displays a graph in a nicely zoomed window.
  • Picture Display - Displays a picture stored in your calculator more easily.
  • Quadratic Equation Workshop - Solves all sorts of quadratic equations.
  • Roman Numeral Conversion - Converts decimal numbers into Roman Numerals.
  • Root - Finds the roots of an number.
  • Stoned - A game of falling rocks.
  • Temperature Conversion - Converts units of temperature.
  • Tic-Tac-Toe - The classic two player game.
  • Triangle Information - Geometry - Given three dimensions of a triangle, compute the others.
  • Twenty-One - The card game.
