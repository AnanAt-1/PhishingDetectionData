NJGUNStat

The comprehensive monthly report breaks down the number of guns recovered by county and city and additionally lists the type and caliber of gun, number of individuals arrested with more than one crime gun, total crime guns recovered, and a breakdown of shooting victims. The report will also identify the “Source States” of crime guns. The NJGUNStat Quarterly report lists the top 15 “Source States” from where these guns originate.

OPERATION RISE
(Recovery Initiative Support & Engagement)

A proactive comprehensive and collaborative approach. "OPERATION RISE" facilitates recovery support services to individuals and their families in communities served by the New Jersey State Police.

  • Provide linkage to care for both individuals and the families impacted by substance use disorder
  • Reduce recidivism rates of crime associated with drug use
  • Reduce stigma associated with the disease of addiction
  • Reduce stigma associated with law enforcement's role
When treatment and support is what is needed most call Operation RISE
