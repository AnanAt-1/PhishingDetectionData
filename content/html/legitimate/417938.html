Cliff Arquette

Stars

Comedian
Born Dec. 28, 1905 in Philadelphia, PA
Died Sept. 23, 1974 of heart attack in Burbank, Calif.

Cliff Arquette was better known to millions of television viewers as "Charley Weaver." The veteran comedian also was a panelist on "Hollywood Squares" from its inception in 1966 until his death eight years later.

— Kathy Burke in the Los Angeles Times Sept. 24, 1974
