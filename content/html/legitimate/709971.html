Fire Retardant Auto Racing Shoes


Auto Racing Shoes are purpose built with flame retardant materials for protection and thin, grip enhanced soles to give the driver optimum pedal feel. The auto racing shoe selection at RaceDay Safety includes Alpinestars Racing Shoes, Allstar Performance Racing Shoes, Crow Racing Shoes, G-Force Racing Shoes, Impact Racing Shoes, K1 Race Gear Racing Shoes, RaceQuip Racing Shoes, and Zamp Racing Shoes. All are SFI 3.3/5 rated, except for the Zamp Drag Racing Shoes, which carry an SFI 3.3/20 certification.
We also carry Kids Racing Shoes and Zamp's Wide Width Racing Shoe, the ZR-50 in Black.

Auto racing shoes are constructed and labeled in men's sizes. To convert sizing for women's auto racing shoes, subtract 1 and a 1/2 sizes. For example, a girl with a woman's shoe size of 7.5 would typically wear a men's or youth size 6.

We carry SFI certified Racing Shoes for adults and kids from Alpinestars, Crow, Impact, K1 RaceGear, RaceQuip, and Zamp. Racing shoes are available in mid top and high top styles as well as half and full sizes. We also stock the hard to find wide width racing shoes and larger racing shoe sizes 15, 16, 18 and 20 and Little Kids sizes 6, 8, 10 and 12.

Racing Shoes are designed with a thinner sole for better pedal feel. The thinner soles on race shoes are less durable than regular athletic shoes therefore to make your racing shoes last longer we recommend only wearing your race shoes in the car to drive not walking around the pits or while working on your car.
