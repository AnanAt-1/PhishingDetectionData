By Jim Porter, modified by anetode

This exciting festival is world-renowned and it’s divided into three distinct phases, which are interactive, film and music. Based in Austin, Texas, the SXSW (South by Southwest) festival will run from March 13 to March 22nd, 2015. The first phase, interactive, is held for the first four days. The second phase, film, runs for almost the entire duration of the festival (minus the last day). The final phase, music, runs for the last 5 days of the festival.

Some of the world’s “best and brightest” people will show off their talent and ingenuity at this popular and hip festival! The musical portion always features some of the better (albeit lesser known) musical acts of the year. The film segment will feature a range of entries for best short film, music video and feature film. The interactive portion will feature a host of speakers who have proven themselves as vanguards in the milieus of music, technology and entrepreneurship.
