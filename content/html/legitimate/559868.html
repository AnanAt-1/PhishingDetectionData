Golf Instruction in the Greater Toronto Area


Liam O'Neil is a CPGA Class "A" Professional in the Greater Toronto Area.

Over the past 18 years, Liam has been an active member of the golf community as a club professional at many esteemed GTA golf courses, a golf instructor and a participant in CPGA sanctioned Professional and Pro-AM tournaments.

Since 2005, Liam has been the Associate Professional at Markland Wood Golf Club in Etobicoke, Ontario. His goal is to service the membership to the highest of standards with a focus on golf instruction, junior player mentorship and club fitting.

Prior to joining Markland Wood Golf Club, Liam contributed to the successful golf operations of such prestigous clubs as York Downs Golf and Country Club, Lionhead Golf and Country Club and Royal Ontario Golf Club.
