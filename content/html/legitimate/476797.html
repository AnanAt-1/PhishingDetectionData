About

As the Owner and Principal Broker of Solution Partners NW it is my job to support the sales team while ensuring that processes required for a successful transaction are met to our heightened ethical and business standards.

Residential real estate is one area of our business. We also consult builders & developers in land acquisition, feature selections, staging, marketing and sales. Solution Partners NW has sold homes for national and local builders successfully closing out communities and providing outstanding service to the builder and the buyers. Thousands of successful home closings across the region gives us pride when we know we have helped so many families make their home ownership dreams come true.

I personally sell multi-millions in real estate annually. The greatest honor is working with referrals and repeat clients who entrust their largest investment to my caring and competent team.
