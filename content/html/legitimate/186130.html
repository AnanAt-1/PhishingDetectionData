Meaning and definition of cladistics :

 

A taxonomic approach that classifies organisms according to the order in time at which branches arise along a phylogenetic tree, without considering the degree of morphological divergence.

 

For the term cladistics may also exist other definitions and meanings, the meaning and definition indicated above are indicative not be used for medical and legal or special purposes.
