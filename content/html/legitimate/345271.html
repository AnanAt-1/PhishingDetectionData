The O'Jays - Stairway To Heaven




The O'Jays - Stairway To Heaven

The O'Jays put their entire souls in this song................

A classic of great masters with voices such a beautiful song indeed.

I remember this song when life was so so so sweet..when my grandma and grandpa would dance while smiling at each other...Lawd only knows what he was whispering to her..I remember the po-ke-no games, the collard greens, fried chicken, and potato salad I remember grandpa loving his seagrams v.o and ginger ale..I remember Mr and Mrs. House, Wooten, Noble and Burton, (that's when folks believed in getting married & kids stayed out of grown-up conversations) they would all gather at my grand ma & pa's for that Saturday night party..Life was good and songs like this take you back to those memories with a smile on your face and a song in your heart...way to go O'Jay's !!!! :)



Xavier Dunson
