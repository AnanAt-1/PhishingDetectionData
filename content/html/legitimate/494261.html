OneCalifornia Bank buys ShoreBank Pacific

By The Columbian
Published: August 24, 2010, 12:00am
Share:

OneCalifornia Bank has signed a stock purchase agreement for the acquisition of ShoreBank Corp.’s Pacific Northwest subsidiary, ShoreBank Pacific, based in Ilwaco.

The purchase, subject to regulatory approval, will result in an institution with combined assets of approximately $300 million. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed.

Oakland, Calif.-based OneCalifornia Bank’s history of providing affordable financial services to low-wealth communities compliments ShoreBank Pacific’s 13-year focus on financing environmentally sustainable businesses, Harry Haigood, chairman and chief executive officer of OneCalifornia Bank, said.

ShoreBank Pacific operates a Portland office that has provided financing to companies such as Bi-O-Kleen Industries Inc., a green cleaning products company in Vancouver.
