Welcome to our Health Service

Over the years, Nhulundu Health Service has evolved to be one of the most proactive and largest Indigenous community organisations in regional Queensland and proudly remains answerable to the community and our clients. Our job is to not only ensure the health and wellbeing of our people ‚Äì but to reinstate confidence and improvement person by person, family by family and onto generations to come.

Nhulundu Health Service is the leader in Indigenous health and social support in our regional city.¬† With a bright future, we intend to help sustain a legacy for our people and the community as a collective.

It is our hope that we enable and empower Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people to improve their own health through awareness, education and example.

It is our aim to ensure we have the right professional staff to assist with any problems that might arise and that we make a positive difference to the lives of our clients and their families.

Excellence in health care

Nhulundu Health Service is an Aboriginal Community Controlled Health Service delivering integrated, comprehensive primary care health services to Gladstone and surrounding communities. Our team of clinical professionals strive for excellence in healthcare and are proud partner with James Cook University with their GP Training Program.

Our Mission

To provide culturally appropriate, comprehensive primary health care that is of the highest standard and is directed towards ‚ÄòClosing the Gap‚Äô in Indigenous health.
