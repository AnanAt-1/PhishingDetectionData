The Interactivity Foundation (IF) is a not-for-profit organization that promotes democratic discussion through the development of discussion materials, support for community discussions, and advancement of education initiatives. We help to advance thoughtful discussion about policy possibilities on issues that press the national and global agenda. This account is no longer maintained.
