Welcome

We Sell CD's

Welcome to Glad Music Company and our online store. Thanks for stopping in and please enjoy browsing our music catalog. We sell music CDs only from this website.

Should you not be able to find a particular CD due to a discontinuation or unavailability of that item, please search any of the music streaming services such as Apple Music, Spotify, Amazon Music, Pandora, You Tube Music and other streaming and downloading music services. We offer a variety of musical genres, mostly originating from Texas, but enjoyed the world over since our creation in 1931, in Houston, Texas by our founder H.W. “Pappy” Daily. Glad Music has been a big part of Texas music history for 93 years!
