    Welcome To New Haven Pet Hospital!

    PROUDLY SERVING THE PETS AND PET OWNERS IN THE COMMUNITY OF NEW HAVEN, IN.

    New Haven Pet Hospital offers comprehensive veterinary services for dogs, cats, and exotics (small mammals, reptiles, and birds). Your pet is an important member of your family. Our experienced and dedicated veterinary team strives to provide the highest quality veterinary care possible. Our wellness care services focus on safety and disease prevention and include exams, vaccinations, microchipping, Puppy Start-Right Pre-school, boarding, grooming, and more.

    We Treat Your Pets Like the Valued Family Members They Are

    At New Haven Pet Hospital, we strive to provide our patients, clients and the New Haven community with quality veterinary medicine, surgery, and dentistry in an affordable, hometown atmosphere.

    We look forward to seeing you and your pet, and strive to create a warm, comfortable, low stress, caring atmosphere of health and wellness.
