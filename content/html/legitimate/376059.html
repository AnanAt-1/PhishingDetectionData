Greyhound is actually the cheapest but there's a trick, you have to book from Toronto to Montreal, then just get off in Ottawa.

Bus service is controlled by the gov't so Greyhound controls TO to Ott, but another company controls TO to MTL, so each charges high prices for the routes they have no competition in. To get around the TO to MTL limitations Greyhound has you change buses in Ottawa and counts it as two trips, and offers a lower price to compete with the other company.

If you book 7 days in advance the cost is $29 plus tax, no need to be a student or anything. I did this a couple months ago, you can book through greyhound.ca/home
