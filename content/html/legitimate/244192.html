Lucas Group was acquired by Korn Ferry in 2021. Lucas Group is a leading professional search and contract staffing firm. Key areas of focus span specializations such as accounting & finance, human resources, information technology, legal, military transition, sales & marketing, and supply chain & operations. Lucas Group also serves a diverse set of markets, including consumer, healthcare, legal, manufacturing, oil and gas and technology.

Lucas Group, a leading professional search and contract staffing firm, enhancing Korn Ferry’s already industry-leading search portfolio but offering areas of additional specialization.

Why Korn Ferry acquired Lucas Group

As organizations face the reality of the future of work, they might not have the right executives to lead their business—executives who have the right skillset and mindset to perform now and transform their businesses for the future. The broader professional search and contract staffing market is a fragmented, multi-billion-dollar global opportunity.

The addition of Lucas Group to Korn Ferry’s search portfolio—spanning Executive Search, Recruitment Process Outsourcing, and Professional Search—is expected to accelerate Korn Ferry’s ability to capture an additional share of this significant market.
