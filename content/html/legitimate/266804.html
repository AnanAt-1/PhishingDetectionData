Department of Finance

Finance touches every industry. And we study every aspect.

 Here, we do more than prepare students to excel in the world of finance—our faculty take the field to new heights with revolutionary research and industry collaboration.

Online Master of Science in Finance

With an emphasis on advanced analytical and technical skills, the Online Master of Science Finance is designed to prepare you for competitive positions across dynamic industries. 

Explore the Online Master of Science in Finance

Master's in Finance

Intensive, immersive, and hands-on, the Master of Science in Finance helps you hone real-world professional skills at the top of your field. And our world-renowned faculty are there to guide you every step of the way.

Explore the Master of Science in Finance

Undergraduate Minor in Finance

The finance minor is a customized 18-unit program available to non-business (BSBA) majors. The curriculum is designed with two prerequisite courses to be taken during the academic year, with four core courses to be taken during the summer.

Academic Programs

Undergraduate Program

Our rigorous, four-year finance program propels you to the top of your field. Graduates go on to work for top firms, including Goldman Sachs, Intel, JPMorgan Chase and Vanguard.

Explore Finance Undergraduate Programs »

Master's Program

Hone your advanced analytical and technical skills in our nationally recognized, STEM-designated Master of Science in Finance that offers two areas of specialization: corporate finance and investment management (including prep for the CFA and CAIA exams).

Explore the Master's in Finance »

Online Master's Program

As an Online Master of Science in Finance student, you will gain applied expertise in current financial topics, projects, and methods. Our students conduct in-depth finance research and work alongside faculty researchers studying everything from mergers and acquisitions to asset management.

Explore the Online Master of Science in Finance

Doctoral Program

The doctoral program in finance is designed to prepare you for a career as a top university professor and leading researcher in corporate finance, investments and financial institutions.

Explore the Finance PhD »

Wall Street Scholars Program
