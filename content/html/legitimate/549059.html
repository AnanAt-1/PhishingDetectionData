Saint Jean de la Lande Cemetery
Saint Jean de la Lande, Temiscouata, Quebec

Lat: 68° 41' 00"N, Lon: 47° 26' 00"W

Contributed by Charles Gravel, Jul 21, 2010, last edited Dec 21, 2010 [charles_gravel@hotmail.com]. Total records = 167.

To get to Saint Jean de la Lande, take Transcanada highway 185E and exit at Degelis. Then, follow Saint Jean Road for about 20 km. In the village of Saint Jean de la Lande, drive on Principal Road for about 1km. The cemetery is located at the cross roads of Principal and Lake Baker Road.

The village of Saint Jean de la Lande is located east of Temiscouata county, close to the New Brunswick border. It was fonded by farmers in the early 1930s. This catholic cemetery is under the supervision of the common vestry.

I walked and read this cemetery and completed compiling this transcription in May 2010.

- Charles Gravel
