What Are the Best Things to Do In Downtown Kansas City, MO?

Kansas City is known for its barbecue. Book a tour with KC Barbecue Tours so you can sample some of the best barbecue restaurants that Kansas City has. Be sure to bring your appetite. If you enjoy hand-crafted spirits, such as bourbon and gin, stop by Tom's Town and take a tour of the distillery. You can learn about their distillation process and sample Tom Town's spirits. There are over 200 fountains in Kansas City. Take a tour of the city on a double-decker bus to see some of the famous fountains and sculptures and to learn about the history of the city.

Take a break from sightseeing and eating barbecue and head over to the SEA LIFE Kansas City Aquarium, where you can view thousands of tropical fish. To see a variety of top-name performers live, check out the Sprint Center's schedule. They have performances year-round, so check often and book your room early when a big show is in town.

On a nice day, explore some of the little parks hidden around the downtown area, such as Case Park, Berkeley Riverfront Park, or Columbus Park. Each park has something different to offer, so schedule time to visit them all. There is so much to see and do in Kansas City that you'll want to stay for as long as you can, and you'll find just the right hotel room to make your visit comfortable and enjoyable.
