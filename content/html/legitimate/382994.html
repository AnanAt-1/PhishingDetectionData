Gordon Korman ~ September 21

Gordon Korman at Hicklebee's!

September 21st ~ 3:00 (presentation at 3:30)

Framed is his new book about Griffin Bing (The Man with the Plan) and friends.

When he was 12 years old, Gordon Korman wrote his first book, This Can't be Happening at Macdonald Hall for a coach who suddenly found himself teaching 7th grade English. He took that experience and created a book out of it, too - Sixth Grade Nickname Game -  in which Mr. Huge was based on that 7th grade teacher.

Gordon Korman currently lives on Long Island with his wife and three children. 

Date: Tue, 9/21/2010
Time: 3:00pm - 4:30pm
