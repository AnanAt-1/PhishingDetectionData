Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, Argentina
DOB: 24 June, 1987
Age: 35
Place of Birth: Rosario, Argentina
Citizenship: Argentina, Spain
Height: 1.70 m
Position: RW (Right Winger)
Current Team: Argentina , PSG (Paris Saint-Germain)
Caps: Argentina - 165 , PSG - 51
Goals: 91 Goals for Argentina, 23 Goals for PSG
Joined: Argentina: 17 August, 2005 , PSG: 10 August, 2021
Contract expires: PSG: 30 June, 2023
Outfitter: Adidas
Social-Media: Facebook, Instagram
Market Value: Current Market Value: 4.17 Bn (In Rupees)
TRANSFER HISTORY: 6.67 Bn (In Rupees) to PSG
Achievements: 7-Times WINNER BALLON D'OR, 3-Times UEFA BEST PLAYER IN EUROPE, 2-Times THE BEST FIFA MEN'S PLAYER, 22-Times TOP GOAL SCORER

Possibly the best football player of this generation, will be eager to win the world cup for the first time in his career. He is the prolific goalscorer and creative playmaker, Messi holds the records for most goals in La Liga (474), most goals in a La Liga and European league season (50), most hat-tricks in La Liga (36) and the UEFA Champions League (8), and most assists in La Liga (192), a La Liga season (21) and the Copa América (17). Messi started playing football as a boy and in 1995 joined the youth team of Newell’s Old Boys (a Rosario-based top-division football club). Messi’s phenomenal skills gained the attention of prestigious clubs on both sides of the Atlantic. At age 13 Messi and his family relocated to Barcelona, and he began playing for FC Barcelona’s under-14 team. He scored 21 goals in 14 games for the junior team, and he quickly graduated through the higher-level teams until at age 16 he was given his informal debut with FC Barcelona in a friendly match. After that, There was no looking back for this prodigious talent which even he wouldn’t have believed what he would be in the coming future. At the international level, Lionel Messi chose to represent homeland Argentina, rather than Spain, despite his dual citizenship. He made his debut with Argentina’s senior team in 2004 and has been the squad’s captain since 2011. For his national team, He has scored 91 Goals and Capped 165 times.
