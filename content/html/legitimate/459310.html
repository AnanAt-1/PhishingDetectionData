Benefits of Abraham Lincoln University

AFFORDABLE

We pride ourselves in keeping our costs down and programs accessible. Students can stay employed full-time while earning their degree. Federal financial aid is available for the Juris Doctor, Bachelor of Science in Legal Studies, Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice and Master of Science in Law degree programs for those who qualify. We strive to make higher education accessible to a diverse and non-traditional student body. Click here to view our tuition rates.

FLEXIBLE

Study from anywhere, anytime. Students can view and review lectures as many times as needed, based on their unique schedules.ALU strives to provide its university and online law school students with the flexibility they need to study and keep their jobs. Videos are archived and can be accessed at any time.

INNOVATIVE

We strive to teach real-world skills that meet students’ academic and personal-development needs, using engaging and straightforward learning technology designed to fit any schedule or lifestyle. You can achieve your educational goals online at ALU.

SUPPORTIVE

We support our students with outstanding administrative, academic, and technical services. Our online modules are easy to learn and fun to use, and we’re always here to help. Our professors are accessible and actively engaged with students.
