Mike Grant Decals range of water-slide decals have excellent registration, colour density and thin carrier film, and come with full colour placement/painting instructions where applicable. All of our decals are screen-printed and respond well to mild decal solvents such as MicroSol/MicroSet.

Shipping

Mike Grant Decals are based in Calgary, Canada but will ship anywhere in the world. For up to 10 sets, a flat shipping fee of $7.50 will be charged to orders within Canada and the U.S.A., while international orders will be charged $9.50. The decals are shipped with a cardboard stiffener for extra protection.

Payment

All prices on the site are in Canadian Dollars (CAD). The PayPal payment process will automatically handle any currency conversion, and will allow you to use your credit card even if you don’t have a PayPal account. Although we endeavour to process orders as quickly as possible, please allow 28 days for delivery.

Custom decals

Please note we no longer offer a custom decal service.
