Study Computer Science

The Computer Science Department is based in the brand new Computational Foundry, which opened in 2018. This £32.5M world-class computational science facility provides the most up-to-date, high-quality teaching and research facilities, as well as networking and inspiration space for students, staff and industrial partners.

Most of our undergraduate courses have study abroad or year in industry options and our employability team will help you find and prepare for your placement in industry.

The research interests of the department include Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Human-Computer Interaction, Robotics, and Visual Computing.
