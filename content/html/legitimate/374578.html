What are FIRMSconsulting.com and StrategyTraining.com?

StrategyTraining.com is the world’s largest strategy, operations, implementation and leadership video/slides/podcast/books service all prepared by former McKinsey, BCG et al. senior partners and banking directors. Firmsconsulting.com is where we make our articles available and StrategyTraining.com and Strategy Training apps is where we have our audio/video memberships.

 

We have the world’s largest library of practical and detailed videos mapping out the rules, tools and approaches to solve strategy, operations, implementation, leadership, start-up etc. problems. For one monthly price you can watch thousands of episodes whenever you want, as much as you want, without any commercial breaks, all for members of our loyalty program.

 

The Strategy Control Room Advanced contains all our books (most of our books are no longer available anywhere else), analyses tools and methodologies, proposals etc. (A separate subscription is needed.)

 

We also offer private executive coaching to a select group of clients.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel your membership when you login into your account on StrategyTraining.com. There are no cancellation fees. You can start or stop your membership anytime.

Do you have a free trial?

We release a lot of free materials including hundreds of podcasts on iTunes (refer to Strategy Skills podcast and Case Interviews and Management Consulting podcast) and FIRMSconsulting YouTube channel, as well as hundreds of articles on Firmsconsulting.com. You can also find affordable books, Mavis and Turquoise Eyes, on Amazon. Those materials will give you a very good introduction to our training and how unique and powerful our resources are. From there you can make an informed decision if you want to join as a paid member to get access to our premium resources. 

06
How do I receive a 33% discount?

FIRMSconsulting Insider is our loyalty program and contains the most advanced programs. Like all loyalty programs, access, and continued access, must be earned. Members have two options to earn FIRMSconsulting Insider status.

 

Option A: Join Premium monthly membership on StrategyTraining.com and earn FIRMSconsulting Insider status after 6 months of consecutive membership (on your 7th renewal).

 

Option B: Join Premium and earn FIRMSconsulting Insider status immediately. Skip the 6-month waiting period by paying for the previous 6-months as if you were a member during that time. This is not a pre-payment and a monthly Premium subscription is required to remain as an FC Insider. To request this option, email [email protected].

 

Option C: Subscribe annually and immediately earn FIRMSconsulting Insider status.

 

Option A/B:

Members must pay for 6 consecutive months of Premium membership BEFORE becoming an Insider: 6 x $167 = $1,002.

 

Thereafter, 12 months of FC Insider access costs: 12 x $167 = $2,004.

 

Therefore, the total cost for 12 months of FC Insider access is: $1,002 + $2,004 = $3,006.

 

Option C:

Members skip the 6-month waiting period to earn FC Insider status.

 

Total cost for 12 months of FC Insider access = $2,004

Total savings = $3,006 – $2,004 = $1,002

Percentage savings   = $1,002 / $3,006 = 33.3%

 

If you like what you see, you can subscribe and cancel at any time.

 

Once you cancel, you will receive a cancelation confirmation email and your membership will not renew. You will continue to have access until the end of your last billing period. Please email [email protected] if you have any questions.

 

Members of our loyalty program will have to re-earn access to the programs if they cancel.
