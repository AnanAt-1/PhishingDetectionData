Mental Health Partners is now Clinica Family Health & Wellness

So many factors influence your health and well-being. That’s why "whole-person" or "integrated" healthcare matters. It ties together your physical, oral, and mental health. Clinica Family Health & Wellness is leading the way by treating you as a whole person and providing all the care you need to be healthy and well. Select the buttons below to learn more about our whole-person health services. Welcome to your new health care home!

WHO WE SERVE

Community-based care for every chapter of life

Whether you are looking for mental health care, addiction treatment, or wellness support for yourself or someone else, finding a qualified provider who is equipped to meet your individual needs is important. At Clinica Family Health & Wellness, we offer comprehensive programs and services for people of all ages. Our experts can help match you with the right care team and treatment plan to begin your wellness journey.

PATHWAY TO WELLNESS

Healing communities – one person and family at a time

We believe your mental health is just as important as your physical health. We believe every individual and family in our community should have access to quality, compassionate, and culturally responsive care – regardless of one’s ability to pay or documentation status. We believe we can achieve health equity by eliminating barriers to health and wellness care. This is our continued mission and vision.
