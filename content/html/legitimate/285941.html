Greywolf's 'Deadlands: Weird Wars' Page

Deadlands is a role-playing game set in an alternate reality that has been affected by the very real forces of the supernatural. In the 20th century, the "War to End All Wars" (which didn't) has rocked the Earth, but it hardly compares to the bizarre battles between the forces of good and evil that take place in the shadows.
