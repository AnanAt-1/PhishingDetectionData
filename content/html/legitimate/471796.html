Why American Top Team

Since opening our doors in 2010, American Top Team of Indianapolis has been dedicated to transforming lives one person at a time through mixed martial arts, education, and coaching. We strive to provide a safe, comfortable, and welcoming atmosphere for all students and family members. With 10 different classes available and our top-rated boxing program, we are proud to have been Golden Gloves champions for the last four years.

We believe that mixed martial arts and boxing can benefit everyone, regardless of age, gender, or experience level. Our programs not only teach the essential skills needed for self-defense, but our realistic and practical approach ensures that every member can achieve greatness and live their best life. We are open six days a week, committed to helping you develop, succeed, and reach your personal goals in a supportive environment.
