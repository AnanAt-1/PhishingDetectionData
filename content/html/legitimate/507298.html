Related Information for Gyroscope
There are two bands with the name Gyroscope.The first is a Pop-rock band from Perth, Western Australia formed in 1997 by Daniel Sanders (vocals, guitar), Zoran Trivic (guitar), Brad Campbell (bass), and Rob Nassif (drums).
