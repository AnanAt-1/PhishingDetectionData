FIRST ALERT: Severe Storms Possible Friday And Saturday

Large Hail Is The Primary Concern
Updated: 55 minutes ago

Friday and Saturday are now First Alert Weather Days in Central Texas. The potential for strong to severe thunderstorms will certainly be there, with all hazards possible - but the main concern being the potential for very large hail. Friday’s storms will remain more isolated along the dry line, but Saturday’s storms will increase in coverage a little more. Storm timing for Friday will be between 4 and 10 PM, with Saturday’s timing between 4 PM to midnight. Areas near and west of I-35 will need to stay weather aware, while the chances will decrease to the east throughout each day. You can find more details about the severe weather risk Friday and Saturday in our First Alert Weather Day article.
