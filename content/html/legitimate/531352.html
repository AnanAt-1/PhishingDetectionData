About the Viljoen surname

VILJOEN FAMILY WEBSITE

The website of the Viljoen family, descendants of the French Huguenot refugee Francois Villion and his wife Cornelia Campenaar (from Middelburg, the Netherlands), who married at the Cape of Good Hope in 1676 can be found at:
[http://www.hugenoot.org.za/Viljoen/]
