Cardinal numbers

To express amounts cardinal numbers are used as follows:

un hombre; una mujer
a man; a woman
dos hombres; dos mujeres
two men; two women
treinta y un días
thirty one days

cuarenta y una banderas
fourty one flags

When the number uno is used in plural, it means some or a couple:

unos hombres; unas mujeres
some men; some women

Ordinal numbers

To express an ordering, ordinal numbers are used als follows:

la primera vez
the first time
el quinto coche
the fifth car
