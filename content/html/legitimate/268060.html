Marin Java Users Group

MarinJava was a discussion group for users of Java in Marin County, California (just north of San Francisco). The group met in the early 2000s on the second Monday of each month to discuss all things Java. Meetings were free, and open to anyone with an interest in Java.

As far as I know, there haven’t been any meetings since around 2004.
