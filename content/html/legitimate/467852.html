    About This Property

    FOR A LIMITED TIME, RECEIVE ONE MONTH FREE WITH A 12-MONTH LEASE ON VACANT APARTMENT HOMES! RESTRICTIONS APPLY - CALL FOR DETAILS. The KC High Line is a modern, pet-friendly apartment community in Downtown Kansas City, MO, near The Power and Light District, I-35 and I-70. We offer studio, 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom apartments for rent with impressive features, including stainless steel appliances with gas ranges, quartz countertops and custom backsplash, custom European cabinets, luxury wood-style flooring, carpeting in bedrooms, remote control ceiling fans, energy-efficient LED lighting and more! Redesigned for those with a refined sensibility and an appreciation for value, The KC High Line surrounds you with mid-century modern detail updated to fit the needs of a new generation. Imagine all the benefits of urban living while residing in the premier historic district of downtown Kansas City. Residents have convenient, easy access to shopping, dining and entertainment at Crown Center, Kansas City River Market and Country Plaza Kansas City. We are close to major employers and schools such as Cerner Corporation, Children's Mercy Hospital, Hallmark Cards, H&R Block, DST Systems, Saint Luke's College of Health Sciences, the University of Missouri-Kansas City and the Kansas City Public Schools. The KC Streetcar connects to downtown Kansas City as well as I-70, I-35 and I-670, and the greater Kansas-Missouri area. Community amenities include a well-appointed fitness center with a virtual trainer, a resort-style pool with a surrounding sundeck and cabana and a resident lounge with complimentary Wi-Fi and a coffee bar with Starbucks. The KC High Line is situated on a 200-foot-high bluff overlooking the confluence of the Missouri and Kansas Rivers, creating spectacular views of both Downtown and the West Bottoms. Contact us today to see what's available! Or stop by: 929 Jefferson Street, Kansas City, MO 641
