    The Beach Club

    Welcome to The Beach Club. We're amongst the most characterful hotels in Penzance, and your 'home from home' in West Cornwall.

    Find us in historic Penzance. Like the town, we are crafted by the coast and the generations of creatives it has inspired. The Hotel walls are filled with works of original artists. We're nestled in the heart of the promenade, ten minutes from the centre.

    The Beach Club has soaring sea views of Mount's Bay. From the Lizard Peninsula to Newlyn Harbour and across to St Michael's Mount. Most of our rooms overlook the sea, infused with its unique light.

    On Cornwall's southwesterly limb there is no inland. Every country lane leads seaward. Cornwall's wild west is soul-stirring. Where rugged cliffs drop into the blue-green glow, and vast skies unfurl like sails.

    The Magic of Cornwall

    Explore Cornwall

    The Land's End peninsula is an enchanting place. Where the Atlantic ocean meets the English channel, you'll find miles of picturesque coastline, with hidden coves and tropical sandy beaches aplenty. Browse the world famous art galleries and visit ancient sites. Swim in the open sea and in the open air Jubilee Pool, or embark on a boat trip.
