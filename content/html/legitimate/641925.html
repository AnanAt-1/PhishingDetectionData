Kerr Research Group - Marine Natural Product Lab

 
 
The Marine Natural Products Group at UPEI is engaged in a range of projects broadly directed at the discovery of new natural products from our marine microbial library. Projects range from: evaluation of microbial diversity of unique marine habitats to the development of fermentation and molecular methods to access cryptic natural product biosynthetic pathways. The lab enjoys productive collaborations with both local and foreign industry partners as well as a number of academic and government research groups.
