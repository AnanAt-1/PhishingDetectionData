There is a variety of locks that railroads used to secure railroad property. The keys for these locks were carefully guarded and were carried at all times while on the job. Locks and Keys from yesterday's railroads are prized by collectors and some of the hard-to-find ones command a substantial value. Here's your chance to own a piece of Railroad History.
