Staff are in the office periodically (M, W or F), so please call our office at

Welcome to the homepage of the Chinese Presbyterian Church of Oakland. We are a bi-cultural church of English and Cantonese (and some Mandarin) speakers located in Oakland's historic Chinatown, serving our community in the San Francisco East Bay Area.

If you are seeking a church home, we hope you will consider the Chinese Presbyterian Church of
Oakland. We offer a traditional style worship service which includes hymns and liturgy.

Music: Chancel Choir, organ, traditional and contemporary hymns.
Attire: Sunday casual.

The Chinese Presbyterian Church of Oakland believes in traditional marriage between a man and a
woman, and any wedding to take place in our sanctuary needs to be Session approved.
