About

Jennifer Grant is a 59 year old American Actress. Born Jennifer Diane Grant on 26th February, 1966 in Saint Joseph Medical Center, Burbank, California, USA, she is famous for her roles as Susie Knight in the ABC crime comedy-drama series Moon Over Miami, Celeste Lundy in the Fox teen drama series Beverly Hills, 90210, Nina Bookbinder in the NBC sitcom Friends, Nicky Carter in the 1996 science fiction action thriller film Savage, Ellen in the 1996 comedy-drama film The Evening Star, and Jacey Wyatt in the WB sitcom Movie Stars in a career that spans 1993–present and 1993–present. Her zodiac sign is Pisces.

Jennifer Grant has been in 3 on-screen matchups, including George Newbern in The Evening Star (1996), Ian Ziering in Beverly Hills, 90210 (1990) and Paul Johansson in Beverly Hills, 90210 (1990).
