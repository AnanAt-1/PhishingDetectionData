For kids who love to dance!

Step into the enchanting world of Kindy Dance Time, where the magic of dance sparks joy and wonder in the hearts of toddlers and preschoolers! Since 2001, we've been dedicated to nurturing the love of dance in children aged 2-5, igniting imaginations and inspiring little feet to dance with delight. Our highly acclaimed and exclusive dance syllabus is guided by experienced teachers, fostering love and joy in every twirl. Join the enchantment and fun with over 40,000 toddlers and preschoolers nationwide who have discovered the joy of dance with Kindy Dance Time!
