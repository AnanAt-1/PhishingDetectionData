Erica Ehm’s survey on parents’ sex life + moms talk Valentine’s Day

by shirley on February 11, 2011

Tweet

Keeping it Yummy!

Yummy Mummy Club's Erica Ehm

Bringing the ‘yummy’ back into a parent’s sex life after having a child can be challenging. Just in time for Valentine’s Day, the sexiest and most romantic day of the year, Erica Ehm, founder of YummyMummyClub surveyed a thousand Yummy Mummies and Delicious Daddies to find out what’s really happening (or not) between the sheets of Canadian parents.

The survey tackled it all, comparing what both men and women have to say about orgasms, sexual preferences, location, time and self-esteem. No stone was left unturned. No question too risqué to ask.

Erica worked with a team of experts to analyze the data and is eager to share with you all of the findings, plus some practical and easy solutions to get the ‘yummy’ back into the bedrooms of parents all across Canada!

Following my chat with Erica, I’ll be joined by a panel of moms and bloggers, Kimberley Aitkin Salmons from Modish Mama and Jennifer Roberts-Hall from Jennifer’s Story. We’ll discuss their thoughts, expectations &  etiquette surrounding Valentine’s Day with their husbands and kids.

Tune in Saturday, Feb. 12 at 10am PST on CFAX 1070 and streaming live at cfax1070.com

Kimberly Aitken, Modish Mama
