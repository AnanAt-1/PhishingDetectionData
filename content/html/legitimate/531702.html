★★ DJ Phil - Silly, Professional, Unique Energetic Entertainment! ★★

Welcome to Get Up and Groove with me, DJ Phil. I have become One of Bournemouth, Poole & Dorsets Favourite Disco Entertainers, DJ and Host. Specialising in Children’s and Family Party Entertainment Covering Dorset & West Hampshire. I am a full time professional Kids DJ providing a reliable and modern disco show for children's birthday parties, pre-schools, nursery, school disco’s, presentations, fun days, corporate events, clubs, celebrations & more. So if you're looking for Party Ideas, Event Suggestions and Games let me take care of everything

I'm often told my children's parties are Funny, Witty, and really a bit very Silly. I add in some very Kid-Friendly Banter that enables me to grab the kids attention and ensure lots of laugh out loud moments. I always try to be engaging, interactive and i have a genuine connection with the children which I receive great reviews for. From the start I will take control and play games/competitions that children want to join in with.

I have been providing an up to date (not cheesy) disco service for over 15 years, and i have become one of the most popular entertainers in the area. Gaining lots of fantastic feedback and testimonials, which can be seen on my Testimonial Page and on my Facebook Page.
I have tried and tested methods plus the natural ability to create a brilliant, fun & memorable party that will keep children fully entertained. I will host the whole party and include fun games, competitions, dance routines, and much more.

Of course i'll also bring my great sound system (BOSE Pro for all you sound geeks) and plenty of lighting effects. The ever popular Bubble show and Snow effects machines which are very popular are always included as well.

I have also recently launched 'Get Up and Gameshow', a 'Fun Interactive Game Show' that can be added to any event. The show includes lots of TV based games (so they are very popular and recognisable) plus plenty of Completely Original family competitions. Much like a traditional Magic Show the audience will sit down and volunteers will be chosen to take part in challenges, mini game shows and comedy routines.

Children's Theme Disco Parties such as the UV Theme Party, or the VIP Theme Party. Check out the Theme page UV Theme Package. Plus popular themes such as Halloween and Christmas. I have the props, music, games and routines for each theme.
Disney Frozen Theme Birthday Parties also available at £15 extra. Click here for more info Theme Parties
