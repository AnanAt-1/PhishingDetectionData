Folks talk about Kansas City Barbecue, they're likely going to mention Arthur Bryant's. It's a Kansas City tradition, as good-tasting as ever.

My favorite Arthur Bryant's sandwich - Burnt brisket ends as shown above. One thing has changed. That sandwich, with the fries, used to come on a sheet of butcher paper. Kind of wish it still did.

The ribs are as good as they come. Topped with their original sauce, of course.
