Wedding Receptions

Get ready for some serious fun! Weve performed for hundreds of wedding receptions, and our experience will help you to plan the best party ever! We will work with your wedding planners, photographers, and caterers to make sure that everything runs smooth as silk. Wedding Ceremonies

When you share your wedding ceremony with your friends and family, everyone should be able to hear every word, but the sound equipment should also be out of the way and not interfere with your photography. We use wireless lapel mics for a truly professional look, and utilize battery powered units for those times when there is no electric outlet close by.

Photo Booths

Photo Booths are incredibly fun and entertaining for everyone, no matter what their age. Watch your guests grab their friends, add a few props, and smile for the fun photos that will last long after the party’s over. We use DSLR cameras to give you quality high resolution images, and all of your photos are uploaded to a custom made photo site that lets you and your guests explore and download all of the photos from your event.
