Welcome!

 

Welcome to the BUHS Class of 1964 web site. This site is intended to serve as a source of information about the class and its activities, and about our classmates. Please take time to occasionally visit this site, and to share your ideas with us. Ray Puffer will be serving as the webmaster.

 

45th Reunion.

 

 

 

From Rick Farnham, “I can’t speak for everyone but for reasons I hold dear, I never tire of re-acquainting with the friends and classmates of my youth!  The bond remains strong and even time and separation fail to diminish my enthusiasm, when embraced in the throngs of so many familiar faces!”

  Class of ’64 attendees at the Saturday night gala for our 45th reunion in 2009. How many classmates do you recognize?

Class Gift and Fundraising

 

Go to this page for information about our glass gift. The BUHS Class of 1964 Memorial Scholarship is given in memory of all those classmates whom we have lost far too early in life. It is intended to give a helping hand to graduating seniors who desire to further their education, but cannot afford to do so without financial assistance.
