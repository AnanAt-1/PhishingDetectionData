Saint Suzanne Cemetery
Stanhope, Stanstead County, Quebec

Lat: 45° 01'N, Lon: 71° 48'W

Contributed by Leslie Nutbrown, Jul 25, 2008, Last edited Aug 28, 2008 . Total records = 317.

From Coaticook follow route 147 south almost to the U.S. Border. Turn right onto Baldwin Barnston Road. Then take the first left which leads into the small village of Stanhope. You will see Sainte Suzanne church on your left. The cemetery is behind the church. Warning!! Do not continue past the church very far or you will go right into the United States and therefore must report to the nearest customs office.

This Catholic cemetery appears to have begun around 1878 as that is the date of the earliest stone. Some stones are hard to read but most are legible. One can drive down to the cemetery in back of the church. There is a fence and gate and walking in the cemetery is not difficult.

Susan Nutbrown and I walked and read this cemetery in July 2008 and made a complete recording of all legible tombstones.

- Leslie Nutbrown
