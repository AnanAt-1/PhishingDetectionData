Inspection Equipment

Bench-Top AOI System complements high-mix environments.

Vi TECHNOLOGY, Inc. Mar 21, 2006

  • Company Profile
  • Company News
  • Company Website

Share:

Press Release Summary:



Suited for high-mix, low-volume manufacturing environments, Vi-1K is powered by vectoral imaging and uses precision XY motion control. Bench-top AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) system can identify component presence/absence, position and polarity lifted leads, solder bridges and tombstones, and provide OCV. Offered with Vision 2006 Software Suite, product enables repairs to be completed without removing board from system.


Original Press Release:



ViTechnologyÂ® Unveils Bench-Top AOI System at APEX 2006



Brings Full-Featured Inspection to High-Mix Manufacturing

Anaheim, CA February 6, 2006 - ViTechnologyÂ®, a leading worldwide provider of advanced solutions to enhance the yield of electronics assembly, will introduce the Vi-1K bench-top inspection system to North America at APEX 2006, Feb 8 to 10, 2006.

The compact Vi-1K brings affordable, high-performance bench-top inspection to the high-mix, low-volume manufacturing environment found in North America. The system uses state-of-the-art precision XY motion control based on the company's in-line systems. The Vi-1K is the industry's first bench-top system to provide the same inspection capabilities as the most sophisticated in-line systems. The Vi-1K can identify component presence/absence, position and polarity lifted leads, solder bridges and tombstones, and provide OCV. Repairs can be completed without removing the board from the system. The Vi-1K is powered by Vectoral Imaging and comes fully equipped with the new Vision 2006 Software Suite, including Match Maker, VieWeb and Profilertm.

"The Vi-1K brings in-line-system technology down to an affordable bench-top solution," said Mark Norris, Executive Vice President Sales and Marketing. "Manufacturers of all sizes can now benefit from all of the speed, accuracy as well as state-of-the-art optics and imaging systems normally reserved for in-line products with the Vi-1K."

The system joins ViTechnologyÂ®'s family of AOI (Automated Optical Inspection) systems, which include the proven Vi-3K series and the high precision, award-wining Vi-5000 AOM (Automated Optical Measurement) system. ViTechnologyÂ® will exhibit its full range of AOI and AOM systems at APEX. All systems will operate using a variety of hardware and software configurations to demonstrate the line's versatility, adaptability and upgradeability.
