    2025 GTOA Registration - NOW OPEN!!

    ​

    Registration for the 2025 Football Season is now open!!!  For the 9th consecutive year, we are NOT increasing dues payments.  We've worked very hard to be as responsible with your dues as possible.  This year, even though some of our expenses have dramatically increased (such as our HUDL account), we are passing those additional costs onto our contracted schools since they are the ones that directly benefit from our association having access to game film.  2025 GTOA Dues will remain $45 per official through Friday, June 6, 2025.  After June 6th, dues will increase to $60 per official.  New officials (including "new to GTOA" officials) will only be charged the $45 early registration cost.

    ​

    This year's GTOA Camp will be held July 11-12 (Fri/Sat) at Broken Arrow High School.  The cost for this year's camp is $55 per official and the deadline for registration is Monday, July 7th.  Attendance at this year's camp will also count as a regular summer meeting credit (normally held on Monday nights).  Attendance will verified throughout the weekend.
