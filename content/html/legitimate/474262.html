The 1960 Kansas City Athletics played 154 games during the regular season, won 58 games, lost 96 games, and finished in eighth position. They played their home games at Municipal Stadium (Park Factors: 101/103) where 774,944 fans witnessed their 1960 Athletics finish the season with a .377 winning percentage.
