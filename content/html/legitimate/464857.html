American Timber and Steel


The increased demand of lumber and posts has directed our focus to investing in continued improvements to production of our treated round posts.  While there is still no clear end in sight of these increased demands, please know that we are addressing the situation as fairly and aggressively as possible for all parties involved as our customers are the heart and soul to what we do.

 

American Timber and Steel Corp. manufactures nearly all of the materials required for your outdoor timber projects. We care about providing you with the highest quality materials and trusted service. Our team has been helping Contractors, Engineers, Departments of Transportations, Fencers, Park and Recreation Departments, Marinas, Farmers and Home Owners all across the country since 1983.
