At Sion, we value and are committed to building a diverse and loving school community.

School Notes

Four points of excellence serve as a foundation for the Notre Dame de Sion experience:

ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE
  • At Notre Dame de Sion we challenge our students to achieve academic excellence through a 21st-century learning curriculum and personalized instruction that emphasize the skill sets necessary for success. Our renowned staff and cutting-edge academics challenge students to reach their full potential, inside and outside of the classroom.
GLOBAL CONNECTIONS
  • Notre Dame de Sion Schools of Kansas City is just one of 22 Sion schools around the world. Our global reach allows students to engage with Sion students from different backgrounds, languages, and cultures all around the world. Sion proudly opens doors for students to be truly immersed in an international community and to learn through the lens of an authentic global perspective. At the middle and high school levels, our students have the opportunity to travel nationally and internationally through Sion exchange and study abroad programs.
SPIRITUAL GROWTH
  • Sion is committed to the values of the Catholic Church through spiritual growth, interfaith inclusion, social justice, and service. We have an innate sense of caring for the world and mankind, and are committed to creating a world of peace, justice, and love. We pursue a moral and ethical life, developing within our students the self-assurance and strength needed to withstand life challenges.
TRADITION
  • Sion is built upon time-honored traditions that link our past, present, and future. Memories are made every day at Sion with each experience tying together the hearts and souls of our students. From middle school international excursions, 8th grade retreats, and Sion's unique class ring, to the beautiful white gowns worn at high school graduation, the pride and joy in Sion's traditions are steeped in a spirit that promotes unity, camaraderie, and lifelong friendship.

School Reviews

  5.00 out of 5  (2 reviews)
5  5/17/2024
We just completed our first year at Sion for my daughter (kindergarten) and son (preschool). I've been thrilled with the experience all around! Both kids are thriving there in all the ways socially, emotionally, and as young learners. I've gotten a chance to volunteer at the school quite a bit, so have witnessed the great environment that staff and teachers help to create and you get the sense that they all enjoy being there and feel supported in their work. School administration has been great -- very approachable, available, and actively solicits parent input. (Kudos to Principal Patricia Gray not only does she provide great leadership, I'm pretty sure she knows every student by name!) I love the school values and their emphasis on encouraging students to be good citizens of the world. As a non-religious family, one important focus of the school is interfaith dialogue; though its primary focus is around the Catholic faith, students learn about all the major world religions and the common values that underpin them all. I think this is so great because the student body is quite diverse, not only when it comes to religious background, but also around race/ethnicity, economic background, etc. The thing I've enjoyed most about the school is the community they strive to build across students and families. The school has an active Parent Volunteer organization and schedules all types of in-school and out-of-school activities to give families a chance to connect with each other and the school. We've been so pleased with our Sion experience thus far and look forward to many more years in the Sion community!
- Posted by Parent - Elizabeth S.
