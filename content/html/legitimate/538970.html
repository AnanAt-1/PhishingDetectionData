NHL AWARDS AND HONORS
Miscellaneous: Scored first two NHL goals in his hometown of Montreal during Vancouver's Feb. 9, 1984, game at Canadiens. ... Signed with Boston as an unrestricted free agent on July 1, 1988, but never played for parent team. ... Wore No. 19 in Boston's 1988 training camp. ... Traded by Boston to New Jersey in exchange for Dan Dorion on Dec. 9, 1988, but never played for parent team.
NON-NHL CAREER
Post-Draft Teams: Laval (QMJHL); Fredericton, Maine, Utica (AHL); Fort Wayne (IHL)
NON-NHL AWARDS AND HONORS
Miscellaneous: Played on Sorel (QMJHL) team that folded after 1980-81 season. He was claimed by Laval in the August 1981 dispersal draft of Sorel players. ... Played on line with Mario Lemieux for Laval in 1982-83. ... Missed part of 1989-90 season with pneumonia, forcing him to be hospitalized on Oct. 25, 1989. ... Played on Vancouver's off-season charity softball team.
Personal: Full name is Jean-Marc Lanthier Jr.
