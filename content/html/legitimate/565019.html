Music types: Country, Americana
Description:
Robert Sharp and the Dusty Roads Band were originally formed in 2008 by Robert Sharp and Dusty Corgill. After a year of shuffling through band members they finally met "Big" Will Hancock out of Bangs, TX. Although he had been playing with "Metal" bands over the years he fit right in and has been with them every since. Will introduced them to their drummer Sean Potter who also has a rock and “heavy metal” background. After going through a couple of lead guitarists Adam Muniz has finally settled in. He is a skilled blues guitarist and is experienced in other genres. He adds a unique as well as exciting sound. Robert Sharp and the Dusty Roads Band currently plays several venues around Midland and Odessa, TX. They enjoy what they do and are always looking for more venues. Go check them out every chance you get they put on a great show.


Band Members:
Robert Sharp- Lead Singer,Guitarist/ Dusty Corgill- Backup Vocals, Rythm Guitar, Harmonica/ "Big" Will Hancock- Bass Guitar/Backup Vocals/ Adam Muniz- Lead Guitar/ Sean Potter- Drums
