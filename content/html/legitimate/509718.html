ALEXIS GRAY-LAWSON basketball profile


Alexis Gray-Lawson is USA basketball player born on April 21 1987 in Oakland, CA. She is a 5'8'' point guard who most recently played at Berkeley Royals in the WPBA. Gray-Lawson graduated University of California in 2010. She was drafted by Washington Mystics (WNBA) in 2010 in the 3rd round (30 overall). Gray-Lawson is represented by agency. She has played in Israel, Turkey and USA.
