Is This Your Business?
 

This place is great when it's hot outside and it offers lots of benches to sit and have some fun on.
Crowd: Young guys, old guys, middle aged guys ...all sorts. Some just to hang out and others make it clear what they want.

Neighbourhood: NDG

Who's Coming

Young guys, old guys, middle aged guys ...all sorts. Some just to hang out and others make it clear what they want.

Hang a right off of CSL road and go up Rosedale. Some of the cars parked have guys already stroking or getting some. Hang another right on Chester and find parking and take a walk through the park or go directly to the bathrooms.
Cross street: Rosedale and Chester and Chester and Coronation
  • Crowd:Young guys, old guys, middle aged guys ...all sorts. Some just to hang out and others make it clear what they want.
  • Hours:After dark but the bathrooms close at 10pm
    Best times: Between 7-10pm for the washrooms and later for the cruising in the park.
  • Cruising Info/ Tips:Just be cool and make good eye contact.
    Cruisiest Spots: At the washrooms in the little pavillion and in and around the trees or guys sitting in their cars on Rosedale or Coronation.
  • Wheelchair Accessible:Yes
  • Warnings:The city workers come around at 10pm and sometimes before that to lock up the washrooms.
