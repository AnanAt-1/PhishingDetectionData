Located in Burbank, California, Bang Zoom! Studios combines professional expertise with a personalized approach. We handle a wide range of projects while maintaining individual attention to each client.

 

Our facilities incorporate multiple ADR recording stages, including spacious ensemble cast stages, a Foley stage, and Dolby Certified Mix Stages with Dolby Atmos capabilities.

 

Our audio services include sound design, mixing, voice-over production, localization, and asset delivery for streaming platforms. Our team consists of experienced professionals including award-winning engineers, mixers, and sound designers who bring skill and dedication to projects of all sizes.

Our Services

Audio Post

Our audio team includes a top-notch team of editors, recordists, and mixers who are dedicated to bringing your project to life. From capturing the perfect voiceover to mixing  a feature-length film, we will ensure that your project will sound amazing!

Voice Casting

Our acclaimed casting directors are ready to match the very best talent to the unique needs of your project. Top Hollywood celebrity talent and incredible fresh discoveries – from auditions to capturing mind-blowing memorable performances in the studio, we specialize in all things voice acting. Bang Zoom is well-respected by our clients and fans for cultivating exciting new talent!

Video Post

We offer comprehensive video post-production services including video editing, color grading, VFX, graphic creation, and more! From quick paint fixes to assembling the locked picture, our video team will give your story the attention it deserves.

Animation

Bang Zoom! Animation is a team of passionate and creative folks who will make your project come to life. We strive to innovate workflows and build pipelines that make animation what it should be — fun!

Mixing

Our Emmy and Golden Reel award-winning engineers have vast experience mixing an extraordinary list of top film and television projects. From the most popular network shows on TV to high-profile original animation, commercials, and innovative new media projects, our cutting-edge Dolby Certified and Dolby Atmos stages are world-class.

Dialogue Recording

Our directors and engineers have worked on thousands of projects, large and small. Combining 30+ years of industry experience with a drive for audio perfection, we’re the trusted choice for producers worldwide.

Visual Effects

Whether your shot needs enhanced atmospheric effects or a high octane explosion blowing up the frame, our team has the particle simulating, hand-painting, tracking, and compositing skills to make it happen in any style needed.

Color

Our rooms allow our colorists to accurately correct and grade any project to achieve the exact look and tone the story demands. From gritty realism to hyper-stylized, we can handle it all and deliver to industry standards.

SAG-AFTRA Signatory

We can guide your project from SAG-AFTRA setup to completion, handling the complexities of union agreements so you can focus on creativity. Our team will ensure your production meets all industry standards.

Sound Design

Our sound designers have created scenes ranging from imaginative childlike adventures to epic, large-scale battles. We love bringing a project to life by designing textured ambiances and organic sound effects and recording truly realistic Foley on our spacious Foley stage.

Video Game Audio

At Bang Zoom, gaming isn’t just our job—it’s our passion. With over 30 years in the industry and a portfolio boasting dozens of beloved titles, we’re committed to creating audio experiences that resonate with players. Our comprehensive services spanning casting, recording, sound design, and beyond will ensure that every aspect of your game’s audio hits the mark.

Editorial

From animatics to final picture, trailers, title sequences, sizzles, and everything in between, Bang Zoom knows how to put it all together!

Fulfillment

File delivery shouldn’t be a nail-biting experience. Our state-of-the-art systems and rigorous protocols guarantee your files arrive intact, on time, and to spec. You can rest assured your assets will be handled securely and delivered to all streaming services properly and efficiently.

Social Media & Marketing
