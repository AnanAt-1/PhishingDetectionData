    Find Your Legislator

    Giving you access to your government.

    FiscalNote was founded with the principle of bringing people closer to their governments. We’ve created a tool to help you quickly identify and contact elected officials including, members of Congress currently representing you. Regardless of your political affiliation, you have the power to make your voice heard by contacting your representatives and advocating for the issues that matter most to you.

    It’s easy. Follow these three simple steps:

    1. Enter your zip code and address. A list of the elected officials that represent your address will appear.
    2. Select all of the individuals you wish to contact and enter a note to your representative(s).
    3. Click submit and you can assure your voice will be heard.


    FiscalNote is a technology-powered global software data and media company that uses powerful machine learning to provide clients with the right policy information and insights at the right time so that they can better navigate market risk and uncertainty and maximize new opportunities.
