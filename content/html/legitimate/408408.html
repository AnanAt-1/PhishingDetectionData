Medberry: Basic Facts & Information

Medberry is a populated place located within the Township of Wano, a minor civil division (MCD) of LaMoure County.

Medberry appears on the Medberry U.S. Geological Survey Map. LaMoure County is in the Central Time Zone (UTC -6 hours).
