A Dance in Time

An Autumn Celebration

The air has grown crisp rather suddenly and the changes that are passing over our earth lend excitement to our daily life. In the old days, when America was young, celebrating that excitement was tradition... corn husking, barn raising, autumn feasts... and dancing.

Now, with the Bonnie Blue Ball, we have the opportunity to re-create that old fashioned joy, the opportunity to truly celebrate the blessings God has given us, to celebrate each other, and to celebrate our inspiring history, when a gentleman was a gentleman and a lady was lady, when a handshake meant something, when God was a part of everything our country did, and the American people rose to praise him.

2010 Bonnie Blue Ball

Yes!  The annual Bonnie Blue Ball has come around again.


October 30, 2010
5:00 in the evening
Dinner, and dancing, and tons of fun for the whole family.

You can come in costume, or in evening wear.  And, this year's event boasts a unique bonus, a costume contest!  Entry in the contest is an extra $5 at the door, and all attendees will take a vote on the evening's best costumes.  Prizes to be announced.


Ticket prices are here.  Be sure you register right away to take advantage of the Early Bird Discount.



For more information about this unique event, view last year's post on the subject.  Or... view a slideshow of last year's photos.  Do leave us a comment if you have any questions!
