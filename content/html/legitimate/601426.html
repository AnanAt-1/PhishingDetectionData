      Grand Prix de Cycliste - Quebec and Montreal

      By flying dog on Sep 5, 2011, 9:28pm EDT 101

      Tweet Share
       Tweet Share on Twitter Share Share on Facebook Pin Pinterest 101 Comments

          Podium Insight of course has a great write up on the races and will be at the races Tweeting too @podium_live  Check them out for some first class race coverage.

          A couple of my favorite races are run this week in what can be called the The Great White North, it's the beauty way to go.  I found a start list for both the Grand Prix Cycliste de Quebec and Grand Prix Cycliste de Montreal from the awesome Cycling fever website.  As if we need another link I really should give the Official race website a nod where you can find the course maps and profiles.

          Apparently Thomas Voeckler won't be back to defend his title even though his team is a wild card invitee.  Robert Gesink will be racing to defend his Montreal title.  But is this all moot?  Philippe Gilbert has set the goal of being the UCI's highest ranking rider at years end which would  be remarkable for a One Day Race specialist.  And one other thing, don't put too much weight in the start lists, they only have one Sep Vanmarcke scheduled to take the starts. (They fixed that, Sep is in Spain this week for those who wonder.)
