The Beginning

Black Eyed Peas
POP · 2010

Will.i.am’s musical explorations and Fergie’s dancefloor-ready wail remain front and center, but The Black Eyed Peas’ sixth album adds a chilled-out vibe to their globally minded hip-pop. “The Time (Dirty Bit)” combines the Dirty Dancing floor-filler “(I’ve Had) The Time of My Life” with bouncing-ball synths; “Fashion Beats” rides a smeary Chic sample and Fergie’s croon; and the pulsing, anthemic “Someday” coasts on apl.de.ap’s up-from-nothing boasts. The Beginning shows the Peas at the peak of their power, even when they luxuriate in mellowness.

January 1, 2010 12 Songs, 54 minutes ℗ 2010 Interscope Records
