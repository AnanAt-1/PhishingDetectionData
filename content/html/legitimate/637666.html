    1. Re: Air Transat - vancouver to manchester and carryon luggage
    13 years ago

    Thomas Cook are very strict on luggage allowances and often your hand luggage will be weighed at check in. If they don't weigh it they usually ask you to show it to them so they can assess it's size and weight. Five kilos is the standard hand luggage allowance on UK charter airlines.
