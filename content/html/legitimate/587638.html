How do I change my name?

You may be thinking about changing your name for any number of reasons -- maybe you're getting divorced, or you just don't like your name and want one that suits you better. Whatever the reason, you'll be glad to know that legal name changes are common -- and fairly easy to carry out. Our do-it-yourself forms include detailed filing instructions to legally change your name.
