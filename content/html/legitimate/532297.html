                FAQs of Wholesale Electric Bus

                • What safety features should be verified in wholesale electric bus?

                  When engaging in wholesale procurement of electric bus, it's imperative to scrutinize key safety features. Anti - lock braking systems (ABS), electronic stability control (ESC), and efficient fire suppression systems are non - negotiable. These not only safeguard passengers during transit but also fulfill the mandatory requirements set by regulatory authorities across diverse nations, ensuring legal compliance.

                • How do manufacturers determine MOQ for luxury electric bus?

                  For luxury electric bus, MOQ is usually lower, around 3 - 5 units. Since these buses have high - end finishes, custom interiors, and advanced tech, production is more labor - intensive. Manufacturers need a smaller volume to recoup costs and maintain quality across the batch.

                • What affects the pricing of electric electric bus compared to diesel ones?

                  Electric electric bus are pricier initially, mainly due to battery costs. A diesel electric bus may cost $100,000 - $200,000, while an electric equivalent can start at $300,000. However, electric electric bus have lower long - term operating costs, which can offset the higher upfront investment.

                • What incoterms are suitable for long - distance electric bus exports?

                  For exporters looking to ship electric bus over long distances, CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight) emerges as an excellent choice. This comprehensive incoterm takes care of shipping expenses, insurance premiums, and ensures the seamless delivery of the electric bus right to the destination port. By doing so, it alleviates the buyer's logistical headaches and mitigates potential risks.

                • What is the warranty period and return policy for electric bus?

                  Warranty periods typically range from 1 - 2 years for electric bus. For major mechanical failures, the supplier may offer a full replacement or repair of electric bus. Returns are usually accepted within 30 days for non - conforming products, provided proper documentation is available for electric bus.
