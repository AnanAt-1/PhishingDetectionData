Births: June 20, 2010

Staff Writer
St. Augustine Record

FRITZ

Joshua Fritz and Christina Brackett, Jacksonville, announce the birth of a son, Hunter Aiden Fritz, 7 pounds, 4 ounces, June 1 at Flagler Hospital.

Maternal grandparents are Rick and Debbie Brackett, Ponte Vedra Beach; paternal grandparents are Marvin Fritz and John and Jill Beatty, all of St. Augustine.

Maternal great-grandparents are Frank and Peggy Connor, Glen St. Mary, and Carl and Eileen McDonald, Sterling, Ill.

William Wallace and Amber Aracich, St. Augustine, announce the birth of a daughter, Allana Hope Wallace, 5 pounds, 8 ounces, June 4 at Flagler Hospital. She joins, Tyler Ashton Hudson Baker, 8, and Kaylee Michelle Lord, 2 ½.

Maternal grandparents are Scott Aracich and Linda Powers and Mark Flanders, San Mateo; paternal grandparents are Jimmy McLellan Sr., and Christine McLellan, Boston, Mass.

Maternal great-grandparents are Charles and Barbra Aracich, Tampa.; paternal great-grandparents are William and Fransis Wallace, Baltimore, Md.
