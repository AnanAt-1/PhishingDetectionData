Lucha Libre Taco Shop
We are a family-owned taco shop in San Diego, inspired by the vibrant culture of Mexican wrestling, or Lucha Libre. Our menu features a variety of delicious tacos, burritos, and specialty dishes, all crafted to bring bold flavors to your table. Our atmosphere celebrates the spirit of Lucha Libre, creating an exciting, high-energy vibe. Whether you're a local or just visiting, we invite you to join us for a meal that feels like a victory.
