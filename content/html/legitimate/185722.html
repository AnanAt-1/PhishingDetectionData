Welcome to paper-cut! 
In this site you will find (almost all) past papers, mark schemes, specifications and other resources for Edexcel IGCSE and A level (GCE & IAL) examinations to help you revise. Solving past exam papers is a great way to test and assess how far you've come along and definitely helps you to prepare for exams and achieve your desired grade. So use these resources well, and start early!
