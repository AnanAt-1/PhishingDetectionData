The Gateway Arch Experience

Gateway Arch Entrance

Enter the monument through the Gateway Arch Visitor Center, which faces Fourth Street and the Old Courthouse. The Gateway Arch legs are exits only. Please allow at least 30 minutes to go through security.

Purchase Tickets in Advance

Tram Ride to the Top tickets sell out early and often, advance reservations are strongly recommended.

Be a Part of Our History

Do you have a photo from a time you visited the Gateway Arch? We’re in search of visitor photos of the Gateway Arch from the past 50 years and beyond. Submit your photo below for a chance to have it displayed in an exhibit inside of the Gateway Arch tram loading areas.

Plan Your Visit

Spending a few hours or an entire day is easy at and around Gateway Arch National Park. Whether you are looking for free tourist attractions to experience in St. Louis, planning a riverfront cruise, or checking another national park off your list, unforgettable moments await above and below!

Plan Your Visit

Take a Ride to the Top

Take the Tram Ride to the Top to see a new view of St. Louis from inside the Gateway Arch. We encourage all visitors to purchase their tickets in advance since tram rides sell out early and often!

Buy Tickets

Cruise on the Riverboats

Whether you join us for a dinner cruise, a private event with live music, or for a moment to enjoy the St. Louis Riverfront, we have a sightseeing cruise and skyline views just for you.

Buy Tickets

Go on a Journey in Our Museum

Explore six innovative galleries inside the Museum under the Gateway Arch. The museum is FREE to all visitors and features universal design, interactive elements, and more.

Learn More

VR Adventures Through History

Transport yourself to the 1850s St. Louis Riverfront era when you slip on a headset in our award-winning Virtual Reality Theater. Experience our newest indoor attraction during your next visit!

Buy Tickets

Explore the Old Courthouse

Journey into the past with a free visit to the Old Courthouse and learn about the pivotal Dred & Harriet Scott trials, the history of American judicial processes, and African American life in St. Louis.

Learn More

A Park in the Heart of the City

Create your own national park adventures with over 91 acres of urban green space to explore in Gateway Arch National Park. Discover reflection ponds, gardens, paved paths, and more.
