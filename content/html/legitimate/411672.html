Tar Bridge Jacques Cartier National Park Quebec

From the visitor's center at Jacques Cartier National Park in Quebec, you'll cross a bridge to get to some of the local trails that take you through the forest.

No Comments

Leaving from the the visitor’s center at Jacques Cartier National Park in Quebec, you’ll cross this bridge to get to some of the trails that take you through the forest.

The fall foliage in the background on the mountains dot the landscape in its normal yellow and orange colors.

This bridge had a strong smell of tar like it had just been covered in it, I’m guessing to preserve the wood longer before the winter sets in.
