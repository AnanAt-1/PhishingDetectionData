Miracle Mart was the discount store chain created by Steinberg's supermarkets to attract more shoppers and to face other discounters that expanded in Canada like Kresge, Woolworth's and others.

The chain never became a leader of the industry because the managers were from the grocery business.  In the 80's or early 90's, the chain was renamed "M" stores in an attempt to save the company but they finally closed later along with the supermarket chain wich bankrupted.

Nice pictures, Miracle Mart is a discount chain started by Steinberg's, a supermarket chain.  They paired existing stores with Miracle Mart stores to boost traffic.  It was a very profitable move because Steinberg's owned almost every shopping centers in which they had stores.

Miracle Mart eventually became "M" stores in the late 80's. The chain was liquidated in 1992.
