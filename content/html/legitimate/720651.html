Before booking a flight with Volaris, it’s important to be aware of their customer service, including the contact information, reviews, ratings, and complaints listed below. Volaris is a low-cost Mexican airline offering domestic and international flights, primarily serving Mexico, the United States, Central America, and the Caribbean.

How to Contact Volaris

You can reach Volaris customer service using the following details:

  • Phone Number (Customer Support – English U.S.): 1-855-865-2747
  • Whats App: +52 55 5898 8599
  • Email Support: Volaris does not offer direct email support; customers are encouraged to use the online contact form available on their website.
  • Chat: Available on the Volaris website and through their mobile app during business hours.
  • Customer Service Hours: Support is available 24/7 for general inquiries and assistance.
  • Corporate Address: Volaris, Av. Antonio Dovalí Jaime 70, Tower B, 13th Floor, Zedec Santa Fe, Álvaro Obregón, 01210 Mexico City, Mexico
  • Website: https://www.volaris.com/

Volaris Customer Support Reviews and Complaints

When considering flying with Volaris, it’s helpful to review customer service feedback. Volaris competes with other low-cost carriers such as Viva Aerobus, Interjet, Southwest Airlines, and Spirit Airlines. Customer reviews often highlight the affordability of Volaris flights and the availability of numerous routes between Mexico and the United States. Many passengers appreciate the option to customize their travel experience by choosing from various add-ons like extra baggage, seat selection, and in-flight services. However, some complaints focus on issues related to baggage fees, flight delays, and the responsiveness of customer support.
Comparing Volaris with other low-cost carriers like Viva Aerobus, Interjet, Southwest Airlines, and Spirit Airlines can help you determine which airline offers the best combination of pricing, routes, and customer support for your travel needs.
