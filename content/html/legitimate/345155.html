Reputation for Being #1 in Sales

ClaimPost Realty Ltd. has a solid history of sustainability and growth since 1981. Whether the real estate market has been active or weak, ClaimPost Realty Ltd. has provided industry knowledge and skilled Sales Representatives and Brokers to the people of Timmins, Iroquois Falls, Matheson, Cochrane and the surrounding areas of Northern Ontario.

The Sales Team at ClaimPost Realty Ltd. have consistently proven that their drive to provide the best possible service to their Sellers and Buyers is the reason Claimpost Realty Ltd. is involved in more MLS transactions than our competitors. We have managed to maintain our reputation for being #1 in sales for the serviced area for many years due to our large contingent of outstanding Sales Representatives and Brokers. We are extremely proud of who we have become and constantly strive to maintain and better our services to all our customers and clients in this ever changing real estate market

Our Sales Representatives and Brokers are always available to service you and we look forward to meeting with you and making your dreams of Ownership a reality!
