Join us to build a society that is more connected, healthy and secure.

With a focus on people, data and code, UQ's School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science is at the leading-edge of computer science, IT and electrical engineering.

We take pride in our reputation for teaching excellence and expert research. Our students, graduates, teachers and researchers are revolutionising technology to solve the world's greatest challenges.
