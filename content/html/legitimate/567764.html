Welcome to Mad Science of Long Island!

We believe that fostering a love for science, technology, engineering and math at a young age will give children the confidence they need to excel in these fields. For over 30 years, Mad Science has focused on designing and testing experiments and activities that resonate with kids, allowing us to provide innovative, hands-on children's science programs to our communities.

About Us

STEM After-School Programs for Kids 5-12

Bring our educational and engaging STEAM after-school programs to the students at your school! We have a range of programs to choose from that teach fundamentals like chemistry, physics, biology, and engineering.

Our classes help children see the fun side of science and allow them to explore the world in an interactive environment that feels like play.
