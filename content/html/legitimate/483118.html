Bus Service from Singapore to Malaysia

Superior Coach & Tour Pte Ltd was incorporated in Singapore in 1990. The company offers both local and international tours in Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, China and Educational tour, MICE as well.

Superior Tour Express Bus Service & Reputation

Superior Coach & Tour has a variety of coaches to meet the needs of their customers, featuring different types of transportation like bus and vans. Some of the coaches available are (according to seat availability): 10, 12, 26 (Super VIP seated), 40 & 45 seaters.

These coaches are perfect for weddings, office use and even for factory workers within Singapore and Malaysia. This service is aligned with the company’s core values, which are to provide excellent service by being a reliable transportation provider and enable superior value for money to the customers.

Latest Update
Superior Tour is proud to bring you daily buses from Singapore to LEGOLAND® Malaysia. You can purchase the transfer + entrance tickets to LEGOLAND® Malaysia at the cost of S$60 per person. For bus transfer only, the price is S$10 per person. LEGOLAND® Malaysia is the first international theme park in Malaysia and is located in the state of Johor. To find out more information about LEGOLAND® Malaysia, please visit LEGOLAND® Malaysia homepage.
