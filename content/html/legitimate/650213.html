About

CCH Pounder is a 72 year old Guyanese Actress. Born Carol Christine Hilaria Pounder on 25th December, 1952 in Georgetown, British Guiana, she is famous for her roles as Blake in the 1979 musical comedy-drama film All That Jazz, Jasmine in the NBC serial police procedural drama series Hill Street Blues, Dr. Angela Hicks in the NBC medical drama series ER, Amanda Waller (voice) and Golden Queen (voice) in the Cartoon Network superhero animated series Justice League Unlimited, Detective Claudette Wyms in the FX crime drama series The Shield, Mo’at in the 2009 epic science fiction film Avatar, Sister Abigail in the 2009 psychological horror film Orphan, Mrs. Irene Frederic in the Syfy science fiction series Warehouse 13, Tyne Patterson in the FX action crime drama series Sons of Anarchy, Dorothea in the 2013 urban fantasy film The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones, and Dr. Loretta Wade in the CBS action crime drama and police procedural series NCIS: New Orleans. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn.
