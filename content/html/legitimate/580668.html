Overview

Active bass pickups with full-length dual blades that deliver optimal sound when playing aggressive musical genres on your 4-string.

The full-length dual blades of the Basslines ASB-BO Blackouts accommodate maximum string spacings up to 2.70" for a 4-string electric bass guitar.

The ASB-BO Blackouts are active bass pickups and electronics voiced for the aggressive bass tones of metal, classic metal, punk, and heavy rock. This neck and bridge set includes active bass soapbar pickups specifically voiced for aggressive music. Instead of the radical EQ boosting and cutting required to make flat, sterile pickups come alive, Basslines Blackouts pickups delivers the goods with less tweaking. They have more of what you want, including cleaner highs, deeper lows, and greater dynamic range than traditional active bass pickups. This high energy, expansive sound you get from this Basslines pickup is also capable of covering your other gigs with ease.

  • Accommodates maximum string spacings up to 2.70" for a four-string bass model
