NEW PEOPLE, Inc.

1746 Post Street, San Francisco CA, 94115 U.S.A.  

As visionaries behind the building NEW PEOPLE, NEW PEOPLE, Inc. is a team fully dedicated to introducing Japanese popular culture to the U.S. through films, music, fashion, and various events. We also offer event production, PR & marketing, and creative consulting services for our international clients.

NEW PEOPLE CINEMA (B1F)

Temporarily closed until further notice

NEW PEOPLE Cinema is a 143-seat underground cinema located in the basement level of NEW PEOPLE in San Francisco. Equipped with a cutting-edge HD digital projection, 35mm film projection and THX®-certified sound system, NEW PEOPLE Cinema is home to the local film community.

SAN FRANCISCO SOU • SOU (2F)

SAN FRANCISCO SOU・SOU is a unique footwear brand from Kyoto that infuses Japanese classic “Tabi” work shoes with vibrant graphic patterns. SOU・SOU’s combination of form-fitting soles and striking modern looks has created a huge following, from dancers to the sneaker-obsessed. While new and fun, each shoe is crafted by hand, preserving the best of tradition.

Event Production

NEW PEOPLE, Inc. produces various types of events, including music concerts, movie premieres, exhibitions, conferences, and PR events. Entertainment professionals who are well-versed in the U.S. event scene serve as consultants, providing specialized project management for events. We have experience in producing a wide range of events, from those with a few dozen attendees to large-scale events with hundreds of thousands of participants. We ensure the success of both onsite and offline events.

Creative Consulting

NEW PEOPLE, Inc. offers a creative consulting package that combines marketing and creative production services to support Japanese companies advancing to the US market and US companies to the Japanese market. Our clients include Blue Bottle Coffee and Dandelion Chocolate from San Francisco.

Dandelion Chocolate Japan

NEW PEOPLE is behind the launch of Dandelion Chocolate Japan as the subsidiary of Dandelion Chocolate, San Francisco's beloved bean-to-bar chocolate factory in the Mission district. As a creative agency specializing in Japan and its market, we are proud to make a difference in the world with innovative partners like Dandelion Chocolate!
