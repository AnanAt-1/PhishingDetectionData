A statue of a "Hockey Hall of Fame" player.
Joseph Henri Maurice "Rocket" Richard, (August 4, 1921 – May 27, 2000) was a professional ice hockey player who played for the Montreal Canadiens from 1942 to 1960. The "Rocket" was the most prolific goal-scorer of his era, the first to achieve the feat of 50 goals in 50 games.
