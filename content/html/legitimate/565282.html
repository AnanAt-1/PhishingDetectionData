Welcome

Logic Design Inc. (LDI) provides fully integrated technical learning solutions ranging from electronics to robotics, utilizing simulation software, multimedia learning material, textbooks, computer-based testing, and course management software. In addition to its best-selling simulation products, such as CircuitLogix, 3DLab, PLCLogix, and RoboLogix, LDI also has a strong focus on computer-based training (CBT) multimedia learning packages including Electronics, Electro-Mechanics, Programmable Logic Controllers, and Industrial Robotics, which all utilize integrated simulation software with multimedia course content.
