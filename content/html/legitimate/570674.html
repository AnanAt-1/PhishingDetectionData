A History of French Canada 1670 to 1699

20 April, 2010 /

In the interest of currency & accuracy, we have removed our previously published and now “out of date content”. If you wish to read Dick Garneau’s History of Canada we recommend you view the original materials written and published by R.D. (Dick) Garneau.

We provide a significant body of historical source materials on Canada and Quebec; they are freely accessible via our French Canada page.

To obtain more information, we heartily recommend researching the following web sites:

  • Canada in the Making
  • Canada’s History
  • InfoPlease Canada
