The 1982 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 68 games, lost 94 games, and finished in fifth position. They played their home games at Oakland Coliseum (Park Factors: 96/96) where 1,735,489 fans witnessed their 1982 Athletics finish the season with a .420 winning percentage.
