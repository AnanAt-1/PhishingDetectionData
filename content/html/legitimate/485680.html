The Carl King Show

Because the beauty of art lies in the details, or perhaps it doesn’t.

Every week, I explore music, filmmaking, and the other creative arts. I learn, then I share what I learned. I deconstruct and reverse engineer composition, screenwriting, and how to correctly pronounce the word “folks.”  I want to understand, and I want YOU to understand, because understanding is useful.

Who am I? I’m Carl King: musician, animated show creator, and author. You might even remember me as Sir Millard Mulch or Dr. Zoltan. I’ve worked with Dweezil Zappa, Mike Keneally, Devin Townsend, Morgan Agren, Virgil Donati, and Brendon Small. Past guests on my show include Steve Vai, Jon Schnepp of Metalocalypse, Mark Borchardt (Coven), and Trey Spruance of Mr. Bungle.

So give it a watching, and let’s get beginned!
