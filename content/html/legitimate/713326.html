Manufacturers & Services

These companies can fulfill your needs for standard and semi-rigid coaxial cable, ribbon cable, twisted pair, & braided multiconductor cable & wire. Some of the companies can fabricate custom cabling with necessary connectors to your specifications.

As with many manufacturers' websites, you can usually find well-written application notes for how to properly utilize the products, as well as electrical specifications.

Note that attenuation values can - and do - often have significantly different values at other frequencies. Always check with a coaxial cable vendor for values specific to the type you plan to use.
