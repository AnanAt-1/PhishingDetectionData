Who We Are

First Congregational Church UCC, Norman, is an Open and Affirming, Peace and Justice, Beloved Community of Jesus Followers.  We believe Christianity is a way of life, not a set of creeds and doctrines demanding total agreement.  We honor intellectual and spiritual freedom, trusting that head and heart can be equal partners in faith.
