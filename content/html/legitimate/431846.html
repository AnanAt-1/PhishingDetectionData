Our Mission

Tempe Woman's Club is a 501(c)3 non-profit service organization of women (and men!) that actively promotes fellowship, 

encourages community involvement and supports causes 

and charities advancing women's issues and education.

Tempe Woman's Club is a charter member of the General Federation of Woman's Clubs.

Help Us Restore our Legacy for the Future!

Since 1912, the Tempe Woman’s Club has promoted education, service, and civic engagement in the community. We are raising funds to preserve our 1936 clubhouse and continue our mission. Your support helps keep our legacy of service alive for future generations. Thank you!
