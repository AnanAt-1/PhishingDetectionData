Telecommuting Help Desk Jobs

Sort by: Date | Relevance
Browse the latest help desk jobs that let you work online from a remote office. The Virtual Vocations staff screens thousands of job listings so you don’t have to. No matter what you need from a telecommute job, we can help.

In an online help desk job, you don’t work at traditional help desk, but you perform the same duties. Your job in one of these positions is to help customers fix problems with their products. This is usually a job in the tech field, so you should have strong computer skills. You can find positions with little experience, but if you deal with more technical products, you may need a degree in computer science. Remote jobs are common, but they may not always have the same name.

To succeed in an online management level job, you primarily need strong technical and communication skills. Job offerings include Remote Help Desk Specialist, Remote Help Desk Program Organizer, and Remote Help Desk Technician.
