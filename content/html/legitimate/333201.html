Power and Data Raceway Systems

Connectrac by Legrand offers advanced floor-based raceway solutions that integrate power, data and communication access seamlessly into commercial interior spaces. With under-carpet and over-floor installation options, these raceways provide the adaptable power access you need without sacrificing the aesthetic of your commercial space.
The Flex® Raceway System

A modular, 4-circuit system that features moveable power, expandable data capacity and multiple entrance fitting options.

Learn More
Express Kits

A single-circuit, grab-and-go raceway solution.

Learn More
GO Wireway

A portable, pluggable wireway solution that plugs directly into any existing wall outlet and can be installed on top of any flooring surface.
