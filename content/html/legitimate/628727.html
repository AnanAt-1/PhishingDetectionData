Escape To A Haven Beyond The Market Town Of Skipton

Tempest Arms

Set in rolling picturesque countryside, The Tempest Arms is a newly refurbished 17th Century dog-friendly Inn and restaurant with 21 bedrooms and suites, on the edge of the Yorkshire Dales and Skipton.

Rooms

Our 21 individually styled rooms are modern, welcoming, and comfortable all with ensuite facilities. We have a range of rooms including suites, premium suites, and deluxe suites. Our more premium rooms have private terraces or Juliette balcony’s overlooking the babbling beck. The perfect space for taking in the great outdoors from the comfort of your own bedroom!

Our family-friendly menu is packed full of great pub food created with fresh, locally sourced seasonal produce that’s served with flair. Don’t miss our famous Sunday Roast.
