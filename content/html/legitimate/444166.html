Sir Thomas Roddick Hospital

Sir Thomas Roddick Hospital

142 Minnesota Drive
Stephenville, NL, NL
Canada A2N 2V6
709-643-5111

Sir Thomas Roddick Hospital in Stephenville is a forty-four bed acute care center with a comprehensive range of inpatient and outpatient services for the people of the Bay St. George catchment area.  The catchment area services by Sir Thomas Roddick Hospital is between the Port au Port Peninsula east to Gallants and south to the Jeffrey’s-St. Fintan’s area serving a population of approximately 24,000.

The Health Services provided include:

  • Medical Services
  • Nursing Services
  • Surgical Services
  • Visiting Obstetric/Gynecology
  • Emergency Services
  • Renal Care
  • Outpatient Clinics
  • Specialty Clinics (Travelling)
  • Pharmacy Services.
