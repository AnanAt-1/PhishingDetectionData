About Me

D. Sine
I'm an old punk rocker who picked up a camera many years ago so that I could fill up the pages of the fanzine that I was doing at the time. And even though the fanzine is long gone, I still love exploring the world of film photography. I work almost exclusively with film and often times with vintage/antique cameras. I do this because I love it.
Enjoy.
