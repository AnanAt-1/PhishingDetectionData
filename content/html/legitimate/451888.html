While we praise Vernon Davis with his good stats, well-manicured locks, celebrations after a short gain on a pass caught...I recall another 49er tight end: Monty Stickles. He wasn't pretty, nor was he gifted or talented. He was a tough guy. He always backed up his teammates on and off the field. He was mean and nasty. He got in fights on and off the field. I once saw him kicked out of a game because he threw a ball at a ref. He was scary. Do we really have any scary guys on the current offense of our favorite team? They have pretty hair and trim their eyebrows, but do they scare anyone? I miss you Monty Stickles.
