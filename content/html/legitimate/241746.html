Our Philosophy...

M.E. Neal Studios is devoted to bringing your love story to life through the seamless storytelling of imagery. With an artistic eye for compostion,lighting and emotion M.E. Neal Studios blends tradional formal portraiture and timeless photojournalistic documentation with avant guard techniques in film processing, printing and publishing.

Our Finest Quality and Standards

As a proud member of the Professional Photographers of America our studio upholds the highest of standards and supreme quality imaging. In order to provide each couple with their own individual wedding story M.E. Neal Studios does not charge an hourly rate. We begin our coverage from the moment the bride is getting ready with her friends and family through the cermony and on to the last grand event of the reception. All fine art prints are color corrected and printed on archival acid free,matte paper to reduce glare and fingerprints. You may choose from the finest leather bridal albums of museum qualtiy or for a contemporary style a hard bound coffee table book.

M.E. Neal Studios is proud to serve the entire Eastern Shore including St.Michaels, Cambridge, Stevensville, Kent Island, Ocean City, as well as Ocean View Delaware, Rehobeth, Dewey, Lewes, Georgetown, Millsoro and lower shore Delaware.
