May 2025
 
On Sunday 11th May we are at the spectacular Borde Hill Gardens Haywards Heath (RH16 1XP) with Plant Fairs Roadshow. Where we will have a range of choice and rarely offered shrubs and small trees.
 
 
  The catalogue describes most of the plants we have available but if a variety is not priced then they are not available but will be growing on the nursery for future availablity. Our specialities include some interesting and rarely offered shrubs and small trees,often grown in only small quantities.
 
Some more interesting shrubs and small trees have been added to the catalogue recently,these include a return of Cornus kousa varieties,Daphne bholua varieties,Hoherias,Lagerstroemia and Pseudopanax.
 
 
Plants can also be ordered for local delivery or sent further afield by courier,please enquire by telephone or fill in the enquiry form on the last page of the catalogue.
 
Please either e-mail (info@charleshurstplants.co.uk) or telephone before visiting the nursery as hours and the owners attendance varies.
