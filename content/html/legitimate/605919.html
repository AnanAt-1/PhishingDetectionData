Over 50 Years of Satisfied Customers

After more than 50 years of working on lakes and ponds, Ralph is retiring.  Fortunately, he has passed on all of his experience, knowledge and expertise to his son Peter.  As a result, you will have a finished product that you will enjoy for many years.

Ralph McGillan Excavating has over 50 years experience serving the New Jersey area. We specialize in servicing new and existing lakes, dam rehabilitation, complete aeration systems as well as installation of docks and gazebos. Our major clientele includes golf courses, private individuals on estate-type properties and farms.

When it comes to your property, let the father and son team of Ralph and Peter McGillan be your #1 choice!
