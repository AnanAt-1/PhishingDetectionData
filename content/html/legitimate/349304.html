CAREER MOVES

3/23/1999 Montreal Canadiens traded Vincent Damphousse to the San Jose Sharks for 5th round selection (previously acquired from the Phoenix Coyotes - Marc-Andre Thinel) in 1999, 2nd round selection in 2000 and future considerations


8/27/1992 Montreal Canadiens traded Shayne Corson, Brent Gilchrist and Vladimir Vujtek to the Edmonton Oilers for Vincent Damphousse and 4th round selection (Adam Wiesel) in 1993.

9/19/1991 Edmonton Oilers traded Craig Berube, Grant Fuhr and Glenn Anderson to the Toronto Maple Leafs for Vincent Damphousse, Peter Ing, Scott Thornton, Luke Richardson, future considerations and cash.

6/21/1986 Drafted by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 1st round (6th overall) in 1986.
