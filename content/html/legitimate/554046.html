About the Kingsville Navy League

Our Mission

As a civilian nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization,

our mission is to educate the public about the need for our nation's Sea Services,

to improve the quality of life of the members and their families of our adopted units (listed above),

and to support youth programs. 

Educate. Advocate. Support.


Founded in 1955, we now proudly stand at 114 members strong!
