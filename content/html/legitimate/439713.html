Unity's Philosophy

 Unity teachings are based on universal spiritual principles that run like a thread through the world’s religions. These are laws that seem to govern humankind no matter where we live, how we view the Divine, or even whether we are aware these principles exist. They have been expressed in many ways through thousands of years, but the essence of them are the 5 Basic Unity Principles.

The Unity approach to prayer is affirmative, based on positive prayers and affirmations that have universal appeal. Affirmative prayer is the highest form of creative thought. It includes the release of counterproductive, negative thoughts, as well as holding in mind statements of spiritual truth. Through meditation, we experience the presence of God. Prayer and meditation heighten our awareness and thereby transform our lives.

Reverend Michael Everett Davis is so happy to be serving this community! Michael first started attending Unity of Indianapolis on Christmas Eve, 2009. This was a life changing event for him. 

In October of 2010, he became the center's Music Director. Then in 2015, he started attending One Spirit Interfaith Seminary in New York City, and was ordained as an Interfaith Minister in 2017. He is now serving under Special Dispensation from Unity Worldwide Ministries, (since November 2021), as the Spiritual Leader of Unity of Indianapolis. He is pursuing a path toward ordination through Unity Worldwide. 

Reverend Michael states, "I am a man who 'wears many hats,' and I don’t like to wear hats! I am a singer/songwriter, an actor, and a mystic. In 2017, I took my vow of ministry. My vow is to be PEACE expressing in the world."
