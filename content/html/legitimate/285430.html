THE SONDHEIM CROSTICS

All puzzles copyright IMM-MM Christopher Mason

A collection of puzzles made out of the master's lyrics.

These are low tech puzzles; you will have to print them out onto paper to solve them.

Fill in the missing words in the phrases from the Sondheim lyrics in the numbered blanks. Then fill the letters into the squares above the corresponding numbers in the grid. On most of the puzzles, I have filled in a few letters to get you started.

This will reveal one of my favorite quotes from the book of a Sondheim show. (Please note that the words wrap around the edges of the grid.)
