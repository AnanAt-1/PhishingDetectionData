The Collège is the leader in real estate training.


For several years now, the Collège de l'immobilier du Québec has been, by far, the institution that graduates the highest number of students who register for the Organisme d'autoréglementation du courtage immobilier du Québec’s (OACIQ) certification exam. Also, our graduates’ success rate stand outs compared to others in the province. Furthermore, the Collège holds a licence recognized by the Ministère de l'Éducation et de l'Enseignement supérieur (MÉES).

In other words, the Collège is the #1 choice in Québec for training real estate brokers and agency executive officers!

Comprehensive training that is adapted, recognized and attuned to the needs of the market.

The Collège de l'immobilier du Québec meets all the basic training needs for real estate brokers (residential and commercial) and agency executive officers. Our courses are provided by professionals who are active in their area of expertise (brokers, lawyers, notaries, etc.), allowing for an approach that is current and focused on market realities.

All programs offered at the Collège comply with competency frameworks and are approved by the OACIQ. In addition, some training programs lead to an Attestation of Collegial Studies (ACS) and provide access to student financial assistance.

There are many reasons why the Collège de l'immobilier du Québec is THE LEADER in real estate training!

PROGRAMS THAT MEET THE NEEDS OF FUTURE REAL ESTATE BROKERS 

The College offers a variety of flexible formats—hybrid or distance learning—to make education more accessible to a greater number of students. These options are available across all its campuses: Île-des-Sœurs, Laval, South Shore, as well as its virtual campus, which is accessible throughout Quebec.

Enhanced services that meet students’ needs.

The Collège de l'immobilier du Québec has a policy for the recognition of prior learning and skills, which can facilitate access to courses and educational programs. Some conditions apply.

Our students benefit from various opportunities throughout their training program to adequately prepare for their OACIQ certification exam.

Every student at the Collège has access to the Omnivox portal, a leading-edge technology tool for education, pedagogy and communication.

The Collège’s campuses are strategically located and easy to reach by car and by public transit. Free parking is available, along with free Wi-Fi and other amenities.

The Collège is always up-to-date on the latest legislative and administrative news, which ensures that students receive high-quality training and increases the chances of success on the OACIQ’s certification exam. 

Please contact one of our Admissions' advisors, at any time to learn more about the Collège.
