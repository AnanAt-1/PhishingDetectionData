      Beautiful Campus and cool Men's Basketball Museum

      Review of University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
      Reviewed August 17, 2009

      We toured the campus with my daughter and had lunch on Franklin Street, a short distance from the University. This is a world class school in a beautiful setting. The rolling hills, the trees, the flowers and the architecture are amazing,.

      Then we walked drove over to the Dean Dome on the tip of the campus by the other Athletic fields. We found a FREE basketball museum just to the left of the Dome and what a great collection of alumni's memorabilia !! I didn't realize how many UNC players ended up going to the NBA, besides Micharl Jordan and VInce Carter. And there is many cool exhibits inside including a huge mural of MJ on a large glass window. My favorite items were MJ's college shoes......LOW TOP CONVERSE ALL STARS as well as a letter from Duke's Coach Mike K congratulating him for selecting UNC but expressing his disappointment in not choosing Duke.....it was dated 1980 and seemed to be frozen in time.......shoulda, coulda woulda......
