Mail to Doug Engelbart, Ross Mayfield, and Mai Lin Fung

Date: 15 Jun 2005, 23:53 Uhr +0200
From: Matthias Mueller-Prove
Subject: thanks for reboot

Hi Doug,

it was one of those rare and special moments. I would like to thank you for your time and generosity during the reboot session. The audience and myself felt inspired and very much moved.

all the best to you!
Matthias

PS: Thank you too, Mei Lin and Ross for making this session possible.

Mail from Doug

Date: 15 Jun 2005 21:24 PDT
From: Doug Engelbart - Bootstrap Institute
Subject: Appreciate your note, Matthias
To: Matthias Mueller-Prove

We were four people watching a small screen, with bright California sunlight outside our window, trying to imagine tired people, late at night, thousands of miles away -- being interested.

So your email note was very welcome. We thank you.

Appreciatively, Doug
