Electronic Service Solutions For Commercial And Industrial Repairs

Since 1970, Diversified Electronics of Canada Ltd. has been providing repairs services for commercial and industrial electronic equipment. We also specialize in utility, leak detection and oil tank locating services including onsite repairs for scoreboard and commercial sound systems. Check out our website for service support details.

Diversified Electronics is also an authorized sales dealer for metal detectors, gold panning equipment and accessories including speech aid products. Strategically located in Burnaby, BC, we service clients from many industries, offering real time support and service. Our goal is to ensure the client receives a guaranteed experience whether it be from sales or service.

Diversified Electronics is the answer to today’s technological repairs.

Diversifed Electronics is interested in looking at exploring service opportunities to support manufacturers in the western Canadian market.
