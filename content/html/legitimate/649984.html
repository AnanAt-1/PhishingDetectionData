About

Robert Patrick is a 66 year old American Actor. Born Robert Hammond Patrick Jr. on 5th November, 1958 in Marietta, Georgia, USA, he is famous for Terminator 2, The Unit in a career that spans 1983–present. His zodiac sign is Scorpio.

Robert Patrick has been in an on-screen matchup with Sharon Stone in Lovelace (2013).

Robert Patrick is a member of the following lists: American film actors, American television actors and American voice actors.
