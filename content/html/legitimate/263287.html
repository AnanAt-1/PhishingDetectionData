  PDP 2007    
     
The Fifteen Euromicro Conference on Parallel, Distributed and Network-based Processing covers all the fields of high-performance computing, from advances in scientific and engineering applications to new proposals in programming and problem solving environments, models, languages and architectures. The conference provides a forum for presentation of all the issues related to parallel, distributed and network computing.

The scope of  PDP 2007 is further expanded with additional themes emphasized also by special sessions.
