Australian Open

The Australian Open is one of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments, held annually in Melbourne every January. It features men's and women's singles, doubles, and mixed doubles events on hard courts. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, the tournament attracts top players worldwide, with notable champions including Novak Djokovic, Serena Williams, and Roger Federer.
