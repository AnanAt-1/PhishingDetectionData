What is Adoptagnome?

Adoptagnome stands for "adopt a gnome". We just wanted to make a fun site where you can adopt a little creature and put it on your website.

What do I have to do?

Just download the picture and put it on your website! We do NOT want you to modify the picture in any way though. There's only one thing we ask of you in return for your adoption. The only thing you have to do is link the image back to this page("http://adoptagnome.cjb.net")
