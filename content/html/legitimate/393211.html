LCC Principal Search
Wednesday, June 8, 2011

To:  The LCC Community
I am very pleased to report that our search process was successfully concluded this past week, and that the next leader of Lima Central Catholic will be joining our school effective July 1, 2011.  The Board of Governors, in conjunction with the Diocese of Toledo, accepted the recommendation of the Search Committee and we have successfully completed the hiring process.  We will hold a press conference at the high school at 3 PM this Friday, June 10th, to introduce our new leader.   Following the press conference, we will hold a community open house reception at the Knights of Columbus on Cable Road at 5 PM.  Please come join us and meet the new leader of LCC.

Best regards,
Jeff Oravitz
Vice President, LCC Board of Governors
Search Committee Chairman

LCC Principal Search

Sunday, May 15, 2011

To:  The LCC Community

Our interview process with a number of candidates continues at the current time.  We are blessed to have interest in the leadership of our school from a selection of very strong candidates.  We are progressing through initial face-to-face interview sessions as well as follow-up discussions with additional questions to and from the candidates. 

Our search is comprised of both external and internal candidates, and the process continues to be thorough and deliberate.  We anticipate refinement of the candidate pool over the next couple of weeks, and have a tentative target to recommend a candidate to the LCC Board of Governors and Diocese of Toledo near the end of the current school year.

Best regards,

Jeff Oravitz
Vice President, LCC Board of Governors
Search Committee Chair 

LCC Principal Search

Saturday, March 12, 2011

To:  The LCC Community

This is the first of several messages you will receive over the next couple of months, all intended to keep you informed about the progress of the search for LCC’s next principal. The Search Committee (scroll down for a list of committee members) has been formed and is accepting applications for the position now through March 31st.  While we feel it will be necessary to maintain strict confidentiality throughout the process, we still believe that the community should stay informed regarding our progress.  The position has been posted on numerous websites, including the National Catholic Education Association (NCEA), and is also being communicated throughout many Catholic communities both within and outside the Diocese of Toledo.   

Once all applications are received, we plan to conduct a comprehensive, multi-stage interview process in order to select the right individual that will bring the school forward.  Our goal is to complete this process by the end of May so that the new principal will have plenty of time to make the transition.

On a side note, the Board of Governors has also formed a new Academic Affairs Committee that will provide council, assistance and direction to the Board regarding the academic programs offered by LCC. Specifically, the committee will oversee the development, implementation and assessment of all academic programs at LCC as well as the school’s academic policies. The Chair of the Academic Affairs Committee, Eric Baumgartner, is seeking to fill two at-large positions on the committee. If you or someone you know within the greater LCC community has an interest in contributing to this committee, please send contact information to Eric via e-mail at [email protected]. The deadline for the receipt of nominations is Friday, March 18.

Stay tuned for updates as we progress through this important search process.  Thank you for your interest and support.

Best regards,

Scott G. Koenig
President, LCC Board of Governors
