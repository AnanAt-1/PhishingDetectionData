Perl training

Stonehenge has been teaching Perl since 1992, and our trainers are the authors of the bestselling tutorial books Learning Perl and Intermediate Perl which are based on our classes. We've taught for dozens of clients, in locations from Silicon Valley to Wall Street, from Chicago to the desert Southwest, and even Alaska and England.

We provide on-site Perl training almost anywhere in the world. Our classes range from our most popular course, Learning Perl, to advanced, custom designed courses on topics important to our clients. Our course list includes specialized topics including CGI programming, database programming, and many other Perl topics.

For more information on how Stonehenge can meet your training needs, email us at info@stonehenge.com.
