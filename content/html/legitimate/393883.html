From The Library

Play Summer Book BINGO with the Library!

Join us in a game of book bingo from home this summer! Students who get bingo get a free library sticker. All you have to do is present the bingo card to the front desk at the beginning of the fall semester.
Find cards around Lydon and O’Leary libraries or print one for free from our Library Activities LibGuide.

Article Quick Search and Other EBSCOHost Databases Getting Refreshed in May!

Starting in May, you’ll notice some changes to our Article Quick Search and other databases we get from EBSCOHost. We’re excited to offer you an easier way to find the articles you need.

Watch for more information on Today at UMass Lowell and this webpage over the summer. If you want a preview you can watch a YouTube video demonstration that will show you where to find some of your favorite features.
