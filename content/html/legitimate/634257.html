DUCKTOWN CEMETERY

Ducktown Cemetery is located across the street from the Methodist Church, called the Southern Methodist Church in earlier days. What follows is a composite of two lists, one sent several years ago by Mr. Grayson Newman, and another recently given to us by Mrs. Walter S. Hamby. Dates were not verified by the editor although there were occasional discrepancies between the two lists, especially with dates which contain the numerals 6, 8 and 9 - all of which look almost alike on worn stones. Known error corrections are requested and appreciated. Send to PCHGS, P. O. Box 636, Benton, TN 37307.Printed with permission and appreciation.
