Book title: Stormbreaker
Book author: Anthony Horowitz

Very good

Alex Rider lives with his uncle Ian Rider until one day Ian is killed. At this point, Alex must become a secret agent and take his uncle’s last mission, which is a big conflict for Alex.

This is a very good book because it is very thrilling and exciting to read about Alex’s mission.
