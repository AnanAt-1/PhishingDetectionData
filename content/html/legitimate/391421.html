Finally, a marketing firm that understands professionals!

Founded in 1991, Kevin Brown Marketing & Consulting specializes in marketing for small- to mid-sized law firms, CPAs, financial services firms, insurance brokerages and other professional services firms. We understand how to market professionals as few others do!

We offer virtually every marketing service that your firm might need.  Our services include: “Part-Time Director of Marketing™” (outsourced marketing department) or Project-Oriented services (Marketing Planning & Analysis, Business Development, Branding & Positioning, Digital Marketing, Advertising & Marketing Materials, and Public Relations & Publicity).

Kevin Brown Marketing & Consulting is widely recognized as one of the pioneers and premier marketing firms in California specializing in professional services firms.  Our reputation is based upon designing and implementing high-quality services with expertise, courtesy and professionalism.  Our clients include some of California’s most well-known firms as well as many smaller firms. We also serve selected clients across the nation.

Part-Time Director of Marketing™

On a monthly retainer contract (non-employee) basis, we provide hands-on management and implementation of your marketing objec­tives, at a fraction of the cost of a full-time employee. This is as an alternative or supplement to an internal marketing depart­ment.

Marketing Planning & Analysis

Through our unique process, clients are able to be improve the effectiveness of their marketing efforts, while appropriately managing the use of human and financial resources.   Our goal is to help you work smarter, not harder.

Business Development

Training, personal coaching, partner retreats, business development meetings, tracking systems, and more!  We help to get your sales program in gear.

Digital Marketing

Are your internet marketing efforts a disjointed mess?  If so, our affiliate company Exemplar Digital Marketing offers a solution to your dilemma: Integrated Internet Marketing.  Our goal is to bring all of your internet marketing efforts together into a combined, effective and cohesive campaign!

Branding & Positioning

Advertising & Marketing Materials
