About the Martial Arts Programs

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Team Popovitch Martial Arts Academy offers the highest quality instruction of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu available. The program is lead by 4X World Jiu-Jitsu and ADCC World Champion Pablo Popovitch. His students have shown vast improvements in a short time winning and placing in many World Championship Tournaments. This is a result of the curriculum and program set up by Professor Pablo Popovitch.

Kickboxing 

Kickboxing came out of Thailand and over the last 50 years or so has quickly spread to Europe, Australia and North America. Although it is a full-contact sport, special care is taken to protect students from injury while still benefiting from the full demands of the sport. Hardly any other sport comes close in the physical demands and benefits of kickboxing. If you want to lose weight, tone your muscles or just be at your physical peak, then our kickboxing program is for you.

Mixed Martial Arts

Team Popovitch Academy offers an MMA program that will prepare an individual for combat in the cage ring or get you in the best of your life. Training is centered around the principals of Brazilian Jiu-jitsu. Wrestling takedowns and striking are also offered in specialized classes to fully learn each facet fully .
