Ottawa Branch

Ottawa Branch is one of over 30Branches of theOntario Genealogical Society(OGS). The Branch is responsible forCarleton, Lanark, Renfrew, Prescott and Russell Countiesin Ontario. The Branch consists of members who seek and share information about family history. Its mission is To encourage, assist and bring together all those interested in the pursuit of family history. If you want to discover more about your family origins, or how and where to locate information about your ancestors, hundreds of our members will be there to help you.

Memberships and Volunteers

Membership in the Ontario Genealogical Society provides for the ability to join Ottawa Branch. Please refer to the OGS Membership page for complete details.
