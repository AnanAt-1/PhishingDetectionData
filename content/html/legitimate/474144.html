MLB Stats for Ernie Lombardi

Ernie Lombardi Baseball Stats | Baseball Almanac

Ernie Lombardi, the Major League Baseball player, was born on Monday, April 6, 1908, in Oakland, California. Lombardi was 23 years old when he broke into the major leagues on April 15, 1931, with the Brooklyn Robins. Ernie Lombardi stats, height, weight, career totals, schools, positions, and more historical research are in the Baseball Almanac.
