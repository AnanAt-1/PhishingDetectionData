Families Can Count on Florida KidCare


Florida KidCare offers high-quality, income-based health insurance just for kids. Costs are based on a child’s age, household size and adjusted gross annual income. Many families pay just $15 or $20 a month to cover all qualifying children in the household.

Florida KidCare is designed specifically with kids in mind, giving them access to the health services they need throughout each stage of growth and development.

Easy, Year-Round Enrollment

Our simple, free application makes it easy to apply online through the Florida KidCare Parent Portal or call 1-888-540-5437 to request a paper application.

Enrollment is open year-round, so the best time to apply is always now!

Affordable Monthly Payments

Florida KidCare coverage is income-based and monthly payments start as low as $15 or $20 per month.

Some families will qualify for low-cost Florida KidCare coverage through the Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP). With CHIP, the government pays part of your monthly payment amount. This is called a subsidy.

But affordable options are available to families at all income levels.

Check out the Cost Calculator to estimate your monthly payment for Florida KidCare coverage.
