Our Story

At Lantic, our mission is to be a great company to partner with, work for and invest in, by offering a best-in-class portfolio of natural sweeteners. We believe life should be sweet!

Lantic Inc. is a proudly Canadian-owned company specializing in sugar and natural sweetener solutions. As a subsidiary of Rogers Sugar Inc. (traded as RSI on the Toronto Stock Exchange), Lantic has been a cornerstone of the Canadian food industry since 1888. From classic pantry staples beloved by households to industrial-sized solutions that support businesses across Canada and the U.S., our products fuel innovation in the food, baking, confectionery, beverage, agriculture and pharmaceutical sectors.

Headquartered in the historic Notre-Dame district of Montreal, Lantic operates a sugar cane refinery currently undergoing a transformative modernization through Project Leap. Complementing our Montreal operations is a second refinery on the West Coast in Vancouver, a distribution plant in Toronto, and Canada’s only sugar beet factory in Taber, Alberta. In addition, through our maple division, we operate plants across Quebec and Vermont, USA.

Lantic is proud to offer an expansive and growing product portfolio of trusted brands including Lantic Rogers, Rogers Golden Syrup, Nature’s RAW, Highland Sugar Works, The Maple Treat Corporation, to name just a few, ranging in size and format from retail to industrial specifications.
