PROGRAM SPOTLIGHT

Bloch Launchpad

Bloch Launchpad offers a unique professional and leadership development opportunity to incoming freshmen and transfer students, with up to $10,000 in scholarship funding. If you live in Kansas or Missouri and plan to major in business or accounting, Bloch Launchpad could be the program for you.

PROGRAM SPOTLIGHT

Professional MBA

Ranked as the best professional program in the region, the Bloch School Professional Master Business Administration degree is for college graduates or mid-career individuals who seek to enter or rise within the ranks of management. The curriculum focuses on experiential learning, with in-person and online classes scheduled to accommodate working professionals.
