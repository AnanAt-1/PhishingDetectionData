Isoft incorporated in June 1985. From inception Isoft has applied its resources entirely to the needs of the Life Insurance Industry in Canada. Through this concentrated effort, our people  have developed the ability to understand the business issues as well as the technical systems issues that confront its clients. Isoft uses these strengths to develop cost effective systems and enhancements resulting in improved productivity with a higher level of customer service and competitiveness for the insurer.
The application experience of our personnel includes Life, Disability, Critical Illness, Long Term Care, Medical, Dental and Creditor Claims processing. We have implemented solutions using Multi-tier client server, System/390 and AS/400 development platforms. In addition, we have combined these platforms using gateway technology to connect a Graphic Interface to a legacy mainframe system. 

In addition to supplying claims systems we have experience with selecting and implementing claims systems built by other vendors.
Our people have extensive experience with many areas of insurance data processing. This knowledge covers a multiplicity of hardware and software platforms including mainframe environments such as MVS, VSE, AS400, CICS, DLI, DB2 and IMS DB/DC.
In the client server environment we have expertise with an assortment of MS-WINDOWS development tools such as PowerBuilder, Visual Basic, SQLWindows and databases such as Sybase, Oracle, MS-SQLServer, SQLBase and DB2/2. To complement our systems expertise we have accumulated extensive experience with project management and systems design.
