GURPS: Blue Planet

This is an unofficial conversion of Biohazard Games' science-fiction roleplaying game Blue Planet into GURPS. The reason for the conversion is obvious: I was extremely impressed with their setting, but rather unimpressed with the system they provided. This rather rudimentary conversion is now obsolescent since an actual GURPS Blue Planet supplement (which I helped playtest) is on its way.
