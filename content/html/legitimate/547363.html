Place Telus Montreal

Montreal Downtown Skyscraper




Picture Alexis Hamel 2009:01
  • Add to my list
  • Next building


    Informations and facts

    Among the first modern skyscrapers in Montreal, The Telus tower was built at the same time that the 2 other major towers: Place Ville-Marie and CIBC Tower. This skyscrapers is the only achievement in Montreal of the internationally known firm architect Skidmore Owings & Merrill LLP (SOM). This firm has made the biggest buildings and skyscrapers in the world, including the gratter of them: Burj Dubai.

    Architect

    Skidmore Owings & Merrill LLP, Greenspoon, Freedlander & Dunne, Plachta & Kryton

    Constructor and other companies involved in construction:
    Dominion Bridge Co. , Shector Barbacki Shemie & Associés Ltée


      Gare Centrale, Place Ville-Marie Area

      This building is located in the «Secteur de valeur patrimoniale exceptionnelle Gare Centrale, Place Ville-Marie» AKA : "Area of outstanding heritage value Gare Centrale, Place Ville-Marie".
