Ramona Amiri - Miss World Canada 2005



Education: Graduate, University of British Columbia. Major in Biology.

Career Ambition: To follow in my mother's footsteps and become a successful dermatologist.

Current Goals: To continue my modeling career, travel the world and gain new life experience before entering medical school.

Words To Live By: Work like you don't need the money, love like you've never been hurt, dance like no one's watching.

Languages: English, French, Assyrian, Persian and some Spanish

Hobbies: Cooking, reading, fashion, riding motorcycles and belly dancing.

Proudest Achievement: Graduating with my degree on the Dean's Honour List at age twenty.

Favourite Food: Okra

Favourite Book: The Power of One by Bryce Courtenay

An Inspiring Canadian: Pierre Trudeau
