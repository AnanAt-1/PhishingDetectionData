Friz Freleng

AKA Isadore Freleng

Born: 21-Aug-1906
Birthplace: Kansas City, MO
Died: 26-May-1995
Location of death: Los Angeles, CA
Cause of death: unspecified
Remains: Buried, Hillside Memorial Park, Culver City, CA

Gender: Male
Race or Ethnicity: White
Occupation: Cartoonist

Nationality: United States
Executive summary: The Pink Panther

Cartoonist on Loonie Toons and Merry Melodies, inventing the characters Porky Pig, Yosemite Sam, and Sylvester the Cat. After creating over 300 cartoons, Friz later went on to create the Pink Panther animations.
