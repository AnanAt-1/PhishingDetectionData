WEBKDD 2001

Mining Log Data Across All Customer TouchPoints

http://robotics.Stanford.EDU/~ronnyk/WEBKDD2001/index.html
webkdd@cs.stanford.edu


August 26, 2001, San Francisco, CA

Held in conjunction with the ACM-SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery in Databases (KDD'2001)

The WEBKDD'01 workshop aims to bring together practitioners of web-commerce, wap-commerce, call centers, and brick-and-mortar stores with tool vendors and data mining researchers in order to foster the exchange of ideas and the dissemination of emerging solutions related to customer interactions across multiple touchpoints and to the customer retention and acquisition policies that can be derived from the analysis of these interactions.

Attendance in the workshop is by invitation only.
You must fill the "request to attend" form below if you wish to attend.

The workshop papers will be published by Springer-Verlag in LNAI (Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence) as a post-proceedings book.
