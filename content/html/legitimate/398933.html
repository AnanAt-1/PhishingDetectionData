Custom Injection Molding Services

August 29, 2012/29,341 Comments/in Uncategorized /by mcadmin

MERI China is a EMS contract manufacturing company with over 20 years of experience specializing in 2 shot molding technology, weld line elimination, mold cooling and hot runner technology. Its superior quality custom injection molding service uses electrical presses to come up with high tech custom molded parts either for consumer products, computer components, outdoor furniture, automotive or medical equipment including defense products.

Injection molding is the most commonly used manufacturing process for producing plastics from thermosetting and thermoplastic materials. The process involves material being fed into a pre heated barrel where it is mixed and poured into a mold cavity by a ram injector. In the mold cavity, the material cools and hardens to take up the shape of the mold cavity. Injection molding is ideal for manufacturing of large volumes of plastic products as it allows multiple copies of the same part to be produced in one shot or cycle. The result is a wide range of material selection at minimal labor cost and efficiency as the process has very limited scrap losses.

Our engineers and the mold design professionals oversee the manufacturing of each product to ensure accuracy and timeliness. An appropriate mold design ensures a fast and economical production with increased efficiency as there will be minimal shrinkage. MERI China has perfected the custom injection process, with the use of a set of professional equipment; CNC, EDM , SMP and wire cutting machines all come into play allowing designing of superior quality tools. CNC machining makes complex and more detailed molds in less time compared to other traditional methods. The electrical discharge machining (EDM) is built so as to facilitate the process of building complex molds.

Our Injection mold design experts will make sure that even your most complex designs are completed according to specifications in a timely manner. Our advanced injection mold machines and highly skilled production team will work closely with you to make sure your product is exactly what you need.
