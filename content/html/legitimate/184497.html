Our Story

After Dr. Carl Melcher was diagnosed with Barrett's Esophagus, a potential precursor to esophageal cancer, he thought there had to be a better treatment for symptoms than what was already out there. A medical professor at UCSD, Dr. Melcher created the study-backed MedCline Reflux Relief System as the better solution he was looking for. We are confident that the Reflux and Pain Relief Systems can truly help you get more restful, refreshing sleep that helps you live a better, healthier life.

Frequently Asked Questions

MedCline has two Sleep Systems. Which one is right for me?

We offer several sleep solutions to help you sleep better at night and feel better during the day. If you aren't sure which solution is best for you, we have created a Product Comparison Tool to help!

If talking it through would help, feel free to give our Sleep Specialists a call at 800-610-1607 or set up an appointment to chat at at time that works for you. We are here to answer any question you have.

Does insurance cover MedCline products?

MedCline products are not covered by private insurance plans. The only insurance plan that currently offers coverage is through the Veterans Administration (VA). For information about possible VA coverage, click ‘VA’ in the footer below.

Are MedCline products covered by HSA/FSA Plans?

Yes, MedCline Relief Systems are covered by most FSA and HSA Plans and can be purchased on our site using your plan-issued debit card. If you have questions about coverage under your specific plan, please contact your plan administrator.
