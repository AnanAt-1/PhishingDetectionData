  • Hi there! I'm going to use this space here to tell a little bit about myself.

    I have worked in the top ad agencies of Finland. And I was fortunate enough to bag some of the most prestigious awards in the advertising world. Too bad it turned out to be a double-edged sword. A very heavy burnout hit me in 2024.

    At that point I knew something had to change. I wanted to keep doing work that deliveres, but in a way that it doesn't suck the life out of me anymore.

    So feel free to hit me up when you need a fresh brain of a seasoned creative & senior art director. Or if you want to know more about my paintings. I'm at my best doing concepts that lean on the funny side, because life is too short for boring shit.
