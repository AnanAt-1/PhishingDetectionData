Elite 80 Expo Profile: Jaren Sina

Name: Jaren Sina

Position: Point Guard

HT: 6’0

Hometown: Lake Hopatcong, NJ

AAU Team: Hoop Heaven AAU

Strengths: Jaren is one of the top point guards in the country. He runs a team like a quarterback and has top notch court vision. He pushes the rock with his head and knows how to make plays. Unlike some point guards Sina also has a fluid outside stroke. When he gets hot his range extends from anywhere in the gym. He should have a few more inches to grow and if so look out.

Areas of Improvement: Like most grammar school plays Sina will have to get stronger once he hits high school. He also tends to fall in love with his jump shot and should look to mix it up a little especially in transition. On the defensive end his improved strength will help him guard his position better.
