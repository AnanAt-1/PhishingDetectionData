    The UCD Safety, Insurance, Operational Risk and Compliance (SIRC) Office is tasked with advising the university on a broad range of matters concerning health and safety. The UCD SIRC Office also manages the university’s insurance programme including all insurance claims and also dealing with day to day enquiries regarding insurance from the university community. 

    The SIRC Office also provides a range of safety training courses, and maintains a large document library to assist all staff and researchers in the University.
