KRZZ is an FM radio station broadcasting at 93.3 MHz. The station is licensed to San Francisco, CA and is part of that radio market. The station broadcasts Regional Mexican music programming and goes by the name "La Raza 93.3" on the air. KRZZ is owned by Spanish Broadcasting System. This station broadcast a majority of its content in Spanish.
