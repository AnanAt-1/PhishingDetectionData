Mission and values


Our goal is to enable managers and executives to improve performance, achieve their fullest potential and shape the future of business.

Exceptional leaders value lifelong learning. As a client-focused organization, we support your career advancement with relevant, experiential and research-proven learning solutions that apply to your day-to-day reality.

With a history of excellence spanning over seventy years in executive education, the McGill Executive Institute adheres to four core values that drive our strategy and the work we perform.

Which format is right for you?

On-campus programs

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the McGill campus with our in-person programs and dive into intensive, hands-on learning alongside industry peers in an engaging classroom environment. Led by instructors leading their respective fields take advantage of the dynamic Montreal setting. While on campus enjoy direct access to instructors with opportunities to network with your peers during catered breaks. On-campus programs provide a deeper connection with faculty and participants, making it ideal for those who thrive in face-to-face collaboration.

Discover learning on-campus

Online programs

Perfect for busy professionals, our online programs allow you to learn from anywhere in the world including the comfort of your own home or office. This accessibility facilitates collaboration with individuals from across the globe while participating in live virtual sessions with interactive content led by world-class instructors. These programs are structured to fit into your schedule without compromising on the quality of education that the McGill Executive Institute is known for.
