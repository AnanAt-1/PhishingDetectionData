Our Recruiters - Achieve Your Goal with us

The Indian Beauty and Wellness sector is projected to grow from INR 240,000 crore in 2024 to over INR 500,000 crore by 2030, driven by a rising affluent population, shifting consumer preferences, and organised retail expansion. By 2030, the sector is expected to generate 26.3 million job opportunities, with the Beauty and Salon sub-sector contributing 70% of these roles. To meet this demand, around 5.4 million professionals will need vocational training between 2022 and 2030, highlighting the need for enhanced training infrastructure in this thriving industry.

Source: Skill Gap Study for the Beauty & Wellness Sector of India 2023-2030, B&WSSC, March 2023
