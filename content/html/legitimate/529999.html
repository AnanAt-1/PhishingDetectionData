East of Chicago Pizza Company

Do you dream of making pizzas, experimenting with ingredients, and being your own boss? Dream no more. East of Chicago is the franchise opportunity for you!

Franchise Profile — East of Chicago Pizza Company

East of Chicago franchises enjoy a unique advantage as a result of their authentic taste, broad selection and high quality food, all at a competitive price. They have made Entrepreneur Magazine's Franchise 500 for six years in a row and made Success Magazine's Franchise Gold 200 List for three years running.

East of Chicago has an award winning pan crust, two other crust options, as well as an unlimited list of subs, salads, side orders, and their famous PB&J pizza dessert.

East of Chicago is a great franchise opportunity and they are looking to open more stores every year. They make it easy to be a franchisee because of their success with fundraising, amazing pizza, and how they give you the versatility to customize store configurations to meet your individual goals.
