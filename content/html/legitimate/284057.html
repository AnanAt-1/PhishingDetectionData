May 16-18, 2025: Golden State Pinball Festival at Lodi Grape Festival Grounds at 413 E. Lockeford St., Lodi, California, 95240. Hundreds of games on free play, silent auction, big raffle, adult & kid fun tournaments, Pinball University(R) workshops, swap meet, and more. For more info visit the website.

June 20-22, 2025: Southern-fried Gameroom Expo at Marriott Renaissance Waverly Hotel & Convention Center and the Cobb Galleria Convention Center, 200 Interstate North Parkway East Southeast Atlanta, Georgia. This is the 10th annual convention focused solely on classic arcades, pinball machines, and home gamerooms. The show boasts over 400 full-sized arcade and pinball machines, numerous consoles, vendors, speakers, and more. For more info visit the website.

July 19-20, 2025: California Extreme, at the Hyatt Regency in Santa Clara, California. This will be the 29th annual show. This is a video game and pinball show. All games on freeplay. Buy/Sell/Swap/Compete. Also, there will be a lineup of speakers and special activities. For more information send email, or visit the website.

October 15-18, 2025: Pinball Expo at Renaissance Schaumburg Convention Center Hotel, 1551 N. Thoreau Drive in Schaumburg, Illinois. This is the big show every year and this will be the 40th annual show. This show puts emphasis on the collector and enthusiast and usually includes a factory tour, auction, designer/artist/author autograph session, seminars, parts, machines, and other pinball-related merchandise. This show has major manufacturer support. For more info visit the website, or call 1-800-323-FLIP.
