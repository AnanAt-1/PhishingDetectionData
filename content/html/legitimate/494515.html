Highlights

DCCED Marketing and Economic Development Initiative

The Governor’s FY24 budget included funding to support an initiative to market Alaska and promote the social and economic opportunities in the state. DCCED will work with a qualified offeror to develop an initial marketing plan and advise on strategies to effectively market Alaska.

Alaska Broadband Office

The Alaska Broadband Office (ABO) is responsible for managing the State of Alaska’s federal program responses to the Digital Equity (DE) Act and the Broadband Equity, Access, and Deployment (BEAD) Program. These programs will bridge the digital divide by providing grants to eligible parties to build out broadband infrastructure and increase capacity for unserved and underserved Alaskans. Visit the ABO website for information and updates.

International Trade Assistance Grant (ITAG)

DCCED's ITAG program helps Alaskan small businesses enter and expand into international markets by offsetting certain export costs. Grant funding can be used for eligible export activities, including travel, completed before September 29, 2025. Visit our International Trade Assistance Grant (ITAG) page to learn more.
