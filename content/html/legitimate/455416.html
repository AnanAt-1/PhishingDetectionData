St. Mark's Church

Hello! We are a vibrant Anglican church in the Tollington area of Finsbury Park in London. Our heart is to see the area of Tollington transformed.

We meet every Sunday at 11am for worship, teaching and fellowship, as well as in small groups throughout the week. All are welcome to join! 

Go to our website to find out more and don't hesitate to contact us if you'd like to hear more.

The parish of St Mark, Tollington Park is committed to the safeguarding of children, young people and adults.

Safeguarding

Our parish of St Mark, Tollington Park is committed to safeguarding children, young people and adults from harm. We follow the House of Bishops guidance and policies and have our own Parish Safeguarding Officer (PSO). Our Diocese of London’s safeguarding pages contain vital links and information including contacts for the Diocesan Safeguarding Advisor who advises our PSO.
If you are concerned that a child or adult has been harmed or may be at risk of harm please contact the PSO, Rachel D'Souza ([email protected]) or the Diocesan Safeguarding Advisor, Jo Bingham ([email protected]). If you have immediate concerns about the safety of someone, please contact the police and your local authority’s children or adult social care services.
