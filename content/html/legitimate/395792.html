LOST: Male in Denver, CO


Hello,

My name is Stephany Hulstrom and my French BullDog's name is Puck. We live in Colorado. Denver to be exact. Puck was last seen on Tuesday August 16th, 2011 in the very early morning. He was last seen at the Overlook apartments on Colfax and Irving street in Denver.

My baby Puck is all Black, with a white spot on his chest. It makes him look like he is wearing a tuxedo. Puck will be two years old come December. He weighs 22 pounds. He is beautiful boy that is the most friendly dog you will ever meet. He wants everyone to be his best friend and thinks everyone is. He loves to play with me everyday and cuddle with me every night. He loves his Martha stewart tennis balls and loves to eat every food he can get his paws on. He has been the light of my life for the last two years. And now he's gone.

My search for Puck began on Tuesday morning the second I realized he was gone. A door was left open and he got out. I have flyers and posters everywhere in the apartments I live in and the surrounding neighborhoods. I have contacted every shelter in the Denver areas. I have posts on craigs list that I update every hour. I have put out several lost pet notifications online. Puck is Micro chipped and i stay in contact with the Micro chip company to see if they hear anything about him. They have put out lost pet reports as well. I check all the shelter websites everyday. I have called a lot of Vets in the area as well. I have had no luck on finding Puck and I am in such pain without him.

I need help in finding my baby. If you can help me in anyway I would greatly appreciate it. I miss Puck and I know he misses me. No one will ever love him like I do.

Please help me, anything helps.
