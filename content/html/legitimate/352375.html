VISUAL ARTIST & FILMMAKER DIMITRI LURIE

DodoFilm Productions is an artist-run, independent film company based in the city of Oslo, Norway. The company's profile includes production of artistic documentaries, short and experimental films, video art; photography, promo videos, documentation and live streams of various cultural events (Dimitri Lurie) as well as design and making of theatre and cinema costumes (Vera Mokhova).


Our work are to be found at The National Library of Norway, Videokunstarkivet -The National Museum (Oslo), Filmbib - Deichman Library (Oslo), LightCone (Paris), SKMU - Southern Norway Art Museum (Kristiansand), Cyland video archive (St. Petersburg).


We do also produce and curate various cultural events with an international dimension such as art exhibitions, film screenings and concerts.
