Google Nexus S(GT-I9020) battery

  Admin Sun Dec 12, 2010 4:23 am

The Google Nexus S is made by Samsung. Actually it belongs to Samsung Galaxy S series. The Nexus S is also called Samsung GT-i9020.

The Nexus S uses a 1500mAh rechargeable battery. Here is spec reported by Google.
Standard battery, Li-Ion 1500 mAh
Stand-by: Up to 713 h (2G) / Up to 428 h (3G)
Talk time: Up to 14 h (2G) / Up to 7 h (3G)
