New York City Housing Market Stats

As of 2008, the rental vacancy rate in New York City stood at 4%, relatively low compared to the rest of the country. There are about 4,599,325 housing units, averaging $502,800 in value, 59% of which are occupied by renters. The rent to price ratio in New York City is 0.2%, slightly lower than the New York average. For 19% of New York City tenants, 30% or more of their income goes to paying rent.

New York City Demographic Info

New York City has a population of 11,630,419, comprised of 4,218,683 households, with on average 3 members per household.
