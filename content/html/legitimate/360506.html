The Grandfather (Spanish: El abuelo) is a 1954 Argentine melodrama film of the classical era of Argentine cinema, directed by Román Viñoly Barreto and written by Emilio Villalba Welsh. It was based on the novel of the same name by Benito Pérez Galdó[1] and stars Enrique Muiño, Mecha Ortiz[2] and Elsa Daniel.[3]

  • Carlos Lagrotta as Dr. Gutiérrez
  • Alberto Barcel as Monseñor
  • Pedro Pompillo as Don Carmelo
  • Víctor Martucci as Amigo de Lucrecia
  • Lina Bardo as Vicenta

Other adaptations

[edit]

The novel has also been adapted in 1925 and 1998, both by Spanish directors.[4]
