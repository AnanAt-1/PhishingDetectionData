Welcome to Hodges Street Sails
A friendly loft for the repair of cruising and racing sails

Just a 2 minute walk from the Town Dock, Hodges Street Sails provides sail repair in the village of Oriental.

Sailmaker Laura Turgeon has over 20 years of sail repair experience having worked at Banks, Doyle and Ullman Rep. lofts. Gil Fontes provides technical & stitching support, and is our service manager.

  • Sail Repair
  • Sail Cleaning

All in Oriental, NC - North Carolina's Sailing Capital.

Hodges Street Sails provides real local service. Specializing in repairs helps us provide unmatched attention to detail while saving customers money.

We keep records of all repairs and items we service so that we may provide you with a plan to get the most out of your equipment and save in the process.
