a new Blackbox for Windows generation

  • bbKeys - configurable hotkeys
  • bbLeanBar - a fluxbox style taskbar
  • bbLeanSkin - window skinning

  • bbSlit - a side-(top-bottom-)bar for other plugins

  • bbStyleMaker - what you see is what you get
