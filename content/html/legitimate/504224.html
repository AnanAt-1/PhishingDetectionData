East Coast Countdown

The East Coast Countdown is a weekly, nationally-syndicated radio program that showcases the hottest in Atlantic Canadian music. The show was founded in 2004 by longtime friends Jack Bonaparte and Jimmie Inch, and has been hosted by Cameron MacEachern since 2014. The ECC airs on more than a dozen radio stations across North America.
