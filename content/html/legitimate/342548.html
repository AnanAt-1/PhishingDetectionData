    Welcome to Cardiff Triathletes

    Dive into the thrilling world of multisport athleticism with our Cardiff-based triathlon club! Join us as we swim, bike, and run our way through the challenges, forging friendships and achieving personal victories. 

    Fully coached sessions

    With our selection of level 1 and 2 coaches, we provide supervised swim, bike and run sessions all year round. These are included in the membership fee with no additional cost is required.

    Sessions

    Excellent community

    You'll find plenty of experience and enthusiasm in the club to help you enjoy the world of multisport. With plenty of social events, team races, and organised support our aim is to boost the profile of our club throughout the UK.

    Suitable for all levels

    Whether you're dipping your toe into the world of triathlon or a seasoned vet looking to go for their A race. We cater for all ability levels to help you hit your triathlon goals!
