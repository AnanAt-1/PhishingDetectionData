Centrally located in the West Island of Montreal, our hotel is only five minutes from Pierre-Elliot Trudeau International Airport (YUL) using our complimentary airport shuttle available from 4:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Experience our newly renovated Cognac Restaurant & Bar serving breakfast, lunch and dinner every day from 6:30 AM. Enjoy our spacious rooms and suites all equipped with microwave, mini-fridge, coffee maker and free high-speed Internet. Relax in our pool, hot-tub and sauna facilities or work up a sweat in our 24hr fitness room. We are also conveniently located near major shopping centers such as Les Jardins Dorval, Fairview Pointe-Claire, Rockland and Place-Vertu. Downtown Montreal is easily accessible by car or public transport. Perfect for business meetings, our hotel offers four newly renovated meeting rooms with full on-site banquet and catering services.
