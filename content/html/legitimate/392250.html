Like many lakes across Quebec, Lac Simon faces many concerns: blue-green algae; E Coli; ineffective septic systems; excessive lake front development; and increased boat traffic. The blog is a collection of articles from the Local Press that discusses the environment with special focus on water quality. The blog includes a Lac Simon Photo Gallery, sometimes accompanied by Letters. It will be sparkled with Go Green hints and Videos. The global perspective will be described in World View. Part informative, part activist but always progressive. The goal is to empower citizens to change lake culture and thus safeguard Lac Simon for future generations. Indeed, the prime culprits of lake pollution are property owners. However responsibility rests not only with residents but also with municipal & provincial governments. Too often municipal councils hide behind the tired & shameful "droit aquis" or look the other way. Moreover, citizens & all 3 levels of government must adhere to the principle: Responsibility to Protect. Our lake is indeed magnificent. Our children deserve our energy. Enjoy.

    Mission statement

    lacsimon.blogspot.com is an independent progressive web site which uses online photography to expose local environmental problems, and to force the solutions which are essential to a green & healthy future. Specifically we seek to:
    (1) protect the biodiversity of Lac Simon and
    (2) prevent pollution and abuse of Lac-Simon's land and fresh water.
