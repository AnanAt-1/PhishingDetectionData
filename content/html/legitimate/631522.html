KELLY KRUGER'S BEST HAIRSTYLES AND HAIRCUTS

by TheHairStyler.com
Updated on January 05, 2012

View yourself with Kelly Kruger hairstyles. We provide easy "How to style" tips as well as letting you know which hairstyles will match your face shape, hair texture and hair density.



1 of 2
Images © MediaPunch/TheHairStyler.com

Kelly Kruger Medium Wavy Dark Copper Blonde Hairstyle with Light Blonde Highlights

This highlighted mane is styled into large waves through the back and sides giving the over-all look plenty of bounce and movement making this hairdo perfect top frame a long face shape. This is easy to re-create at home with the right tools and products.


2 of 2
Images © MediaPunch/TheHairStyler.com

Kelly Kruger Medium Wavy Dark Honey Blonde Hairstyle with Blonde Highlights

This simple look is left out to sit on the shoulders showing off the jagged cut layers through the ends which lighten the length and allow the soft flicks of the waves added to the mid-lengths to ends. This low-fuss hairstyle doesn't take to long to style and will need product for hold and shine.
