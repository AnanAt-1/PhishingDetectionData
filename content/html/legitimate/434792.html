Welcome to Cook Forest Top Hill Cabins!

Escape to nature at Top Hill Cabins—your ultimate destination for relaxation and adventure in the countryside. Whether you seek peace or excitement, our cozy accommodations cater to every type of getaway. With a range of options, enjoy modern amenities paired with rustic charm in a variety of cabin options. Each cabin is complete with breathtaking views, cozy fireplaces, fully equipped kitchens, and private outdoor spaces.

Immerse yourself in the great outdoors, surrounded by pristine forests and majestic mountains. Our location provides endless opportunities for exploration, from hiking trails and biking paths to fishing spots. Furthermore, you can embrace the opportunity for birdwatching and wildlife spotting or simply soak in the refreshing air.

In the evenings, unwind around the fire pit beneath a starlit sky. To enhance your stay, our dedicated staff ensures your stay is seamless and enjoyable, allowing you to focus on rejuvenation.

Conveniently located near a wealth of local attractions, you can easily dive into charming towns, savor delicious cuisine, and explore cultural landmarks. To make your visit even better, our knowledgeable team is ready to help you create the perfect itinerary tailored to your interests.

Don’t wait—book your peaceful getaway at Top Hill Cabins today and experience the tranquility you deserve. We look forward to welcoming you to your new favorite escape!

Cook Forest State Park has over 30 miles of hiking trails and specific designated biking trails. For cycling enthusiests, you may want to try the Rail 66 paved 20 mile bike trail about 3 miles from our cabins. To learn more, check out this link at www.rail-66.com.

Our “Old Growth Forest”, at the “Forest Cathedral”, as well as the Fire Tower and Seneca Point, are popular hiking spots. If you’re interested, check out a video on our blog of the hike to the “Forest Cathedral”. In addition to hiking, there are other outdoor programs produced by Cook Forest State Park. For more information, call us at 800-531-1545 or call the Cook Forest State Park office at 814-744-8407.

About the Owners

Larry and Lois McFadden

UPDATE: It is with great sadness that we share the passing of Larry McFadden, co-owner of Cook Forest Top Hill Cabins, on June 28, 2024. Larry dedicated his life to providing a welcoming retreat for guests to enjoy the beauty of Cook Forest. His passion for hospitality, along with his love for the outdoors will always be remembered. We deeply appreciate the support of our loyal guests during this time.

—————–

Larry had worked for Owens-Illinois Glass Containers for 40 years until 2001. He started working for O-I in Streator, Illinois where he was born and raised. The McFadden’s transferred within Owens-Illinois from Rimersburg, Pennsylvania to Zanesville, Ohio and then on to Crawford, Texas and back to Cooksburg, Pennsylvania.

When it came time for Larry to retire in 2001, the McFadden’s looked into purchasing a business in NW PA largely due to Lois being born and raised in the area. Most of Lois’s family still resides in Rimersburg, Johnstown, Batavia, NY, Marienville, PA, Durham, NC and Ft Worth, TX.

In retirement, the McFadden’s are enjoying keeping busy with renting out cabins in addition to the comradery of other business owners in the Cook Forest area. Larry’s role in the business is doing everything!! Larry is a man of many talents and can do office reservations, etc as well as plumbing, rebuilding, and renovating cabins. Meanwhile, Lois does reservations, marketing, and advertising.
