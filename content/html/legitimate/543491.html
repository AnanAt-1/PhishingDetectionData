First Look: United SGP Dresden Klaus 9i Messenger Bag for iPad + iPad 2

Last updated: May 17, 2021 1:15 pm UTC
By Nick Guy

United SGP’s Dresden Klaus 9i Messenger Bag for iPad and iPad 2 ($75) is more of a combined tablet and accessory tote bag than a standalone case. The nylon book-style design has two zipper pulls that run around three of the four edges.

Advertisements

Opening it up reveals large neoprene pockets on both halves, as well as two smaller ones on the left. Either the iPad or iPad 2 can fit into one of the larger pockets, even if the iPad is already inside of a somewhat thick case.

Two metal D-rings on the back are used to attach the nylon shoulder strap’s metal clips. Leather accents are on the spine of the case, as well as on the strap.
