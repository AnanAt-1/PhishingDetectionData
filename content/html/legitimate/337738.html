WarwickBlogs has been retired

After more than 20 years service, WarwickBlogs was retired on 31st March 2025. It was first launched in 2003 and supported the creation of over 10,000 blogs, with over 200,000 entries. The technology used to support the application reached end of life, and its declining usage, coupled with the abundance of new, readily available blogging technologies, led to the decision to retire the service.
