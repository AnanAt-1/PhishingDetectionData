Welcome to J.J. Brown author’s books & journal.

Creatively driven, scientifically inspired. J.J. Brown is an independent author and publisher of books infused with a passion for nature, science and family. Her latest release is an intimate memoir of pregnancy, childbirth and raising special kids. When the Baby Is Not OK: Hopes & Genes revises the story of what to expect from newborn screening with hope. Independently published February 2025. Available most places books are sold as hardcover, paperback or ebook editions at Bookshop.org / Amazon / Apple Books / Barnes & Noble / Fable / Kobo.

J.J. Brown’s 10 prior book publications include novels Vector, a Modern Love Story (2011), American Dream (2012), and Brindle 24: The Last Day in the Life of a Town (2012). Short stories and novellas Death and the Dream (2011, audiobook 2014), The Doctors Dreams (2014), The Finest Mask (2016) and Mosquito Song: Dreams in Old San Juan (2018, audiobook 2023). Poetry collections Natural Supernatural Love (2012) and Comorbidity: Expressions of Love (2018, audiobook 2023). And for children, Stream and Shale Coloring Storybook(2013).
