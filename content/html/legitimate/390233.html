Arashi will release a new DVD concert

11 March 2008 - Arashi News

Arashi will release a new DVD concert entitled "Summer Tour 2007 Final Time - Kotoba no Chikara".

Release Date: April 16, 2008


Description: Concert DVD release from Arashi includes footage from the October 8, 2007 "Summer Tour 2007 Time - Kotoba no Chikara" tour finale at the Tokyo Dome. Concert features plenty of tracks from the group's latest album "Time," plus other singles like "Happiness" and more . . . and you've got to hear these MCs. Get ready for surprises in this outstanding, three-hour-long performance spanning a total of 37 songs (subject to change). Also includes a performance of the song "Fight Song" at the July 31 Yokohama Arena show plus photo set list.
