ABOUT MAXUM

Maxum BJJ is located in Islip Terrace on Long Island, NY and offers BJJ, MMA, wrestling, and kickboxing classes seven days a week. We welcome practitioners of all ages, genders, body types, levels of experience, and reasons for training. We're here to teach those who want to learn the world's greatest martial arts and have fun doing it.


We also offer family, friends, police, and active military discounts.
The hardest part is putting the nerves, ego, or fear aside and walking in the door. Don't be intimidated! Whether you’re 6 or 60 years old, come down to try a FREE class, learn the art of Brazilian jiu-jitsu, and join our great family.

CHILDREN'S
Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Maxum Brazilian Jiu Jitsu's Children's BJJ Program offers kids a fun, safe environment to learn self-defense, discipline, and confidence. Through engaging classes, children develop physical fitness, mental strength, and social skills, setting them up for success in life.
