    64. Re: Days Inn @ Wild Wild West
    13 years ago

    I guess I'm in the minority, but I have actually been in the WWW casino -- but not in the rooms or the restaurant.

    The casino was small and smoky. They had the first craps "tub" that I had ever seen, a tiny table. Would not let me set the dice -- I had to pick them up as they were shoved to me.

    The casino personnel were very gruff and unfriendly.

    The customers were OK, lots of truck drivers, some bikers -- a lot of wanna-be tough guys.

    I like dive casinos, but there was nothing about this dive that I liked. If I remember correctly, I went there in cowboy boots and Stetson (after all, it is named the Wild, Wild West), but it was more redneck than cowboy.

    I was not mugged, I did not get into a bar brawl, my car was not broken into, and no hookers propositioned me. There were no drunks passed out on the floor or on the bar. There were no piles of vomit on the floors (which I have seen at some fancy Strip resorts, often with preppy-looking college kids who appeared to have ownership claims to the piles).

    This is not my choice resort, but I am spoiled, and I enjoy tjhe amenities offered by Venetian and Wynn.
