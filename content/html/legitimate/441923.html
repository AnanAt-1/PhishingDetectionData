The actress and film producer used to live here.

Sold in 2018.

According to Roger Davies' 'Beyond the Canyon,':
"Once owned by Motown Records' Berry Gordy, the Beatles stayed here during their '66 tour, the movie 'Point Blank' with Lee Marvin and Angie Dickinson was filmed here, and Elvis would drop by occasionally to shoot guns in the basement shooting range"
