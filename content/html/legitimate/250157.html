The first ACiD Workshop was held from Friday 8 July – Sunday 10 July 2005.

Invited speakers:

  • Michael Fellows (University of Newcastle, Australia):
    Fixed-parameter tractability is polynomial-time extremal structure theory
  • Pavel Pudlák (Academy of Sciences of the Czech Republic):
    Quantum Deduction Rules
  • Carsten Thomassen (Technical University of Denmark):
    Variations on Grötzsch’s Theorem

We are grateful to the London Mathematical Society for providing financial support.

The workshop programme is available.
