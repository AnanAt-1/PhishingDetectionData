The 1986 Oakland Athletics played 162 games during the regular season, won 76 games, lost 86 games, and finished in fourth position. They played their home games at Oakland Coliseum (Park Factors: 93/93) where 1,314,646 fans witnessed their 1986 Athletics finish the season with a .469 winning percentage.
