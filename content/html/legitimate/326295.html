In accordance with the Victims' Bill of Rights Act of 2008:  Marsy's Law, the Criminal Docket Finder was developed to provide victims with information on the defendant's appearance at public proceedings.  The system does not provide juvenile data, and information will only be shown for those individuals with future court hearing dates.  

For more information, visit the District Attorney's website:
  • Marsy's Law
  • Branch Office Locations
