VFW Post 1647
​Brainerd MN USA
Veterans of Foreign Wars, for 125 years, are defenders of freedom from yesterday and today.  We are a non-profit 501 (c) (19) organization. Nestled in the heart of downtown Brainerd, you'll find us at 309 South Sixth Street. The Brainerd VFW is a friendly place, serving our country and our community.  f​
There are no strangers here, only friends you haven't met. Visitors are welcome.  Join our group.
Bingo every Monday at 6 pm. Hamburgers starting at 5 pm.
Tuesday Tacos from 5 - 7 pm. Cribbage starting at 6 pm.
Wednesday 4 - 7 pm, then 500 cards at 7 pm. Join the fun.
Thursday Burgers 5 - 7 pm. Fifteen menu choices.
3rd Sunday Breakfast 8 am to noon. Seven menu choices.
A popular gathering spot for many. Open seven days a week.

Our Mission
To foster camaraderie among men and women United States veterans of overseas conflicts. To help veterans, veteran families, the military, our community and our country.​
