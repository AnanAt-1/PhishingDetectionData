About

American Actress Diana Hyland was born Joan Diana Gentner on 25th January, 1936 in Cleveland Heights, Ohio, USA and passed away on 27th Mar 1977 Los Angeles, California, USA aged 41. She is most remembered for The Boy in the Plastic Bubble. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Diana Hyland was in 5 on-screen matchups, including Henry Winkler in Happy Days (1974), Robert Hogan in The Twilight Zone (1959), Roger Davis in The Twilight Zone (1959), Dick Van Patten in Eight Is Enough (1977) and Robert Reed in The Boy in the Plastic Bubble (1976).
