    Common Pushes Back 'The Dreamer, The Believer' Album Release

    Delayed releases are the theme for this year.

    Complex
    October 20, 2011
    None

    Although the date for Common's new album The Dreamer, The Believer was originally November 22, Com takes it to Twitter to announce that the album is now scheduled for a December release. The project is produced entirely by No I.D. and has seen two singles thus far, "Ghetto Dreams" and "Blue Sky."
