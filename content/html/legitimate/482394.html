Stephen Fitzherbert

1955-2010

Stephen Fitzherbert of Ottawa, ON passed away unexpectedly at his home on August 26, 2010.

Born in Bath, NB on June 21, 1955, he was the son of Vera Fitzherbert (MacDougall) and the late Percy Fitzherbert. Besides his mother, who lives in Bairdsville, NB, Stephen leaves behind his children April McEachern of Royalton, NB, Bryon and Kyle Fitzherbert, both of Duluth, MN; his sisters Debbie (Rob) Kovacos of Lethbridge, AB, Kelly (Chris Newton) of Aroostook; brothers Evans (Rhonda) Fitzherbert of Tinker, NB, Edwin (Brenda) Fitzherbert of British Columbia, Roger (Pam) Fitzherbert of Aroostook, Roderick (Rhonda) Fitzherbert of Hartland, NB, Andrew Fitzherbert of Oakland, NB.

Stephen's memorial service will be held at the Pentecostal Lighthouse Church in River-de-Chute on Thursday, September 2, 2010 at 2 pm. Rev. Claude Smith officiating. Burial will follow in the Wharton Cemetery, Upper Kent, NB. For those who wish, donations to the Salvation Army or to a charity of your choice would be appreciated by the family.

Arrangements have been entrusted to Brunswick Funeral Home, Perth-Andover.
