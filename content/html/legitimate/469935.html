ArticlesBase Updates and Latest Information for Our Readers and Community Members

Since 2005, ArticlesBase.com has been committed to serving the online community with free-to-read informative articles across multiple categories. We have been a pioneer in user-generated content. However, with the changing nature of the internet and how users interact with content online, we have considered pivoting from being a user-generated content syndication platform to providing value-based ingenious articles that are written by our expert authors and verified by subject matter experts.

As such, we have completely rebranded the site and made critical changes to the ways in which we operate. Here’s a list of updates we want to share with all our community members and followers:

  1. ArticlesBase has been relaunched with changes pertaining to design and appearance of the site; however, we continue to be a publishing label and are committed to our mission of making learning accessible to all.
  2. Currently, ArticlesBase is no longer accepting articles from writers; we have an in-house team of content creators and subject matter experts. This decision to disallow any article submission has been undertaken to serve the interest of the community by improving the quality of information in the articles we publish.
  3. At this point, we have articles across Tech, Finance and Business categories. However, we will soon be coming up with other sections including Health, Travel, Entertainment and News to provide our community the similar experience which their favorite go-to article reading platform had been offering for nearly two decades. We can commit to our readers that they’ll only find more value on the site with the relaunched version.
  4. Currently, we have suspended content for certain categories, but we will soon be coming up with valuable and utility-driven content across these segments as well. Here’s a list of content segments which are not live on the site anymore, but will be relaunched soon:
