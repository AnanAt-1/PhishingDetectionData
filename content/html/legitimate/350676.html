“Bonaire SCUBA”

Want to treat yourself to some of the best diving or snorkeling in the world? Our Bonaire immersion is a treat, we could even arrange for your SCUBA certification.Bonaire has been a pioneer in coral conservation since1979. One of the first places to establish a marine protected area (MPA). The park encompasses a reef surrounding the entire island. On the surface there is a rich history and some unique flavors to be experiences.
