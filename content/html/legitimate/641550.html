Eligibility

The Union Plus Credit Card program is available to those who reside in the 50 United States, Washington, D.C., or a U.S. Military location:

  • Dues-paying labor union members of participating unions
  • Retired labor union members
  • Working America members
  • Union family members may also be eligible

Need help?

If you need to ask a question, resolve a problem or get help, contact us.

Need something else? The Union Plus Member Advocacy Program, staffed by OPEIU Local 2 union members, is here to resolve any problems you have with a Union Plus program.

Contact us at 800-472-2005 (8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. ET, weekdays) or complete the Union Plus Feedback Form. If possible, please have the name or employee ID number for the Union Plus benefits provider staff member you spoke to.
