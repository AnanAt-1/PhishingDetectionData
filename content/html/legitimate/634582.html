SUPPORT FOR SLY JAMES’S EDUCATION AGENGA REFLECTED IN THREE NEW ENDORSEMENTS

** School Board Member Crispin Rea, Jr., School Board Member Arthur Benson, and School Board Member Joseph Jackson endorse Sly James for mayor **

KANSAS CITY, MO – Today Kansas City School Board Member Crispin Rea, School Board Member Arthur Benson, and School Board Member Joseph Jackson announced their endorsements of Sly James for mayor. They join School Board Member Kyleen Carroll and the Kansas City Federation of Teachers Local 691 in endorsing James, displaying a broad base of support for James from within the Kansas City education community.

“I firmly believe that if we want to be stronger, safer city, we must begin by giving our kids the best possible education starting today,” said James. “Knowledge is power. That’s why I’m going to work with these education leaders to improve our schools and give every child in Kansas City the tools they need to succeed and contribute to our city in the long-term. I’m honored to have their support.”

Crispin Rea Jr. and Kyleen Carroll are school board members at large; Arthur Benson represents Sub-District 1; and Joseph Jackson represents Sub-District 5.

Sly James’s education platform is part of his “Four E” agenda of employment, enforcement, education, and efficiency.

Some highlights of James’s education plan include giving every Kansas City family access to voluntary, high-quality early learning; visiting every school in Kansas City in his first four years as mayor; using the power of the mayor’s office to better coordinate resources; building a volunteer program to get city workers involved through volunteer and mentoring programs; and working to secure a world-class research facility in Kansas City.
