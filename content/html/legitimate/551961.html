About Jimmy

Since 1980, I have been selling and marketing fine homes in all price ranges across Shelby County, from downtown Memphis to Midtown, East Memphis, Germantown, Collierville, Eads and more. I pride myself on having built a career based upon trust, such an important matter when helping folks in a field that affects their lives in so many ways. 

I also take great pride in being President and co-owner of our wonderful firm, Marx-Bensdorf, REALTORS. Our entire team has an aligned philosophy, put into practice everyday, of "service over self".  Plus, we work diligently to make buying and selling Real Estate a pleasant experience for everyone involved. 

Year in and year out, I am one of the top selling agents in Memphis. This to say that my experience and knowledge of the marketplace is vast, to the benefit of those I'm honored to help. 

And, while I have the pleasure of marketing many good-sized homes, my 40+ years of experience working in many different price points has given me the working knowledge I need to handle properties of many kinds. I take great pride in helping friends, and friends of friends, no matter the price point.

Honest answers and skilled advice are trademarks of my service.
