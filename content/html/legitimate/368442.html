Departure and arrival
When Sun. 28 August, 10 am to 6 pm
Where L'Escalier de Montréal
552 Ste-Catherine East
Berri-UQAM Station

This list of targets for the photographic hunt Wikipedia takes Montreal is sorted by district (arrondissement), and then by borough (quartier). You can also sort columns as you wish before printing.

The point score for each target is calculated according to its distance from the starting point (Berri-UQAM), and. more importntly, its interest for Wikimedia projects. A picture that is not already on Commons is worth more than a picture far from Berri-UQAM.
