  • Mac & Cheese Fest Cancelled

    Unfortunately, the Dairy Days Committee decided Wednesday night (4/2/25) at their monthly meeting to cancel the 2025 Mac & Cheese Fest. Several factors played into the decision to cancel the event including low ticket pre-sales, low number of entries for the competition and the number of other events happening the same day in our community. We are sad and disappointed that we couldn’t make this event work for our community.

  • Mac & Cheese Fest Returns in 2025! Entries Open Now!

    The Platteville Dairy Days Committee is excited to announce the return of Mac & Cheese Fest! Join us for an afternoon full of Mac & Cheese Samples, music and raffles. The fun continues into the evening with more food, live music and 50/50 raffles! Go to our Mac & Cheese Fest page for complete details on cook-off entries and schedule of events.
