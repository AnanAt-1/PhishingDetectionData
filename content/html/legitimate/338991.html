WATCH ONLINE STREAM BOXING: Juan Manuel LOPEZ vs Oliver LONTCHI On PAY PER-VIEW ONLY


World Boxing Organization (WBO) Junior Featherweight champion Juan Manuel “Juanma” LOPEZ defending his title against undefeated North America Boxing Association (NABA) super bantamweight champion Oliver LONTCHI

World champion Juan Manuel Lopez of Puerto Rico will makes his 5th title defense when he takes the undefeated Olivier Lontchi of Montreal.

Lopez (25-0, 23 KOs), returns to Atlantic City where he captured his world title, dethroning defending champion Daniel Ponce De Leon via a first-round knockout on June 7, 2008. Lontchi (18-0-2, 8 KOs), a native of Cameroon who fights out of Montréal, Québec, Canada, has promised to put up a Canadian cold front that will ice Lopez’s winning streak and title reign.

Latin Fury 9 will be available on Pay Per View providers including iN DEMAND, DIRECT TV and the DISH NETWORK. The “Latin Fury 9” pay-per-view telecast, beginning at 9 p.m. ET / 6 p.m. PT, has a suggested retail price of $39.95. The telecast will be available in HD-TV for those viewers who can receive HD. For “Latin Fury 9” fight week updates, log on to www.toprank.com.
