Indiana - Allen County
Allen County Indiana has 50 places on the National Register of Historic Places including 2 places of National significance and 8 places of Statewide significance. Significant places include Allen County Courthouse, Johnny Appleseed Memorial Park, Allen County Courthouse, Craigville Depot and Embassy Theater and Indiana Hotel.

Many famous people are associated with these Allen County historic places including John(Appleseed) Chapman, Alexander Taylor Rankin, Hugh McCulloch, John Henry Bass, Perry A. Randall and Charles S. Bash.

Some of the country's most noteable architects helped create the Allen County places including Brentwood Tolan, Lima Locomotive Works, John Eberson, Wing & Mamurim, Charles Sipe, Frank Lloyd Wright, Wing & Mahurin, Alvin M. Strauss, Charles R. Weatherhogg and Benoit Msgr Julian. Prominent architectural styles found in Allen Country are Romanesque, Bungalow/Craftsman and Colonial Revival.
