CLAY TUCKER basketball profile


Clay Tucker is USA basketball player born on June 14 1980 in Lima, OH. He is a 6'5'' guard who most recently played at Team Facing Addiction in the The Basketball Tournament. Tucker graduated University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee in 2003. He was drafted by Oklahoma Storm (USBL) in 2003 in the 4th round (31 overall). Tucker is represented by Lat Am Basketball agency. He has played in Argentina, Spain, Italy, Lebanon, Puerto Rico, Russia, Turkey, Ukraine and USA.
