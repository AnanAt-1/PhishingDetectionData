Halloween is a 2007 reimagining of John Carpenter's Halloween. The film is about a young boy named Michael Myers who is commited to Smith's Grove Sanitarium for first degree murder after killing three people - his mother's boyfriend Ronnie, his sister, Judith Myers, and Judith's boyfriend Steve - at the age of ten. Fifteen years later, Michael escapes Smith's Grove in search for his baby sister, Angel Myers, now a high schooler named Laurie Strode, the only person he still truly cares for.

Written and directed by Rob Zombie.
