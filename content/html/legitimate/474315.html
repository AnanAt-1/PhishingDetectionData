The 1981 Montreal Expos played 108 games during the regular season, won 60 games, lost 48 games, and finished in second position. They played their home games at Stade Olympique (Park Factors: 101/100) where 1,534,564 fans witnessed their 1981 Expos finish the season with a .556 winning percentage.
