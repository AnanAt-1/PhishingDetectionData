George Frederick Gates

Private George Frederick GATES, Coldstream Guards, service no. 21478. Died on 29 Nov 1917, aged 36. Born at Boldre, Hants. Enlisted at Guildford. Lived at Witley, Surrey. Son of Thomas & Rosinea GATES. Husband of Alice Marie GATES of Candler's Lane, Marleston, Norfolk. Grave/memorial: Windsor Cemetery DN 18.
