Philippe IV. 'le Bel' Capet

Philippe IV. 'le Bel' was born in 1268 in Fontainebleau, France.  Philippe IV. 'le Bel''s father was Philippe III. le Hardi Capet and his mother was Isabelle Aragon, d'.  His paternal grandparents were Louis IX. "Saint" Capet and Marguerite Provence, de; his maternal grandparents were Jaime I. "el Conquistador" Aragon, d' and Yolande "Violante" Arpad. He had three brothers named Louis, Robert and Charles I..  He was the second oldest of the four children. He had a half-brother and two half-sisters, named Louis I., Marguerite and Blanche.  He died at the age of 46 on November 29th, 1314 in Fontainebleau, France.  He was buried in St. Denis, France.
