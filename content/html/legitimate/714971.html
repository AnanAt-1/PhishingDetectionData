Volcano Vaporizer Review

  • Posted by Kate Shulga

QUICK LINKS

The Volcano vaporizer has earned a well-deserved reputation among cannabis enthusiasts, with its status as a premium device remaining unquestioned. The reasons are simple: unmatched quality, precision, and ease of use. Made in Germany, the Volcano embodies the best of German engineering, known for its impeccable precision and bold visual design. This vaporizer is not only powerful but also aesthetically appealing.

The Classic model uses an intuitive knob to quickly adjust heat.

For regular vapers, the Volcano offers exceptional efficiency, extracting the full potential of your material in one session. You can enjoy cool, pure vapor at your own pace, savoring every inhale. The temperature control is simple and intuitive, with easy-to-use buttons and dials that make fine-tuning a breeze.

Tip: Don’t worry about burning your material. The maximum temperature setting won’t cause combustion, stopping 10° below the burning point, ensuring safe and efficient usage.

Pros and Cons: What You Need to Know

The Good:

  • Built to last with high-quality components; German engineering is renowned for its precision and durability.
  • Large vapor bags make it an excellent choice for group vape sessions with friends, allowing everyone to enjoy at once.
  • Perfect for medical users, as it delivers smooth, cool vapor with each inhale, preventing the harsh throat hit that other vaporizers often cause.
  • Simple to operate, allowing for large, satisfying inhales akin to smoking a bong.
  • Highly accurate temperature controls, ensuring you can fine-tune your session to perfection.
  • Extremely efficient herb usage — a great long-term investment for those who want to make the most of their material.

The Bad:

  • A large and bulky unit, making it less portable than other vaporizers.
  • Can be noisy during operation, and the vapor bags may be seen as wasteful by some users.
  • High cost — it’s a significant investment compared to more affordable options on the market.
  • Slower warm-up time compared to competitors like the Herbalizer.

How to Use the Volcano

A full balloon bag, the most efficient way to capture vapor, is about 2 feet long. Nothing is wasted or lost, and herb is efficiently used-up in about 3-5 full bags.

This orange valve ensures no vapor is wasted and balloon bags attach tightly onto the Volcano vaporizer base.

A simple knob twist can increase or decrease the heat intensity. Turn the heat down if your herb is looking too dry and nearly burnt, or turn the heat up if it’s still green after a full bag.

The balloon bag is completely filled with thick, rich vapor in under 30 seconds.

The Volcano’s digital temperature buttons make fine-tuning heat intensity easy and accurate.

Low Maintenance

Bags will naturally get dirty and brown over time, while the screens can become clogged and less effective with frequent use. To maintain peak performance, replace the balloon bags (3 for $10) and herb screens every 50 uses. A good wipe-down every few weeks will help keep your vaporizer looking fresh and prevent dust buildup.

The Downsides: Large Size, High Price, and Bag Usage

As with everything, there are a few downsides to consider. The bags can be crinkly, loud, and not particularly eco-friendly. The Volcano itself is also quite large — standing a foot tall and nearly 3 feet when the bag is inflated. However, these size-related drawbacks can also be seen as advantages. The large bag, while cumbersome for individual use, is perfect for group sessions and can last for around 50 uses before needing to be replaced.

Volcano Classic vs. Volcano Digit: What's the Difference?

The Volcano Classic and Volcano Digit are both top-tier vaporizers, but they offer different experiences based on your preferences.

The Volcano Classic features a simple knob for temperature control. While it's less precise than the digital version, it's incredibly user-friendly and offers easy adjustments for those who don't mind a bit of guesswork. It’s perfect for those who prefer simplicity and efficiency without needing exact temperature measurements.

On the other hand, the Volcano Digit comes with a digital LED display and precise buttons to dial in the exact temperature you need for specific herbs. This gives you full control over the vaporization process, ensuring that each strain is treated optimally. Some strains, for example, are more delicate and require precise temperatures for the best results.

If price is a consideration, you can save around $150 by opting for the Volcano Classic. However, if you value precision and want complete control over your vaporizer's settings, the Volcano Digit is the way to go.
