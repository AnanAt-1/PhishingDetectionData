Welcome to Our Association

 All of Indiana's 92 counties are members of the AIC. Membership consists of entire counties rather than individual office holders. They can be urban, suburban, or rural counties. Their sizes range from Marion County, with a population of more than 900,000 to Ohio County, with a population of slightly more than 6,000.
