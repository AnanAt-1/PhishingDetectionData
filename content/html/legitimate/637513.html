The Hilton Garden Inn Montreal Centre-ville hotel is a short stroll to the gates of McGill University, and minutes away from the world famous underground city, Place des Arts Theater, shops and boutiques on Vibrant Ste. Catherine Street, the downtown business district and Palais des Congres. The Hilton Garden Inn Montreal Centre-ville hotel is a full service hotel, predominately serving corporate travelers: with ample underground parking, sauna, whirlpool, spacious exercise room, indoor rooftop swimming pool and outdoor terrace with a fabulous view. This Montreal Centre-ville Hilton Garden Inn hotel in Quebec features 221 rooms each equipped with dual high-speed Internet access, two telephones featuring voicemail and speaker capability, large work desk, complimentary high-speed Internet, with conveniently placed lighting and outlets. Also available is the Herman Mirra ergonomic chair for comfort.
