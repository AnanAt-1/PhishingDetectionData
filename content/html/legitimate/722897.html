What Is A Batting Tee / Baseball Tee?

What is a batting tee? A hitting tee holds a baseball or softball in position, allowing the hitter to practice and improve their swing.

A batting tee height should be adjustable to allow the hitter to train to hit high and low balls and have a base that provides stability during practice. Some baseball and softball players prefer a lighter weight batting tee for to be more portable, while others prefer a heavy weight batters tee.

Baseball and softball athletes at all levels of play - from beginners to professionals - know that tee work with a batting tee is an essential part of training.
