WELCOME TO INGLENOOK DAIRY

When you purchase Inglenook Dairy products from your local store or drink a coffee for our customers that use our products, know your investing in the sustainability of the dairy industry and to those who really need the support.

What We Make

Milk

We offer a selection of award winning homogenised and unhomogenised milk in retail and bulk sizes.

CREAM

Our award winning cream contain no fillers or thickeners in retail and bulk sizes..

YOGURT

Pot set award winning natural greek yogurt from our farm to you. available in retail size.

BUTTER

Pure, churned, natural unsalted butter in retail and bulk sizes.
