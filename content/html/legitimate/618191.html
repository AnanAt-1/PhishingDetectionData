  PAGES ABOUT SILENT FILM

Jon's Film Sites Jon Marsalis has compiled the WWW's most complete list of links on silent film stars, studios and research.

has a searchable database that not only covers motion pictures but all other media. Many films in the Library's collection are not yet in the database and many titles that are listed exist only in the form of a nitrate positive and are unavailable for viewing.

Lobster Films This Paris-based private archive has been a leader in preserving 35mm nitrate silent comedies. They have a beautiful home page and hopefully will add more information about their comedy collection.

The Silent Film Bookshelf David Pierce's growing library of contemporary silent film trade press articles is an invaluable resource.

is Gloria Bond's collection of links, which discuss all aspects of Charlie Chaplin and silent film in general.

Silents Are Golden is a loving tribute to the art of silent film. The site contains many contemporary reviews of silents when they were first released, some lost forever.

Taylorology is primarily dedicated to scholarship about the William Desmond Taylor murder, but contains fascinating interviews with Taylor's contemporaries, including Chaplin, Keaton and Lloyd, not to mention scurrilous gossip about Larry Semon.
