    1. Re: copenhagen to Brusels fast train
    17 years ago

    There is no highs peed train to Brussels from Copenhagen.

    Denmark has no highs peed trains and ICE in Germany on many routes can not travel at true high speed.

    So it's a long and tedious journey taking everywhere from between 11 and 20 hours.

    However one of the possible connections is a night train from Copenhagen to Cologne and then the Thalys - true high speed - to Brussels.

    Trains are not cheap - it will probably cost about $200-300 one way depending on your age and it you get the good discount.

    Flying will always be a lot faster and most often cheaper
