Konami offers up a thematically interesting but sadly flawed dungeon crawler for the PSP. Read Review

Ren-Goku: The Tower of Purgatory is a dark, science-fiction themed RPG that features character designs by renown Japanese manga artist Jun Suemi. The game takes its name and overall themes from the Purgatory Cantos of the traditional poem in novel form, The Divine Comedy, but turns the concept of going to the farthest reaches of the world on a mission to cleanse the soul and redeem the being into a future tale of action and RPG adventure.
