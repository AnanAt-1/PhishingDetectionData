About

American Model (Adult/Glamour) Yvette Vickers was born Yvette Iola Vedder on 26th August, 1928 in Kansas City, Missouri, USA and passed away on 27th Apr 2011 Benedict Canyon, Los Angeles, California aged 82. She is most remembered for Playboy's Playmate of the Month July 1959. Her zodiac sign is Virgo.

Yvette Vickers is a member of the following lists: American film actors, American women singers and American television actors.
