FUENTE HEMINGWAY CIGARS - BOXES AND SINGLES  

The Hemingway's Perfecto shape is one of the most challenging cigars to make. The Hemingway blend of Dominican filler and binder wrapped in select African Cameroon produces a rich and complex medium-bodied smoking experience. The construction is impeccable and the burn, perfect. Also available in the coveted Connecticut Broadleaf Maduro wrapper in limited quantities, Arturo Fuente's signature Hemingway cigars are world renowned with good reason.
