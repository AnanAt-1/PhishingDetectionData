AsphaltPlanet.ca > Quebec > Autoroute 10

Western Terminus: A 720 in Downtown Montreal.

Eastern Terminus: A-55 in Magog

Regions: Agglomeration de Montreal, Ville de Longueuil, MRC de Rouville, MRC de la Haute-Yamaska, MRC de Missisquoi, MRC de Memphremagog 

Routing: A-10 begins from the Ville Marie Autoroute in Downtown Montreal.  It heads east across the Champlain Bridge into the Eastern Townships of Quebec.  The freeway ends at a trumpet interchange with A-55 between Magog and Sherbroke.

Links:

  • Eastern Townships Autoroute @ MontrealRoads.com -- Steve Anderson
  • A-10 @ AlpsRoads.net -- Steve Alpert
  • A-10 @ Quebec Freeways -- Félix Mathieu-Bégin
