    Fogarty Surname Meaning

    Irish: shortened Anglicized form of Gaelic Ó Fógartaigh ‘son of Fógartach’ a personal name from fógartha ‘proclaimed banished outlawed’.

    It is sometimes Anglicized as Howard.

    Source: Dictionary of American Family Names 2nd edition, 2022
