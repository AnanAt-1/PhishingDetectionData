Canadian actor
Ron Lea
Born (1952-11-02) November 2, 1952 (age 72)
Montreal, Quebec, Canada[1]
Years active1981–present

Ron Lea is a Canadian actor, who has had roles in Wind at My Back (1997–2000), Doc, Street Legal, This is Wonderland, Victor (2008), Flashpoint (2009), Good Witch (2015), and The Strain (2015).

Early life

[edit]

Lea was born in Montreal, and attended Concordia University.[1] He studied acting at the National Theatre School of Canada.[2]

Career

[edit]

Between 1990–1994, Lea played Brian Malony in Street Legal, for which he won the Gemini Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role in 1995.[1] In 2008, he was nominated for a Gemini award again, for his role as Cliff Barry in the television movie Victor (2008).[1] He starred in a single episode of Flashpoint (2009), for which he was nominated for a Gemini award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role.[1]
