PAUL has been writing fiction, non-fiction and drama since 1979. His work includes over 30 published novels, full-length plays, skit collections, screenplays and lyrics. As a producer, writer and director for Focus On The Family's Adventures In Odyssey radio series, he has written over 150 half-hour episodes. His work for Odyssey includes fifteen novels (with three more to come) and two screenplays for the best-selling animated videos. Additionally for Focus On The Family Radio Theatre, he adapted and directed a radio dramatization of Dickens' A Christmas Carol, the life of Dietrich Bonhoeffer and C.S. Lewis' The Chronicles of Narnia.

He is also the author of the critically-acclaimed Epiphany for Zondervan/Harper-Collins, Catacombs for Tyndale Publishing, the Time Twist series for Chariot/ Lion, and You Say Tomato (co-authored with the best-selling British writer Adrian Plass).

He has written a variety of popular plays including Pap's Place, Snapshots & Portraits, and the musical A Time For Christmas with renowned composer/ arranger David T. Clydesdale. He is the author of the forthcoming The Ultimate Drama Book for Monarch Books (UK).

Paul's works are published with such reputable companies as Tyndale, Word, Chariot/Lion, HarperCollins (U.K.), Baker's Plays (Samuel French, Inc.), Group Publishing, Lillenas (Nazarene Publishing House), Monarch Books (U.K.), and Contemporary Drama Services. Cumulative sales on his books have reached over 600,000.

Paul lives in Colorado Springs, with his wife Elizabeth and their son Tommy.
