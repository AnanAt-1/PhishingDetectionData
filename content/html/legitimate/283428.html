Other Scrabble Info

The best Scrabble resource for word study and lookup is Zyzzyva by Michael Thelen.

Statistics and Upcoming Tournaments and Historical Info are available at cross-tables.com, courtesy of Keith Smith and Seth Lipkin.

If you have more Scrabble questions, you might want to check Steven Alexander's Scrabble FAQ. You can find answers to nearly any Scrabble-related questions imaginable.

To play online with other internet opponents, check out the Internet Scrabble Club or the DOoM FAQ for crossword games.

Other Scrabble Clubs and Organizations

  • Mail Sherrie Saint-John for information about joining Scrabble-related mailing lists.
  • Mail Stella for information about joining the OSPD-specific Scrabble-related mailing list.
  • Metagrams has online games using both OSPD and OSW wordlists, as well as a free Windows CE-based study program.
  • Hasbro Scrabble has word definitions and an online anagram tool--just don't use it _during_ the game.
  • Wajin's Japanese Scrabble page (requires Japanese fonts)
  • BrainGamez claims to have an online Scrabble game.
