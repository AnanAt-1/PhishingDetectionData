Welcome to Casa di Perla - bomboniere4u - the original home of Bomboniere in Australia.

Casa di Perla (a Yiannopoulos store) is a pioneer of the bomboniere industry since members of our family were the first to introduce bomboniere to Australia more than 70 years ago.

We are very proud of our reputation as the finest designers and manufacturers of bomboniere in Australia. It ensures that we offer our clientele a wide choice of all the latest European fashion trends, in both traditional and contemporary designs, exclusively made for all occasions and requirements.

Through our web site, we look forward to continuing to provide you with our unique service and expertise that we have provided over the years through our retail store.
 
I welcome you to our online store and look forward to contributing towards making your occasion a very special one at affordable prices.

Olga
