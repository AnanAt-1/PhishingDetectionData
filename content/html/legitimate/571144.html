A PASSION FOR HOME FURNISHINGS

The Best Quality Home Textiles & Linens. Marini & Gerardi furnish the house with care and enthusiasm, creating endless combinations of materials and colours. Combinations that can be used for bed linen, in the kitchen for tablecloths and napkins, in the bathroom for towels and shower curtains, in the living room for transparent or dark curtains, for sofas, armchairs and much more.
