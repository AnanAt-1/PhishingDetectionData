    Coloring Outside the Lines for Over Two Decades

    IT'S ALIVE

    A NEW ERA

    Our new website is finally live—and it’s the biggest overhaul we’ve done in over a decade. We wanted it to reflect not just who we are now, but where we’re going. With fresh work, updated case studies, and more insight into the people and process behind the projects, this site feels like a proper snapshot of Invisible Creature in 2025. You’ll find new merch, new client collaborations, and even a few surprises tucked away. We’re proud of how far we’ve come and excited for what’s next—so take a look around, maybe pick up a hat or two, and let us know what you think. Massive thanks to our friends at Thread for bringing this site to life.
