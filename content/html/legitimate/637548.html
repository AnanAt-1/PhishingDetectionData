Discover an urban oasis at Courtyard by Marriott Los Angeles Burbank Airport in the San Fernando Valley, conveniently located near I-5 at Empire Avenue and close to Hollywood, Glendale, and Pasadena, as well as popular attractions like Warner Brothers and Universal Studios Hollywood. Enjoy fresh amenities, including a renovated lobby and bar with complimentary WiFi, The Bistro for dining options, and spacious guest rooms with modern amenities such as a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee maker. This pet-friendly hotel in Los Angeles also offers an expanded 24-hour fitness center, outdoor pool, and whirlpool for your relaxation.
