Actors needed for roles in upcoming arts center plays

  • Star Beacon
  • Sep 9, 2011
  • Sep 9, 2011

Novice actors and veterans are welcomed to audition for roles in upcoming Ashtabula Arts Center plays, says coordinator Kimberly Godfrey.

The next audition will be for A Doll's House by Henrick Ibsen and directed by Godfrey. The auditions will be held Sept. 18 from 1 to 3 p.m. and 6 to 8 p.m. Prepare a one-minute monologue. Callbacks (by invitation only) will be held Sept. 21 from 6 to 8 p.m. Rehearsals will generally be held Sunday through Thursday from 7to 9 p.m. beginning Sept. 25.
