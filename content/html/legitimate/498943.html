wedding photography & stories from around the world

We have been working as a team since the day we met over 15 years ago. Since then, we’ve traveled the globe to capture wedding stories of all kinds, in places like Bhutan, Hawaii, Greece, Vietnam, Guatemala, Paris and many more.

We get excited by dramatic lighting, high-energy moments, complex tableaus, kids being kids, film noir-inspired vignettes, and most of all, by genuine moments that are shared between loved ones. Our approach to wedding photography embraces all of this,

We are available for wedding and family photography in Canada, Costa Rica, and worldwide.

but mostly it centres around storytelling - capturing the story of a wedding day as it unfolds, and creating lasting memories in the form of art. Originally from Montreal, we spend most of the year at our home in Samara, Costa Rica with our son Max and daughter Charlie.
