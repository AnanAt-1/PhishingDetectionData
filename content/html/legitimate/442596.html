Welcome to Wagner Dance Arts

All Dance Studios Are Not The Same

 Wagner Dance Arts is awarded Mesa Arizona’s Best Year Round Performing Dance School.   Self-esteem is developed to prepare dancers to confidently perform on stage and in life.  Through our unparalleled LEADERSHIP  TRAINING PROGRAM, we  teach excellence in dance education. Dance will inspire you or your child. Fill out the form below to get more information and schedule your introduction which includes a group lesson and placement options.  Serving Gilbert and Mesa area since 1989,we are constantly improving our classes, instructors and facilities to ensure that we always provide our students with the best performing arts experience possible. Our training lasts a lifetime! 

 We provide professional, technically sound dance education in a fun and friendly family atmosphere and develop confidence for students of all ages. iOur Adult Program is amazing! Classes in Ballet, Tap, Jazz, Contemporary and Hip Hop. We include our unparalleled LEADERSHIP TRAINING PROGRAM. We also offer numerous performance opportunities throughout the year including our two annual professionally staged productions. 

Get Started Today

Our 4000 square foot Mesa Dance School is conveniently located in the Sprouts shopping center on the SW Corner of Gilbert and Brown in Mesa, AZ at 1949 E Brown Rd 85203.  We offer classes for absolute beginners all the way to progressive programs for the serious dancer.  From your very first class, you will be challenged and inspired. 

Fill out the form below and we’ll contact you to get your first class scheduled.
