How to retrieve your data

We've saved an archive of all of your blog posts!

There are two ways to retrieve your data.

You can either:

  1. Sign into Xanga and download the archive (for free, of course) from the link at the top of your dashboard, or
  2. Upgrade to Xanga 2.0 and we will upgrade your blog to the new servers using the data in the backup.
