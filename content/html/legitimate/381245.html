Sunday, June 5, 2011

More Details About the 35th!





Hey all- just wanted to update you on the cost of attending the reunion; it's only $ 55 Per Person.

How great is THAT?

The price includes a Mexican food buffet and two soft drinks. A no host bar- (meaning you pay for your own alcohol) will also be available.

So for less than a ticket to a Charger game you'll get great food AND lots of fun, memories and sparkling conversation. (And you never know what MM might do!)




So get back to the nice folks who are putting this party on- RSVP and send them your money!

The 35-Year Reunion for the Class of 1976

                        
                  Start planning to attend!




September 3rd, 2011

Town and Country Hotel in Mission Valley


The reunion committee is doing a great job at expanding the database of classmates so everyone can attend the party! (If you haven't been contacted, please send us an email!)


We will also be inviting all alumni from the Classes of 1975 and 1977 and as with the 30 year reunion, we’ll be inviting our teachers to come join us!  


Town and Country link for reservations and information.

Special Group rates for lodging have been obtained. 

https://resweb.passkey.com/Resweb.do?mode=welcome_ei_new&eventID=3394306

A dedicated booking website was created so you will be able to make, modify and cancel hotel reservations online, as well as take advantage of any room upgrades, amenities or other services offered by the hotel.


This blog is to give you information about the reunion as well as share information about classmates. If you have some photos or fun facts about yourself that you would like to share with the rest of the class, send them to grossmont1976@gmail.com and we will do our best to add them to this site.
