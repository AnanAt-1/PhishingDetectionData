Month of the Military Child

At Jones Magnet Middle School, we proudly recognize and honor the resilience, strength, and courage of our military-connected students. These young individuals face unique challenges and transitions with grace and determination. During the Month of the Military Child, we take a moment to show our appreciation for their sacrifices and the important role they play in our school community. Your Jaguar family sees you, supports you, and celebrates you!
