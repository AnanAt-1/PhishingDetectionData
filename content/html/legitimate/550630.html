About James Cecil

I am a 1962 graduate of the Reisch American School of Auctioneering, Mason City, Iowa, and began my auctioneering career on January 1, 1963. I have been fortunate to have the opportunity to conduct auctions in thirty-five states; although, the majority of the sales are in Arizona, New Mexico, and Texas. Auctions have included all types of retail industries, oil field production, construction equipment, drilling rigs, farm and ranch equipment and land, agriculture related businesses, dairy and registered stock, commercial and residential real estate, and various types of highly specialized equipment of all kinds. Also, in October of 1975, my business "James Cecil Auctioneers" was approved by the United States General Services Administration to sell all types of government owned property.

Our Philosophy

Time and time again, my theory is that a public auction is the very best way of disposing of assets. A well organized and properly advertised auction will create more interest than just putting an item up for sale. Also, it's a true statement to say that two hundred buyers will pay more for one item at a time than just one buyer will pay for two hundred items. Competitive oral bidding always creates the highest price for the seller. A well advertised auction also gives all parties involved equal opportunity.A list of references for your review will be sent upon request. Should you be planning an auction or if you need help in determining the type of liquidation best suited for you, James Cecil Auctioneers will be pleased to consult with you. If we can help you, we will tell you. If not, then we will also tell you why. There is no charge for the analysis. We would be pleased to add you to our list of satisfied customers.
