Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry

  1. Concordia University
  2. Faculty of Arts and Science
  3. Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry

The Department of Chemistry and Biochemistry offers rigorous and marketable degrees at the undergraduate level that can be taken in the co-op format, or combined with the Minor in Multidisciplinary Studies offered by Science College. At the graduate level, we offer master's and doctoral degrees with opportunities for research in both areas.

Special funding for out-of-province students

Up to $4,000 for undergraduate programs.
Up to $9,251 for Master's programs.
