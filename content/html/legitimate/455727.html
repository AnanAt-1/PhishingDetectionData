How Can I Get a Free Bonus Bet?

Australian bookmakers can no longer offer free bets or deposit bonuses to attract new customers. Mind you, sportsbooks can still run these promotions - they just can't advertise them to the public. Bonus bets must be advertised to existing customers only.

So if you're looking to join one of the new sports betting sites, you should do so based on that site's features, not its bonuses. In fact, if you see an advertisement for a site that will give you a free bet if you sign up, it's likely an offshore sportsbook and not a regulated Australian company.
