Mountain Home man dies in motorcycle crash

MERIDIAN, Idaho (AP) The Idaho State Police says a 56-year-old Mountain Home man died in a motorcycle crash on State Highway 21 northeast of Boise.

The ISP says Steven Roach was southbound Thursday afternoon when his BMW motorcycle left the right side of the road and went down an embankment. Roach, who was wearing a helmet, died at the scene.


The crash is still under investigation.
