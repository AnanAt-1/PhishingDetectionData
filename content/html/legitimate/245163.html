Durham Chapter Board Election

2023 Durham Chapter Board Director

Thank you for your interest in volunteering as a Chapter Board Director for the Durham Chapter of the Human Resources Professionals Association.

Nominations have now closed.

Chapter leadership will notify its members of who will serve on it’s Board of Directors for the upcoming Board term.

For more information and/or questions on the Chapter Board Elections, contact Justin Manuel:

By phone:

Chapter Elections Officer
416-923-2324 ext. 334
1-800-387-1311 ext. 334

by email:

 chapterelections@hrpa.ca

If you would like to volunteer in another capacity with your Chapter, please visit HRPA Volunteers and Committees.
