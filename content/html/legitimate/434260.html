Shreya Ghoshal

Music Department SoundtrackPlayback Singer
Born - March 12, 1984 in Rawatbhata, Rajasthan, India
Shreya Ghoshal is one of the India's most popular playback singers. She has received four National Film Awards, six Filmfare Awards and nine Filmfare Awards South. At the age of sixteen, she entered and won the television singing reality show Sa Re Ga Ma Pa. She made her Bollywood playback singing debut with Devdas (2002). She has recorded many songs for film music in various Indian languages.
