50 YEARS OF PORTLAND TIMBERS
June 21st 2025 will mark the 50th Anniversary of my first Timbers match, a 1-0 win against the San Antonio Thunder in 1975. So far the team is off to a 6-3-4 start and gotten 11 points in the standings the last 6 matches. Currently third in the table with 3-1-2 home record after a tough draw against LAFC and a 3-2-2 road record after an embarrisng loss at San Jose and a good draw at Salt Lake mid week.
