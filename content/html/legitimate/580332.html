    ARTIST PROFILE

    I am a Midwestern USA native, who graduated from the University of Wisconsin in 1992.

    Currently I use a pressure sensitive tablet and natural media emulation painting programs to create my art pieces. I use these painting apps as an artist’s tool to produce one-of-a-kind images.

    My choice of subject matter and painting style often is meticulous and detailed. I paint whatever attracts my eye. The process in which I paint is comprised of multiple experiments and paintings of one image. That is why my personal style is constantly changing and evolving.
    In past years, I have displayed my artwork in galleries on and off the Internet. My current work is being displayed at various on-line web galleries and private collections.
