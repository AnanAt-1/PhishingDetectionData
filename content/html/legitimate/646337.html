Norma Shearer (1902 - 1983)

Canadian actress, popular from the mid-1920s through the 1930s. Winner of an Academy Award for The Divorcee.

* August 10, 1902 Montréal, Canada
 as Edith Norma Shearer
† June 12, 1983 Woodland Hills, California, USA
