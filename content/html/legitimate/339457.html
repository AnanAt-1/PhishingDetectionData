BIO

Broadway: Rock of Ages, Sweet Charity, Beauty and the Beast, Peter Pan, Cyrano, Nine, Victor/Victoria, Sunday in the Park with George. Other NY: Encores! Concerts at Town Hall and other venues, numerous Off Broadway productions and national tours. Paul has played lead roles in theaters across the country and overseas. Extensive commercial and TV work.
