John Lawrence Delaney - 75, Halifax, passed away peacefully Friday morning, June 24, 2011, in Halifax Infirmary, QEII, surrounded by his loving family. Born November 11, 1935, in New Glasgow, he was a son of the late John and Dorothy (McKay) Delaney.

John was an active member of Saint Catherine of Siena Roman Catholic Church from 1962-2005 where he was an altar server and served on various committees and was also a member of Saint Agnes Parish. He worked at National Cash Register, starting out in the shipping department, delivery and finally in administration. John’s strong faith was an important part of his life and he passed that on to his children and grandchildren. He will be remembered as a devoted husband, loving father, grandfather and friend to all who knew him.

John is survived by his loving wife and best friend of 48 years, Anna M. “Anne” (Snook); son, John (Antigone), Vienna, Virginia; daughter, Mary (Jason) Shannon, Truro; grandchildren, who were the love of his life, Megan Delaney and Jacob and Matthew Shannon; sister, Genevieve (Donald) Bell, Melmerby Beach; many special nieces and nephews. The family would like to say a special thank you to the doctors, nurses and support staff at Halifax Infirmary for their compassion and excellent care of John.

John’s family will receive friends from 2-4, 7-9 p.m., Monday, June 27, 2011, in Cruikshank’s Halifax Funeral Home, 2666 Windsor Street, Halifax. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated 10:30 a.m., Tuesday, June 28, 2011, in Saint Catherine of Siena Roman Catholic Church, 6476 Bayers Road, Halifax, Father Roberto Donato officiating. Reception will follow in the church basement. Interment will be held in Oakridge Memory Gardens, Lower Sackville, at a later date. Family flowers only. If so desired, donations in John’s memory to Saint Vincent de Paul Society or a charity of choice are welcomed. Arrangements have been entrusted to Mattatall ~ Varner Funeral Home, 55 Young Street, Truro.
