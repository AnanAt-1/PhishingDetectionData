Intelligent Freight Management Software

Banyan Technology’s patented LIVE Connect® shipping software is the industry’s leading all-in-one platform for over-the-road (OTR) freight management.

Let's Chat

Smarter Freight Management Solutions Banyan delivers a smarter freight management solution with the most carrier connections for Less-Than-Truckload (LTL), Truckload (TL), Parcel and Final Mile shipping. Our LIVE Connect automated freight management software features a comprehensive transportation management system (TMS) and/or API connectivity supported by an expanded suite of AI and Business Intelligence (BI) tools.

Serving All Over-the-Road Modes

LIVE Connect offers innovative logistic operations solutions for all OTR freight management modes, streamlining operations across North America.

Truckload (TL): Expanded features including direct API carrier integrations, load boards, predictive pricing, geofencing for added security, intra-Mexico shipping and enhanced flexibility and speed for Truckload freight management.

Less-Than-Truckload (LTL): With more than a decade of leadership in LTL freight management technology and more carrier connections than any other provider, Banyan is setting the standard for efficiency and innovation through scalable and flexible freight management solutions.

Final Mile: Delivering access to a network of more than 10,000 final mile carriers across the U.S. and Canada, offering multiple pricing options for primary and secondary markets including white glove and threshold services.

Parcel: Providing competitive rates and real-time tracking for small package shipping, ensuring efficient deliveries, in addition to opportunities for significant savings on parcel operations.

Comprehensive Shipping Solutions

Banyan’s comprehensive shipping software streamlines OTR freight operations management for Shippers, 3PLs, carriers, and supply chain partners, helping boost efficiency and reduce costs across operations.
