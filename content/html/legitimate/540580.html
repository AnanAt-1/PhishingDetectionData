Hotel Blake

A Historic Hotel in Downtown Chicago

Uncover a unique city experience at our boutique hotel in the heart of downtown Chicago. Hotel Blake, known previously as the 19th century Morton Building, breathes new life as a premier Chicago luxury hotel along the historic Printer’s Row. Guests will enjoy personalized service and a quaint and welcoming vibe near the top attractions and things to do in the city. 

Listed on the National Historic Registry, Hotel Blake offers guests authentic Chicago experiences curated by a team devoted to our colorful city. Feel refreshed after spending the night in one of our 162 guest rooms and suites and take comfort in our selection of convenient hotel amenities, including a 24-hour business center, fitness center and on-site dining.

Explore a new side of Chicago during your next stay at Hotel Blake.

Exceeded expectations!

The hotel is beautiful, clean and conveniently located. We took the train and it was a 10 minute Uber ride from the station. We walked most places and it was March so the temp was still pretty cold. The Harold Washington Library is across the street and was a wonderful visit. The restaurant attached was lovely and there was a marvelous coffee shop, Hero, nearby. The staff was lovely and the high ceilings in the room were beautiful.

Classy and spacious

Having never visited downtown Chicago and all it sights, we selected this hotel for its location. We were very pleasantly surprised. The rooms are spacious and nicely appointed. Be prepared for a little downtown noise, though it does get nice and quiet in the middle of the night. The hotel staff are excellent and the hotel location is ideal for touring the sights in and around Chicago (Willis Tower, Cloud Gate - the Bean, Buckingham Fountain, the art museum are all walking distance). Starbucks right across the street. Mels restaurant is in the hotel though we had a great dinner and a so-so dinner there. We were told their breakfast is superb, but we eat light and preferred Starbucks. If you need adjoining rooms there are a limited number so make sure you are specific about it when you book. Hotel Blake made our visit to Chicago just right and we heartily recommend it.
Alex W.
