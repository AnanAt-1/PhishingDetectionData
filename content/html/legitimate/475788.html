Jacob Two-Two Episode Guide -Nelvana Limited


Based on the children's books of Montreal author Mordecai Richler. Jacob Two-Two was named after Richler's real-life son Jacob. Jacob Richler saw early episodes of the series in advance and approved of them highly.
