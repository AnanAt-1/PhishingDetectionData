This page consists of a selection of School Internet Access Policies, also known as "Acceptable Use Policies" (AUPs). Sites are organized provincially with a link to each policy. To identify links, the World Wide Web was searched using the search engine Google.

Every effort has been made to identify a range of policies, from the very brief to the more in-depth. If your school library's policy has been missed and you would like your policy included on this site, please email Dr. Alvin M. Schrader.
