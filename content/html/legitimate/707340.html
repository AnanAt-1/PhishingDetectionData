GALILEO empowers, connects and improves the lives of Georgia residents as the state’s virtual gateway to credible, authoritative library resources.

GALILEO Programs

GALILEO Search

This research portal provides access to thousands of subscription-only information resources that are not available through free search engines or internet directories.

Visit website

Affordable Learning Georgia

This initiative promotes student success by supporting the use and creation of affordable alternatives to expensive commercial textbooks used on college campuses.

Visit website

Digital Library of Georgia

This program collaborates with Georgia libraries, archives, museums, and other educational and cultural groups to provide access to key resources on Georgia history and culture.

Visit website

GALILEO Interconnected Libraries

This initiative provides students, faculty and staff with expanded access to library resources at all 26 University System of Georgia institutions.

Visit website
