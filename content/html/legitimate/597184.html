Comets' Comeback Takes Down Utica City FC in OT

March 28, 2025 - UTICA, NY - Late-game heroics helped guide the Kansas City Comets (15-6-2) to their largest comeback victory of the season as they defeated Utica City FC (12-9-2) by a 7-6 score in overtime on Friday evening at the Adirondack Bank Center in Utica, New York. The Comets trailed by three early in the contest and once again in the third quarter, but a second-half comeback was completed when Stefan Mijatovic found Nacho Flores for the equalizer to erase the deficit with 14 seconds left in regulation.
