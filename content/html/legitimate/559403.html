Fireworks in Quebec City and Lévis

The Grands Feux Loto-Québec are back in force from July 31 to August 23, 2025 for a 29th edition. Come experience a world-class pyromusical event that promises to amaze you!

A free event on both sides of the River

We are proud to present the only major event in the Capitale-Nationale and Chaudière-Appalaches regions that is free and open to all!

  • Quebec City

    Place des Canotiers is located in front of the côte de la Montagne, in Old Quebec.

  • Lévis

    The Paquet dock is located in the sector of the traverse in Lévis.
