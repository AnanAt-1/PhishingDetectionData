What parts/products does Mack make?

Mack primarily manufactures heavy-duty truck parts and products, focusing on components specifically designed for Mack trucks. This brand offers high-performance engines, rugged transmissions, durable axles, and robust Mack suspension components. If it makes your Mack haul, climb, or conquer any terrain, there's a good chance it bears the Mack badge. Mack dabbles in powertrain components for other heavy-duty vehicles and even the occasional marine engine. So, think of Mack as the muscle builder for the heavy-duty world sculpting the giants that move our mountains and fuel our industries.

What vehicles are Mack parts used for/in?

Mack parts are used exclusively for Mack trucks, which include models like the Mack Anthem, Mack Granite, Mack MD Series, Mack LR, Mack Pinnacle, and Terrapro. These parts are specifically designed and engineered to maximize the functionality of Mack trucks. However, Mack engines are utilized by trucks of various kinds, making Mack parts suitable for a broader range of heavy-duty vehicles beyond just Mack trucks.
