Eastern Townships Peaks


Owls Head - Appalachians (Eastern Townships)

The southern limit of the region is marked by the tall mountains lying along the border separating Quebec from the states of Maine, New Hampshire and Vermont. The mountains of the Eastern Townships rise to an average height of 760 metres. Some of the highest peaks – Mount Hereford, Mount Megantic and Mount Gosford – are related to the White Mountains of New Hampshire, while others, like Mount Sutton and Mount Orford, are a continuation of the Green Mountains of Vermont.
