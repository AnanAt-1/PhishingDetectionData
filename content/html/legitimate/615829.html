Exclusive Interview: PLL's Tyler Blackburn

As things heat up between his character and Hanna, get to know the cutie who plays Caleb, the bad boy on Pretty Little Liars.
By Neha GandhiPublished: Feb 22, 2011

If you're still giddy with excitement from the love that's finally happening between Caleb and Hanna on Pretty Little Liars, you're definitely not alone. Sean who, right? That's why Seventeen sat down with Tyler Blackburn to get the dish on what life on the PLL set is like, and what Tyler's really like, when he's not playing Caleb, and you know, hanging out in the shower with Hanna.

17: What's your character Caleb like, in a nutshell?

TB: Caleb's definitely had a rough life and had to fend for himself for a long time. So he's pretty street savvy. He's a hustler in some ways. You know the girls in Rosewood have a certain air about them, and he kind of laughs it off—he doesn't really take that. So in a way, he feels better than them.

17: What's it like being the newbie on set?

TB: It's just familial. You get there, and everybody's just like, "How was your weekend? Did you see any good movies?" The girls just welcomed me with open arms, and we exchanged music and that type of thing. And then they got me to join Twitter. So it's been cool. It's been so fun.

17: What music have they introduced you to?

TB: Well, Troian and I have very similar taste in music. She and Shay and I talked a lot about this band, called The XX, who we're really into. They're from England. I saw them this year, and they blew my mind. I actually gave Shay The Black Keys, which is my favorite band. They're kind of bluesy-rock, and just amazing. It's fun to exchange that with them and see if they have similar interests—and they do!

17: What do you do with your downtime on set?

TB: I usually just read or talk to people. I actually just finished reading The Hunger Games. And I heard they're making it into a movie, and I'm just like, "What do I need to do to get in there?!"

17: Things between Caleb and Hanna are clearly heating up, but if you had to pick, would you want to date Hanna, Aria, Emily or Spencer?

TB: I'd like a little bit of all of them, as far as personality goes. Like, I find something appealing in all of them. But I guess Aria would be the closest to someone that I would date. At the same time, I really like Spencer's intelligence and her quick comebacks, so it's hard to decide!

17: What about things you don't like? What are your real-life relationship dealbreakers?

TB: I don't like cattiness, or people who are too judgmental of others, or other situations. I don't like that. It's a big turn-off.

17:[SPOILER ALERT] Hanna and Caleb finally had their first kiss last night. Can you remember your first kiss?

TB: Yeah, yeah, yeah. It was 8th grade—that was the first time that things started shifting a little bit, and it was like, 'Oh, girls don't have cooties.' My first kiss was with a girlfriend. And it was at a party. I think I was hopeful, that that would happen, but I didn't plan it. You know, honestly, I think if there's a little too much planning, it's almost just like self-sabotage. Because then, you want it to go a certain way and it just doesn't. And then you're like, 'Awww, why!?!'
