Transmission Fluid Exchange Services

Stay on top of your transmission maintenance and schedule a transmission fluid change at your local Jiffy Lube®. 


How Transmission Fluid Works

Transmission fluid helps lubricate, cool, and clean internal components of the transmission. It also helps maintain the hydraulic pressure necessary for the transmission to function properly.


How Often Should I Change the Transmission Fluid?

You should change your transmission fluid according to your vehicle manufacturer's recommendation. However, since the transmission is so important for your vehicle, you should also be aware of some common warning signs, including:

  • Delayed engagement
  • Slipping
  • Burning smell
  • Strange noises
  • Leaking fluid
  • Check engine light illuminated

If you run into any of these issues, bring your vehicle in for a transmission service as soon as possible. 


Choose Your Local Jiffy Lube

Changing transmission fluid yourself can be a messy, complicated process. Save time and energy with a transmission fluid exchange appointment at your nearest Jiffy Lube®. A trained technician will remove the used fluid and replace it with new transmission fluid that meets or exceeds your manufacturer's specifications. If you have any questions about your transmission or transmission fluid, don’t hesitate to ask! Our friendly, knowledgeable technicians are more than happy to answer any questions or concerns and provide recommendations based on your vehicle, such as how often you need to bring your vehicle in for transmission service.
