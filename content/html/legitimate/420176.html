About Dolores Shelter Program:
The Dolores Shelter Program (DSP) began in 1982 as a sanctuary for refugees. Today, DSP provides support and emergency housing for up to 85 working homeless men every night.

We provide hot meals every night to our shelter guests, and we maintain pantries at each shelter, as well as our community center office. We can use canned or packaged foods that are instant or microwaveable, as well as low sodium and vegetarian choices.
