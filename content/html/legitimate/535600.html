Announcements

  • For Prospective Continuing Berkeley Students
    • Continuing Berkeley students interested in applying to the business major, LSBE or M.E.T. programs may complete the Haas prerequisite UGBA 10 (Principles of Business) at a community college. Please utilize assist.org to identify approved UGBA 10 equivalent courses.
    • The admissions committee has no preference for applicants who take UGBA 10X at UC Berkeley or those who take pre-approved Principles of Business courses at community colleges. Both courses will be viewed equally.

Admissions

If you’re considering applying to the Berkeley Haas undergraduate program, you’re about to embark on an exciting journey that could shape your future in countless ways.

Community

Entering the program in cohorts, students are members of a close-knit, supportive peer network. Through their diversity of ideas and backgrounds and shared experiences at Haas, they build bonds that will last a lifetime.

Student Experience

The Haas experience offers opportunities for personal growth beyond the classroom, including leadership development, career development, and close interaction with staff, faculty and alumni.

Our Programs

Spieker Undergraduate Business Program

This highly competitive program accepts applicants from high school, transfer, and continuing UC Berkeley students.

Global Management Program

A freshman admit program for students interested in learning about business from a global perspective.

Robinson Life Sciences, Business, and Entrepreneurship Program

A joint-degree program for Berkeley students interested in studying both biology and business.

Management, Entrepreneurship, & Technology Program

A dual degree program for students interested in studying both engineering and business.
