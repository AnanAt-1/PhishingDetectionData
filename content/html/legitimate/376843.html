Felton Cemetery



Felton Cemetery is located in the N/E quarter of section 4, Akan Township. According to the 1874 Akan plat map, the cemetery shares its boundries with the Rev. Jacob Felton and the B. Higginbotham properties.

From Richland Center travel West on State Hwy. 14 about 13 miles to Sand Hill Rd. Turn South on Sand Hill Rd. and proceed about 1/2 mile. Your first right turn will be Town Line Rd. Travel on Town Line Rd. to the top of the hill. Felton Cemetery is on the south side of the road.
