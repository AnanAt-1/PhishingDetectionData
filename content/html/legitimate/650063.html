About

Meaghan Rath is a 38 year old Canadian Actress. Born on 18th June, 1986 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, she is famous for her roles as Adena Stiles in the YTV coming-of-age comedy-drama series 15/Love, Tatianna Caine in the series Aaron Stone, Rigby Hastings in the N sitcom The Assistants, Mary Margaret in the 2010 teen vampire sex comedy horror short film You Are So Undead, Sally Malik in the Syfy supernatural drama series Being Human, Sue in the 2013 romantic comedy-drama film Three Night Stand, May in the Fox sitcom New Girl, Nicole Mullen in the ABC mystery anthology series Secrets and Lies, Officer Tani Rey in the CBS action police procedural drama series Hawaii Five-0, Klair in the CBC sitcom Schitt's Creek, Astrid Berney in the CTV original comedy series Children Ruin Everything, and Parker in the Hulu original sitcom How I Met Your Father in a career that spans 2001–present. Her zodiac sign is Gemini.

Meaghan Rath has been in an on-screen matchup with Aaron Abrams in Children Ruin Everything (2022).
