    Ohio BMV Office Contact Information

    Search Ohio DMV offices near zip code:

    The Ohio Department of Motor Vehicles has several locations throughout the state to serve your driver and motor vehicle-related needs. Use the list below to find the OH BMV location nearest you that provides the services you require. In addition, information such as address, telephone number, and BMV hours of operation are listed for each office.

    Each Ohio BMV location handles different services.The most frequently requested services are applying for a noncommercial or commercial drivers license, getting an ID card, or getting new license plates. In some areas, you may have to visit one location to get a drivers license and visit another BMV center to take your road test. Although, some locations offer some or all of your Ohio BMV service needs. Refer to the list below for services offered at the location of your choice.

    Check to find an OH BMV location that has Saturday hours. You will also learn the holiday hours for each BMV in Ohio once you select on a location.
