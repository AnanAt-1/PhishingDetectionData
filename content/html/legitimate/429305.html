Saturday, September 10, 2011

VIDEO: Sauve, Anthony Talk Canucks Camp


Vancouver Canucks rookie camp is underway. Here are some CanucksTV interviews with former Saint John Sea Dogs defenseman Yann Sauve and current/former forward Steven Anthony.


The Canucks will play in the Penticton Young Stars Tournament beginning tomorrow. Their full schedule looks like this:
September 11 – Vancouver vs Edmonton
September 12 – Calgary vs Vancouver
September 14 – San Jose vs Vancouver
September 15 – Winnipeg vs Vancouver
Along with Sauve and Anthony, former Sea Dogs goaltender Karel St. Laurent and current Saint John defenseman Ian Saab are also on the Canucks roster.

From the Canucks website:
Rogers Sportsnet Pacific and Rogers Sportsnet West will broadcast games Sunday, September 11th beginning at 7:30 p.m. (PT) Vancouver Canucks versus the Edmonton Oilers prospects and Monday, September 12th beginning at 4:00 p.m. (PT) Vancouver Canucks versus the Calgary Flames prospects.
All eight games of the tournament will be aired live on Canucks.com.
