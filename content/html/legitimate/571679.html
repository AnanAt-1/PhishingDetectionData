    • MASC (Mid-American Soccer Classic)
      The Mid-American Soccer Classic (MASC) tournament, highly regarded as one of the best-run events in the midwest region, is held annually in April. This event is played on all grass fields at Fairfield Optimist Park and Joyce Park fields, both located in Fairfield Ohio. 
      The MASC is jointly hosted by Fairfield Optimist Soccer Club (FOSC) and the Fairfield Optimist Club (FOC) and is staffed mostly by volunteers from both organizations.
