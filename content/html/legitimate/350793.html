Welcome to DFK International/USA

DFK International/USA is an association of independent, full-service public accounting and consulting firms with offices serving major markets throughout the United States. The association is affiliated with DFK International, with over 300 offices in over 80 countries.

DFK provides independent member firms with a forum to exchange practice management information and technical expertise, opportunities for continuing education and leadership development, and the resources to serve their clients on a national and international level.

Membership Benefits

Knowledge Sharing

Knowledge sharing is a key member benefit of DFK, fostering an environment where professionals can exchange insights, best practices, and solutions to common challenges. Through regular peer group meetings, members have the opportunity to collaborate, learn from each other’s experiences, and stay updated on the latest industry trends and regulations. This collaborative approach not only enhances individual expertise but also strengthens the collective knowledge of the entire membership, creating a more supportive and informed community.

Conferences & Committees

Conferences and industry, service line and operations committees are valuable aspect of DFK that provide unique opportunities for professional growth and networking. By attending conferences, members gain access to industry leaders, thought-provoking sessions, and the latest trends in accounting, helping them stay at the forefront of the profession. Special interest committees allow members to dive deeper into specific areas of accounting, engage in focused discussions, and collaborate on relevant projects. These offerings not only enhance members’ expertise but also foster a sense of community and engagement within the profession.

Staff & Leadership Development

Staff and leadership development is a key member benefit that empowers individuals at all levels of an organization to grow professionally. Through targeted training programs, members can enhance their skills, build leadership capabilities, and prepare for advanced roles within the accounting field. This focus on development not only helps members excel in their current positions but also supports long-term career growth, fostering a pipeline of skilled professionals who can contribute to the continued success and evolution of the accounting profession.

National & International Coverage

National and international coverage is a valuable DFK asset that broadens members’ perspectives and opportunities in the accounting profession. By being part of a network with global reach, members gain access to a wealth of resources, insights, and best practices from across the country and around the world. This coverage helps members stay informed about international standards, regulations, and trends, ensuring they remain competitive and adaptable in an increasingly globalized market. Additionally, members can connect with trusted peers for business referrals, enhancing their ability to form valuable partnerships and expand their client base. Whether for networking, knowledge exchange, or professional development, this wide-reaching network enhances members’ ability to thrive in diverse and dynamic environments.
