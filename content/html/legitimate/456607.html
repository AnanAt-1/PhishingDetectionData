About the Game

Welcome to Airline Empires, where you can run a virtual airline and compete against hundreds of other players just like you! If you like the idea of buying jets, flying all over the world and making money but can't afford to do it in real life, this is the game for you. AE is completely free to play (no hidden fees or in-game purchases), so why not register an account and try it out?
