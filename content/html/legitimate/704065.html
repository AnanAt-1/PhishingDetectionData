TMPGEnc Authoring Works 4

Want more menu templates? Then you've come to the right place. Menu templates let you quickly create menus based on a theme; just add your favorite videos! Download free menu templates or purchase our Premium Menu Template Packs to add even more template options to TMPGEnc Authoring Works 4!

Note: These template pack installers cannot be used with any software other than TMPGEnc Authoring Works 4. However, projects created in TAW4 using these templates MIGHT be importable into TMPGEnc Authoring Works 5 if the same template exists there already. Due to differences between version 4 and version 5, we cannot guarantee full importability.

Directions:

  1. Download: Click on the download link for the template pack you want to download (some templates must be purchased first).
  2. Install: Once the download is completed, double click the file to start the installation process.
  3. Create! After it's done installing, your new menu templates will show up in the menu template list ready for your next project!

Uninstalling:

  • To uninstall a template pack, simply run the installer again and choose the uninstall option.
