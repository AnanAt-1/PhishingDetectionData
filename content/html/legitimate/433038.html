About the Area

Lowell, Michigan, is a small city located in Kent County, about 15 miles east of Grand Rapids. Lowell is best known for it’s charming downtown area, historic buildings, and the scenic Flat River that runs through the city. The city also boasts a rich history, including ties to the railroad industry and early Michigan settlers.
