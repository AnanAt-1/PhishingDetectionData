October 10/11 11:16 am - Roc D'Azur: Bisaro 7th


Posted by Editoress on 10/10/11
 

The 28th edition of the Roc D'Azur took place from October 5th through 9th in France

Below are the Elite and U23 Women's results from October 8th.  2010 World Champion Maja Wloszczowska was first across the line.  Kiara Bisaro, former CDN National team member, now living in France  finished 7th.
