Hercules: The Legendary Journeys

6 Seasons, 111 Episodes

Hercules: The Legendary Journeys is an American television series. It was produced from 1995 to 1999, and was very loosely based on the tales of the classical culture hero Hercules (Heracles). It ran for six seasons, producing action figures and other memorabilia as it became one of the highest rated syndicated shows in television history.
