BANGSA SUG DECLARATION: ASSERTION OF INDEPENDENCE, DINAR AND DIRHAM IS THE CURRENCY!!!
Neldy Jolo

Sultanate of Sulu has been in existence long before the concept ofnation was born. Its sovereignty can never be questioned. It had signed the international treaties between Spain, America, France, Germany and Britain. It has also long trade relations with these countries, including the Peoples Republic of China.


The Judge reading the rules on Kosovo's Declaration of Independence on 17 February 2008.

After long been longing for total and complete freedom and liberty, 17 November 2010 would be the sacred and historical date and moment for the people of Sulu, the date that could remind them of the rebirth of their country's independent, the Sultanate of Sulu.
The people group themselves as United Tau Sug People, the citizens of the Sultanate of Sulu. The cause of the people here is legal and followed and adheres to the principle of international law. The people are committed to the principle the international peace and security by “denouncing war and renouncing terrorism.”

22 July 2010, reported by Dan Bilefsky posted at http://www.nytimes.com/, that “World Court Rules Kosovo Declaration Was Legal”.

By these treaties, Sultanate of Sulu was strongly recognized as sovereign and independent country of sovereign native people. No country that could ever signed any treaty with other country if it does not recognized each country's sovereignty. Sovereignty is perpetual.


Serbia was actually against the declaration of Kosovo's independence that thought, the courts' decision could trigger other secessionist movement in the world. The most important here are the recognition and conferment of other countries that legitimized Kosovo as independent and sovereign nation-state.

Bilefsky reported that, “Kosovo's unilateral declaration of independence from Serbia in 2008 did not violate international law, the United Nations’ highest court said Thursday in a ruling that Kosovo heralded as a victory but that legal experts warned could spur separatist movements around the world.”

Bilefsky quoted Hisashi Owada, president of the International Court of Justice, saying that the “Kosovo's unilateral declaration of independence from Serbia in 2008 did not violate international law, the United Nations’ highest court said Thursday in a ruling that Kosovo heralded as a victory but that legal experts warned could spur separatist movements around the world.”

Kedudukan Sebenar!!

In LAUREL VS. MISA, 44 O.G. 1176, the Philippine Supreme Court said that,

“The absolute and permanent allegiance of the inhabitants [TAUSUG CITIZENS] of a territory [SULTANATE OF SULU] occupied by the enemy [FILIPINOS OF REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES] to their legitimate government [SULTANATE OF SULU GOVERNMENT]

or sovereign is not abrogated or severed by the enemy [PHILIPPINE] occupation, because sovereignty of the government or sovereign de jure [SULTANATE OF SULU] is not transferred thereby to the occupier [FILIPINOS OF THE PHILIPPINES], and if it is not transferred to the occupant [FILIPINOS], it must necessarily remain vested in the titular government [SULTANATE OF SULU].

What may be suspended is the government [SULTANATE GOVERNMENT] of the territory [SULTANATE OF SULU] occupied by the enemy [FILIPINOS] which passes temporarily to the occupant [PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT].

And, since sovereignty itself is not suspended and subsists during the enemy [PHILIPPINE] occupation, the allegiance of the inhabitants [TAUSUGS] to their legitimate government [SULTANATE OF SULU GOVERNMENT] or sovereign, SUBSISTS and therefore,

there is no such thing as suspended political laws of the occupied territory [Sultanate of Sulu] are suspended or held in abeyance during the military occupation [by the Philippines] is a rule intended for the government of the civil inhabitants [civilian Tausugs] of the occupied territory [Sultanate of Sulu] and DOES NOT BIND THE ENEMIES [Tausugs of Sultanate] AT WAR".

Rujuk/see: Ruffy, et al. vs. Chief of Staff, 75 Phil. 875.

Mantan Presiden ABIM ke-5


Saya ucapkan setinggi tahniah atas penulisan buku ini. Bagi saya yang telah mengikuti perjuangan Bangsa Moro sejak tahun 70-an lagi, setelah membacanya, kefahaman saya mengenai perjuangan suci Bangsa Moro semakin jelas. Fakta-fakta yang dikemukakan begitu meyakinkan. Kekeliruan mengenai siapa Sultan yang sah dan layak untuk menuntut kedaulatan Kesultanan Sulu juga terhurai dengan kajian yang susah untuk dipertikaikan.

Penerangan mengenai asal usul Bangsa Tausug sebagai bangsa majoriti di Kepulauan Sulu banyak membantu kefahaman mengenai pelbagai bangsa yang menghuni Kepulauan ini yang dinamakan Bangsa Moro oleh Penjajah Sepanyol.

Apa yang menarik dari buku ini ialah penulisan sejarah yang agak lengkap bagi sesiapa yang ingin mengkaji dan memahami perjuangan Bangsa Moro yang dikatakan sebagai perjuangan yang terpanjang dan yang paling berdarah umat Islam didalam sejarah penentangan Penjajah Barat.

Saya rasa buku ini harus menjadi bacaan wajib bagi sesiapa juga yang terlibat didalam sebarang usaha damai di Selatan Filipina. Kegagalan untuk memahami sejarah ini sebagaimana yang tercatit didalam buku ini, adalah suatu kesilapan besar, kerana nantinya penyelesaian tidak akan tercapai kerana kegagalan memahami sejarah dari perspektif pejuang.

Tahniah sekali lagi kepada Asreemoro kerana inisiatif yang membuka mata ini. Semoga ALlah ta'ala jua akan membalas segala usaha dan perjuangan saudara dalam mengangkat martabat Kesultanan Sulu.

Ikhlas,

Ahmad Azam Abdul Rahman
Pengerusi,
Future Global Network Foundation
Kuala Lumpur, 7 April 2009

Tumpung Tausug


PENJELASAN:

Tumpung dalam bahasa Sug bermaksud tabung dalam bahasa Melayu, kata asalnya adalah TAMPUNG yakni menampung sesuatu. Kamahardikaan bermaksud kemerdekaan. Tabung ini telah dilancarkan oleh Council of Regents Sultanate of Sulu di Jolo, Sulu atau Rumah Bichara pada 17 Disember 2009.

Tujuan tabung dilancarkan adalah untuk menghimpun dana rakyat Tausug agar dapat menunjangi program demi program ke arah terealisasinya kemerdekaan Sulu darul Islam pada 2010 dan selepasnya. Pada masa sama Tausug dapat menyimpan wang sebagai simpanan masa depan mereka.

Tabung hanya terbuka untuk rakyat Tausug sahaja di seluruh dunia. Urusniaga menggunakan matawang dari alamat semasa penabung. Kalau mereka tinggal di Amerika Syarikat maka urusniaga menggunakan USD, demikian RM (Ringgit Malaysia), DS (Dollar Singapore), DB (Dollar Brunei), P (Peso Phil.) dll.

Dua Tabung:
Tabung terbahagi 2,

Pertama: Tabung Kansiya. Maksud kansiya sendiri adalah tabung dalam bahasa Spanyol (el-Kansya). Council of Regents (Rumah Bichara) jadikannya sebagai jenis tabungan. Polisi Tabung Kansiya berbentuk tetap-bebas secara bulanan. Iaitu simpanan boleh dibuat setiap bulan serendah RM2 seorang tanpa mengira peringkat umur.

Jumlah simpanan pula bergantung berapa banyak yang hendak disimpan. Urusan boleh dibuat melalui wakil-wakil yang dilantik, buku simpanan turut disediakan dan data akan disimpan secara online. Tempoh matang simpanan adalah 6 bulan, ia boleh dikeluarkan oleh penyimpan secara PINJAMAN selepas tempoh matang dan perlu dikembalikan secepat mungkin kerana ia adalah simpanan TETAP-BEBAS. Bebas bermaksud simpanan boleh dibuat dan dikeluarkan pada bila-bila masa sehingga kemerdekaan. Keuntungan atau dividend tahunan ialah 20%.
