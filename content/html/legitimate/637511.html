Auberge (Motel) Le Pigeonnier is located at the entrance of Easter townships of Quebec, 40 minutes' drive to Montreal. It is also the start point of the Brome-Missisquoi wine route and the perfect stop for the cyclist whoever from Montreal or from the border of the United States. The 2 star establishment is pursuing 3 star's quality with the continuous renovated rooms, utilities and more value added services. For the tourist, there are all kinds of activities in the region: cycling on the green-route, visiting zoo-Granby, discovering wineries, watching hot-air balloons, experiencing parachuting, playing golf, deer hunting, Mountain skiing. For the business or other type of visitors, it is also an ideal place to stay out of home.
