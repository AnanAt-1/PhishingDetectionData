Welcome to The Kung Fu Studio!

Where the individual works to become the artist of their own story.

ANCIENT WISDOM FOR MODERN SELF-DEFENSE

We are a premier martial arts school providing a modern self-defense solution through the ancient wisdom and teachings of the Shaolin Temple.

We offer certified instruction in Shaolin Kung Fu and Wing Chun. We are one of very few elite schools in the world certified by the internationally famous Ving Tsun Museum to teach both the Hong Kong Wing Chun System as well as the Shaolin WIng Chun system. We are also a certified member and representative of The World Shaolin Wing Chun Martial Arts Federation.

The Shaolin Wing Chun Kung Fu Martial Arts System is based in science and teaches harmonizing with the laws of physics so that practitioners can attain maximum efficiency in human combat.

Although the system is renowned for teaching the close quarter combat efficiency of Wing Chun, we also ensure that our students are proficient and effective in all ranges of martial arts to include long kicking, long striking, short striking, and grappling.

The teaching methodology we use to pass on this knowledge is the same method that has been used in the Shaolin Temple for centuries.

Progressing through the Shaolin Halls Method, a practitioner learns to control their mind, body, and emotions so they can be both effective and efficient in daily life, as well as in situations where self-defense or combat may be required.
