Just Sick:MAN RAPES THEN BRUTALLY KILLS HIS OWN SISTER

  • news
  • by brother, murder, rape, sister
  • Leave a comment

This news article is from the Detroit Free Press and it is disturbing….We Warn you….

Jesika died early Thursday morning, following a New Year’s Eve party at her mother’s and stepfather’s Highland Township home, said Oakland County Sheriff’s Detective Lt. James A’Hearn. Steven Kelsey was home on Christmas leave from the U.S. Army and temporarily sharing a room with his sister.

About 1:30 a.m., he went into their bedroom, investigators said. “I got a knife, and I killed her,” A’Hearn said Kelsey told investigators.

He stabbed her in the throat and, because she was in pain, stabbed her in the head, A’Hearn said. He added that Kelsey told investigators he believed his sister was alive while he sexually assaulted her.

Her mother, thinking Jesika was sleeping in after the late party, found her daughter’s nearly nude body and a bloody knife in her bed on Thursday afternoon. Steven Kelsey was nowhere to be found.

Steve Kelsey,faces life in prison without parole.
