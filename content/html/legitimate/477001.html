BUNKER, Edward
         
The Animal Factory

New York: Viking, (1977).

Sold

Hardcover. First edition. Fine in a fine dustwrapper. Prison novel by an ex-con. Bunker has appeared often as an actor in small, hip roles (most notably in *Reservoir Dogs* as Mr. Blue). Bunker also co-wrote the screenplay for another hit cult film, *Runaway Train*. This novel was filmed and directed by Steve Buscemi, who appeared in the movie along with Willem Dafoe, Edward Furlong, and Mickey Rourke. Bunker both wrote the screenplay and appeared in the film. Seldom found in this condition.

Item #79044
ISBN: 0670127094
