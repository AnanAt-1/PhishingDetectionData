HOW TO USE OUR SERVICES

From your registered Home, Office or Cell phone

  1. Dial our access number 1-313-828-3733.
  2. Listen for your balance, then dial the number you wish to reach.

From an unregistered phone (e.g. a hotel or friend's house).

  1. Dial our access number 1-313-828-3733.
  2. When prompted enter your account number (which is your phone number) and security code.
  3. Listen for your balance, then dial the number you want to reach.

Please note - toll free access rate is 2¢/min higher.

When it is best to use the local access number?

  1. When using the service from your cell phone
  2. or if you have an unlimited usage long distance plan with your land line carrier
  3. or you have a VOIP provider such as your cable company or Vonage etc.
